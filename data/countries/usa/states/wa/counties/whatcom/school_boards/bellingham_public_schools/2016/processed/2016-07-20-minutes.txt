OPENING ITEMS
Meeting Date and Location
July 20, 2016 – Western Washington University Office – 1155 North State St., Bellingham, WA
Call to Order/Roll Call
Board President Camille Diaz Hackler called the meeting to order at 2 p.m. Present: Directors
Kelly Bashaw, Douglas Benjamin, Camille Diaz Hackler, Quenby Peterson, Steven Smith, and
Superintendent Greg Baker.
INFORMATIONAL ITEMS/DISCUSSION
Board Community Engagement: The board members discussed their role in community
engagement, including their roles in advocacy, visibility, accessibility, and participation. They
spoke about linkages and how they currently are structured and whether any refinements might be
made.
Annual Calendar: General review and discussion of the draft 2016-17 board meeting calendar,
including regular meetings, linkages, study session, school visits, and policy reports. This will be
an agenda item at the August 25 regular meeting.
Linkages and Study Sessions: General discussion regarding potential topics for 2016-17.
School Board Adoptions of Schools: Review of draft proposal.
WSSDA Boards of Distinction: Discussion regarding the application process. It was agreed to
begin work on draft language.
OTHER DISCUSSION ITEMS
Board Meetings: Review of start-of-meeting protocol and refinement of agendas at board
meetings.
Tour of Happy Valley Elementary School: The board recessed at 5 p.m. and resumed the meeting
at 5:15 p.m. at Happy Valley Elementary School, where they toured the new building.
