Blaine School District No. 503
  Special Board Meeting:Executive Session
Wednesday, January 12, 2022
  Minutes
  1. Special Board Meeting
A. Call to Order
In accordance with guidelines related to the COVID-19 pandemic emergency, this meeting was conducted with both virtual and in-person components. Live public observation of the meeting's opening and adjournment was provided online.
  President Thomas called the meeting to order at 7:02 p.m.
  Directors Thomas, Creydt, and Gibson were in attendance in person when the meeting was called to order.
Director Nunamaker was in attendance virtually during the executive session.
    Todd Nunamaker, Dougal Thomas, Charles E Gibson, Erika Creydt
  2. Executive Session
A. Executive Session
President Thomas announced that the board would move into executive session for the purpose of reviewing the performance of a public employee. The executive session was anticipated to last for approximately two (2) hours, and no action would be taken by the board after the executive session. Live online observation of the boardroom stopped and the executive session began at 7:05 p.m.

At 8:58 p.m. it was announced verbally in the online meeting that one (1) hour would be added to the executive session.

At 9:58 p.m. it was announced verbally in the online meeting that the board would continue their discussion in executive session for one (1) more hour.

At 11:00 p.m. it was announced verbally in the online meeting that the board's discussion would again continue for up to an additional thirty (30) minutes.
  3. Reconvene and Adjourn
A. Reconvene and Adjourn
At 11:12 p.m. the board reconvened into regular session.
There being no further business, the meeting was adjourned by a unanimous vote.
Motion by Erika Creydt, second by Charles E Gibson
Final Resolution: Motion Carries 3-0, 1 not present at vote
Yea: Dougal Thomas, Charles E Gibson, Erika Creydt
Not Present at Vote: Todd Nunamaker
