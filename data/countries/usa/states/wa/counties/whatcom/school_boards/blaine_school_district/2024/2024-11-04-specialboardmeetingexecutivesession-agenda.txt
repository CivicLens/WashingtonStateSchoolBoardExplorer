Monday, November 4, 2024
Special Board Meeting: Executive Session
Opening and adjournment of this meeting will take place in the districts boardroom at 770 Mitchell
Avenue in Blaine. The directors will meet in executive session for the purpose of consulting with legal
counsel pursuant to Policy 1410.

Public Content
Pursuant to Policy 1410, the board may choose to convene in executive session for specific purposes
allowed by state statute. President Freeman will announce that the board will move into executive
session for the purpose of consulting with legal counsel. The executive session is anticipated to last one
(1) hour. Any extension of time will be announced verbally in the boardroom, and no final action will be
taken by the board following the executive session.
