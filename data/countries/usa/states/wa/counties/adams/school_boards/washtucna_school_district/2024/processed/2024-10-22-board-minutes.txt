Washtucna School District #109
Regular Board of Directors Meeting
Washtucna, WA
October 22, 2024

The meeting was called to order at 7:00 p.m. by board chair Jake Sitton. Other board members
present were Bridget Coon, Steve Crouse, Alta Hunt and Derrick Kehn. Also attending were
Superintendent Rob Clark and Business Manager Katie Lasen. Principal Staci Gloyn and Debbie
Sitton attended online via Zoom.
ADOPTION OF THE AGENDA
Steve Crouse moved to approve the agenda. Passed 5-0.
COMMENTS FROM GUESTS
Drake and Hannah from the Class of 2025 were present to share their senior trip plans. They
would like to travel to Long Beach, Washington on May 1 and return on May 4. Dr. Clark asked
them to return with a firm proposal in January for board approval.

BUSINESS MANAGER’S REPORT
Budget Status Reports for September were included in board packets and discussed at the meeting.
Our State Audit is finished with no findings or recommendations. The Assessment Audit Report
was shared with the board. Katie explained an issue with the capital projects grant claim we
encountered this month.
PRINCIPAL’S REPORT
Staci Gloyn gave a report on school news including training with ESD 101/Barn Program for
chronic absenteeism, a hatchery field trip today, MS and HS Mass Band Trips, Columbia Basin
Allied Arts performance, and sports update. The grant finalization field trip is on hold.
ADMINISTRATIVE REPORT
Dr. Clark gave an update on the Lead Water Grant Project. There was a walkthrough today and
the plumbers are scheduled to finish out their work over the next week. Derrick Kehn motioned

to approve 6 p.m. meetings for November through February. Passed 5-0. December’s meeting is
scheduled for December 17th.
EXECUTIVE/CLOSED SESSION
Steve Crouse motioned for the board to go into closed session for a student issue pursuant to RCW
42.30.140(2), for 20 minutes, beginning at 7:18 p.m. Passed 5-0. Closed session ended at 7:40
p.m.
ADJOURNMENT
Steve Crouse motioned to adjourn the meeting at 7:40 p.m. Passed 5-0.
