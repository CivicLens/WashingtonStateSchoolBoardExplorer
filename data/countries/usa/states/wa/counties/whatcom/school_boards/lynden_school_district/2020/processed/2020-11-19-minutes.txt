8. Board Work Session
• Reopening Planning
David VanderYacht, along with secondary principals, Ian Freeman, Molly Mitchell-Mumma, Ellie Meenk,
and Student Director, Riley Anderson shared how the start of in-person learning for grades 6-12 is
proceeding. The Board discussed the recent statewide recommendations from Governor Inslee and the
impact to schools and protocol’s to performing arts, athletics, and other extra-curricular events. David
informed the Board of recent conversations with Whatcom County Health Department and county
superintendents about recent COVID cases county-wide.
• Technology Update
Dusty Gulleson, IT Director provided a report to inform about system supports; student and staff devices;
family internet access; apps and program integration; and the success and challenges for families and
staff. The Board of Directors thanked the IT team for all they have done and are doing to support
students and staff.
• Social Emotional Learning (SEL) Standards
Tim Metz, Executive Director of Student Services provided an update on programs Safe and Civil School
and Character Strong. Tim provided examples of Character Strong being used in the elementary schools
to meet the social emotional learning and our student needs.
• School Board Meeting Schedule
The Board reviewed and discussed the dates and times of the board meeting schedule. The Board
determined that they will continue with the current schedule of the second and fourth Thursday of the
month at 7:00 P.M., unless otherwise noted.
