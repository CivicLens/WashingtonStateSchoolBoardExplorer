Tuesday, November 24, 2020
Special Board Meeting
The purpose of the special meeting is for the board to consider action directing the superintendent on
next steps in returning secondary students to in-person instruction. Pursuant to extended proclamations
from the Washington State Governor related to the COVID-19 pandemic emergency, participation in this
meeting will take place remotely. Live public observation of this meeting will be available online. Click on
the "Video" link above to access this meeting as an observer. (Meeting Passcode: 855858)

2. Executive Session
Subject :

A. Executive Session

Meeting :

Nov 24, 2020 - Special Board Meeting

Category :

2. Executive Session

Type :

Discussion

Public Content
As per legal guidelines, the board may choose to recess into executive session to consider employment
or dismissal of personnel, to review the performance of a public employee, to consult with legal counsel,
to consider the position to be taken in collective bargaining, or to consider acquisition or sale of real
estate.
Prior to the start of executive session, the president will announce the purpose and length of the
session.
At the close of the executive session, the directors will reconvene in regular session at which point
they may take formal action or the meeting will be adjourned.
The board will move into executive session for the purpose of consulting with legal counselregarding the
legal risks of a proposed action. The executive session is anticipated to last for up to one (1) hour. This
consultation will be followed, in regular session, by consideration of action to direct the superintendent on
next steps in returning secondary students to in-person instruction.
