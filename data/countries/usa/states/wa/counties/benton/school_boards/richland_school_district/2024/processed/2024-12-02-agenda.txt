Rivers HomeLink Delta High School Tri-Tech Skills Center Home > District > School Board > Board Members Board Members Members of the Richland School Board are elected by our community to four-year terms. Board members welcome your comments, concerns, and questions. The Board may direct your inquiry to the RSD administrator who is most knowledgeable about the subject. For direct messages to the Board, send an email to boardfeedback@rsd.edu . Richland School Board Mission To provide effective governance of the Richland School District through leadership, accountability, and transparency, while valuing the importance of student, family, and staff advocacy and engagement. Board Meetings Richland School Board meetings are typically held the second and fourth Tuesdays of each month at 6:30 p.m. Interested community members are encouraged to attend.
