Blaine School District No. 503
  Special Board Meeting
Monday, June 6, 2022
  Minutes
  1. Special Board Meeting
    A. Call to Order
President Thomas called the meeting to order, in the district boardroom, at 6:00 p.m.
Directors Gibson and Nunamaker were unable to attend. All other directors were in attendance.
     Dougal Thomas, Erika Creydt, Don Leu
  2. Action Items
  A. Resolution 2122-11 Application to Waive Two (2) Missed School Days
The board voted to adopt Resolution 2122-11 authorizing an application to OSPI for the waiver of two (2) missed
school days on May 26 and May 27.
Motion by Erika Creydt, second by Don Leu
Final Resolution: Motion Carries 3-0
Yea: Dougal Thomas, Erika Creydt, Don Leu
  3. Adjourn
  A. Adjourn
There being no further business, the meeting was adjourned by a unanimous vote at 6:03 p.m.
Motion by Don Leu, second by Erika Creydt
Final Resolution: Motion Carries 3-0
Yea: Dougal Thomas, Erika Creydt, Don Leu
