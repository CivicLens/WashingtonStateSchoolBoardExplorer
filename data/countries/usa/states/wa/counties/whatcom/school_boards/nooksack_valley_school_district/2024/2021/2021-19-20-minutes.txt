approve the minutes of the July 15, 2021, regular meeting.
M.C. 4-0

MINUTES APPROVED
REGULAR MEETING

The school board heard from 27 community members regarding
various aspects of the school opening requirements and the
governor's mask mandate. Community members shared
personal stories of their child/children struggling with masks,
provided perspectives on the lack of effectiveness of masks,
advocated for a mask choice policy, and discussed concerns
about further mandates. (A complete list of speakers is included
on page 4 of minutes.)

COMMUNICATIONS

The Board of Directors thanked the audience for their time to bring
concerns, questions, and information to their attention. The Board
explained the district will continue to make decisions that are
in the best interest of the students and community by following
our local public health department’s directions.
