The special session of the Nooksack Valley School Board
of Directors was called to order by Chairman, Randy Wright,
at 6:00 p.m. on September 12, 2017.

CALL TO ORDER

The Board of Directors interviewed applicant Jason Heutink
for the open Director #4 position at 6:00 p.m.

BOARD INTERVIEW

The Board of Directors interviewed applicant Joshua Clawson
for the open Director #4 position at 6:45 p.m.

BOARD INTERVIEW

Randy Wright convened an executive session at 7:15 p.m. to
discuss the candidates.

EXECUTIVE SESSION

Randy Wright reconvened the special meeting at 7:25 p.m.
Mark Olson moved and Cheryl Thornton seconded the motion
to appoint Jason Heutink to the open school board position in
District #4.

RECONVENE AND
APPOINTMENT

Meeting adjourned at 7:25 p.m.
