Information: A. Student Board Representatives Report

  Information: B. Board Comments
Paul Hill noted that all of the legislative proposals sent by MLSD received a pass. The resolutions committee was looking at the permanent positions. Mr. Hill noted he hopes the district will heavily involve the community and Board with planning the budget.
Mr. Fuhr, Ms. Urias and Ms. Jensen expressed disappointment that the levy didn't pass.
Ms. Breitenstein asked if there is a policy about having Chromebooks at home. Ms. Lewis noted 5th graders and older typically have chromebooks issued to them. They check them out, sign them out, are taught the proper care of them and have the opportunity to purchase insurance and hopefully avoid fines. Ms. Breitenstein went on to ask if a 4th grader could be issued a chromebook so they didn't fall behind in the event they missed much school. Ms. Lewis noted this would be handled at the school and would be on a case by case basis.
Ms. Urias noted she hopes we can work with the community and earn trust .
Ms. Jensen stated we have strong leaders with good minds and working together we will do well going forward.
