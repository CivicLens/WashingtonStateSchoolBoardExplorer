OPENING ITEMS
At 6:00 p.m. President Ann Whitmyer called the regular meeting to order.
Roll Call
Present: Directors Kelly Bashaw, Steve Smith, Scott Stockburger, Ken Gass, President Ann
Whitmyer, Superintendent Greg Baker and recorder Nancy Merry.
Executive Session
President Ann adjourned the regular meeting at 6:04 p.m. for executive session for the purpose
of discussing property acquisition and real estate negotiations. President Whitmyer announced
the regular meeting would reconvene at 6:30 p.m. At 6:25 p.m., executive session adjourned and
at 6:30 p.m., the regular meeting reconvened.

Out-of-State Travel
a) Five Birchwood Elementary School teachers to attend the “PLC at Work” Summit in Las
Vegas, Nevada, June 6–8, 2011.
b) Ten Kulshan Middle School teachers to attend the AVID Summer Institute in San Diego,
California, August 1–3, 2011.
c) One Northern Heights Elementary School teacher to attend the International
Baccalaureate-PYP Level 3 training in Toronto, Canada, June 26–29, 2011.
d) Two Parkview Elementary School teachers to do a site visit to observe Response to
Intervention in San Juan Capistrano, California, June 2–3, 2011.
e) Two Sehome High School teachers to assess Advanced Placement exams—AP
Environmental Science in Cincinnati, Ohio, June 1–9, 2011, and AP World History in Ft.
Collins, Colorado, June 1–9, 2011.
f) A teacher of the visually impaired to participate on the American Printing House for the
Blind Annual Review Panel in Louisville, Kentucky, May 23–24, 2011.
g) One teacher and two administrators from Squalicum High School to attend the Advanced
Placement Conference in San Francisco, California, July 22–23, 2011.
h) Three staff from Squalicum High School to attend the AVID Summer Institute in San
Diego, California, July 27–29, 2011.
i) The Superintendent and Director of Communications and Community Relations to attend
the National School Public Relations Association Annual Conference in San Antonio,
Texas, July 9–14, 2011.
Student Trips
a) The Squalicum High School Thespians to attend the National Thespian Festival in
Lincoln, Nebraska, June 20–25, 2011.
b) The Squalicum High School Cheer Squad to attend the NCA Summer Camp in Grand
Mound, Washington, June 27–30, 2011.
c) Fifteen Bellingham High School students to attend the “Imagine Tomorrow” science
competition at Washington State University, Pullman, Washington, May 20–22, 2011.
Personal Services Contracts
a) Scott Dyer to provide Highly Capable Learner (HCL) program math enrichment activities
for selected sixth grade students at Fairhaven Middle School, effective April through
June 2011.
b) Michael Coleman to provide tutoring and sign language practice for students enrolled in
the American Sign Language Class, effective May through June 2011.
c) John Lehni to provide curriculum lessons to juveniles in County Adult Jail, effective
April through June 2011.

Action on personnel recommendations as submitted.
