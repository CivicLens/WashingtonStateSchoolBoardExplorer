INFORMATIONAL ITEMS/DISCUSSION
The board members and Dr. Baker engaged in general discussion relating to school
district initiatives:
Review EL-10, Race and Equity
The board discussed the policy and monitoring report document and whether to make any
refinements. There will be a discussion at the board’s next regular meeting regarding any
possible changes.
School Board Professional Development
The board discussed the value of attending the annual WSSDA conference, pros and
cons, and suggestions on how to ensure it is a meaningful experience for all.
Superintendent Mid-Year Evaluation and Contract
The superintendent reviewed key accomplishments for the year, and the board shared
strong appreciation and support to the superintendent, including future discussions
regarding a renewed contract.
Communications/Board Visibility
The board reviewed the current communications plan for board visibility and discussed
progress. Another conversation with the district’s communications department might be
helpful in moving forward.
WSSDA Self-Assessment
The board discussed whether to take the WSSDA self-assessment again. The board
discussed taking the self-assessment in January 2021 in preparation for the winter retreat.
