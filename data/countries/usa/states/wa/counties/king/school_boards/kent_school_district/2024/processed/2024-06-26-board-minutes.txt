Agenda Review
A motion was made to remove Item 7.02 from the published agenda, City of Kent American Rescue Plan Act
Federal Grant Fund Acceptance 2024-2026 due to additional changes both in language and an anticipated
increase in funding. The City of Kent will provide a new document following their next city council meeting.
It is anticipated to have the updated item on one of the July special meeting agendas.
The motion also included a second change to the Consent Agenda which was the addition of a Supplemental
Personnel Report. With the removal of Item 7.02, the Consent Agenda numbering was adjusted, and the
Supplemental Personnel Report will be added as the new Item 7.11.
The motion carried.
