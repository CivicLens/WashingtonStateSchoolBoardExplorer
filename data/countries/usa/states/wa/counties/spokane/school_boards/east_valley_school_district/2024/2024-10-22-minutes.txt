  4.3 Legislative Report
Neale Rasmussen and Brian Talbott responded to an email from Representative Suzanne Schmidt regarding East Valley's inability to collect the maximum tax monies due to being a property-rich district. They will be following up with Representative Leonard Christian and Representative Suzanne Schmidt at a later date.
  4.4 October 11 Debrief
Chief David Ellis and Brian Talbott debriefed on the October 11, 2024, protest.
  4.5 Superintendent Report
Mr. Talbott spoke about the outdoor lighting adjustments at EVMS, which now highlights the new mural. He also updated the board that he is working with the county to remove the weeds along Sullivan Rd. Mr. Talbott discussed the upcoming Facilities Improvement Team meeting, noting what information will be shared with the team, what the goal is for the team, and where the team intends to have their meetings. He then spoke to the board about the 3 Hagen grant applications that have been submitted and gave some background on which schools submitted the applications and what their hope is to accomplish should they be awarded the grant. Mr. Talbott invited the board to the WSSDA Conference which will be held in Spokane from November 21, 2024, through November 23, 2024. He completed his report by informing the board that they would view a recorded PLC Board Session at the next board meeting on November 12, 2024.
