Friday, November 19, 2021
Special Meeting, 6:00 p.m.
The Board of Directors will attend the Washington State School Directors Association (WSSDA) Annual
Conference from Thursday, Nov. 18 to Saturday, Nov. 20 at the Hyatt Regency in Bellevue, Washington.
While in Bellevue, the board will hold a special meeting on Friday, Nov. 19 at 6:00 p.m. at John Howie
Steak, 11111 NE 8th Street #125, Bellevue, WA. The purpose of the meeting is to discuss school district
initiatives, issues and business in an unstructured manner. This is not a regular school board meeting
and will not include public comment time.
For more information, call 360-676-6501.
