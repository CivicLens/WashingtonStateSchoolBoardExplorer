DRAFT MINUTES OF THE SCHOOL BOARD SPECIAL MEETING
BELLINGHAM SCHOOL DISTRICT
BOARD OF DIRECTORS
March 12, 2018
OPENING ITEMS
Meeting Date and Location:
4:30 p.m., Mar. 12, 2018 – Options High School, 2015 Franklin St., Bellingham, WA
Call to Order/Roll Call:
Ms. Bashaw called the meeting to order at 4:30 p.m. Present: Directors Kelly Bashaw
(President), Douglas Benjamin, Camille Diaz Hackler, Jenn Mason, Quenby Peterson,
and Superintendent Greg Baker.
INFORMATIONAL ITEMS/DISCUSSION
Community Linkages – General Discussion
 The board reviewed the schedule for upcoming linkages and discussed possible topics
for future linkages.
Policy Governance and Board Handbook
 GP-7 Agenda Planning and its alignment to Operating Policy 1420 were discussed.
The directors agreed to consider a revision to Policy 1420 at a future board meeting.
Superintendent Update – General Discussion
 Dr. Baker gave an update on major bodies of work through this year, including
budget items and bond projects.
Superintendent Mid-Year Evaluation – General Discussion
Other topics as Determined
 WSSDA Board of Distinction: Applications open mid-June to early July and close
in late September.
 WSSDA Annual Conference: General discussion regarding presentation
opportunities. It was agreed that the board would focus on other work at this point.
 Board Self-Assessment: The directors agreed to complete a self-assessment again
this spring.
 Superintendent Update: Dr. Baker will update the board on the 1:1 technology
initiative at the Mar. 28 board meeting.
 School Safety Resolution: General discussion. The directors agreed to form a
sub-committee to draft a resolution for board consideration at a future meeting.
 Equity: General discussion regarding the need to use an equity lens when reviewing
board policy revisions. Resources include policies of other schools districts and the
National School Board Association.
