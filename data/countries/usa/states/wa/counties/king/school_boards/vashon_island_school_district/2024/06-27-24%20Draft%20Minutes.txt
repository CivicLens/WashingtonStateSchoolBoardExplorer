Superintendent Report
Provided update for Student Advisory Board. Had 11 students apply for at-large position
to replace Nora LaVigueuer.
VISD Pupil Transportation Services Request for Proposal Update
Provided update on transportation services RFP. Noted board will need to take action in
July on a first-read only.
Board members made comments.
Social Media Litigation Update

Informed board that district had withdrew from social media mass action lawsuit due to
amount of work required in discovery and lack of hours available among staff.
Noted that administrators are taking a look at grades 6-12 cell phone “off and away
during instructional time” policy. McM Principal Greg Allison especially will be taking a
deeper look at cell phone policy.
Upcoming Events
• Work Session, 11:00 am, July 1
• Regular Board Meeting – July 25
• August 8 Work Session, WSSDA potentially coming for board selfassessment.

health collaboration.
Board members discussed the proposal.

Discussion
VISD 2024-25 Budget Resolution #815
Dr. McSheehy and Kim Mayer presented the board the 2024-25 budget.
Board members engaged in discussion about the budget.
Amy Sassara presented the general fund for salaries.
Kim Mayer presented the materials, supplies, and operating costs.
Dr. McSheehy updated board on district information and budget assumptions.
Board engaged in discussion with Kim Mayer about fund balance.
Kim Mayer presented the summary of 2024-25 budgets.
Amy Sassara presented on the 2024-25 staffing budget.

Board members engaged in discussion with Kim Mayer about grants.
Dr. McSheehy presented 2024-25 enrollment summaries.
Board members engaged in discussion on enrollment numbers.
Board members engaged in discussion on fund balance numbers.

Decision
Policy 6701 – Recess and Physical Activity
Dr. McSheehy recommended the board hold off on any activity or changes to the policy
until stakeholders can engage in process.
Board members engaged in discussion about codifying current practices and feasibility
study.
Board members engaged in discussion about how to move forward.
Board members agreed to take no action on policy at this time.
Instructional Materials Adoption
Dr. McSheehy recommended the board approved the instruction materials adoption.
Martha Woodard moves to approve the adoption, Juniper Rogneby seconded.
Voted, Approved, Passed, 4-0
