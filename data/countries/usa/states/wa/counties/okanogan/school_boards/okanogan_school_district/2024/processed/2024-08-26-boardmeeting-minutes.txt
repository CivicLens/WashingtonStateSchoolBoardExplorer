1) CALL MEETING TO ORDER
a) Chair Rae Jean Kelley called the August Regular Board meeting to order at 6:00p.m.
School Board members present: Rae Jean Kelley, Heather Fisher, Ryan Lafferty, Luke
McKee and Terri Mail (via zoom) with Steve Quick, Superintendent, and about 26
audience members. The meeting took place at the MS/HS Library.
2) APPROVAL OF JULY MINUTES, CHANGES OR ADDITIONS TO AUGUST
AGENDA
a) The Board approved the July Regular Board Meeting Minutes, as well as, the August
Regular Board Meeting Agenda with the additions of Susan Reisenburg, Transportation
Safety Specialist; Glafira Apolonio, School Bus Driver and Kaitlan Klepec, Assistant
High School Girls Soccer Coach, and all hires to be added to the consent agenda.
McKee/Fisher/passed/5 for/0 against
3) PUBLIC COMMENT Citizens wanting to speak on topics not on the agenda may
comment at this point in the meeting. Personnel issues and employee performance are not
discussed in public meetings and shall be referred to the superintendent. Other comments
are limited to 3 minutes per person and 10 minutes per topic.
The board engaged in a healthy and constructive discussion with the teachers present at the
board meeting. Board members listened attentively to the feedback provided and posed
follow-up questions for clarification. The conversation covered various aspects of the issues
raised.
4) ADMINISTRATION REPORTS
Mr. Quick reported to the Board that our enrollment for the 2024-2025 school year is

school year. He informed the Board about the new staff orientation that took place and how
