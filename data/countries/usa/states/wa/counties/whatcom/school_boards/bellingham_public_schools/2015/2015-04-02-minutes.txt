DRAFT MINUTES OF THE SPECIAL MEETING OF THE
BELLINGHAM PUBLIC SCHOOLS BOARD OF DIRECTORS
April 2, 2015
The Bellingham School Board of Directors held a special meeting April 2, 2015 at the District
Office, 1306 Dupont Street, Bellingham, Washington.
President Kelly Bashaw called the meeting to order at 6:30 p.m.
Roll Call
Present: Directors Kelly Bashaw, Douglas Benjamin, Kenneth Gass, Steven Smith, and
Superintendent Greg Baker. Director Camille Hackler was excused from the meeting due to
another commitment.
The Board was joined by the following community members:
 Ahhria Fosdyck
 Ken Hanna
 Amy Cloud
 Jelena Sacharoff
 Christine Morrow
Agenda
Dr. Gass facilitated an orientation session for individuals who may be interested in serving on the
school board. Discussion focused on handouts which included Orientation for School Board
Candidates (attached) and Board Participation/Time Expectations (attached).
Adjournment
The meeting adjourned at 8:45 p.m.
