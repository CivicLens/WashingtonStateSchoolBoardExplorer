MERIDIAN SCHOOL DISTRICT #505
REGULAR SCHOOL BOARD MEETING
MEETING HELD IN PERSON & VIA YOUTUBE LIVE STREAM
October 9, 2024

REGULAR MEETING
The regular meeting was called to order by Board President Steve Corwin at 6:00 p.m. Steve welcomed
everyone that was in attendance.
Steve Corwin, Jarrod Karuza, Jana Haugen, and Michelle Simmons were present at the meeting. Marty
Gray was absent from the meeting.
Student Representatives Kennedy Brzozowski and Logan Ignacio were absent from the meeting.
The pledge of allegiance was recited.
A motion to adopt the agenda was made by Jana Haugen and seconded by Jarrod Karuza. The motion
passed 4-0.
CONSENT AGENDA
A. Approval of Meeting Minutes
B. Personnel Report
C. Accounts Payable
D. Payroll
E. 2024-25 County Mobile Response Team MOU
F. Donation Listing
G. 2024-25 NWESD School Nurse Corps Contract
A motion to remove item G. 2024-25 NWESD School Nurse Corps Contract from the consent agenda and
later discuss as new business was made by Michelle Simmons and seconded by Jana Haugen. The
motion passed 4-0.
A motion to approve consent agenda items A-F was made by Jarrod Karuza and seconded by Jana
Haugen. The motion passed 4-0.
RECOGNITION
IRE Staff Recognition
Irene Reither Principal Tyler Dockins presented a IRE staff recognition report to the board. The report
highlighted new staff, family engagement efforts, and intervention celebrations.
PUBLIC COMMENT
There was no public comment.

PRESENTATIONS
Review and Approval: Highly Capable Program
Highly Capable Learning Coordinator, Rebecca Montgomery, presented a report on the Highly Capable
Learning Program. Rebecca shared details about how HiCap students are identified, demographics,
instructional programs and services, the program goals, and next steps.
A motion to approve the highly capable program, as presented was made by Michelle Simmons and
seconded by Jana Haugen. The motion passed 4-0.
2024-25 Staff Professional Development Plan
Director of Teaching and Learning, Adrienne Somera, presented a 2024-25 professional development
plan. The report highlighted building and district level professional development training that is planned
for teachers, student support teams, administrators, directors, and classified staff.
Overview of the PLC Process
Director of Teaching and Learning, Adrienne Somera, presented an overview of the Professional
Learning Communities process. The presentation shared information about assessment data, the steps
of the PLC cycle, and the work of the PLT and Guiding Coalition leadership teams.
NEW BUSINESS
2024-25 NWESD School Nurse Corps Contract
After discussion, a motion to approve the 2024-25 NWESD School Nurse Corps Contract as presented
was made by Michelle Simmons and seconded by Jana Haugen. The motion passed 4-0.
