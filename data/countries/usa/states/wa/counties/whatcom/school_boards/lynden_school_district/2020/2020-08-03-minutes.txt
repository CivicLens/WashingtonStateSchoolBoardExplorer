August 3, 2020
School Board Meeting
Minutes
via Zoom/Phone Conference
7:00 P.M.
1. Call to Order, Welcome, Pledge of Allegiance and Roll Call
Meeting called to order at 7:03 P.M. Board members in attendance were Steve Jilk, Brian Johnson, CJ Costanti,
and Kelli Kettels. Board Director, David Vis was absent and excused.
2. Information from the Audience
Comments and questions were provided to the Board of Directors prior to the meeting and were addressed
during the meeting.
3. Board Work Session
 Re-Opening Schools Discussion
The Board of Directors discussed the comments, questions, and concerns of families, staff, and
community members regarding the decision to open the 2020-2021 school year. Jim Frey shared a
document regarding the impacts of COVID-19 for re-opening schools and the three learning options of:
(1) Lynden at School – in person learning; (2) Lynden Home Connect – parent and school partnership with
parent as primary teacher; (3) Lynden On-Line – on-line instruction via asynchronous or synchronous
video by either a third-party vendor or Lynden School District staff. The Board discussed the challenges
regarding hybrid learning, and how to address learning gaps.
4. Adjourn Meeting
Meeting adjourned at 7:45 P.M.
