INFORMATIONAL ITEMS/DISCUSSION
WSSDA Self-Assessment: The Board reviewed the history and purpose of this selfassessment and discussed whether to take it this year. There was support to move forward
with the assessment and try to have the results in time for the November WSSDA event
where it can be discussed.
School Board Calendar for 2021-22: The Board discussed ideas around future study
sessions, linkages, student roundtables, school visits and regular board meetings. Given
the current context of COVID-19 in our community, board meetings will remain online
for the time being and continue to be re-evaluated each month.
Superintendent Updates: The superintendent gave updates on preparations for the start
of the school year, capital projects and future bond preparation.
