Tuesday, October 3, 2023
School Board Visit - Shuksan Middle School
The Board of Directors of Bellingham Public Schools regularly visits schools throughout the school year.
The purpose of these visits is to learn how each school is delivering upon The Bellingham Promise. The
directors will visit Shuksan Middle School, 2717 Alderwood Avenue, at 7:30 a.m., on Tuesday, October 3,
2023. This is not a regular school board meeting and will not include public comment time.
For more information, call 360-676-6501.
