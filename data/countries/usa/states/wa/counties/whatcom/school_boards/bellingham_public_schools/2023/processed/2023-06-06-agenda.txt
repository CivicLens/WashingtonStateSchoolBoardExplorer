Tuesday, June 6, 2023
School Board Visit - Roosevelt Elementary School
The Board of Directors of Bellingham Public Schools regularly visits schools throughout the school year.
The purpose of these visits is to learn how each school is delivering upon The Bellingham Promise. The
directors will visit Roosevelt Elementary School, 2900 Yew Street, Bellingham, at 7:30 a.m., Tuesday,
June 6, 2023. This is not a regular school board meeting and will not include public comment time.
For more information, call 360-676-6501.
