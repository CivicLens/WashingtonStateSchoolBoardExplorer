3. Public Comment
Subject :

A. Public Comment Period

Meeting :

Jan 27, 2020 - Regular Board Meeting

Category :

3. Public Comment

Type :

Procedural

Public Content
At this time, the board president will recognize those who wish to address the directors. Patrons wishing
to address the board are asked to sign in and acknowledge their topic of interest.
The board will provide time during the meeting for those with said interest to speak directly to the board.
Participants are asked to keep their comments to three (3) minutes; and
At any given board meeting, comments on a common topic may be limited to three (3) individuals.
Other individuals interested in making comments specific to that common topic will be asked to put
their comments into writing and the administrative assistant will be responsible for forwarding these
comments on to all board members.
Parents and patrons should note that Policy 4220 and its related procedure preclude the board from
hearing complaints against a specific staff member, or specific students, in an open public meeting forum.
Those presenting concerns in an open meeting venue, are asked to refrain from reference (by name) to a
specific staff member or a specific student.

Those with complaints specific to an individual staff member or student are asked to put the concern in
writing, and then submit that concern to the administrative assistant (Tina Padilla) so that the issue can
be addressed with the board in the appropriate venue.

8. Executive Session
Subject :

A. Executive Session

Meeting :

Jan 27, 2020 - Regular Board Meeting

Category :

8. Executive Session

Type :

Discussion

Public Content
As per legal guidelines, the board may choose to recess into executive session to consider employment
or dismissal of personnel, to review the performance of a public employee, to consult with legal counsel,
to consider the position to be taken in collective bargaining, or to consider acquisition or sale of real
estate.
Prior to the start of executive session, the president will announce the purpose and length of the
session.
At the close of the executive session, the directors will reconvene in regular session at which point
they may take formal action or the meeting will be adjourned.
The board will move into executive session for the purpose of reviewing the performance of a public employee. The executive session is
anticipated to last for up to thirty (30) minutes. No action is anticipated following the executive session.
