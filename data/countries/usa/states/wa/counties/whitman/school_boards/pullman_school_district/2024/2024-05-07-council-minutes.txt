City of Pullman
Council Minutes
Special Meeting
Goal Setting
City Council Chambers
190 SE Crestview Street
Pullman, WA 99163
5:30 p.m.
Tuesday, May 7, 2024
Members present
Mayor Francis Benjamin, Ann Parks, Nathan Weller, Pat Wright, Megan Guido, Carla
De Lira, and Trymaine Gaither.
The meeting was called to order at 5:30 p.m.
A. Call to Order
Action, Motion: 1. Roll Call
B. Discussion
Mayor Benjamin summarized what the Council discussed at its previous Council Retreat
and their identified goals.
The Council continued reviewing the community's goal suggestions received at the open
house.
The Council identified the following as long-term goals that work groups will facilitate.
• Bypass
• Housing
• Repurpose Railways
• Partnerships
• Promote Tourism
The Council identified the following as potential two-year goals.
• Staff Support
• Community Engagement
• Economic Development
• Support Regional Efforts that also impact the City of Pullman.
C. Adjournment
The meeting adjourned at 9:19 p.m.
