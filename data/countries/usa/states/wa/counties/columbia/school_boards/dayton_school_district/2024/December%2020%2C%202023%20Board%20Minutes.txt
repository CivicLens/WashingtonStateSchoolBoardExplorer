1. MEETING OPENING
A. Call to Order
Board chair McCowen called the meeting at order at 6:30 p.m. Board members present included Grant Griffen, Jeffrey McCowen, Zac
Fabian and Ryan Paulson. Aneesha Dieu was in attendance via Zoom. Also present were Superintendent Stewart and Business
Manager Eaton.
B. Flag Salute
Jeff McCowen led the flag salute
C. Recognition of Representatives
None
D. Additions or Changes to the Agenda
None
E. Oath Of Office
The following board members were sworn into office:
Grant Griffen - School Board Director Position 3
Aneesha Dieu - School Board Director Position 4 At Large
Zac Fabian - School Board Director Position 2
F. Board Reorganization - Election of Officers
1. Board member Griffen nominated, director Fabian seconded, Jeffrey McCowen for the role of Board Chair.
Roll Call Vote: Griffen- Aye, Fabian- Aye, Dieu-Aye, Paulson-Aye, McCowen-Aye. Motion carried 5-0.
2. Board member Fabian nominated, director Paulson seconded, Grant Griffen for the role of Board Vice Chair.
Roll Call Vote: Fabian- Aye, Dieu-Aye, Paulson-Aye, McCowen-Aye, Griffen- Aye, . Motion carried 5-0.
3. The Board appointed Grant Griffen and Zac Fabian as Facilities Representatives which is a non-voted position.
4. The Board appointed Grant Griffen and Aneesha Dieu as Budget & Finance Representatives which is a non-voted position.
5. The Board appointed Aneesha Dieu and Ryan Paulson as Curriculum Representatives which is a non-voted position.
6. The Board appointed Jeffrey McCowen and Zac Fabian as Community Outreach/Public Relations Representatives which is a nonvoted position.
7. The Board appointed Ryan Paulson as Custodial Negotiations Representatives which is a non-voted position.
8. The Board appointed Jeffrey McCowen as Classified Negotiations Representative which is a non-voted position.
9. The Board appointed Aneesha Dieu as Certified Negotiations Representative which is a non-voted position.
10. The Board appointed Zac Fabian as the WIAA Representative
11. The Board appointed Aneesha Dieu as the Legislative Representative

3. PUBLIC COMMENT
Carly Benavides read a prepared statement detailing her activities and volunteerism in the school district and community. Ms. Benavides
appreciates her supervisor and the support she provides. Ms. Benavides stated she is hopeful the district can go back to a better place.
4. REPORTS
A. Superintendent's Report
Superintendent Stewart updated the board on the superintendent search and timeline. The closing date for submitting an application is
January 12.
Superintendent Stewart updated the board on levy activities to date. Superintendent Stewart has made a number of presentations to
community groups about the levy. Denise Hoon has been lining up dates and community groups for Mr. Stewart. Information includes the
projects that would be undertaken with levy funds over the next four years. The district continues to apply for grants to cover the cost of
some of the projects as well. Mr. Stewart also updated the board on the asbestos abatement project and a complaint currently in
progress that may involve some additional training for staff.
B. Budget Status Report
Business manager Jana Eaton updated the board on the November budget status and reports currently due to OSPI. Mrs. Eaton
continues to apply for grants as they become available to help cover the cost of some of the needed repairs and upgrades to our
buildings.
5. POLICIES - Second Reading

6. ACTION ITEMS
A. Board member Dieu moved, seconded by director Paulson, to approve the Non-Representative Contract for 2023, 2024 and
2025. Board members Griffen & Fabian recused themselves. Motion carried 3-0.
B. Board member Griffen moved, seconded by director Fabian, to approve the second reading of Policies 6215, 6700, 2190,
2230, 5050, 3207, 3241, 1400, 6220 & 3225. Motion carried 5-0.
7. EXECUTIVE SESSION
Board chair McCowen called for an executive session at 7:28 p.m. to review the performance of a staff member. The session is expected
to take 25 minutes with no action to follow. At 7:55 p.m. the session was extended for another 10 minutes. The board returned to open
session at 8:06 p.m. with no action taken.
8. ADJOURNMENT
Board member McCowen Adjourned the meeting at 8:10 p.m. The next meeting will be a board work session on January 4, 2024 at 6:30
p.m. in the high school library/media center and via zoom.
