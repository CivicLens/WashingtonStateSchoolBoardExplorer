Blaine School District No. 503
  Special Board Meeting - Executive Session
Monday, January 11, 2021
  Minutes
  1. Special Board Meeting
A. Call to Order
Due to restrictions resulting from the COVID-19 pandemic emergency, this meeting was conducted with remote director participation. President Gibson called the meeting to order at 5:06 p.m.

Director Thomas was not present at the start of the meeting, but joined during the executive session at 5:26 p.m. All Directors were in attendance:  Todd Nunamaker, Dougal Thomas, Joan Lotze, Charles E Gibson, Laura McKinney
  2. Executive Session
A. Executive Session
President Gibson announced that the board would move immediately into executive session for the purpose of reviewing the performance of a public employee. The executive session was anticipated to last for sixty (60) minutes, and any extensions of time would be announced in the open meeting. No action would be taken by the board after the executive session.

The directors left the open meeting and transitioned to the closed session. Due to some delay in this transition, the executive session started at 5:16 p.m.

At 6:00 p.m., the Superintendent's Administrative Assistant, Tina Padilla, provided a verbal announcement in the open session that the executive session would be extended for one additional hour, ending at approximately 7:00 p.m.

At 7:00 p.m., Ms. Padilla provided another verbal announcement in the open session that the executive session would be extended for thirty additional minutes, ending at approximately 7:30 p.m.
  3. Reconvene and Adjourn
A. Reconvene and Adjourn
After the executive session, the board transitioned back to the open meeting and reconvened in regular session at 7:18 p.m.
  There being no further business, the meeting was adjourned by a unanimous vote.
Motion by Laura McKinney, second by Todd Nunamaker
Final Resolution: Motion Carries 5-0
Yea: Todd Nunamaker, Dougal Thomas, Joan Lotze, Charles E Gibson, Laura McKinney
