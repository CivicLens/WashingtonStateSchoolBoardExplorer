Tuesday, February 13, 2024
Regular Board Meeting
Regular Board Meetings are held monthly on the 2nd and 4th Tuesdays, unless otherwise posted. Board
meetings are open to the public and are designed to conduct board business rather than to serve as a
public hearing, unless a public hearing is scheduled.
.
The regular board meeting will begin at 6:30 p.m. at the Central Services Center at 25720 Maple Valley
Black Diamond Rd SE in Maple Valley. (It will be called to order and adjourn in Executive Session at 6:00
p.m.) A virtual option for the regular board meeting will be available via Zoom by clicking on the 'Video'
link above.
.
A video/audio recording of the meeting will be available after the meeting by clicking on the 'Video' link
above.
.
Individuals who would like to make public comment may do so at the meeting by signing up on the 'Public
Comment Sign-In' sheet. Guidelines outlined in agenda item 3.01 (Public Comment) must be followed.
.
Individuals with disabilities who may need a modification to participate in a meeting should contact the
Superintendent's Office no later than three days before a regular meeting and as soon as possible in
advance of a special meeting so that arrangements for the modifications can be made.

3.01 Public Comments

Meeting :

Feb 13, 2024 - Regular Board
Meeting

Type :

Information

Public Content
Individuals who would like to make Public Comment may do so at the meeting by signing in on the 'Public
CommentSign-In' sheet. Guidelines outlined below must be followed:
Each person will be given three (3) minutes to comment and a maximum of 20 minutes per any one
topic will be allowed. Speakers whose comments are less than three (3) minutes in length may not defer
their remaining time to others. If there are multiple speakers on one topic, speakers may be limited to
points of view not yet expressed.
Any person wishing his/her comments retained as part of the permanent record may submit a prepared
statement to the board secretary prior to the oral presentation.
The President may interrupt or terminate an individuals statement when it is too lengthy, personally
directed, abusive, obscene or irrelevant.
Comments/concerns relative to district personnel will not be heard in public; rather, they should be
directed through the Complaints Concerning School Personnel or Programs Policy 4220.
Formal presentations must be scheduled with the Superintendent, in writing, no later than Tuesday,
noon, one week preceding the regular meeting.
