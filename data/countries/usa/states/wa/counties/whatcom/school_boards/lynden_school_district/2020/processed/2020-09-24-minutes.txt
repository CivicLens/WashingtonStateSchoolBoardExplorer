9. Board Work Session
• Reopening Planning
Jim Frey provided an update and timeline for a reopening phase as conversations continue with the
Whatcom County Health Department for planning details necessary to safely provide in-person learning
at the schools. After further discussion it has been decided to postpone the reopening of school until the
second week in October for K-2 grades.
