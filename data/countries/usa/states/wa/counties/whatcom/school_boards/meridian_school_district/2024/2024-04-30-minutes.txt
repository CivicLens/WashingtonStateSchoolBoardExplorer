MERIDIAN SCHOOL DISTRICT #505
SPECIAL SCHOOL BOARD MEETING
MEETING HELD IN PERSON
April 30, 2024

SPECIAL MEETING
The special meeting was called to order by Board President Steve Cowin at 5:05 p.m.
Board directors present at the meeting were Steve Corwin, Jarrod Karuza, Marty Gray, and Michelle
Simmons.
A motion to adopt the agenda was made by Marty Gray and seconded by Jarrod Karuza. The motion
passed 4-0.
EXECUTIVE SESSION
The Board moved into Executive Session for the purpose of reviewing the performance of a public
employee. No action was taken during the executive session.
Board director Jana Haugen joined the meeting at 5:10 pm.
ADJOURNMENT
There being no further business, the Board meeting was adjourned at 5:40 pm by Board President Steve
Corwin.
