7. Board Workshop
 Inclement Weather Days
The board discussed the school calendar to make-up the 6 missed school days due to inclement weather.
The board agreed to use the already scheduled June 16, 19, & 20 weather make-up days. Based on the
status of requesting a 2 day waiver with state approval, the board agreed to use the April 10th, 2017
Professional Learning Time day to make-up the 6th day of inclement weather. Lynden High School
graduation will remain June 9th, and last day of school will be June 20th, based on the decision of the 2
day waiver request.


Construction Update
Bryan Visscher of Construction Services Group, ESD 112 provided information on current construction
status, financial status, and change order proposals. The board discussed the addition of classrooms at
both the new Fisher Elementary and Lynden Middle School and will motion to approve funding at a
future board meeting.
