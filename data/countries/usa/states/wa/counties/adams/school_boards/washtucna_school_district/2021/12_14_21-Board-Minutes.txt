Washtucna School District #109
Regular Board of Directors Meeting
Washtucna, WA
December 14, 2021

The meeting was called to order at 6:00 p.m. by board chair Steve Crouse. Other board members
present were Jeremy Jessop, Jake Sitton, and Ginger McKenzie. Also attending in person were
Superintendent Dr. Rob Clark and Business Manager Katie Lasen. Teresa Correll and Debbie
Sitton attended online via zoom. Principal Michael Amend attended over speaker phone.
ADOPTION OF AGENDA
Jake Sitton moved to approve the agenda. Passed 5-0.

Budget Status Reports for November were included in board packets and discussed at the meeting.
ADMINISTRATIVE REPORT
Dr. Clark administered oaths of office to Teresa Correll, Jacob Sitton, Jeremy Jessop and Steve
Crouse. Jake Sitton moved to leave board chair, vice chair, and WIAA representative the same,
and appoint Ginger McKenzie as the legislative representative. Passed 5-0.

great community turnout for the concert last week. We welcomed seven new students in the last
month.
