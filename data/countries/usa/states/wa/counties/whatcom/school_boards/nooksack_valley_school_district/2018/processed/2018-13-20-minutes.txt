REGULAR MEETING

Rhonda Juergens and Matt Galley discussed a private donation to
her program. The board told her to move forward with processing
the donation while they approve a corresponding policy.
Tex Laddish introduced the Director of CSG, Kirk Pawlowski, to the
board. They explained the final close out of the Middle School project
and the commissioning process for the school.
Cheryl Thornton discussed her recent participation in the annual state
WSSDA conference and some things she learned. She also
was part of the panel discussion on “Farm to School” initiatives.
