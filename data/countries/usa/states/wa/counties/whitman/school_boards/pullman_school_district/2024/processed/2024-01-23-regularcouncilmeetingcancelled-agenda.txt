If you wish to provide oral public comment virtually at a City Council meeting,
please fill out the required form located at https://www.pullman-wa.gov
/government/mayor___city_council/city_council_remote_public_comment to be
added to the Speaker List. The deadline to sign up is by 5:00 p.m. the night of the
meeting. Directions will be sent to you by 6:45 p.m. the day of the meeting in
which you requested to speak on how to participate remotely.
