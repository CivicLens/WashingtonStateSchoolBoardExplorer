Colville, Washington
SPECIAL MEETING OF THE BOARD OF DIRECTORS, FEBRUARY 28, 2024 5:00 PM
Members Present: Joe Fazzari, Dave Naff, Kim Gallo and Kevin Knight, Secretary
Excused: Joseph Schweitzer, Bob Gumm
Administrators and Guests: Zachary Levchenko, two guests and Heather Clark, Recording Secretary
1. Opening Items
1.01 Call to Order
President Fazzari called the meeting to order at 5:00 PM at the Colville School District Office.
The purpose of the special meeting is:
•
•
•

Interview Candidates for Board Director District #3
Hold an Executive Session to evaluate the qualifications of candidates for appointment
to the Board of Director District #3 vacancy
Appoint a Director, District #3, if a decision is reached by the board

1.02 Pledge of Allegiance
Flag salute followed.
2. Discussion Item
2.01 Interview Candidates for Board Director District #3
One candidate applied for Board Director District #3 vacated by Joseph Schweitzer. Interview questions were asked
of the candidate. The candidate interviewed was Brianne Zier.
3. Executive Session
3.01 Pursuant to RCW 42.30.110, Subsection (1) (g), Evaluate the Qualifications of Candidates for Board of Director,
District #3 Vacancy
President Fazzari announced the board will go into executive session for approximately 10 minutes.
The board went into executive session at 5:30 PM.
The board came out of executive session and reconvened into the open meeting at 5:40 PM.
4. Action Item
4.01 Appointment of Director, District #3 – if a Decision is Reached – Roll Call Vote
It was moved by Director Naff and seconded by Director Gallo to appoint Brianne Zier as Director of District #3.
Roll call vote taken: : Director Naff-aye, President Fazzari-aye, Director Gallo-aye. Motion passed unanimously, 3-0.
