BOARD OF DIRECTORS
October 6, 2011
The Bellingham School Board of Directors met in regular session October 6, 2011 in the Board
Room of the District Office, 1306 Dupont Street, Bellingham, Washington.

OPENING ITEMS
At 7:00 p.m. President Ann Whitmyer called the regular meeting to order.
Roll Call
Present: Directors Kelly Bashaw, Steve Smith, Scott Stockburger, Ken Gass, President Ann
Whitmyer, Superintendent Greg Baker and recorder Janet Kennedy.

CONSENT AGENDA ITEMS
Smith moved and Stockburger seconded the motion to remove from the consent agenda item the
following item “Approval of Purchase and Sale Agreement” for discussion in Executive Session.
The motion carried three to one with President Whitmyer abstaining.
Gass moved and Smith seconded approval of the consent agenda without the “Approval of
Purchase and Sale Agreement.” The motion carried unanimously.
