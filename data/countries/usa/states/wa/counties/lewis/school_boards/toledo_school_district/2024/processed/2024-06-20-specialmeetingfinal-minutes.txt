Toledo School District
Board of Directors
June 20, 2024
The special meeting of the Board of Directors was called to order at 5:42 p.m. by Chair Heidi
Buswell. Board members in attendance included Heidi Buswell, Brad Dykstra, Lance Maier and
Adam Hutchinson. Mr. Buswell was absent.
The flag salute was led by Mrs. Buswell.
Land AcknowledgementMrs. Buswell read the land acknowledgement.
Board AgendaMr. Dykstra made a motion approve the June 20, 2024 special meeting agenda as presented. Mr. Maier
seconded the motion. Motion passed.
Public CommentBethany Hutchinson noted she had heard concerns about Mr. Bailey’s attire, attendance at events and
communication.
Mandy Kruger noted that he always has the best interest of the students in mind. His calendar is reflective
of the events he attends and it is a lot.
Stephanie Shiflett noted she sees him a lot at TES. She noted she knew of times he slept at school to
evaluate roads and staff know he cares.
Adam Hutchinson, on behalf of a community member, noted he fixed things like budget, life skills, brought
back grandparents lunch, passed the levy. He has found good ways to save money. They like his financial
management style. One area of improvement would be eliminating late starts.
Executive SessionThe board convened into executive session at 5:55p.m. to evaluate the performance of an employee. Mrs.
Buswell announced executive session would end at 6:25p.m. and action may be taken. The board returned
from executive session at 6:25p.m.
Miscellaneous ActionMr. Maier made a motion to extend the superintendent’s contract for an additional year. Mr. Hutchinson
seconded the motion. Motion passed.
AdjournmentMr. Hutchinson moved to adjourn the meeting. Mr. Dykstra seconded the motion. Motion passed.
Meeting adjourned at 6:25p.m.
Approved this 18th day of July, 2024
