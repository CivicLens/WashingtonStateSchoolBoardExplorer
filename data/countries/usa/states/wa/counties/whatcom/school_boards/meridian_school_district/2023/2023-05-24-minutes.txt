REGULAR SCHOOL BOARD MEETING AND EXECUTIVE SESSION
MEETING HELD IN PERSON & VIA ZOOM
May 24, 2023
REGULAR MEETING
The regular meeting was called to order by Board Chair Sara Bernardy at 7:00 p.m.
Board Chair Sara Bernardy welcomed everyone that was in attendance.
Board members present at the meeting were Sara Bernardy, Marty Gray, Craig Mitchell, and Jarrod
Karuza. Student Board Representative Mirage DeNeal was also in attendance.
Board member Steve Corwin and Student Board Representative Kurtis Kettman was excused from
attending.
The pledge of allegiance was recited.
A motion to adopt the agenda was made by Craig Mitchell and seconded by Marty Gray. The motion
passed 4-0.
CONSENT AGENDA
A. Approval of Meeting Minutes
B. Personnel Report
C. Accounts Payable
D. Approval of Revised Board Yearly Plan of Work
E. Student Travel
A motion to adopt the consent agenda was made by Jarrod Karuza and seconded by Craig Mitchell. The
motion passed 3-0. Marty Gray abstained from voting.
RECOGNITION
Masonic Lodge Educator of the Year
Dr. Everett and Masonic Lodge member Mr. Dick Decima presented MHS teacher Fred Wiley with the
Lynden/Ferndale Masonic Lodge No. 56: Meridian School District Educator of the Year Award. Mr.
Decima thanked the Board for allowing the Masonic Lodge to be present.
Student Board Member Recognition
Dr. Everett recognized student board members, Kurtis Kettman and Mirage DeNeal for their service as
student Board representatives.
REPORTS
Review Progress & Strategic Plan Amendments; Policy 700
Dr. Everett and Teaching and Learning Director Adrienne Somera reported to the Board on the progress
of our Strategic Plan implementation plan. The presentation included an identified outcome area of
focus and measurement metrics for each of the strategic plan core values that will be prioritize for the
2023-24 school year.

PUBLIC COMMENT
There was no public comment.
NEW BUSINESS
Curriculum Materials Proposal
Teaching and Learning Director, Adrienne Somera reviewed the curriculum review and adoption process
as well as the board policy guidelines that curriculum must meet. Discovery Education Techbook, World
History Project 1200-Present, and Bridge to College Math curriculum materials were presented to the
Board for a first review. The proposed curriculum will be brought before the Board for a second review
and approval at the June 21 Board meeting.
FINANCIAL REPORT
Financial Report
Director of Finance Daniel Yorton gave a financial update to the Board. The financial update included
student enrollment comparisons, our budget status and budget projections. Daniel shared updates
about the audit.
