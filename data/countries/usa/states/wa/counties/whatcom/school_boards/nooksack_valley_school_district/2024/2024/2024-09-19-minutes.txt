CALL TO ORDER
The regular meeting of the Nooksack Valley School Board of Directors was called to order by
Chairman, Steve Jones, at 7:00 p.m. on Thursday, September19, 2024.
MINUTES APPROVED REGULAR MEETING
Ty Elmendorf moved, and Tanya Silves seconded the motion to approve the minutes of the August 15,
2024, regular meeting.
M.C. 4-0
COMMUNICATIONS
• Mark Shintaffer raised questions about safety at the high school. He cited the number of fights
to start the new year, ongoing concerns about vaping, and his continued concerns about the
safety of restrooms for students, again mentioning the number of incidents he hears about from
his and other children. Supt. Galley discussed some of the details regarding the incidents and
pledged to work with HS administrators to address the concerns.
• Supt Galley shared his experiences at “Septemberfest” over the weekend, a fundraiser for Be
The One. Steve Jones and Tanya Silves echoed positive experiences, and Mark Shintaffer spoke
to the group’s plans to improve next year.
• Supt Galley shared information about the upcoming “Rally the Valley” fundraiser on
September 21, at Samson Winery. Proceeds will benefit the general ASB account to ensure
students have access to extracurricular, club, and other enrichment opportunities.
• Tanya Silves shared the positive experiences she observed while attending the fair in August
with FFA students, citing the multitude of different activities students are engaged in.
• Ty Elmendorf mentioned the upcoming community “Tail Gate” sponsored by the Nooksack
Valley Athletic Club and the District before Friday night’s football game. It has been a great
community gathering for the last few years.

APPROVE SUMAS ELEMENTARY SCHOOL 5TH GRADE STUDENTS TO ATTEND

Sumas Elementary’s vision for student experience and vision for student learning, its connection to the
science standards, and the great opportunity it is for students. There was a brief Q & A from the board
