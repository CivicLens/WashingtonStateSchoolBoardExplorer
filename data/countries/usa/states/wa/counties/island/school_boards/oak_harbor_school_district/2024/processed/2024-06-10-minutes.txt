Call to Order

A regular meeting of the Board of Directors was held on Monday, June 10, 2024, in the
Administrative Service Center Board Room and was available for live streaming.
President Lynn Goebel called the meeting to order at 6:00 p.m. and led the flag salute.

Roll Call

Board members Lynn Goebel, Jessica Aws, Nicole Tesch, Sharon Jensen, and Josh
MacLean were present. Student board representative Maggie Litvachuk was present.
Student representative Josiah Villano was absent and excused.

Adoption of Agenda

The agenda was adopted by unanimous consent.

Consent Agenda

Vice President Aws moved to approve the consent agenda. Director MacLean seconded
the motion. The motion passed unanimously.

Superintendent
Report

Dr. Kuss-Cybula thanked everyone who participated in the year-long strategic planning
process. She provided a regular update on her goal work as it relates to multi-tiered
systems of supports for students, instructional reviews, and student testing data. She
voiced her pride in the work of district staff to help students grow in proficiency.

OHPS Strategic Plan
2024-2030

Adoption of OHPS
Strategic Plan
2024-2030

Vice President Aws moved to adopt the OHPS Strategic Plan 2024-2030. Director Tesch
seconded the motion. The motion passed unanimously.

State associations in order to advocate with a strong, unified voice to the legislature.
