Staff submitted the Constructability Review Report in
connection with the Sunnyland Elementary School rebuild.
As superintendent, I have reviewed and approve this item.

Explanation of Issue:

One of the requirements for receiving OSPIs state construction assistance (SCAP) funding for the new
SunnylandElementary School is for the district to conduct a constructability review of the projects
construction documents. The process involves reviewing the plans associated with the project in order to
identify errors, ambiguities, omissions, discrepanciesand conflicts that could become potential problem
areas during the construction phase and result in cost and/or schedule impacts.
Our GC/CM partner, Dawson Construction, provided this constructability review of the new Sunnyland
Elementary School plans and specifications. This report then went to the design team who reviewed the
comments and provided a response to each comment/question. Our Capital Projects team has also
reviewed the report and responses with the design team and our GC/CM partner. A copy of the report is
attached.
