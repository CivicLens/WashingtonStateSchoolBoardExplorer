Thursday, November 17, 2022
Special Board Meeting:Executive Session
While in attendance at the annual WSSDA Conference in Spokane, Washington, the directors will hold a
special meeting, moving into executive session, for the purpose of reviewing the performance of a public
employee. Telephonic public observation of this meeting's opening and adjournment will be available
locally in the districts boardroom at 770 Mitchell Avenue in Blaine.

Public Content
President Thomas will call the meeting to order at 3:00 p.m. Director Creydt has indicated she will not be
in attendance, and she has been excused. The other three directors are expected to be in attendance
while at the WSSDA Conference in Spokane, Washington. Telephonic observation of the meeting's
opening and adjournment will be provided in the districts boardroom at 770 Mitchell Avenue in Blaine.

Public Content
Pursuant to Policy 1410, the board may choose to convene in executive session for specific purposes
allowed by state statute. President Thomas will announce that the board will move into executive session
for the purpose of reviewing the performance of a public employee. The executive session is anticipated
to last for approximately two (2) hours, or until 5:00 p.m. No action will be taken by the board after the
executive session.
