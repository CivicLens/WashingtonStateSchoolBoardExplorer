Washtucna School District #109
Regular Board of Directors Meeting
Washtucna, WA
July 28, 2020

The Budget Hearing of the Washtucna School Board was called to order at 7:30 p.m. by board
chair Steve Crouse. The meeting started late due to establishing a quorum. This meeting was
conducted online via Zoom with the following attendees: Board members Steve Crouse, Jeremy
Jessop and Teresa Correll, Superintendent Todd Spear, Business Manager Katie Lasen, Mary
Jessop and Kiera Buriak. Board members Jake Sitton and Vickie Boland were absent.
BUDGET HEARING
The budgets for all funds were discussed, including the 4-year projections and the 2020-21 MSOC
Disclosure form, pursuant to RCW 28A.505. The regular meeting of the Washtucna School Board
began immediately after the budget hearing.

Teresa Correll moved to approve the agenda. Passed 3-0.

ADMINISTRATIVE REPORT
Board approval of the Washtucna Education Association Bargaining Agreement will be held over
until our next meeting. The board scheduled a special meeting to approve our Re-Opening Plan
on August 10th at 7:00 p.m. Mr. Spear discussed staff position openings including bus driver,
maintenance, and technology. Mr. Spear has designated our district person for asbestos training
as Michael Amend. Jeremy Jessop motioned to approve the Science textbook adoption. Passed
3-0. Teresa Correll motioned to approve the History textbook adoption. Passed 3-0. The shack
water damage was discussed and Mr. Spear will be in contact with our insurance company.
Information about Qualtrics, a product to help track COVID symptoms, was also discussed. Teresa
Correll motioned to approve the 1st and 2nd readings of board policy #3413 and 3413P, Student
Immunization and Life-Threatening Health Conditions. Passed 3-0. Graduation is scheduled for
this Saturday, August 1, 2020.
