Meeting Minutes
Lynden School District
Board Room

06/09/2023 7:30am

1. Call to Order, Welcome & Pledge of Allegiance, Roll Call
Meeting called to order by CJ Costanti at 7:30am. Board of Directors in attendance were CJ
Costanti, David Vis, Cindy Petersen, Tonya Hickman, and Ken Owsley. Superintendent
VanderYacht was also in attendance.
2. Board Development and Processes
Discussion was held regarding the Board of Directors handbook. This was followed by a review
of the WSSDA Board Self- Assessment results.
3. Key District Performance Measures
The group participated in a prioritization activity to compile a list of potential measurement data
that will allow the board to identify priorities.
4. Strategic Plan Development
The current Lynden School District Strategic Plan expires in 2024. Discussion was held regarding
the development of the 2025-2028 Strategic Plan and corresponding priorities.
5. Adjourn Meeting
CJ Costanti motioned to adjourn the meeting at 11:30am
