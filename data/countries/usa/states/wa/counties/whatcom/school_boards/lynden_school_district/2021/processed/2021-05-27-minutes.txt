5. Reports
Superintendent
• Health Department Updates
Jim Frey provided an update on recent communication with the Whatcom County Health
Department and the K-12 School Guidance for summer school and re-opening in the fall
pertaining to face covering requirements, quarantine recommendations, current case rates, and
vaccination discussions for staff and students.

Teaching and Learning
• Re-Opening Plan 2021-2022
Elizabeth Hamming shared the preliminary re-opening plan that will need to be submitted to
Office of Superintendent of Public Instruction (OSPI). Elizabeth shared the phases of this work
and how we will continue to collect data to monitor progress.
Finance
• Budget Update - April
The Board reviewed information on the budget for February containing a summary of financial
status, enrollment, general fund comparative, and revenues and budgeting.
6. Board Workshop
• School Improvement Plan (SIP) Discussion
The Board discussed what to expect from the SIP reports that principals will provide in June. The
focus will be on topics of achievement; student wellbeing; and district support.
•

Boundary Adjustment Discussion
David VanderYacht provided realignment boundary maps for review of adjusting neighborhood
boundaries to accommodate growth within our elementary schools. The Board thanked David
for his work on this project, along with the transportation department and Student Data System
Specialist, Kim Olson.

7. Action Items
• Boundary Adjustment Approval
After review of the boundary maps, the Board has commissioned the District Office to move
forward with the neighborhood boundary adjustments.
CJ Costanti motioned to approve a boundary adjustment seconded by David Vis. Motion carried by voice
vote.
