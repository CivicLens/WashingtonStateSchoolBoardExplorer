1.3 Public Hearing on Academic and Student WellBeing Recovery Plan

Meeting :

May 26, 2021 - Special Board Meeting with Public
Hearing

Type :

Discussion

Public Content
The Bellingham School District No. 501 will submit its Academic and Student Well-Being Recovery Plan,
due to OSPI on June 1. The plan asks districts to identify a planning team and look at data to see student
needs and make plans for recovery and acceleration.
An electronic copy will be available on the Bellingham School District website by June 1. Printed copies
will be available for any citizen who may request one by calling the district business office at 360-6766512 to arrange a pick-up time. The Board of Directors of Bellingham School District No. 501 will
meetMay 26, 2021at 12:00 noonvia Zoom, during which time they will approve the plan. This is a
reiterative process and plans may be edited and changed after June 1 to include more staff and
community engagement and feedback. Any citizen may be heard for or against any part of the plan by
emailing input to sheri.oday@bellinghamschools.org.
