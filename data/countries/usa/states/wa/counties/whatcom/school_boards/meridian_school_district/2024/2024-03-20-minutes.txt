MERIDIAN SCHOOL DISTRICT #505
WORK SESSION & REGULAR SCHOOL BOARD MEETING
MEETING HELD IN PERSON & VIA ZOOM
March 20, 2024

REGULAR MEETING
The regular meeting was called to order by Board President Steve Cowin at 5:00 p.m.
Board members present at the meeting were Steve Corwin, Jana Haugen, and Michelle Simmons.
BOARD WORK SESSION
The board moved immediately into a WSSDA Board Bootcamp work session. No action was taken during
the work session.
RECONVENE REGULAR BOARD MEETING
Board President Steve Corwin welcomed everyone to the regular board meeting at 6:00 pm.
Board Director’s Marty Gray and Jarrod Karuza joined the regular meeting at 6:00 pm.
Student Board Representative Kennedy Brzozowski and Morgan Adams were absent from the meeting.
The pledge of allegiance was recited.
A motion to adopt the agenda was made by Jana Haugen and seconded by Jarrod Karuza. The motion
passed 5-0.
CONSENT AGENDA
A. Approval of Meeting Minutes
B. Accounts Payable
C. Personnel Report
D. Student Travel
A motion to adopt the consent agenda was made by Jana Haugen and seconded by Michelle Simmons.
The motion passed 5-0.
REPORTS
MSD Athletics and Activities Report
Bryant Michaelson, Director of Athletics and Activities, presented an Athletics and Activities Report to the
Board. Bryant shared details about student participation in athletic activities. Bryant mentioned that the
naming committee met recently, and they are currently seeking community suggestions for naming the
north gym.
Legislative Update
Dr. Everett presented a report to the Board on the supplemental legislative session. Dr. Everett shared
that overall, the session was not great for funding k-12 education. Dr. Everett spoke about the small
increases that were approved during the session.
PUBLIC COMMENT
Andrea Ashmore signed in for public comment in error. There was no public comment.

NEW BUSINESS

First Review: Curriculum Materials Proposal

Director of Teaching and Learning, Adrienne Somera gave an overview of the core curriculum adoption
process and presented Eureka Math² and Adíos Textbook curriculum materials to the Board for a first
review. This will be brought back for second review and approval at the April 10, 2024, Regular Board
meeting.
First Reading: Policy and Procedure 2121 – Substance Abuse Program
Assistant Superintendent Kurt Harvill presented Policy and Procedure 2121 – Substance Abuse Program

to the Board for a first review. This will be brought back before the Board for second review and
approval at the April 10, 2024, Regular Board meeting.

First Reading: Policy and Procedure 3116 - Students in Foster Care
Assistant Superintendent Kurt Harvill presented Policy and Procedure 3116 - Students in Foster Care to

the Board for a first review. This will be brought back before the Board for second review and approval
at the April 10, 2024, Regular Board meeting.

First Reading: Policy and Procedure 3225 - School Based Threat Assessment
Assistant Superintendent Kurt Harvill presented Policy and Procedure 3225 - School Based Threat
Assessment to the Board for a first review. This will be brought back before the Board for second review

and approval at the April 10, 2024, Regular Board meeting.

FINANCE REPORT
Finance Report
Director of Finance, Daniel Yorton reported to the Board on the March enrollment compared to
February and enrollment compared to budget. Daniel also presented an update on the annual audit that
was completed this week. Daniel updated the Board on the general status of our district finances,
financial projections and unforeseen expenses that have come up.
SUPERINTENDENT’S REPORT
Dr. Everett began his report began his reporting reminding and encouraging people to submit
nominations for the annual MSD Thrive Awards. Dr. Everett shared information about events and
meetings he recently attended, and the legislative allocated capital budget funds for the Whatcom Early
Learning Center and the Whatcom County Skills Center Preconstruction.
INFORMATION FROM STUDENT BOARD REPRESENTATIVES
There was no information shared.
INFORMATION FROM BOARD DIRECTORS
Happy Birthday to Steve Corwin, who’s birthday is today. Director Corwin allowed Denise Barnes with
the Meridian Boosters Club to share a Booster update with the Board.
BOARD SIGNATURE OF OFFICIAL DOCUMENTS
ADJOURNMENT
There being no further business, the Board meeting was adjourned at 7:44 pm by Board President Steve
Corwin.
