DRAFT MINUTES OF THE SPECIAL MEETING OF THE
BELLINGHAM SCHOOL DISTRICT BOARD OF DIRECTORS
March 16, 2023
The Board of Directors held a special meeting on March 16, 2023 at Options High School, 2015
Franklin Street, Bellingham, Washington. This was an informational meeting for prospective
school board candidates. The fall election filing opportunity with the Whatcom County Auditor’s
Office is May 15-17, 2023.
President Camille Diaz Hackler called the meeting to order at 5:00 p.m.
