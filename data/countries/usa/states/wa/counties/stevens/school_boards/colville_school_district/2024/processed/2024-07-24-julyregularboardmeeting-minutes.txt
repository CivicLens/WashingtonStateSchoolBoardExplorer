6.01 Requirements for Graduation with Honors
Colville High School wants to change the requirements for Graduation with Honors and the selection of
Valedictorian and Salutatorian. Beginning with the graduating class of 2028, there will be one Valedictorian and one
Salutatorian selected. Valedictorian and Salutatorian will be the top individuals from the list of students with
Academic Distinction. Their GPA will be rounded to the nearest hundredth. The top cumulative GPA will be
Valedictorian and the second will be Salutatorian. If there is a tie, the following will be used for determination:
•
•

Most Honors/AP/CiHS courses
Highest SAT score – if there is still a tie

7. Superintendent’s Report
7.01 District Update
Summer Maintenance Update:
The high school track project should be finished this week.
The SMART boards have been arriving and Technology is busy putting them up.
The new coolers at Fort and Hofstetter are up and running.
The Fort playground project should be completed by the start of school.
The WSSDA Annual Conference is November 21-23 in Spokane. Heather Clark will be reaching out to board
members to see if they plan on attending.
The District is not planning to rerun a Capital Levy this Fall. We will try to meet some of the building and facility
needs through grants and other programs we qualify for. Will keep you updated.
Local athletic directors are wanting to put together a resolution amendment and send to the WIAA, supporting
fairness for all athletes. Our district is supportive, but we will want to go into this partnering with a larger school
district. The goal is to have these resolution amendments sent in October.
