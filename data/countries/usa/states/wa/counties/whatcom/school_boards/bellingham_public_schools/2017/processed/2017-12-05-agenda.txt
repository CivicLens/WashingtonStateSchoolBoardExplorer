Tuesday, December 5, 2017
School Board Visit - Columbia Elementary School
The Board of Directors of Bellingham Public Schools regularly visits a different school throughout the
school year. The purpose of these visits is to learn how each school is delivering upon The Bellingham
Promise. The directors will visit Columbia Elementary school at 7:30 a.m. Dec. 5, 2017. Columbia
Elementary School is located at 2508 Utter St.
