Tuesday, January 16, 2024
Special Board Meeting - Executive Session starting at 4:30 p.m. and Regular Board Meeting/Work Study
Session - 5:00 p.m.
The regularly scheduled Work Study Session of the Federal Way School Board will convene at 4:30 p.m.
rather than the regularly scheduled time of 5:00 p.m. The Board will immediately recess for a closed
Executive Session and reconvene to the Regular Board Meeting/Work Study Session at 5:00 p.m. at
FWPS Educational Service Center, 33330 8th Avenue S., Federal Way, WA 98003.
Board of Directors: President Dr. Jennifer Jones, Vice President & Legislative Liaison Luckisha Phillips,
Trudy Davis, Quentin Morris, Joan Marie Murphy
Following current Washington State guidelines for board meetings, this meeting will be held in-person
with an option to attend remotely.
ATTENDING THE BOARD MEETING IN-PERSON:
The board meeting will be held at the FWPS Educational Service Center located at 33330 8th Avenue
South, Federal Way, WA 98003.
