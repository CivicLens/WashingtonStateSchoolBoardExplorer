Monday, January 11, 2021
Special Board Meeting:Executive Session
The Directors will meet in executive session to review the annual evaluation of the Superintendent.
Pursuant to extended proclamations from the Washington State Governor related to the COVID-19
pandemic emergency, participation in this meeting will take place remotely. Live public observation of the
meeting's opening and adjournment will be available online, however the executive session is not open to
the public. Click on the "Video" link above to access this meeting as an observer. (Meeting Passcode:
473012)

Public Content
As per legal guidelines, the board may choose to recess into executive session to consider employment
or dismissal of personnel, to review the performance of a public employee, to consult with legal counsel,
to consider the position to be taken in collective bargaining, or to consider acquisition or sale of real
estate.
Prior to the start of executive session, the president will announce the purpose and length of the
session.
At the close of the executive session, the directors will reconvene in regular session at which point
they may take formal action or the meeting will be adjourned.
President Gibson will announce that the board will move into executive session for the purpose of
reviewing the performance of a public employee. The executive session is anticipated to last for sixty (60)
minutes, but may be extended if necessary. No action will be taken by the board after the executive
session.
