MERIDIAN SCHOOL DISTRICT #505
SPECIAL SESSION MEETING
MEETING HELD IN PERSON
February 26, 2024

SPECIAL SESSION MEETING
The special session meeting was called to order by Board President Steve Cowin at 7:00 am.
Board Directors present at the meeting were Steve Corwin, Jarrod Karuza and Michelle Simmons. Jana
Haugen was present via zoom. Marty Gray abstained from attendance.
A motion to adopt the agenda was made by Jarrod Karuza and seconded by Michelle Simmons. The
motion passed 4-0.
DISCUSSION AND POSSIBLE ACTION
Discussion and Possible Action Opposition to ESB 5462 and ESHB 2331
Steve asked for comments regarding the letter that was drafted to Senator Shewmake, Representative
Rule and Representative Timmons. There were no questions of comments. Michelle Simmons made a
motioned to proceed with signing the letter of opposition to ESB 5462 and ESHB 2331 as presented. The
motion was seconded by Jarrod Karuza. The motioned passed 4-0.
ADJOURNMENT
There being no further business, the Board meeting was adjourned at 7:02 am. by Board President Steve
Corwin.
