Recommended Action :

To adopt Resolution 1920-11 Emergency Suspension of
Policy in response to the current COVID-19 pandemic
state of emergency

Public Content
In order to facilitate the Districts ability to immediately implement written guidance from health and
government agencies as disseminated by OSPI, the board will consider adoption of Resolution 1920-11
Emergency Suspension of Policy. This resolution language was recommended by WSSDA in order to
assist districts in streamlining their actions in response to the COVID- 19 pandemic state of emergency.
