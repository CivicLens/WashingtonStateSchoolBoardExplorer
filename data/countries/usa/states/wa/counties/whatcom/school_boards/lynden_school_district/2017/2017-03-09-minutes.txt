1. Call to Order, Welcome, Pledge of Allegiance and Roll Call
Meeting called to order at 6:31 P.M. Board members in attendance were Brian Johnson, Steve Jilk, Kevin Burke,
and Dominic Shiu. Student Directors Devin Gartner and Katelyn Fullner.
2. Good News

The middle school held Math Fun Night on February 28th. Families enjoyed dinner while math teachers
shared information about the new math standards, instructional shifts and new math materials. The rest of
the night was spent exploring engaging math problems.
Isom has started 3 new after school clubs recently, with a total of over 100 kids participating. The clubs are
Dance Club and Art Club facilitated by Isom PTA, and Chess Club run by LHS Honor Society/Chess Club
students.
Bernice Vossbeck instructors have celebrated the progress their students are making in the use of the new
Writing Progressions and accompanying Learning resources in materials.
The high school Chess Club has 2 teams attending the Chess State Tournament on March 3rd & 4th.
March 13th - 17th is Classified School Employee Week!

6. Reports
 Superintendent
BVE Robotics Club Presentation
Robotics Club Advisor, Jodi Oliver and Highly Capable Program Specialist, Megan Dickson along with
their students Karson Hamasaka, Weston VanDalen, Jesse Steward, Brady Elsner, and Kai Fierst
presented how students design and build programmable robots using high quality motors, sensors,
gears, wheels, axles, and other technical components. It was shared that students gain practical, hands-

on experiences using mathematical concepts such as estimating and measuring distance, time and
speed. The students gave a fantastic presentation of their work in the club
Planning Committee Update
Jim Frey shared the topics of discussion at the March 7th District Planning Committee meeting and the
process that is taking place to continue to review past and present solutions and eventually present at
a future board meeting.


Teaching and Learning
Elizabeth Hamming provided information about the review process and work that the Health Standards
Committee is doing. The topics for review were identified by the Standards Committee as areas of
emphasis. The final choice of the Adoption Committee will be forwarded to the Instructional Materials
Committee for final review and recommendations to the School Board.



Finance and Operations
None at this meeting.

7. Board Workshop
Construction Update
Jim Frey gave an update on the 4 classroom addition at Fisher Elementary and the proposed 4 classroom
addition at Lynden Middle School. The Board reviewed the Delegation of Authority for the
Superintendent to approve construction change orders. Board member, Kevin Burke recommended to
move the amount to $60,000 on change orders before two Board members are a part of the approval
process.
Steve Jilk motioned to approve the Delegation of Authority seconded by Dominic Shiu. Motion carried by voice
vote.
School Report Cards
The Board reviewed school report cards that provides data for the 2015-2016 school year data based on
student demographics, teacher information, assessments, School Improvement Plan, community
involvement, building conditions, and budget revenue and expenditures.
