BELLINGHAM PUBLIC SCHOOLS
Bellingham, Washington
MINUTES OF THE STUDY SESSION
OF THE
BELLINGHAM SCHOOL DISTRICT
BOARD OF DIRECTORS
January 17, 2013

The Bellingham School Board of Directors met in a study session January 17, 2013 at the
District Office, 1306 Dupont Street, Bellingham, Washington.
President Ken Gass called the meeting to order at 6:00 p.m.
Roll Call
Present: Directors Camille Hackler, Steve Smith, Kelly Bashaw, Scott Stockburger, President
Ken Gass, and Superintendent Greg Baker.
The Board was joined by Deputy Superintendent Tom Venable, Assistant Superintendent of
Finance and Operations Ron Cowan, Executive Director of Teaching and Learning Steve Clarke,
Administrator on Special Assignment Rob McElroy, Executive Director of Human Resources
Hank Harris, and Executive Director of Communications and Community Relations Tanya Rowe
for a portion of the meeting.
Agenda
The Board discussed
The Bellingham Promise Priorities for Progress
2012–2013 budget and planning for the 2013–2014 budget
Facilities update
Governance Policies and monitoring reports for Executive Limitations
Each Board member shared comments from their attendance at the Washington State School
Directors Association Annual Conference held in Spokane, Washington in November 2012.
Adjournment
The meeting adjourned at 9:55 p.m.
