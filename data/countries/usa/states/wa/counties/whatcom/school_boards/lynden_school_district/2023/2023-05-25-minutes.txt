a. SuperintendentLynden Middle School Counselors Coral Bartlett and Jack Hallmark presented a
counseling overview for LMS. This was followed by a counseling overview presentation

presentations included a mission statement, statistics comparing Lynden to Washington
state averages, current needs and focus areas, and an overview of services provided to
students on a regular basis.
Staci Lopshire, Bree Maberry, and Jill Sawka presented information to the board about

the work related to the Reduced Educational Plan. He then provided an update on

Planning group and the Bond Planning Task Force group.
