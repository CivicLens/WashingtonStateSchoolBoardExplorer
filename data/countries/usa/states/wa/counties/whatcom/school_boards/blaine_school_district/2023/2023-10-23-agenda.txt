3. Public Comment
Subject :

A. Public Comment

Meeting :

Oct 23, 2023 - Regular Board Meeting

Category :

3. Public Comment

Type :

Procedural

Public Content
A dedicated email has been established for the purpose of accepting written public comments prior to each regular meeting.
Patrons may send their written comments tocomments@blainesd.org. All comments received at least 3 hours prior to the
meeting will be provided to each director for review individually prior to the meeting. Written comments received less than three
(3) hours prior to the start of the meeting may not be reviewed by the directors until after the meeting.
Pursuant toPolicy 1400, the board will set aside up to thirty (30) minutes at the beginning of the regular monthly meeting to allow
an opportunity for the public to address the board on any topic within the scope of the board's responsibility.
During the Public Comment section of the agenda, the board president will recognize those who wish to provide oral comments
to the directors.Patrons who wish to address the board during the Public Comment sectionmust sign in between 6:45pm-6:55pm
and acknowledge their topic of interest. The following information should be included when signing up to address the board with
oral comments:

1. Name
2. Contact Information (Address/Phone/Email)
3. Topic of Interest

Participants are asked to keep their comments to three (3) minutes. At any given board meeting, comments on a common topic
may be limited to three (3) individuals. Other individuals interested in making comments specific to that common topic will be
asked to put their comments into writing, sent to the email address shown above.
Parents and patrons are asked to follow Policy 4220and its related procedure regarding complaints about staff, students, or
programs. Those presenting concerns in an open meeting venue, are asked to refrain from reference (by name) to a specific
staff member or a specific student. Any complaints specific to an individual staff member or student should be submitted in
writing to the email address shown above.
Anyone with questions prior to the meeting may contact the administrative assistant at 360-332-0711.

Subject :

A. 6000 Series Policy Manual Update - Policies 6020,
6114, and 6212: Second Reading and Adoption

Meeting :

Oct 23, 2023 - Regular Board Meeting

Category :

6. Action Items

Type :

Action

Recommended Action :

To approve "Second Reading" and adoption of updates
to policies 6020, 6114, and 6212

Public Content
At the September meeting, the board approved updates tothe 6000 series of the Blaine School District's
Policy Manual, with the exception of policies 6020, 6114, 6210, and 6212 which would be considered at
the October meeting. Upon further review of Policy 6210 Purchasing: Authorization and Control, it has
been determined that language recommended by WSSDA from 2018 is not desirable, and the district's
current, April 2021 version will be retained.
The board is now asked to approve "Second Reading" and adoption of updates to the following policies in
this series:
Policy 6020 System of Funds and Accounts: A minor adjustment to the final section was added
to clarify language in reference to petty cash accounts.
Policy 6114 Gifts or Donations: There have been no changes to the recommended update.
Policy 6212 Charge Cards: There have been no changes to the recommended update.
