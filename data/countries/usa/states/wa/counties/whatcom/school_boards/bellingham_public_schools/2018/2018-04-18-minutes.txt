PUBLIC HEARING:
Ms. Bashaw explained the purpose of the public hearing: to take public testimony, both
for and against a proposed amendment to Resolution 14-13 that will allow bond proceeds
from the 2013 bond issue to be used to start work on projects identified in the 2018 bond
measure. The board will consider approval of the proposed amendment at the regular
meeting immediately following the public hearing.
