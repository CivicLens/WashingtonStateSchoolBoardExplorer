Blaine School District No. 503
  Special Board Meeting:Executive Session
Monday, September 27, 2021
  Minutes
  1. Special Board Meeting
A. Call to Order
In accordance with guidelines related to the COVID-19 pandemic emergency, this meeting was conducted with both virtual and in-person components. President Gibson called the meeting to order at 6:00 p.m. Director Thomas was out of the area and attended the executive session remotely. All Directors were in attendance.
     Todd Nunamaker, Dougal Thomas, Joan Lotze, Charles E Gibson, Laura McKinney
  2. Executive Session
A. Executive Session
President Gibson announced that the board will move into executive session for the purpose of reviewing the performance of a public employee. The executive session was anticipated to last for approximately forty-five (45) minutes. No action would be taken by the board during this special meeting.

Director Thomas joined the meeting in executive session at 6:03 p.m. and left the meeting, prior to reconvening in regular session, at 6:43 p.m.
  3. Reconvene and Adjourn
A. Reconvene and Adjourn
At 6:45 p.m. the executive session ended and the board reconvened in regular session. There being no further business, the meeting was adjourned by a vote of 4-0 with 1 director not present at vote.
Motion by Todd Nunamaker, second by Laura McKinney
Final Resolution: Motion Carries 4-0, 1 not present at vote
Yea: Todd Nunamaker, Joan Lotze, Charles E Gibson, Laura McKinney
Not Present at Vote: Dougal Thomas
