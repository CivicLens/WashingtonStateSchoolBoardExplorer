Blaine School District No. 503
  Special Board Meeting
Wednesday, September 1, 2021
  Minutes
  1. Special Board Meeting
A. Call to Order
Due to restrictions resulting from the COVID-19 pandemic emergency, this meeting was conducted with remote director participation. There was no in-person participation.
President Gibson called the meeting to order at 7:03 a.m.
Director Nunamaker was not present. All other Directors were in attendance. Director Lotze was out of the area and attended remotely.
     Dougal Thomas, Joan Lotze, Charles E Gibson, Laura McKinney
  2. Action Items
A. SEIU Agreement and Salary Schedule
Superintendent Dr. Christopher Granger provided an overview of the agreed upon terms for the 2021-2023 SEIU Collective Bargaining Agreement which was ratified on August 31, 2021 by SEIU members. Director Thomas asked for clarification about the contract term, and Dr. Granger explained that the previous agreement expired yesterday. The board voted to approve ratification of the 2021-2023 agreement with SEIU Classified Employees, as well as the associated wage schedule for 2021-2022
  Motion by Dougal Thomas, second by Joan Lotze
Final Resolution: Motion Carries 4-0
Yea: Dougal Thomas, Joan Lotze, Charles E Gibson, Laura McKinney
  3. Adjourn
A. Adjourn
There being no further business, the meeting was adjourned by a unanimous vote at 7:08 a.m.
Motion by Laura McKinney, second by Dougal Thomas
Final Resolution: Motion Carries 4-0
Yea: Dougal Thomas, Joan Lotze, Charles E Gibson, Laura McKinney
