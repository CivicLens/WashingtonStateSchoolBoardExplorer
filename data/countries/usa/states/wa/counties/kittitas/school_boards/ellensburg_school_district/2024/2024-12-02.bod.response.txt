Cora St., Ellensburg, WA 98926 and are also available via Zoom. Links for the meetings can be found on the District website at esd401.org under Board Announcements, and on the front page of the District website under News and in the Calendar listing for the meeting. Notice of Special Meetings are posted in the same locations. Study Sessions and Regular Board Meetings are held on the same day, normally the 2nd and 4th Wednesday of the month. It is the practice of the Board to cancel or reschedule Study Sessions and Regular Board Meetings that conflict with holidays and the District's winter and spring breaks. Only one meeting is held in the months of July and December. Unless otherwise posted, Study Sessions and Regular Meetings begin at 5:30 PM. The public is invited to attend the School Board meetings.
