MERIDIAN SCHOOL DISTRICT #505
REGULAR SCHOOL BOARD MEETING
MEETING HELD IN PERSON & VIA YOUTUBE LIVE STREAM
November 20, 2024

REGULAR MEETING
The regular meeting was called to order by Board President Steve Corwin at 6:00 p.m. Steve welcomed
everyone that was in attendance.
Steve Corwin, Marty Gray, Jana Haugen, and Michelle Simmons were present at the meeting. Jarrod
Karuza was absent from the meeting.
Student Representatives Kennedy Brzozowski and Logan Ignacio were present at the meeting.
Assistant Superintendent Kurt Harvill was in attendance as secretary to the Board in the absence of Dr.
James Everett.
The pledge of allegiance was recited.
A motion to adopt the agenda was made by Jana Haugen and seconded by Marty Gray. The motion
passed 4-0.
CONSENT AGENDA
A. Approval of Meeting Minutes
B. Personnel Report
C. Accounts Payable
D. Donation Listing
E. Approval of Superintendent Contract Extension
After further discussion about the Donation Listing and the Superintendent Contract Extension, a motion
to approve the consent agenda as presented was made by Marty Gray and seconded by Jana Haugen.
The motion passed 3 in favor, 1 opposed.
PUBLIC COMMENT
There was no public comment.
PRESENTATIONS
PLC Presentations
The MMS Science team gave a PLC presentation to the Board. The presentation identified the PLT
challenge for each grade level, the learning progression tool students are using the reflect on their work,
technique, components and mechanism components.
Brent Feller with the MHS CTE team gave a PLC presentation to the Board. The CTE presentation
highlighted a welding essential standard area of focus. The presentation included initial data, the scoring
rubric the team is using to measure progress, and the resources the team is using to assist in improving
progress.
The IRE fourth grade team presented a formative math PLC presentation to the Board. The team shared
how they are using formative to measure multiplication problems, and the strategies the team is using
to grow skills.

Special Education Program Report
Director of Special Services, Aaron Jacoby, presented a Special Education Program Report to the Board.
The report included information about a dramatic increase in the number of qualified students,
celebrated successes, identified areas of growth, and future challenges.
Review of District Technology Plan
Director of Technology, Joe O’Brien, presented an Educational Technology Plan Review report. Joe’s
presentation included an update and review of the current EdTech Levy as well as the goals for the
2025-28 EdTech levy. Joe’s presentation also included a breakdown of district iPad and Chromebook
expenses.
OLD BUSINESS

NEW BUSINESS
Washington State Skills Center Association Interlocal Agreement
Kurt Harvill presented a Washington State Skills Center Association Interlocal Agreement for first review
and motion to approve. After review and discussion, students favor approval. A motion to approve the
Washington State Skills Center Association Interlocal Agreement as presented was made by Marty Gray
and seconded by Jana Haugen. The motion passed 4-0.
Levy Certification Resolution 12-24
Director of Business and Finance, Daniel Yorton, presented Levy Certification Resolution 12-24 for first
review and motion to approve. After review and discussion, students favor approval. A motion to
approve the Levy Certification Resolution 12-24 as presented was made by Michelle Simmons and
seconded by Jana Haugen. The motion passed 4-0.
