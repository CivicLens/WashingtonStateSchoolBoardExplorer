Washtucna School District #109
Regular Board of Directors Meeting
Washtucna, WA
September 24, 2024

The meeting was called to order at 7:02 p.m. by board chair Jake Sitton. Other board members
present were Bridget Coon, Alta Hunt and Derrick Kehn. Also attending were Superintendent Rob
Clark, Principal Staci Gloyn, and Business Manager Katie Lasen. Debbie Sitton attended online
via Zoom. Steve Crouse was absent.
ADOPTION OF THE AGENDA
Dr. Clark asked the board to remove the Grant Finalization Field Trip and reschedule when we
have a full board. Bridget Coon moved to approve the amended agenda. Passed 4-0.

BUSINESS MANAGER’S REPORT
Budget Status Reports for August were included in board packets and discussed at the meeting.
Our State Audit has begun. We will also have a Consolidate Program Review later this year.
PRINCIPAL’S REPORT
Staci Gloyn gave a report on school news including our new para educator, Teacher of the Year
Retreat, Pacific Science visit, elementary field trip and sports participation. She also gave a shout
out to Gen Yost for his hard work on cleaning up our school grounds.
