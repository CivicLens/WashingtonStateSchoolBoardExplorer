Call to Order

A regular meeting of the Board of Directors was held on Monday, January 8, 2024, in
the Administrative Service Center Board Room and was available for live streaming.
President Lynn Goebel called the meeting to order at 6:00 p.m. and led the flag salute.

Roll Call

Board members Lynn Goebel, Jessica Aws, Nicole Tesch, Sharon Jensen, and Josh
MacLean were present. Student board representatives Josiah Villano and Maggie
Litvachuk were present.

Adoption of Agenda

The agenda was adopted by unanimous consent.

Consent Agenda

Vice President Aws moved to approve the consent agenda. Director Jensen seconded
the motion. The motion passed unanimously.

Student
Representative
Report

Student Representative Josiah Villano gave a welcome to everyone for the first meeting
of 2024. He noted the success of the high school girls varsity basketball team and spoke
about upcoming athletic events. Student Representative Maggie Litvachuk also
emphasized the recent win by the girls basketball team as well as the first place finish
for the girls wrestling tournament. She highlighted an upcoming NJROTC competition,
Page 1 of 3

the Oak Harbor High School Martin Luther King Jr. assembly, a HomeConnection play,
school family nights and socials, State of the Schools, and Solo and Ensemble Festival.
Superintendent
Report

Superintendent Michelle Kuss-Cybula congratulated the girls basketball team and coach
and thanked the Information Services and Facilities teams for their work on front door
safety and visitor management systems. The first front door intercom system was
successfully rolled out at Olympic View Elementary School. She announced that
advisory committees for special education, facilities, and budget are starting up this
month. Superintendent Kuss-Cybula also brought up the need for parent and
community volunteers at North Whidbey Middle School following a recent middle
school students.

Special Programs
Report

Special Programs Director Rebecca Washington and Assistant Director Tonia Johnston
presented an update on the work of the Special Programs Department, which included
a report on the McKinney Vento Program.

Transportation
Report

Transportation Director Cathi Gutierrez presented an update on the work of the
Transportation Department.

Legislative Update

Director Tesch stated that today was the first day of the Legislative Session. The board
had a successful Legislative Forum with Senator Muzzall, Representative Dave Paul, and
Representative Clyde Shavers on December 15. She thanked them for participating.
Legislative committees will be discussing special services and free meals for students.

Collective Bargaining
Agreement between
OHSD and PSE 20232026

Vice President Aws moved to approve the Collective Bargaining Agreement between
OHSD and PSE 2023-2026. Director Tesch seconded the motion. The motion passed
unanimously.

November 2023
Board Financial
Reports

Executive Director of Business Services Vicki Williams presented the November 2023
financial reports.

Board Comments

President Goebel thanked former Student Representative Thinalyn Ramier for attending
the meeting.

Superintendent
Comments

Superintendent Kuss-Cybula thanked the presenters and how they demonstrate One
Oak Harbor in their work. The annual state auditing process has started.

Upcoming Meetings

Regular meetings are scheduled for Monday, January 29, and Monday, February 12,
2024, at 6:00 p.m. in the ASC Board Room. A Board Appreciation Lunch hosted by Oak
Harbor High School leadership students is planned for Tuesday, January 23, 2024, at
11:00 a.m. at Oak Harbor High School. The second annual State of the Schools event is
scheduled for Wednesday, January 24, 2024, at 4:30 p.m. at Oak Harbor High School.

Adjourn

The meeting adjourned at 7:21 p.m.
