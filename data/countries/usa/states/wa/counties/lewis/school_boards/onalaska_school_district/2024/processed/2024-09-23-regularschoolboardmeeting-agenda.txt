Audience Participation as allowed in Policy 1430
The board recognizes the value of public comment and the importance of involving members of
the public in its meetings.
Those wishing to be heard by the board shall first be called on by the board president from the
sign in list. Individuals, after identifying themselves, can then proceed to make public comments
and must adhere to a time limit of three minutes or less.
Please be aware, the board president may interrupt or terminate an individual's statement when
it is too lengthy, personally directed, abusive, obscene or irrelevant.

Although the board does not engage in discussion or offer an immediate response regarding
your comment, your input is valued and a school admin and /or board president will reach out to
you to follow up if needed.
