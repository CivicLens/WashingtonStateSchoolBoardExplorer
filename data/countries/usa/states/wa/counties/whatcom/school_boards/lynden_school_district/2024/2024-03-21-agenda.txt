2. Good News
Four staff members at Lynden Academy attended the Washington Association for Learning Alternatives (WALA) conference in
Lake Chelan. They returned energized with knowledge to share about uniquely connecting with, and the meeting the needs of,
the families, students and staff in LA's alternative learning environment.
Over 150 students participated in a Teen Heart Screening held on March 7th. The event was hosted by Lynden HOSA
(healthcare) Club and Lynden Christian High School. The screening of high school students from both Lynden Christian
and Lynden High School included a review of their personal & family health history, blood pressure, height, weight, and body
mass index, an electrocardiogram (EKG), and an on-site physician review of their test results. Students also received chestcompression CPR and AED defibrillator training. The event was sponsored by local paramedics, EMT's, Peace Health
Cardiology, and Boston Scientific.
Congratulations to the Isom Elementary PTA! They were selected by the Washington State PTA- Region 8 as one of
the recipients of the R.E.G.I.O.N. Award for Winter 2023-2024. They are being recognized for their Read-a-Thon fundraiser
which raised over $12.000 in two weeks through community donations and sponsorships to support students who logged
reading minutes. These funds are intended to be used toward items that benefit students and staff, including grade level
enrichment opportunities, classroom grants and supplies, and learning tools and equipment at the school.
