2. Draft Redistricting Plan
Subject :

A. Discussion of Redistricting Plan

Meeting :

Dec 7, 2021 - Special Board Meeting

Category :

2. Draft Redistricting Plan

Type :

Discussion

Public Content
Blaine School District is divided into five director districts with one representative from each district
serving on the Board of Directors. In order to ensure equal representation on the board, the boundaries
for each director district must be reviewed every ten years following the US Census. Sammamish Data
Systems has recommended twodraft redistricting plan options, the first of which was presented by Robert
Schweitzer, Redistricting Project Manager, at a special meeting on November 22.
The purpose of this meeting is to offer additionalopportunity for questions and comments about the plan
options. Any public comments that cannot be heard within the time allotted in this meeting can be
submitted in writing tocomments@blainesd.org no later than 12:00 p.m.on December 13 in order to be
considered by the board prior to taking action on adoption of the plan at the December 14 meeting.
Blaine School Districts projected timeline for completing this process is as follows:
November 15 Publication of the draft redistricting plan and notice of the public meeting
November 22 Public meeting to include the opportunity for questions and comments
December 7 - Additional public meeting to review options and provide more opportunity for
questions and comments

December 14 Tentative adoption of draft redistricting plan
