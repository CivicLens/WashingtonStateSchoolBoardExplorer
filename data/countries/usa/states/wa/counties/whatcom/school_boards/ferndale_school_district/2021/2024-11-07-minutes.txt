Members present: Andrew McLaurin, Kevin Erickson, Lee Anne Riddle, Jessie Deardorff, Melinda Cool,

Meeting called to order at 6:47 PM
1. CONVENE
1.01 Call to Order
Mr. McLaurin called the public hearing to order at 6:47 PM and welcomed audience members. He
also reminded the audience that the board will continue to require face masks or coverings at
meetings, due to rising COVID-19 case counts and the large number of people who remain
unvaccinated.
Present from the administration were Superintendent Dr. Linda Quinn, Assistant Superintendent
for Business & Support Services Mark Deebach and Executive Director of Teaching & Learning Kellie
Larrabee. Heather Leighton, principal of Vista Middle School, was also present.
The Pledge of Allegiance was then recited.
2. ADOPTION OF AGENDA
2.01 Adopt Agenda
Dr. Erickson moved to adopt the agenda. The motion carried unanimously.
3. PUBLIC COMMENT
3.01 Receive Public Comment Regarding the Proposed 2021-2022 District Budget
Mr. McLaurin said that the state requires the Board to hold an annual public hearing to receive
comments on the budget. No public comment was submitted, and no one attended the meeting
for the purpose of giving comment.
Mr. Deebach provided a brief refresher on the budget documents. He also told the board that
the district had recently posted new explanations of the various documents on the district
website, to clear any confusion that potential readers may have. Multiple board members
thanked Mr. Deebach for the new summary.
4. ADJOURN
Mr. McLaurin adjourned the public hearing at 7:02 PM. He then reminded those watching on
Zoom to switch to the second Zoom link to watch the upcoming regular board meeting.
