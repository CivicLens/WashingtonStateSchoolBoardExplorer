Wednesday, July 15, 2020
6:00 p.m. - Special Meeting/Public Hearing
The Board of Directors will hold a special meeting to conduct a public hearing to take public testimony,
both for and against, a proposed amendment to Resolution 25-17. RCW 28A.530.020 requires a public
hearing prior to amending the list of eligible projects associated with a bond issue. This public hearing will
be followed by a regular board meeting, beginning at 6:05 p.m., during which the board will take action on
the proposed amendment.

Explanation of Issue:

On September 14, 2017, the board adopted Resolution 25-17which, among other things, specified the
eligible capital improvements that could be implemented using proceeds from the 2018bond approval.
The purpose of this evening's public hearing is to take public testimony, both for and against, a proposed
amendment to Resolution 25-17, for additional purposes including maintenance projects intended to
enhance and maintain existing facilities.
The original 2017bond resolution language will be retained. The proposed amendment will allow for
additional purposesto be considered for the state matching funds.
RCW 28A.530.020 requires a public hearing prior to amending the list of eligible projects associated with
a bond issue.
Following this public hearing, the school board will take action on the proposed amendment during their
regular meeting, which begins at6:05 p.m.
