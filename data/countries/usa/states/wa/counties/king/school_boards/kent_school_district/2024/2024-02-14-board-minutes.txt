NEW AGENDA CATEGORY (Added during the meeting.) – Personal Legal Fees Incurred by Director Cook Discussion and Approval
NEW AGENDA ACTION ITEM 7.01 (Added during the meeting.) – Reimbursement of Personal Legal Fees
Incurred by Director Cook
Director Margel asked for a motion to open the public hearing.
A motion was made by Director Cook to discuss and vote on reimbursement for personal legal fees incurred
by Director Cook. (Legal Counsel Note: Fees were incurred without prior approval.)
The motion was seconded.
On advice of legal counsel, a motion was made by Director Cook to amend the agenda by adding a new item,
‘Reimbursement of Personal Legal Fees Incurred by Director Cook’.
The motion carried.
The agenda was amended to include ‘Reimbursement of Personal Legal Fees Incurred by Director Cook’ as a
new agenda category and new agenda action Item 7.01.
President Margel asked for the initial motion to be made again for clarification purposes.
A motion was made by Director Cook to discuss and vote on reimbursement for personal legal fees incurred
by him over the last three weeks.
The motion was seconded.
A motion was made by Director Cook to table the previously made motion to discuss and vote on
reimbursement of personal legal fees incurred by him over the last three weeks. (Director Cook clarified that
his motion to table his motion was to afford Director Song the opportunity to make and discuss a separate
motion on a related topic.)
The motion was seconded.

NEW AGENDA ACTION ITEM 7.02 – Waiver of Attorney/Client Privilege
A motion was made by Director Song to waive attorney/client privilege.
The motion was seconded.
Following clarification and discussion for the purpose of Director Song’s motion, President Margel called for
a vote on all those in favor of waiving attorney/client privilege on the district obtaining legal counsel for
anything having to do with why Director Cook may have received legal counsel.
The motion failed.
