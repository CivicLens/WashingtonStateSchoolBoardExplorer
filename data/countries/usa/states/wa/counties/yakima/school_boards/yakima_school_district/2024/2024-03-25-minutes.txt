  2. Approval of Resolution 04.23.24 Program Reduction in Force for 2024-25 - Jake Kuper, Asst Supt of Finance
This report starts at 1:15:30 in the recorded meeting. Supt Greene shared the step-by-step timeline that the Administration has taken from July 2023 to current day. Jake Kuper, Assistant Supt of Finance, presented on the attached powerpoint. There are four documents attached to this meeting: An action letter, Resolution 04.23.24, a narrative overview of the Program Reduction in Force and a powerpoint. Jake explained the historical student and staff FTE for YSD. He also recapped the enrollment and staffing numbers. He shared the graphs of the General Fund Balance Trend. The 2024-25 Summary of Proposed Program Reduction in Force for board action is $14,669,456. Mr. Kuper shared the contractual reductions from vendors, the 24-25 Executed Program Reduction in Force positions and a summary by Unit, and then a chart that showed the beginning fund balance with line items of increased costs or loss of revenue and increases in revenues. The goal is to stabilize the budget.

That the Board of Directors Approves Resolution 04.23.24 for the proposed 2024-25 Program Reduction in Force as presented.
  Motion by Martha Rice, second by Graciela Villanueva. Motion carried 4/0/1.
Yea: Graciela Villanueva, Martha Rice, Norm Walker, Ryan Beckett
Abstain: Raymond Navarro Jr
