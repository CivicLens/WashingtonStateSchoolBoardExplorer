Call to Order

A regular meeting of the Board of Directors was held on Monday, March 25, 2024, in
the Administrative Service Center Board Room and was available for live streaming.
President Lynn Goebel called the meeting to order at 6:00 p.m. and led the flag salute.

Roll Call

Board members Lynn Goebel, Nicole Tesch, and Sharon Jensen were present. Jessica
Aws and Josh MacLean were absent and excused. Student board representatives Josiah
Villano and Maggie Litvachuk were absent and excused.

Adoption of Agenda

The agenda was adopted by unanimous consent.

Consent Agenda

Director Jensen moved to approve the consent agenda. Director Tesch seconded the
motion. The motion passed unanimously.

Recognition of
Business Services for
State Auditor’s
Stewardship Award

Superintendent Michelle Kuss-Cybula introduced State Auditor Pat McCarthy who
addressed the board and explained the significance of the Stewardship award.
Certificates were presented to all Business Services staff members for this achievement.

Superintendent
Report

Dr. Kuss-Cybula reported on the NAFIS Conference and the importance of Impact Aid.
She provided updates on the strategic planning process and the recent committee work
regarding special programs, budget, and facilities.

Legislative Update

Director Tesch spoke about the recent legislative session and reviewed the district’s top
three legislative priorities.

WIAA Update

Director Jensen reported that the voting on amendments has been delayed until May.

February 2024
Financial Reports

Chief Financial Officer Amber Porter presented the February financials and March
enrollment. She prepared the board to expect a budget extension for the capital
projects fund at the April 8 regular board meeting.

Board Comments

Director Jensen appreciates receiving Dr. Kuss-Cybula’s weekly Board Briefs. She spoke
about attending school events and supporting ways to get community members
involved in volunteering. Director Tesch also expressed appreciation for Board Briefs
and shared about participating in school events. President Goebel highlighted the
recent Oak Harbor High School multicultural assembly and the strategic planning work.

Superintendent
Comments

Dr. Kuss-Cybula pointed out a recent Alumni Spotlight story published by the
Communications Department about 21 Oak Harbor Intermediate School staff members
who all graduated from Oak Harbor High School.

Upcoming Meetings

Regular meetings are scheduled for Monday, April 8, and Monday, April 29, 2024, at
6:00 p.m. in the Administrative Service Center Board Room. There will be a study
session for strategic planning work prior to the April 29 meeting at 5:00 p.m.

Adjourn

The meeting adjourned at 6:59 p.m.

Minutes prepared by Katey Andrews.

Michelle Kuss-Cybula, Ed.D, Board Secretary
