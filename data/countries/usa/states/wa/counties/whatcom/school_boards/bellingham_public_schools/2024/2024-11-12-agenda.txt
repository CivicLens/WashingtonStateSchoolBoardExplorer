Public Content
The Board of Directors of Bellingham Public Schools will meet in Special Session at 12:00 noon, March
14, 2010 in the Board Room of the Administration Building, 1306 Dupont Street, Bellingham, Washington
98225. The meeting will begin with roll call and the Board will immediately adjourn to Executive Session
to review the qualifications of applications for superintendent. Search Consultants Janet Barry and Brian
Benzel, Hazard, Young, Attea & Associates, Ltd. will also attend the meeting to review the qualifications
of the applicants.
Persons with disabilities who may need assistance to attend or participate in the public meeting may
contact the Superintendent's Office at 360-676-6501 for arrangements.
