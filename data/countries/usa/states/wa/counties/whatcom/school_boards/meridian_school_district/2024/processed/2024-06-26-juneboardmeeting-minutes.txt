OLD BUSINESS
Second Review and Approval: Facility Naming and Dedication Committee Recommendation
The facilities naming and dedication committee recommendation was presented to the Board for a
second review and approval. The School Board is in favor of naming the North Gym at Meridian High
School Diane Axelson Court. A motion to approve the facilities naming and dedication committee
recommendation of naming the North Gym at Meridian High School, Diane Axelson Court, was made by
Jana Haugen and seconded by Jarrod Karuza. The motion passed 4-0.
Second Review and Approval: General Obligations Bond Resolution 06-24
General Obligation Bonds Resolution 06-24 was presented to the Board for a second review and
approval. A motion to approve General Obligation Bonds Resolution 06-24 as presented was made by
Jarrod Karuza and seconded by Marty Gray. The motion passed 4-0.
Second Review and Approval: Reduction in Force Resolution 07-24
Reduction in Force Resolution 07-24 was presented to the Board for a second review and approval. A
motion to approve Reduction in Force Resolution 07-24 as presented was made by Jana Haugen and
seconded by Jarrod Karuza. The motion passed 4-0.
Second Review and Approval: MAT Furlough MOU
MAT Furlough MOU was presented to the Board for a second review and approval. A motion to approve
the MAT Furlough MOU as presented was made by Marty Gray and seconded by Jana Haugen. The
motion passed 4-0.

FINANCE REPORT
Finance Report
Director of Finance, Daniel Yorton presented a budget update report to the Board. The budget update
included June enrollment compared to May, enrollment compared to budget, and a general status
update of our district finances.

SUPERINTENDENT’S REPORT
Dr. Everett began his report by sharing information about recent events in our schools, details from the
WASA/AWSP Summer Conference he recently attended and information about the summer support
programs we are offering to support students and families. Dr. Everett updated the Board on the NCTA
Open Doors at Whatcom program, the focus areas for the 2025 legislative platform, and upcoming dates
of importance.
