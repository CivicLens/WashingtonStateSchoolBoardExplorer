Call to Order

A regular meeting of the Board of Directors was held on Monday, October 14, 2024, in
the Administrative Service Center Board Room and was available for live streaming.
President Lynn Goebel called the meeting to order at 6:07 p.m. and led the flag salute.

Roll Call

Board members Lynn Goebel, Jessica Aws, Nicole Tesch, Sharon Jensen, and Josh
MacLean were present. Student board representatives Maggie Litvachuk and Allena
Locklear were present.

Adoption of Agenda

The agenda was adopted by unanimous consent.

Consent Agenda

Director Jensen moved to approve the consent agenda. Director MacLean seconded the
motion. The motion passed unanimously.

Approval of
Superintendent
Goals for 2024-2025

Director Tesch moved to approve the superintendent goals that were presented as a
first reading at the September 30, 2024 board meeting. Vice President Aws seconded
the motion. The motion passed unanimously.

Board Comments

Director Tesch reported on district and community events. Director MacLean
highlighted the upcoming groundbreaking ceremonies and City Council presentation by
Superintendent Kuss-Cybula. Student Board Representative Allena Locklear spoke about
the swim team and an AVID informational meeting at North Whidbey Middle School.
Student Board Representative Maggie Litvachuk shared about an upcoming meeting
with the high school ASB and her reading of the draft land acknowledgment at the choir
concert.

Superintendent
Comments

Dr. Michelle Kuss-Cybula recognized Indigenous Peoples Day and shared that the
student representatives will be getting student input on the land acknowledgement.
