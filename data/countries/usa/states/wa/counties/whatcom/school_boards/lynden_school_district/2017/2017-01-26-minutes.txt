January 26, 2017
School Board Meeting – Work Session
Minutes
Lynden High School Library
6:30 P.M.
1. Call to Order, Welcome, Pledge of Allegiance and Roll Call
Meeting called to order at 6:32 P.M. Board members in attendance were Brian Steve Jilk, Mike Haveman, and
Kevin Burke. Brian Johnson and Dominic Shiu were absent and excused. Student Director Devin Gartner was
present, Katelyn Fullner was absent and excused.
2. Approval of Meeting Minutes
 Meeting Minutes January 12, 2017
Kevin Burke motioned to approve the meeting minutes seconded by Mike Haveman. Motion carried by voice vote.
3. Consent Agenda
 Personnel Action Items – January 26, 2017
 Donations –January 26, 2017
Mike Haveman motioned to approve the consent agenda seconded by Kevin Burke. Motion carried by voice vote.
4. Information from the Audience
None at this Meeting.
5. Finance and Operations
 Budget Update - December
Jim Frey provided information for the December budget review, containing a summary of financial
status, enrollment, general fund comparative, and revenues and expenditures.
6. Action Items
 Resolution No. 01-17 – Van Surplus
Jim Frey explained the reasoning for surplus of the van
Kevin Burke motioned to approve Resolution No. 01-17 seconded by Mike Haveman. Motion carried by voice vote.


High School Schedule Change
Jim reviewed what has been provided to the board regarding the high school schedule change.
Mike Haveman motioned to approve the High School Schedule Change for 2017-2018 seconded by Kevin Burke.
Motion carried by voice vote.


Resolution No. 16-16 – Amending Resolution No. 01-15
Jim Frey provided information on the facility project needs and how excess bond funds may be utilized.
Kevin Burke motioned to approve Resolution No.6-16 seconded by Mike Haveman. Motion carried by voice vote.
7. Adjourn Meeting to go into Board Work Session
Meeting adjourned at 6:45P.M.

8. Board Work Session
 New Pedagogies for Deeper Learning
Elizabeth Hamming shared with the board what Pedagogies & Deeper Learning means and how the
learning experience we prepare for Deeper Learning supports students. Elizabeth shared the focus of
information on the success of Deeper Learning from a Think Tank perspective, Education perspective,
and Business perspective. Elizabeth explained the importance of the “6 C’s” in Deeper Learning.


Technology Work Group Update
Elizabeth Hamming informed the board about the discussions of the Technology Work Group
Committee. Elizabeth shared the suggested focus on technology learning tools for students and staff.
The board also reviewed cost estimates for various technology equipment.
