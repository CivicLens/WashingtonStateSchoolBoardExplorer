Washtucna School District #109
Regular Board of Directors Meeting
Washtucna, WA
April 23, 2024

The meeting was called to order at 7:00 p.m. by board chair Jake Sitton. Other board members
present were Bridget Coon, Steve Crouse, Alta Hunt and Derrick Kehn. Also attending in person
were Superintendent Dr. Rob Clark, Principal Staci Gloyn, Business Manager Katie Lasen, and
Debbie Sitton. There were no online Zoom attendees.
ADOPTION OF THE AGENDA
Steve Crouse moved to approve the agenda. Passed 5-0.
COMMENTS FROM GUESTS
Debbie Sitton gave a recap of Reading Month. This was our 32nd year and the theme was ‘Grow
Where You’re Planted.’ Kindergarten through 5th grade read a total of 20,915 pages!

BUSINESS MANAGER’S REPORT
Budget Status Reports for March were included in board packets and discussed at the meeting.
PRINCIPAL’S REPORT
Staci Gloyn updated the board on April events including Town and Roadside Cleanup this week.
May activities include State and AP testing, DSHS Mobile Truck, Book Fair, Math Night and
Screenagers, State FFA and more. Mrs. Gloyn presented the board with Math Curriculum
Adoption Recommendation of Eureka Math for K-3rd grades and Illustrative Mathematics for 4th12th grades with supplemental curriculum as needed for AP or Dual Credit. Steve Crouse moved
to adopt the math curriculum as presented. Passed 5-0. Mrs. Gloyn then led the board on a field
trip to the Ag Shop and dungeon to see the progress of the Lead in Water Remediation Grant
project.
ADMINISTRATIVE REPORT

Dr. Clark submitted the Economies and Efficiencies waiver for the 4-day school week today.
Steve Crouse motioned to approve Board Procedure #2410P-High School Graduation
Requirements. Passed 5-0. Steve Crouse motioned to approve the Reasonable Assurance list for
2024-25. Passed 5-0. Due to the building being closed for many weeks this summer, the board
discussed holding the June 25th meeting at Sonny’s.
EXECUTIVE/CLOSED SESSION
Executive/closed session not needed.
ADJOURNMENT
Steve Crouse motioned to adjourn the meeting at 7:43 p.m. Passed 5-0.
