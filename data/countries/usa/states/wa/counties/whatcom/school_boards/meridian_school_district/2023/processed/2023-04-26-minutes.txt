REGULAR MEETING
The regular meeting was called to order by Board Vice-Chair Craig Mitchell at 7:01 p.m.
Board member Marty Gray was present at the meeting and Board Chair Sara Bernardy was present via
Zoom. Student Board Representatives Kurtis Kettman was also in attendance. Student Board
Representative Mirage DeNeal arrived after the consent agenda motion.
Board members Steve Corwin and Jarrod Karuza were excused from attendance.
The Pledge of Allegiance was recited.
A motion to adopt the agenda was made by Marty Gray and seconded by Sara Bernardy. The motion
passed 3-0.

REPORTS
Study and Survey Report
Maintenance and Facilities Director Jay Yeager and Tim Faber with King Architecture presented a longrange study and survey reported to the Board. The report included capital facility needs based on future
growth and aging building needs. Jay and Tim shared cost estimates for the proposed major capital
projects and current and future financing models. Jay mentioned one of the next steps will be to get a
facilities committee up and going. The board has requested additional information about the SCAP
funding formula and how it is calculated.
Food Services Report
Food Services Director Rob Dunn updated the Board on the latest things that are happening in the food
services department. Rob shared changes with the free, reduced, and paid lunch classifications. Rob
spoke about the supply chain and product procurement improvements, in addition to department
improvements and the increase in scratch cooking.

Whatcom Skills Center Federal and Legislative Process Update
NCTA Director Lynette Brower and Dr. James Everett presented a report to the Board on the status of
the Whatcom County Skills Center. Information was shared about federal funding submissions and
legislative efforts.
PUBLIC COMMENT
There was no public comment.
NEW BUSINESS
First Reading: Resolution 05-23, Adoption of Long-Range Study and Survey
Dr. James Everett presented a first reading of Resolution 05-23, Adoption of Long-Range Study and
Survey to the Board. The resolution 05-23 will be brought back to the Board for approval at the May 10,
2023, Board meeting.
First Reading: Policy and Procedure 3205P
Assistant Superintendent Kurt Harvill presented a first reading of 3205P - Sexual Harassment of Students
Prohibited. This policy is being brought back for a first reading after receiving input from our attorney.
FINANCIAL REPORT
Director of Finance Daniel Yorton gave a financial update to the Board. Daniel began by speaking about
the audit we are currently in the middle of. The financial update included student enrollment
comparisons, our budget status and budget projections.
