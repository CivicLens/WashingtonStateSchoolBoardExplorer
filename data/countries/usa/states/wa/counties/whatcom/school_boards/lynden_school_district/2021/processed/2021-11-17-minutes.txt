4. Information from the Audience
Topics of discussion from the audience included: Thanks to the Board of Directors for the positive
experiences in the Lynden School District; Appreciation of our communities coming together as
neighbors, No change in any positive outcome from the school board ~ no survey, but the election
results for new board member spoke for itself; Mask mandates don’t work, Mandated states doubled
in COVID-19 cases, Need more kindness and understanding for not wearing masks, Government
officials not wearing masks so why do kids; Governor Inslee didn’t notice Whatcom County during this
weather event, Why is COVID a priority during this time, Organizing a coalition with other school
districts to form parents against vaccination clinics; Need to address the lunch time at Lynden Middle
School, Board needs to follow the facts and need to be human beings and listen, What does Board
think is right and wrong.
