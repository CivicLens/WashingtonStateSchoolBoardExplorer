MERIDIAN SCHOOL DISTRICT #505
MINUTES OF REGULAR SCHOOL BOARD MEETING AND BUDGET HEARING
MERIDIAN SCHOOL DISTRICT
MEETING HELD VIA ZOOM
November 4, 2020
The regular meeting was called to order by Board Chair, Marty Gray, at 7:00 p.m. Chairman Gray welcomed those
in attendance on the remote Zoom meeting.
Board members present on the Zoom meeting were Sara Bernardy; Marty Gray; Emily Norton; Renier Elenbaas.
The Pledge of Allegiance was recited.
Sara Bernardy made a motion to adopt the agenda. Renier Elenbaas seconded the motion; the motion passed 4-0.
CONSENT AGENDA
A. Approval of Meeting Minutes
B. Personnel Report
C. Payroll
A motion to adopt the consent agenda was made by Sara Bernardy and seconded by Emily Norton. The motion
passed 4-0.
PUBLIC COMMENT
There was no Public Comment
REPORTS
IRE Staff Update
Irene Reither Principal, Dawn Christiana presented the IRE Staff Update to the Board.
Maintenance and Facilities Report
Director of Maintenance and Operations, Jay Yeager presented a report to the Board.
OLD BUSINESS
Second Reading: Policy and Procedure 3535
Assistant Superintendent, Kurt Harvill presented a Second Reading of Policy and Procedure 3535 - Financial Aid
and Advising Day. Renier Elenbaas made a motion to adopt Policy and Procedure 3535, Emily Norton seconded
the motion, the motion passed 4-0.
Second Reading: Policy and Procedure 6112
Assistant Superintendent, Kurt Harvill presented a first reading of Policy and Procedure 6112 – Rental or Lease
of District Property. Sara Bernardy made a motion to adopt Policy and Procedure 3205, Renier Elenbaas
seconded the motion, the motion passed 4-0.

Revised Board Yearly Plan of Work
Superintendent, Dr. James Everett presented a second reading of the Board Yearly Plan of Work to the Board.
Renier Elenbaas made a motion to adopt the Revised Board Yearly Plan of Work, Emily Norton seconded the
motion, the motion passed 4-0.
NEW BUSINESS

First Reading: Policy and Procedure 5270

and Discharge.

Whatcom County.

Student Board Members, Karissa Benson and Jenna McCauley shared about the “Character Dare” challenge at the
High School.
INFORMATION FROM BOARD MEMBERS

BOARD SIGNATURE OF OFFICIAL DOCUMENTS
