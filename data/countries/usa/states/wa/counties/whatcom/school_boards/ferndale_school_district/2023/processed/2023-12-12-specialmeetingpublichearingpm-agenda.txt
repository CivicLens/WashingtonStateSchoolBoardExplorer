Tuesday, December 12, 2023
Special Meeting - Public Hearing 5:45 p.m.
Ferndale School District No. 502 School Board Special Meeting - Public Hearing, 5780 Hendrickson Ave,
Ferndale, WA
The Board of Directors will hold a public hearing for the purpose of receiving comments from the public
on the Budget Extension for the Transportation Vehicle Fund. Any person may appear at the hearing and
be heard for or against any part of such budget under RCW 28A.505.240.
Written comment may be submitted prior to the public hearing to the following email address:
fsdinfo@ferndalesd.org
Also, at any time that you would like to contact your board members, you may do so by using their
contact information that is displayed on this webpage at this link https://www.ferndalesd.org/school-board
/school-board-contacts.
The video link above will provide access to the board meeting. The live recording will begin when the
board meeting starts.

Public Content
Mr. Deebach will present Resolution No. 19-2023 approving the budget extension of the Transportation Vehicle Fund for
the 2023-2024 school year. This approved resolution will petition OSPI to increase the 2023-2024 Transportation Vehicle
Fund from $2,000,000 to $2,700,000 for the purchase of additional school buses that were not anticipated in the original
budget.
Dr. Kevin Erickson will recognize audience members who may have comments about the budget extension. Persons
wishing to speak should sign in with the recording secretary.
Any person may appear and be heard for or against any part of such budget extension, ortheproposeduses of funding
under RCW28A.505.240. If no persons wish to make public comment on the budget, Dr. Erickson may call on Assistant
Superintendent Mark Deebach to explain the budget.
