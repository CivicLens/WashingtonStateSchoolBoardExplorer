Blaine School District No. 503
  Special Board Meeting:Executive Session
Monday, October 25, 2021
  Minutes
  1. Special Board Meeting
A. Call to Order
In accordance with guidelines related to the COVID-19 pandemic emergency, this meeting was conducted with both virtual and in-person components. President Gibson called the meeting to order at 6:00 p.m. All Directors were in attendance.
     Todd Nunamaker, Dougal Thomas, Joan Lotze, Charles E Gibson, Laura McKinney
  2. Executive Session
A. Executive Session
President Gibson announced that the board would move into executive session for the purpose of considering the employment or dismissal of personnel. The executive session was anticipated to last for approximately forty-five (45) minutes, and no action would be taken by the board during this special meeting.

At 6:45 p.m., it was announced that the board's executive session would be extended an additional five (5) minutes.
  3. Reconvene and Adjourn
A. Reconvene and Adjourn
The executive session ended at 6:50 p.m., and the board reconvened in regular session. There being no further business, the meeting was adjourned by consensus of the directors.
