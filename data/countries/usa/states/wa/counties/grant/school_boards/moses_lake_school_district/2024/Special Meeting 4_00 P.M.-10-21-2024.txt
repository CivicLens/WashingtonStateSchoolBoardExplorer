School Board Study Sessions: PLC Initiative
Below are the School Board Sessions with Janel Keating. These sessions will develop further understanding
of the PLC focus of the district to foster a deeper understanding of the work at the School Board level. Below
are the dates and session descriptions.
#1: October 21, 2024
Session Description: This session will review the three big ideas and four critical questions of a PLC to
ensure any new school board members are aligned in their understanding. Participants will continue the
conversation about the important role of superintendent and board members in ensuring learning and
academic equity for all students by implementing the concepts and practices of a PLC districtwide.
