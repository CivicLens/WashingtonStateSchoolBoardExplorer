BELLINGHAM SCHOOL DISTRICT BOARD OF DIRECTORS
August 24, 2017
OPENING ITEMS
Meeting Date and Location:
6:15 p.m. – 1306 Dupont St., Bellingham, Washington
Call to Order/Roll Call:
Mr. Benjamin called the meeting to order at 6:15 p.m. Present: Directors Kelly Bashaw,
Douglas Benjamin (President), Camille Diaz Hackler, Quenby Peterson, Steven Smith,
Superintendent Greg Baker, and recorder Ruth Rasmussen.
Audience – n/a
Superintendent’s Announcements and Report: Summary of Dr. Baker’s comments:
 Two celebratory events were held this week: the Options High School Grand
Opening and Ribbon Cutting Ceremony and the All-Staff Back-to-School
Celebration, which included a viewing of the Loving Our Kids video. Kudos to
the communications team for exemplary work on the video, which has resonated
with many and generated much positive feedback from parents and staff.
 Dr. Baker visited every school in the past two days. He said this has been the
smoothest start he has seen even with major initiatives getting underway,
including new start/end times; a revised high school 4x8 schedule, and the rollout
of the 1:1 technology program at Kulshan Middle School, Squalicum and Options
high schools.
 Kindergarteners started school today. Many district office staff members
volunteered at schools during drop-off/pick-up times to assist as needed.
 Dr. Baker and the leadership team are monitoring enrollment and class size
numbers.
