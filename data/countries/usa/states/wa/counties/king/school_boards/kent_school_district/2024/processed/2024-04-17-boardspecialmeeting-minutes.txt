SPECIAL MEETING
The Board of Directors of Kent School District No. 415 met in a special meeting on Wednesday, April 17, 2024
at 5:00 p.m. for approximately three hours. In attendance: President Meghin Margel, Awale Farah (virtual
attendance), Tim Clark, Andy Song, and Donald Cook. Also in attendance Superintendent Vela.
The purpose of the special meeting was the Board/Superintendent Governance Team retreat. Special
presenters were Curtis Leonard, legal counsel, and Amy Cast, WSSDA retreat facilitator.
Call to Order
President Margel called the special meeting to order at 5:02 p.m.
