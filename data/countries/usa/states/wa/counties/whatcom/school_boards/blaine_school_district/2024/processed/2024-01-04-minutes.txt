Blaine School District No. 503
  Special Board Meeting: Executive Session
Thursday, January 4, 2024
  Minutes
    1. Special Board Meeting
A. Call to Order
President Leu called the meeting to order, in the district's boardroom, at 6:00 p.m.
All directors were in attendance.
     Erika Creydt, Ben Lazarus, Cliff Freeman, Ryan Ford, Don Leu
  2. Executive Session
A. Executive Session
President Leu announced that the board would move into executive session for the purpose of reviewing the performance of a public employee. The executive session was anticipated to last for one (1) hour, until 7:00 p.m. Any extension of time would be announced verbally in the boardroom, and no final action would be taken by the board at this meeting. The directors then moved to the superintendent's office.

At 7:00 p.m., it was announced that the meeting would be extended for an additional fifteen (15) minutes, until 7:15 p.m.

At 7:15 p.m., it was announced that the meeting would be extended for an additional five (5) minutes, until 7:20 p.m.
  3. Reconvene and Adjourn
A. Reconvene and Adjourn
The executive session ended at 7:20 p.m. and the directors returned to the boardroom where the meeting reconvened in regular session.
  There being no further business, the meeting was adjourned at 7:24 p.m. by a consensus of the directors.
