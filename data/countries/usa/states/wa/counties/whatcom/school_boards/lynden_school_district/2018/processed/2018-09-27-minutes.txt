School Board Meeting

1. Call to Order, Welcome, Pledge of Allegiance and Roll Call
Meeting called to order at 6:30 P.M. Board members in attendance were Kevin Burke, Steve Jilk, Kelli Kettels and
CJ Costanti. Board Member Brian Johnson was absent and excused. Student Director Ruby VanderHaak was
present. Student Director Cynthia Torres was absent and excused.

The Board approved to amend the Agenda to move Finance and Operations and the Board Work Session topic
“Glenning Street Property” up in the Agenda.
4. Finance and Operations
• Budget Update - April
The Board reviewed information on the preliminary budget for August containing a summary of financial
status, enrollment, general fund comparative, and revenues and expenditures.
5. Board Work Session
• Glenning Street Property
Harlan Credit, a spokesman for the Glenning Street “Save the Playfield” Committee spoke about the steps
the committee has taken to act to preserve the Glenning Street property to remain an open space and
what will happen in the coming months if the Board approves an extension of time on their decision to
surplus.

6. Information from the Audience
Sean Withrow – Sean thanked Superintendent Frey, Board Directors Steve Jilk and Kelli Kettles for the time spent
to inform him of the actions of the School Board of Directors to support student education and
the opportunity to express the interest of the Glenning Street Support Committee to advocate
to keep the area as open space.
David Vis – David thanked Mr. Kredit for his report to the Board. Dave commented that he understands the needs
of the School District to move forward with supporting education. He also encouraged the Board to
give the Glenning Street Support Committee more time to follow through with ways to maintain
Glenning Street as an open space area.
7. Board Work Session
• Glenning Street Property
President, Kevin Burke revisited the purpose of surplusing the Glenning Street property, the scope of how
the Board has processed requests to provide more time for community input and informed that the Board
has decided to extend the surplus of Glenning Street property until February 27th, 2019.
Steve Jilk Motion to extend the Glenning Street property surplus action until February 27th, 2019 seconded by CJ
Costanti. Motion carried by voice vote.
•

District Student Performance Data
The Board reviewed multiple assessment data in math, science, and English pertaining to student districtwide averages by grade and year trends compared to State averages. The Board also discussed success
indicators on academic performance.

6. Meeting Adjourned
Meeting adjourned at 7:10 P.M.
