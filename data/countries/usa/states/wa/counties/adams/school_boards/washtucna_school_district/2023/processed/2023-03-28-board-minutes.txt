Washtucna School District #109
Regular Board of Directors Meeting
Washtucna, WA
March 28, 2023

The meeting was called to order at 7:00 p.m. by board chair Jeremy Jessop. Board members
attending in person were Steve Crouse, Jeremy Jessop and Ginger McKenzie. Jake Sitton arrived
at 7:04 p.m. Also attending in person were Superintendent Dr. Rob Clark, Dean of Students Staci
Gloyn, Business Manager Katie Lasen, Carol Blauert and Matt Anderson. There were no zoom
attendees. Teresa Correll was absent.
ADOPTION OF AGENDA
Steve Crouse motioned to approve the agenda with the addition of Cooper Jessop for the senior
trip. Passed 3-0.
COMMENTS FROM GUESTS
Senior Cooper Jessop was present to discuss senior trip changes. They will be going to Bend,
Oregon May 4-7. Steve Crouse motioned to approve the changes to the senior trip. Passed 3-0.

BUSINESS MANAGER’S REPORT
Budget Status Reports for February were included in board packets and discussed at the meeting.
DEAN OF STUDENTS REPORT
Staci Gloyn updated the board on school trips, Reading Month and other school activities. She
also gave a graduation pathway update. Matt Anderson was present to recap Missoula Children’s
Theater. Carol Blauert was present to discuss transportation surplus and purchasing.
ADMINISTRATIVE REPORT
Jake Sitton motioned to approve the 2nd Reading of the 2023-24 School Calendar. Passed 4-0.
Steve Crouse motioned to approve Resolution #2021-09, Surplus Bus #6. Passed 4-0. Jake Sitton
motioned to approve graduation date and time of June 3 at 11 a.m. Passed 4-0. Jake Sitton
motioned to approve the presented surplus list. Passed 4-0.
