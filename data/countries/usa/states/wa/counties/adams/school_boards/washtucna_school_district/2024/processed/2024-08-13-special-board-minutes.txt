Washtucna School District #109
Special Board of Directors Meeting
Washtucna, WA
August 13, 2024

The special meeting of the Washtucna School Board was called to order at 7:03 a.m. by board
chair Jake Sitton. Attending in-person were board member Derrick Kehn and Superintendent Rob
Clark. Attending online via Zoom were board members Bridget Coon, Steve Crouse, and Jake
Sitton, and Business Manager Katie Lasen. Alta Hunt joined at 7:09 a.m.
ADOPTION OF THE AGENDA
Derrick Kehn motioned to approve the agenda. Passed 4-0.
AGENDA
Bridget Coon moved to approve the 2024-25 Student Handbook. Passed 4-0. Bridget Coon
moved to approve the resignation of Tiffanie Longsdorff (Fulton-Rodgers). Passed 4-0.

ADJOURNMENT
Steve Crouse moved to adjourn the meeting at 7:09 p.m. Passed 4-0.
