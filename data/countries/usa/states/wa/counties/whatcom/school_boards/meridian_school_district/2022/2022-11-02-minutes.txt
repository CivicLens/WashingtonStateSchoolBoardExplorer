REPORTS
Irene Reither Staff Recognition
Irene Reither Principal, Tyler Dockins recognized staff for their various teaching strategies including work time,
targeted intervention, number talks, writing conferences, cold calls, and independent reading.
PLC Presentations
MP3
Jeff Caraco and Sara Warnock presented on their approach to Social Emotional Learning. They spoke on how they
helped students identify their emotions and give them language to communicate how they are feeling. They
realized the benefits of communicating feelings and the increased awareness of them.
IRE
Rebecca Kratzig presented about the 5th grade team’s work on narrative writing. They started with sharing a picture
and asking students to write a story with a beginning middle and end. They created an extended rubric to score
from Pre-k through 5th grade so they could track student improvement even if it was not at grade level. This creates
a system and rubric for the entire school to track writing.
MMS
Courtney Price presented on the teaching students to cite evidence and utilizing text to support their writing. The
middle school also focused on creating a rubric that is aligned throughout grade levels and classrooms. Looking at

data helps drive the lessons. They have learned that different students need feedback in different ways. The
students had 70-80% growth.
MHS
Kayla Tuner presented on the focus of Theme in our high school English classrooms and its connection and
importance in writing and reading.
