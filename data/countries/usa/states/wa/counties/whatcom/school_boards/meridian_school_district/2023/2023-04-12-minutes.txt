REPORTS
PLC Presentations
Principal Tyler Dockins and second grade teacher Jill Hammack gave a second-grade PLC Reading
standard presentation to the Board. Information was shared about what we want students to know
about character responses in a story, how the knowledge is measured, the tools that that are being used
to track growth, and the strategies that are being used to grow and coach the skills.

Physical Education teachers from MMS gave a PLC presentation on the foundational principles of fitness
we want students to understand. The team spoke about the participation measurements and grade
level rubric for each grade level. Sample grading log and student progress data was shared with the
Board as well as action steps that are taken to celebrate or grow skills.
Math teachers at MHS gave a presentation on the high school math roadmap. The team spoke about
what is going on with Algebra, Algebra II, and Geometry at the High School. Information was shared
about the variety of math offerings, assessment methods being used, recent changes in the curriculum
that is being used, inclusive classrooms, co-teaching, and collaborative team initiatives.
MMS School Report
MMS Principal Todd Torgeson presented a MMS school report to the Board. Student and staff information
was shared in addition to assessment data sources MMS is using to measure progress and growth. Details
were shared about the MMS social and emotional learning. Todd spoke about the experiential learning
through creating engaging experiences that is happening at MMS, and the choices that are being offered
to students for electives. MMS is using a systems approach based on feedback and data collection that
identified different areas where growth was needed.
