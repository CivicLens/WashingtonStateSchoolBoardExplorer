None at this meeting.
5. Finance and Operations
 Budget Update - May
Patti Fairbanks provided information on the budget for May containing a summary of financial status,
enrollment, general fund comparative, and revenues and expenditures. Patti spoke of the factors that
impacted the difference in the budget such as COVID-19 expenses.
7. Board Work Session
 Re-Opening School 2020-2021
Jim Frey spoke of the guidelines of returning to school and conditions of school facility capacity,
enrollment, learning options, and family needs. More information will be coming from OSPI and will need
to be reviewed before final decisions are reconciled and reported. The Board discussed alternative
learning options and ways to welcome students back in the fall.



Community Discussions
Board of Director, CJ Costanti addressed communication with families and ways to engage with families
surrounding community discussions such as equality, distant learning, and re-opening of schools.
