MERIDIAN SCHOOL DISTRICT #505
REGULAR SCHOOL BOARD MEETING
MERIDIAN SCHOOL DISTRICT
MEETING HELD IN PERSON & VIA ZOOM
February 22, 2023
REGULAR MEETING
The regular meeting was called to order by Board Chair Sara Bernardy at 7:00 p.m.
Board members present at the meeting were Sara Bernardy, Marty Gray, and Craig Mitchell.
Student Board Representative present at the meeting was Kurtis Kettman.
Board members Jarrod Karuza and Steve Corwin and student Board Representative Mirage DeNeal were
excused from attending.
The pledge of allegiance was recited.
A motion to amend the agenda to include acting on Resolution 03‐23, Supporting 2023 American Rescue
Plan Act application for funding to establish an Early Childcare Development Center during tonight’s
meeting was made by Craig Mitchell and seconded by Marty Gray. Motion passed 3‐0.
A motion to adopt the agenda with the above change was made by Marty Gray and seconded by Craig
Mitchell. The motion passed 3‐0.
CONSENT AGENDA
A. Approval of Meeting Minutes
B. Personnel Report
C. Accounts Payable
D. Donation Listing
E. Student Travel
F. Addition of Instructional Materials Committee Member
A motion to adopt the consent agenda was made by Craig Mitchell and seconded by Marty Gray. The
motion passed 3‐0.
PUBLIC COMMENT
There was no public comment.
OLD BUSINESS
Curriculum Materials Proposal
Dr. Everett presented Writing Skills and Math with Confidence MP3 curriculum for second review and
approval. Student Board Representative Kurtis favors support of adoption. A motion to adopt the
proposed writing and math curriculum as presented was made by Craig Mitchell seconded by Marty
Gray. The motion passed 3‐0.

NEW BUSINESS
Agreement to Participate ‐ Resolution 02‐23
Dr. James Everett presented resolution 02‐23, Agreement to Participate in the Branch/Core Campus of
the Northwest Career & Technical Academy in Bellingham, WA. This was a first reading of resolution 02‐
23. At the Board meeting on March 15th, the Board will be asked to approve this resolution.
Support of Early Childcare Education Center ‐ Resolution 03‐23
Dr. James Everett presented resolution 03‐23, Supporting 2023 American Rescue Plan Act application for
funding to establish an Early Childcare Development Center. Student Board Representative Kurtis favors
support of adoption. A motion to adopt the proposed Resolution 03‐23 as presented was made by Craig
Mitchell seconded by Marty Gray. The motion passed 3‐0.
Surplus of District Property
Dr. Everett presented a surplus request submitted by maintenance and operations director, Jay Yeager.
The surplus request is asking the Board to approve two vehicles for surplus. A motion to surplus the
vehicles as presented was made by Marty Gray and seconded by Craig Mitchell. The motion passed 3‐0.
First Reading: Policy and Procedure 3231
Dr. Everett presented a first reading of Policy and Procedure 3231; Student Records.
First Reading: Policy and Procedure 6970
Dr. Everett presented a first reading of Policy and Procedure 6970; Naming Schools, Facilities, and
Teams, and Use of School Mascots, Images, or Logos.
FINANCIAL REPORT
Financial Report
Director of Finance Daniel Yorton gave a financial update to the Board. The financial update included
student enrollment comparisons, our budget status and budget projections.
SUPERINTENDENT’S REPORT
Dr. Everett presented to the Board updates on the National Education conference he and Kurt recently
attended. Updates were also provided on the JUUL lawsuit, legislative bills, and information about the
Whatcom County Skills Center. Dr. Everett recognized recent donors who contributed time, labor, and
money toward the MMS scoreboard and MHS field renovation projects. Dr. Everett shared information
about upcoming training workshops and opportunities.
INFORMATION FROM BOARD MEMBERS
The next Regular Board Meeting is scheduled for Wednesday, March 15th, 2023, at 7:00 p.m. The
wrestling team was recognized for their recent competition in the state MAT meet.
INFORMATION FROM STUDENT BOARD MEMBERS
No information was presented.

There being no further business, the Board meeting was adjourned at 7:46 p.m. by Board Chair Sara
