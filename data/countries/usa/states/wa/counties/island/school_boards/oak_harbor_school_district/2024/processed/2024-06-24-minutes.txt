Call to Order

A regular meeting of the Board of Directors was held on Monday, June 24, 2024, in the
Administrative Service Center Board Room and was available for live streaming.
President Lynn Goebel called the meeting to order at 6:00 p.m. and led the flag salute.

Roll Call

Board members Lynn Goebel, Jessica Aws, Nicole Tesch, Sharon Jensen, and Josh
MacLean were present.

Adoption of Agenda

The agenda was adopted by unanimous consent.

Consent Agenda

Director Tesch moved to approve the consent agenda. Vice President Aws seconded
the motion. The motion passed unanimously.

Safety & Student
Support Report

Facilities Director Brian Hunt, Information Services Director Tyler Starkovich, and
Assistant Superintendent Dwight Lundstrom presented a report that included facility
use, building security systems, health services, and discipline.

May 2024 Monthly
Financial Report

Chief Financial Officer Amber Porter presented the May 2024 financial reports.

2024-2025 Draft
Budget Planning
Update

Ms. Porter presented an update for 2024-2025 budget planning.

Superintendent
Comments

Superintendent Michelle Kuss-Cybula thanked the teams for the presentations and
congratulated the Communications Department for receiving the National School
Public Relations Association Golden Achievement Award. She highlighted the theme of
safety in the strategic plan as a primary value of the district.

Upcoming Meetings

A consent-agenda-only board meeting is scheduled for Monday, July 29, 2024, at 12:00
p.m. in the Administrative Service Center (ASC) Board Room. Board members will
attend this meeting remotely through Google Meet. A special meeting/board retreat is
scheduled for Wednesday, August 7, 2024, at 3:00 p.m. in the ASC Board Room. A
regular meeting is scheduled for Monday, August 12, 2024, at 6:00 p.m. in the ASC
Board Room.

Executive Session

At 7:30 p.m., the board went into Executive Session for the purpose of evaluating the
performance of the superintendent, to return to Regular Session at 8:45 p.m.
The board returned to Regular Session at 8:43 p.m.

Adjourn

The meeting adjourned at 8:43 p.m.
