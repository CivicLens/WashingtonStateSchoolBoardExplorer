  A. CTE Annual Program Report *Action*

  Motion to Approve report and program: Director Bedford; Second: Director Slagle
Yes: Melissa Bedford, Nikki Lockwood, Hilary Kozel, Jenny Slagle
Final Resolution: Motion carries.
  B. Budget Development Process *Discussion*
Cindy Coleman presented budget priorities for the 2024-2025 school year, specifically related to middle school transportation service levels, Spokane Public Language Immersion location and student technology device model.
  Next steps include staffing models, operational efficiencies, balance grants, review individual budgets, monitor 23-24 activity and Superintendent Recommendations at the June 19 Board Meeting.
  Heather Bybee and Principal Mauricio Segovia gave a historical perspective of Spokane Language Immersion program and how it has grown in the last 7 years. They are asking the Board to consider moving the school to the Pratt location beginning next school year to support their growth, their goals, and to provide them with a sense of community to help retain students and support more entering the program.
  President Lockwood, Vice President Slagle, Director Bedford, and Director Slagle confirmed their support to keep this as a budget item.
