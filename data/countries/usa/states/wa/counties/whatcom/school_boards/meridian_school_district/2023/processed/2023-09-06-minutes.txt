REPORTS
Annual Staffing Report
Assistant Superintendent, Kurt Harvill presented a report on the 2023-24 hiring report to the Board. Kurt
included details about the hiring process, and updates that have been made to the hiring process with our
strategic plan. Kurt shared details about the number of positions we have posted, filled and unfilled as
well as our current staffing numbers by groups and the number of new staff members at each building.
School Data Introduction
Director of Teaching and Learning, Adrienne Somera presented an overview report on the collaborative
team data presentation plan for the 2023-24 school year to the Board. Adrienne shared the purpose of
the data team presentations. Adrienne reviewed the PLC questions the data teams are using to guide the
work as well as the presentation dates.
PUBLIC COMMENT
There was no public comment.
