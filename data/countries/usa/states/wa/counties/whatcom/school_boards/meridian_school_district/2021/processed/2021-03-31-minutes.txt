MERIDIAN SCHOOL DISTRICT #505
MINUTES OF REGULAR SCHOOL BOARD MEETING
MERIDIAN SCHOOL DISTRICT
MEETING HELD VIA ZOOM
March 31, 2021
Revised February 8, 2023
The regular meeting was called to order by Board Chair, Marty Gray, at 6:15 p.m. Chairman Gray welcomed those
in attendance in person and remotely through Zoom meeting.
Board members present at the meeting were Marty Gray; Emily Norton; Renier Elenbaas, Craig Mitchell, Sara
Bernardy.
Marty Gray adjourned the regular meeting and moved into Executive Session at 6:15 p.m.
The Pledge of Allegiance was recited.
Craig Mitchell made a motion to adopt the agenda. Emily Norton seconded the motion; the motion passed 5-0.
ADJOURNMENT
The meeting was adjourned at 6:16 p.m. by Board Chair, Marty Gray to enter into Executive Session for the purpose
of reviewing an employee contract. The session was expected to last approximately forty (40) minutes.
EXECUTIVE SESSION
RECONVENE
Chairman Gray reconvened the public meeting at 7:00 p.m. to advise the board was returning from Executive
Session.
CONSENT AGENDA
A. Approval of Meeting Minutes
B. Accounts Payable
C. Personnel Report
D. Donation Listing
A motion to adopt the consent agenda was made by Emily Norton and seconded by Sara Bernardy. The motion
passed 5-0.
PUBLIC COMMENT
There was no Public Comment
REPORTS
Meridian Booster Club Report
Superintendent, Dr. James Everett presented on behalf of the Booster Club President, Denise Barnes. He reported
the booster club was able to meet all their financial obligations for the 2020-21 School Year despite the pandemic
and absence of bi-annual auction funds.

Meridian Middle School Staff Recognition
Meridian Middle School Principal, Todd Torgeson recognized the school counselor and secretarial staff at the
Meridian Middle School for their excellent work and support of him as he joined the team.
Athletics and Activities Report
Meridian School District Athletic Director, Bryant Michaelson presented to the Board about the 2020-21 Sports
Seasons.
OLD BUSINESS
Second Reading: Policy 3112
Assistant Superintendent, Kurt Harvill presented a first second reading of Policy 3112: Social Emotional
Climate. Sara Bernardy made a motion to adopt Policy 3112, Craig Mitchell seconded the motion; the motion
passed 5-0.
Second Reading: Policy and Procedure 6000
Assistant Superintendent, Kurt Harvill presented a first second reading of Policy and Procedure 6000: Program
Planning Budget Preparation Adoption and Implementation Procedure. Craig Mitchell made a motion to adopt
Policy 3115, Emily Norton seconded the motion; the motion passed 5-0.
NEW BUSINESS
2021-22 Whatcom Discovery Cooperative Program Interlocal Agreement
Assistant Superintendent, Kurt Harvill presented the 2021-22 Whatcom Discovery Cooperative Program Interlocal
to the Board.
First Reading: Policy and Procedure 2125
Assistant Superintendent, Kurt Harvill presented a first reading of Policy 2125: Sexual Health Education.
First Reading: Policy 3432
Assistant Superintendent, Kurt Harvill presented a first reading of Policy 3432 – Emergencies.

FINANCE REPORT
Director of Business and Finance, Daniel Yorton presented on district finances to the Board.
SUPERINTENDENT’S REPORT
Superintendent, Dr. James Everett presented about increase in student in-person learning, funding and legislative
updates, vaccination clinics and potential for adding some portable building to the district.
INFORMATION FROM STUDENT BOARD MEMBERS
Student Board Member, Jenna McCauley shared about the upcoming sporting events and excitement around the
increase in in-person student learning.
INFORMATION FROM BOARD MEMBERS
The next Regular Board Meeting is scheduled for Wednesday, April 21 st at 7:00 PM in the Meridian School District
Professional Development Room.
