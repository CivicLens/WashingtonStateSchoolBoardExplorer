OPENING ITEMS
Meeting Date and Location:
August 8, 2018 - 1306 Dupont St., Bellingham, Washington
Call to Order/Roll Call:
Ms. Bashaw called the meeting to order at 6 p.m. Present: Directors Kelly Bashaw
(President), Camille Diaz Hackler, Douglas Benjamin, Jenn Mason, Quenby Peterson,
Superintendent Greg Baker, and Sheri O’Day (recorder).
Audience:
No public testimony was offered.
Superintendent’s Announcements and Report: Summary of Dr. Baker’s comments:
• Support to the Board: Dr. Baker noted that district staff is working on the following
school board requests:
o The Educational Technology Department is working to have microphones set
up at board members’ seats for future meetings to enable the audience to
better hear board members and presenters.
o Jackie Brawley, Executive Director, Communications and Community
Relations, demonstrated upcoming enhancements to the website, which
include more accessibility to users who do not read English and increasing the
ease with which website visitors can access school board meeting agendas and
minutes.
• Board of Distinction Application: Ms. Brawley distributed Board of Distinction
application materials and there was discussion regarding who would complete each
section of the application. Materials should be submitted by August 20, 2018.
• Facilities Update: Dr. Baker noted that the district is in the process of determining a
realtor for the sale of surplus property, and it is also exploring the possibility of
purchasing property in the Barkley area as a future site for the district office.
• BEA Negotiations: Negotiations with the Bellingham Education Association (BEA)
are continuing.
• Family Handbook: The 2018-19 Family Handbook is complete and a copy was
provided to each board member.
• There was a question regarding whether the district is working with any physicians to
offer reduced sports physicals. Staff will investigate whether this is occurring.
