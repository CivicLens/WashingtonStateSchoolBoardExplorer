School Board Special Meeting
The Board of Directors will meet in special session at 5:30 p.m. at Anthony's Hearthfire Grill - Squalicum
Harbor, 7 Bellwether Way, Bellingham, WA. The purpose of the meeting is to discuss school district
initiatives, issues and business in an unstructured manner. This is not a regular School Board meeting
and will not include public comment time.
For more information, call 360-676-6501.
