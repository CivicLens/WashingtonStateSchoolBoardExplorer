The regular meeting of the Board of Education, White Salmon Valley School District, Klickitat and Skamania Counties, White
Salmon, WA was called to order at 7:06 p.m. Thursday, August 22, 2024, at the WSVSD Board Room, Park Center Building,
located at 170 NW Washington St., White Salmon, WA 98672, and via a ZOOM public meeting. Present: Board Chair Laurie
Stanton (online), Vice-chair Peter Harkema, Kris Ostness, Matthew Byrne, Pat Dempsey, Superintendent Rich Polkinghorn, and
guests.
Board Vice-Chair Peter Harkema led the Board in the flag salute.

Reports:
a) Superintendent Update: Superintendent Polkinghorn reported that staff are returning and preparations for the first day
of school are in progress. In recent training K-6 staff received guidance on structured reading, with further training
scheduled, while secondary staff analyzed student data for school improvement plans. The district is seeking a project
management company for safe and sustainable facilities, with a request for qualifications to be published next week. A
community committee is forming to gather input, and a grant application for up to $12 million in improvements has been
submitted, outcome notification is expected to take six to nine months. A "Cell Phone Free School Day" was
communicated to families, with discussions planned for upcoming meetings. The classified staff bargaining resulted in a
tentative agreement awaiting ratification, and the certificated staff contract is on the agenda for approval tonight.
b) Board Member Committee Reports: None.
Public Comments: Public comment was provided by Gabrielle Gilbert and Ashley Becker-Nunley.
Consent Agenda: Pat Dempsey moved to accept the Consent Agenda; July 2024, Regular Board Meeting Minutes, Affidavit of
Payroll and Expenditures for the Month of July 2024, Affidavit of Payroll and Expenditures for the Month of August 2024, and
personnel actions. Seconded by Kris Ostness. Carried.
