Superintendent Greg Baker announced Kathryn Weilage has been selected Director of Business
Services, effective August 1, 2012. Ms. Weilage has worked for the district since March 2001.

be named. This is a new position and the individual will be responsible for providing direction
and leadership to all district programs serving preschool age children and their families.

POLICY GOVERNANCE REVIEW
Executive Limitation EL-2, Organizational Continuity
At the June 14, 2012 school board meeting, the board heard the monitoring report on EL-2,
Organizational Continuity. At the June 28, 2012 school board meeting, the board concurred that
the superintendent was in compliance.
Scott Stockburger moved and Ken Gass seconded the motion to adopt the revised EL-2,
Organizational Continuity, as submitted. Following discussion, the motion carried unanimously.
Ends 3, Life and Learning Skills, and Ends 4, Personal Attributes and Ethics
At the May 24, 2012 school board meeting, the board heard the monitoring report on Ends 3,
Life and Learning Skills, and Ends 4, Personal Attributes and Ethics. At the June 14, 2012 school
board meeting, the board concurred that the superintendent is making reasonable progress toward
compliance.
Superintendent Baker submitted proposed revisions to Ends 1, Ends 2, Ends 3, and GP-1, which
are more reflective of The Bellingham Promise. After discussion, the board will consider
separately each of the proposed policy revisions (Ends 1, Ends 2, Ends 2.1, Ends 3, Ends 4, and
Governance Policy 1) at the August 9, 2012 meeting.
Executive Limitation EL-9, Asset Maintenance and Protection
During the last several meetings, the board has discussed combining EL-9, Facilities Program,
and EL-10, Asset Protection. A revised EL-9, Asset Maintenance and Protection, was submitted
for review. Steve Smith moved and Ken Gass seconded the motion to adopt the revised EL-9,
Asset Maintenance and Protection, as submitted. Following discussion, the motion carried
unanimously.
