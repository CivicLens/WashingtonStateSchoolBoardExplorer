MERIDIAN SCHOOL DISTRICT #505
REGULAR SCHOOL BOARD MEETING AND EXECUTIVE SESSION
MEETING HELD IN PERSON & VIA ZOOM
March 29, 2023
REGULAR MEETING
The regular meeting was called to order by Board Chair Sara Bernardy at 6:00 p.m.
Board members present at the meeting were Sara Bernardy, Marty Gray, Craig Mitchell, and Steve Corwin.
Board members Jarrod Karuza and student Board Representative Kurtis Kettman and Mirage DeNeal were
excused from attending.
EXECUTIVE SESSION
The Board moved into executive session to review the performance of an employee. No action was
taken during Executive Session.
EXECUTIVE SESSION ADJOURNED
The Board moved out of Executive Session at 6:42 p.m.
RECONVENE REGULAR BOARD MEETING
The Regular Board meeting was reconvened at 7:00 p.m. and Board Chair Sara Bernardy welcomed
everyone that was in attendance.
Student Board Representative Mirage DeNeal was present at the reconvene of the regular board
meeting.
The pledge of allegiance was recited.
A motion to adopt the agenda was made by Craig Mitchell and seconded by Steve Corwin. The motion
passed 4-0.

A motion to adopt the consent agenda was made by Steve Corwin and seconded by Marty Gray. The

Meridian School District Athletic Director, Bryant Michaelson presented to the Board a report on district
athletics and activities.

PUBLIC COMMENT
There was no public comment.
OLD BUSINESS
Resolution 04-23: Juul Lawsuit Settlement Approval
Dr. James Everett presented to the Board for approval Resolution 04-23, Resolution to Approve
Settlement in Juul Litigation. A motion to approve Resolution 04-23, Resolution to Approve Settlement
in Juul Litigation as presented was made by Marty Gray and seconded by Craig Mitchell. The motion
passed 4-0.
Curriculum Materials Approval
Dr. James Everett presented DESMOS Math 6-Algebra, and OpenSciEd 6-12 Science curriculum materials
to the Board for second review and approval. Mirage favors supporting approval. A motion to approve
DESMOS Math 6-Algebra, and OpenSciEd 6-12 Science curriculum as presented was made by Craig
Mitchell and seconded by Steve Corwin. The motion passed 4-0.
NEW BUSINESS
First Reading: Policy and Procedure 3117
Assistant Superintendent Kurt Harvill presented a first reading of Policy and Procedure 3117 - Students
In or Released From An Institutional Education Facility.
First Reading: Procedure 3205P
Assistant Superintendent Kurt Harvill presented a first reading of Procedure 3205P - Sexual Harassment
of Students Prohibited. Discussion was had about needing additional clarity added into the WSDA
markup regarding the Board involvement prior to approval. More work will be done on clarifying this
procedure before bringing it back to the Board for consideration and approval.
First Reading: Policy and Procedure 5004
Assistant Superintendent Kurt Harvill presented a first reading of Policy and Procedure 5004 - Infection
Control Program Procedure.
FINANCIAL REPORT
Financial Report
Director of Finance Daniel Yorton gave a financial update to the Board. The financial update included
student enrollment comparisons, our budget status and budget projections. The big 3-year
accountability audit is starting next week.
