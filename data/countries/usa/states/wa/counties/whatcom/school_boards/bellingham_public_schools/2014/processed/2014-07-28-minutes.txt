DRAFT MINUTES OF THE SPECIAL MEETING OF THE
BELLINGHAM SCHOOL DISTRICT
BOARD OF DIRECTORS
July 28, 2014
The Bellingham School Board of Directors held a special meeting on July 28, 2014, at the
District Office, 1306 Dupont Street, Bellingham, Washington.
President Steve Smith called the meeting to order at 6 p.m.
Roll Call
Present: Directors Kelly Bashaw, Ken Gass, Camille Hackler, Steve Smith, Superintendent Greg
Baker, and Recorder Ruth Rasmussen.
Agenda
The purpose of the meeting was to interview finalists for the vacant School Board Position No. 5.
Before each candidate’s interview, Dr. Smith explained that the individual appointed to School
Board Position No. 5 will serve until the next board member election in November 2015. At that
time, the appointee may choose to place his/her name on the election ballot for a two-year term.
In 2017, candidates for School Board Position No. 5 will run for a four-year term.
The board conducted separate interviews of the following finalists:

Following the discussion, Dr. Gass made a motion, seconded by Ms. Bashaw, to select Douglas
