Legislative Communication
After the recent announcement from Governor Jay Inslee on lifting the mask mandate, it was
decided amongst the Board of Directors that a letter requesting changes pertaining to mask
mandates in schools will no longer be of need. The Board will appoint a director to be a liaison
for legislative contact or at the Education Service District level.
