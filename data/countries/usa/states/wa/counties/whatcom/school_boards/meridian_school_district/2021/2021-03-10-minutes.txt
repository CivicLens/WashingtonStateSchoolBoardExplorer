MERIDIAN SCHOOL DISTRICT #505
MINUTES OF REGULAR SCHOOL BOARD MEETING
MERIDIAN SCHOOL DISTRICT
MEETING HELD VIA ZOOM
March 10, 2021
Revised February 8, 2023

The regular meeting was called to order by Board Chair, Marty Gray, at 7:00 p.m. Chairman Gray welcomed those
in attendance on the remote Zoom meeting.
Board members present at the meeting were Marty Gray; Emily Norton; Renier Elenbaas, Craig Mitchell.
The Pledge of Allegiance was recited.
Craig Mitchell made a motion to adopt the agenda. Emily Norton seconded the motion; the motion passed 4-0.
CONSENT AGENDA
A. Approval of Meeting Minutes
B. Accounts Payable
C. Payroll
D. Personnel Report
A motion to adopt the consent agenda was made by Renier Elenbaas and seconded by Emily Norton. The motion
passed 4-0.
PUBLIC COMMENT
There was no Public Comment
PRESENTATIONS
Educational Support Professional Appreciation Week
Superintendent, Dr. James Everett presented about Educational Support Professional Appreciation Week and
honored the work these professionals do in the Meridian School District.
Asset Preservation and Maintenance Report
Director of Maintenance and Operations, Jay Yeager presented about the current state of the Districts assets and
ongoing maintenance for the property.
Meridian High School Report and Student Recognition
Meridian School District principal, Derek Forbes, presented about the Meridian high school students, recognizing
the yearbook students for their excellent work getting the yearbook prepared during remote and hybrid learning.
Principal, Mr. Derek Forbes and Assistant Principal, Andy Donahue then presented about the current state of the
programs at the Meridian High School highlighting the 5 goals. Meridian High School is currently above the county
average for graduation rate.

Revised and re-signed at Feb. 8, 2023 Board meeting to correct typographical errors that were
previously made in original minutes.

Career Technical Education Program Report
Principal, Derek Forbes, presented about the Career Technical Education Program to the Board. Over 500 kids are
involved in at least one class in the CTE Program at Meridian High School.
OLD BUSINESS

2021-22 NWESD Whatcom Detention Center Interlocal
Assistant Superintendent, Kurt Harvill presented the 2020-21 NWESD Whatcom Detention Center Interlocal
Agreement to the Board. This is a yearly Interlocal Agreement. Emily Norton made a motion to adopt the 202122 NWESD Whatcom Detention Center Interlocal, Craig Mitchell seconded the motion; the motion passed 4-0.
Second Reading: Policy 2107
Assistant Superintendent, Kurt Harvill presented a first second reading of Policy 2107: Comprehensive Early
Literacy Plan. Reinier Elenbaas made a motion to adopt Policy 2107, Emily Norton seconded the motion; the
motion passed 4-0.
Second Reading: Policy and Procedure 3115
Assistant Superintendent, Kurt Harvill presented a first second reading of Policy and Procedure 3115:
Homeless Students Experiencing Homelessness – Enrollment Rights and Services. Craig Mitchell made a motion
to adopt Policy 3115, Emily Norton seconded the motion; the motion passed 4-0.
NEW BUSINESS
First Reading: Policy and Procedure 3112
Assistant Superintendent, Kurt Harvill presented a first reading of Policy 3112: Social-Emotional Climate.
First Reading: Policy 6000
Assistant Superintendent, Kurt Harvill presented a first reading of Policy 6000 - Program Planning
Budget Preparation Adoption and Implementation Procedure.
SUPERINTENDENT’S REPORT
Superintendent, Dr. James Everett presented about the MSD committees, a report on the vaccination clinics, an
update on MSD Athletics, the Mount Baker Foundation Donation, upcoming bargaining, upcoming legislative
updates and the new Meridian Middle School Principal.
INFORMATION FROM STUDENT BOARD MEMBERS
Student Board Member, Jenna McCauley shared about the upcoming sporting events and how the leadership class
is working to honor seniors.
INFORMATION FROM BOARD MEMBERS
The next Regular Board Meeting is scheduled for Wednesday, March 31 st at 7:00 PM in the Meridian School District
Professional Development Room.
