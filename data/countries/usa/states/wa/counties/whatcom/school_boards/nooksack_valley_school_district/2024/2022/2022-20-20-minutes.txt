MINUTES APPROVED
REGULAR MEETING

Steve Jones communicated appreciation and gratitude for the dedication
and commitment Randy Wright showed as a board member for 36 years
and honored his passing. Hannah Ordos commented on concerns about
the direction of public education, asserting that politics have become too
present in many schools. She voiced her appreciation that the NVSD has
maintained a neutral stance on many political issues, specifically gender
expression and gender identity, and encouraged the district to maintain
that stance.
