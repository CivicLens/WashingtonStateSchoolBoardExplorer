The regular meeting was called to order by Board Chair, Craig Mitchell, at 7:00 p.m. Chair Mitchell welcomed those
in attendance.

Corwin.
The Pledge of Allegiance was recited.
Sara Bernardy made a motion to adopt the agenda. Jarrod Karuza seconded the motion; the motion passed 5-0.

OLD BUSINESS
ADA Accessibility Swingset Contract
Superintendent, Dr. James Everett presented a second reading of the ADA Swingset Contract to the Board. A motion
to approve the purchase of the ADA Accessibility Swingset was made by Sara Bernardy and seconded by Marty
Gray. The motion passed 5-0.
Maintenance and Safety
Superintendent, Dr. James Everett presented a second reading of three contracts of urgent matters in regard to
maintenance and safety.
A motion to approve the purchase of the Sanding and Refinishing of the gym floor was made by Marty Gray and
seconded by Sara Bernardy. The motion passed 5-0.
A motion to approve the purchase of new hurdles for Track and Field was made by Steve Corwin and seconded by
Marty Gray. The motion passed 5-0.
A motion to approve the purchase of handrails for the gym bleachers was made by Sara Bernardy and seconded by
Jarrod Karuza. The motion passed 5-0.
ParentSquare Quote
Superintendent, Dr. James Everett presented a second reading about ParentSquare, an application that will replace
a few of the current apps we have been using to communicate with our families and students. A motion to approve
the purchase of ParentSquare was made by Jarrod Karuza and seconded by Steve Corwin. The motion passed 5-0.
Vaping Lawsuit
Superintendent, Dr. James Everett presented a second reading of the vaping lawsuit that is occurring on a national
stage. Sara Bernardy moved the Board authorize the Superintendent to work with the Stevens Clay law firm to have
the School District join the pending vaping lawsuit against Juul and Altria, the successor to Phillip Morris. Marty
Gray seconded the motion. The motion passed 5-0.
Second Reading: Policy and Procedure 3131
Assistant Superintendent, Kurt Harvill presented a second reading of Policy and Procedure 3131 – Transfer to the
Board. A motion to adopt Policy and Procedure 3131 was made by Sara Bernardy and seconded by Jarrod Karuza.
The motion passed 5-0.
Second Reading: Policy and Procedure 5000
Assistant Superintendent, Kurt Harvill presented a second reading of Policy and Procedure 5000 – Recruitment and
Selection of Staff, to the Board. A motion to adopt Policy and Procedure 5000 was made by Sara Bernardy and
seconded by Jarrod Karuza. The motion passed 5-0.
First Second Reading: Policy and Procedure 5260
Assistant Superintendent, Kurt Harvill presented a second reading of Policy and Procedure 5260 – Personnel
Records to the Board. A motion to adopt Policy and Procedure 5620 was made by Sara Bernardy and seconded by
Marty Gray. The motion passed 5-0.
NEW BUSINESS
Sammamish Data: Re-Districting Proposal
Superintendent, Dr. James Everett presented a first reading of the Sammamish Data: Re-Districting Proposal to the
Board.
