CALL TO ORDER
The regular meeting of the Nooksack Valley School Board of Directors was called to order by
Chairman, Steve Jones, at 12:00 p.m. on Thursday, January 25, 2024.
MINUTES APPROVED REGULAR MEETING
Tanya Silves moved, and Halli Kimball seconded the motion to approve the minutes of the December
21, 2023 regular meeting.
M.C. 4-0
COMMUNICATIONS
• Steve Jones welcomed newly elected board members Shelley Harder and Tanya Silves to their
first full meeting.
• Katie Devries shared her appreciation for the board, and provided the board with thank you
cards and coffee cards from the NVMS PTSA and students.
• Superintendent Galley read Gov. Jay Inslee’s proclamation delivered in December recognizing
January as School Board Recognition month and presented each director with a certificate of
appreciation.
