Public Content
Staff Contact: Simone Sangster, Assistant Superintendent of Finance
Explanation of Issue:
Resolution 13-20, if approved by the board, will formally adopt the 2020-21 Bellingham School District
budget.
The final proposed 2020-21 budget is attached. This includes the district's budget book and the state
required budget documents, Form F-195 and F-195F. Copies of these documents have been distributed
toboard members and madeavailable for members of the public.
General Fund expenditures for 2020-21 are projected at $193,960,438an increase of $9,160,893(4.1
percent) compared to 2019-20budged expenditures. This increase is the result of a combination of
factors including mandatory obligations associated with Collective Bargaining Agreements, such as

salary increases, education/experience step increases for applicable certificated and classified staff
andinvestments in 2020-21 programs and services through additional certificated and classifiedstaffing
for schools,specifically in Special Education.
After a careful2020-21 budget process and a thorough review of the budget assumptions and resulting
calculations, the 2020-21 budget is presented for adoption in accordance with Resolution 13-20.
