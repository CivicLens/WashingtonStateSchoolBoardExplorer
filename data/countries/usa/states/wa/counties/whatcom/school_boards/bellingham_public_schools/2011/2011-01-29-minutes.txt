BELLINGHAM PUBLIC SCHOOLS
Bellingham, Washington
MINUTES OF THE SPECIAL MEETING
OF THE
BELLINGHAM SCHOOL DISTRICT
BOARD OF DIRECTORS
January 29, 2011
The Bellingham School Board of Directors met in special session January 29, 2011 at Cordata
Elementary School and in the Board Room at Whatcom Community College.
President Ann Whitmyer called the meeting to order.
At 8:00 a.m. the Board of Directors met at Cordata Elementary School for a tour of the school,
which is scheduled to open for students for the 2011–12 school year. Following the tour, the
Board reconvened at Whatcom Community College at 9:31 a.m.

Roll Call
Present: Directors Kelly Bashaw; Ken Gass, Steve Smith; Scott Stockburger, President Ann
Whitmyer, Superintendent Greg Baker, Assistant Superintendent Ron Cowan and Director Tanya
Rowe.
During the public portion of the meeting, the Board discussed the following:
• Budget Process
• Board Linkage/Study Session Schedule
• Policy Governance Monitoring of GP 1–4, 8 and 11
• November 2011 election
• Superintendent Baker’s Entry Plan initiatives

Adjournment
The meeting was adjourned at 1:07 p.m.
