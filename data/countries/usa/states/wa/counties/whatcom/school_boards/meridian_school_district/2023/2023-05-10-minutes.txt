MERIDIAN SCHOOL DISTRICT #505
REGULAR SCHOOL BOARD MEETING AND EXECUTIVE SESSION
MEETING HELD IN PERSON & VIA ZOOM
May 10, 2023
REGULAR MEETING
The regular meeting was called to order by Board Chair Sara Bernardy at 6:00 p.m.
Board members present at the meeting were Sara Bernardy, Marty Gray, Craig Mitchell, Jarrod Karuza
and Steve Corwin.
EXECUTIVE SESSION
The Board moved into executive session to review the performance of an employee. No action was
taken during Executive Session.
EXECUTIVE SESSION ADJOURNED
The Board moved out of Executive Session at 6:42 p.m.
RECONVENE REGULAR BOARD MEETING
The Regular Board meeting was reconvened at 7:00 p.m. and Board Chair Sara Bernardy welcomed
everyone that was in attendance.
Student Board Representative Mirage DeNeal arrived during the IRE Student Recognition report, and
student representative Kurtis Kettman was excused from attendance.
The pledge of allegiance was recited.
A motion to adopt the agenda was made by Jarrod Karuza and seconded by Steve Corwin. The motion
passed 5-0.
CONSENT AGENDA
A. Approval of Meeting Minutes
B. Personnel Report
C. Accounts Payable
A motion to adopt the consent agenda was made was made by Craig Mitchell and seconded by Marty
Gray. The motion passed 5-0.
RECOGNITION
IRE Principal Tyler Dockins and Dean of Students Nate Beede recognized fifth grade students at IRE who
were nominated for the self-manager program. Nate gave an overview presentation on the self-manager
program and introduced all the self-manager students that were present.
REPORTS
IRE School Report
IRE Principal Tyler Dockins presented an IRE school report to the Board. Tyler shared information about
strategies IRE is using to create a sense of belonging. Assessment and learning tools being utilizing to grow
learning, wellness and communication data, and techniques IRE is using to support students and families
were also shared by Tyler.

Assessment Program Report
Director of Teaching and Learning, Adrienne Somera, presented assessment information data to the
Board. The presentation included the purpose of assessments, types of assessments, in addition to
screening and monitoring tools.
PUBLIC COMMENT
There was no public comment.
OLD BUSINESS
Second Review and Approval: Resolution 05-23, Adoption of Long-Range Study and Survey
Dr. James Everett presented Resolution 05-23, Adoption of Long-Range Study and Survey to the Board
for a second review and approval. A motion to approve and adopt Resolution 05-23 as presented was
made by Marty Gray and seconded by Jarrod Karuza. The motion passed 5-0.
Second Review and Approval: Policy and Procedure 3205
Assistant Superintendent Kurt Harvill presented Policy and Procedure 3205 - Sexual Harassment of
Students Prohibited for a second review and approval. Student Board Representative favors support. A
motion to approve Policy and Procedure 3205 as presented was made by Jarrod Karuza and seconded by
Marty Gray. The motion passed 5-0.
