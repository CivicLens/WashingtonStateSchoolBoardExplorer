Blaine School District No. 503
  Special Board Meeting: Executive Session
Thursday, February 15, 2024
  Minutes
  1. Special Board Meeting
A. Call to Order
President Leu called the meeting to order, in the district's boardroom, at 6:00 p.m.
Directors Creydt, Lazarus, Freeman, and Leu were in attendance in the boardroom.
Director Ford attended the meeting telephonically from 6:00 p.m. until 6:54 p.m.
     Erika Creydt, Ben Lazarus, Cliff Freeman, Ryan Ford, Don Leu
  2. Executive Session
A. Executive Session
President Leu announced that the board would move into executive session for the purpose of consulting with legal counsel pursuant to Policy 1410. The executive session was anticipated to last for thirty (30) minutes, until 6:30 p.m. Any extension of time would be announced verbally, and no final action would be taken by the board at this meeting. The directors remained in the meeting room and all others moved to the District Office.

At 6:30 p.m., it was announced that the meeting would be extended for an additional thirty (30) minutes, until 7:00 p.m.

At 6:59 p.m., it was announced that the meeting would be extended for an additional fifteen (15) minutes, until 7:15 p.m.
  3. Reconvene and Adjourn
A. Reconvene and Adjourn
The executive session ended at 7:15 p.m., and the meeting reconvened in regular session. There being no further business, the meeting was adjourned by a unanimous vote at 7:16 p.m.
Motion by Cliff Freeman, second by Ben Lazarus
Final Resolution: Motion Carries 4-0, 1 not present
Yea: Erika Creydt, Ben Lazarus, Cliff Freeman, Don Leu
Not Present at Vote: Ryan Ford

DATE MINUTES APPROVED: February 26, 2024
