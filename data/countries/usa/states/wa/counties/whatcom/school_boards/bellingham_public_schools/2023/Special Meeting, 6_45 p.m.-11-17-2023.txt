Friday, November 17, 2023
Special Meeting, 6:45 p.m.
The Board of Directors will attend the Washington State School Directors Association (WSSDA) Annual
Conference from Thursday, Nov. 16 to Saturday, Nov. 18, 2023 at the Hyatt Regency in Bellevue,
Washington.
While in Bellevue, the board will hold a special meeting on Friday, Nov. 17 at 6:45 p.m. at STK
Steakhouse, 610 Bellevue Way NE, Bellevue, WA. The purpose of the meeting is to discuss school
district initiatives, issues and business in an unstructured manner. This is not a regular School Board
meeting and will not include public comment time.
For more information, call 360-676-6501.
