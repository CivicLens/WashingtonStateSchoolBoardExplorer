2.01 Award of Bid for Shuksan Auxiliary

Gym
Meeting :

Jun 24, 2020 - Special Meeting via Zoom

Type :

Action (Consent)

Recommended Action :

Staff submitted this request for board approval in
connection with the Shuksan Middle School Auxiliary Gym
project. As superintendent, I have reviewed and fully
support the award of the bid to Tiger Construction. Ltd.

Public Content
Staff Contact: Curtis Lawyer, Director of Capital Projects

Explanation of Issue:
On June 17, 2020the district received bids from four general contractors for the construction of the Shuksan Middle School
Auxiliary Gym. The bidders were Dawson Construction LLC, Faber Construction Co., SpeeWest Construction Co.and Tiger
Construction, Ltd.
We are looking forward to working with Tiger Construction, Ltd. of Everson, WA. Tiger was the General Contractor for several of
our school projects over the years, most recentlythe new Happy Valley Elementary School and Options High School projects.
Tiger has always performed admirably on our projects.
The attached bid tabulation lists all bidders and their respective bid prices. The base bid listed will be the contract amount, as no
alternates have been selected. After board approval, the bid tabulation will become official.
