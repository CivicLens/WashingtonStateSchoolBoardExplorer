8. Board Workshop
** Director Owsley joined the meeting.

A discussion involving Gender Inclusive Schools took place during the board workshop. The board
acknowledged the importance of this topic and their desire to be transparent. It was noted that
other districts such as La Center, Kennewick, and Woodland have adopted and/or revised their
Policy 3211 and 3211P – Gender Inclusive Schools and that they are not the same as the WSSDA
model policy and procedure. There is an interest among board members to revise Lynden’s
Gender Inclusive Policy (3211). The board would like to engage with legal counsel(s) during an
executive session and potentially include a first reading at the July meeting.
