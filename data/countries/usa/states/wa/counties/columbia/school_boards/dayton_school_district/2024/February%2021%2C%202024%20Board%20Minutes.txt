4. REPORTS
The reports section of tonight's meeting was postponed so board members could discuss the qualifications of the superintendent
candidates in executive session. All reports were submitted electronically for board review however due to the time constraints, the FFA
trip request was presented in person. Board reports can be viewed on Board Docs for tonight's meeting.
A. FFA Trip Presentation - Melanie Morgan
FFA Advisor Melanie Morgan presented information on the request for the FFA trapshooting team to attend the FFA State Trapshooting
Conference in Wenatchee. Also present were chaperone Lucas Collier and students Lyam Donohue, Luke Collier and Andrew Wertz. In
addition to the contest students would also tour the Aplets & Cotlets facility and the Rocky Reach Discovery Center. Students would
leave Dayton on April 18th and would return the evening of the 20th.

5. POLICIES
Postponed until next meeting.
6. ACTION ITEMS
A. Board member Paulson moved, seconded by director Fabian to approve the FFA overnight trip to Wenatchee for the State
Trap Shooting contest April 18-20, 2024. Motion carried 5-0.
Director Dieu commented she appreciated the thoroughness of the information presented for the trip.
B. Board member Griffen moved, seconded by director Paulson approve Resolution 2023-04 Cancel Outstanding Warrants.
Motion carried 5-0
7. EXECUTIVE SESSION
The board went into executive session at 6:50 p.m. to discuss the qualifications of a candidate for public employment per RCW
42.30.110. The session is expected to last for 2 hours with possible action to follow. At 8:50 p.m. the session was extended for another
15 minutes. The board returned to regular session at 9:05 p.m. after reviewing nine candidates for the position of superintendent.
Board member Dieu moved seconded by director Paulson to interview the following superintendent candidates on March 6,
2024.
Dr. Jodi Thew
Guin Joyce
Jeremy Wheatley
Motion carried 5-0
8. ADJOURNMENT
Board member McCowen adjourned the meeting at 9:06 p.m. The next meeting will be on March 6, 2024.
