REVISION OF POLICY
3510- ASSOCIATE STUDENT
BODIES APPROVED

Cindy Stockwell and Kim McGee updated and engaged to board
in a conversation about our reopening with K-5 students. They
provided a summary of the start, what is going well, and where
adjustments are being made. Matt Galley provided an overview
of the middle and high schools start up plans. Questions and
dialogue occurred throughout. Mark Johnson also provided a
budget update for the board.
