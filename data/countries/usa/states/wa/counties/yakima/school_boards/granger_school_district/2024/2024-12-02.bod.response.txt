Environmental Health Coordinator Salary (starting 1/1/2025) $81,181-$105,923 annually (10 Salary Steps) + Benefits Hiring Range: Steps 1-4 Benefits: Medical, Dental, Vision, FSA, Life Insurance, Retirement, as well as a generous annual, holiday and sick leave program. The Environmental Health Coordinator is responsible for coordinating and implementing environmental health programs to promote the health and safety of the community. This position involves planning, organizing, and overseeing various environmental health initiatives, including inspections, investigations, and public education. The Environmental Health Coordinator ensures compliance with local, state, and federal regulations and works closely with other health professionals, community partners, and the public to address environmental health issues. This position reports to the Environmental Health Director and the Environmental Health Manager. Those interested should apply by 11:59 pm on Sunday, December 8, 2024.
