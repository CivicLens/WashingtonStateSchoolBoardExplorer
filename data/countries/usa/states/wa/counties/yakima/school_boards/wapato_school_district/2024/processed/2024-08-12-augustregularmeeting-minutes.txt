3. Reports
• Superintendent's Report: Kelly Garza, Superintendent, reported:
• The District conducted interviews for Construction/Architect team earlier this month.
• Cabinet and AD Team Retreat were held last week at the Yakima Valley Museum in
preparing for another school year. Administrators participated in professional learning
activities.
• Last Tuesday, the District participated in the City’s National Night Out event at the Wapato
Lions Community Park. The event was very successful. The District handed out over 150
backpacks and other items such as bottle waters, coffee cups, pens, pencils and other items
during the 2-hour event. Mr. Garza thanked all staff that participated and made the event a
successful on.
• On Wednesday is New Teacher Orientation at the DO Boardroom. The event will begin at
8AM and he invited the Board to attend and welcome the new teachers.
• Tuesday, August 20th is the annual All Staff Welcome Back event at Wapato High School at
8AM. This event kicks off the return of staff to the new school year.
Construction Update: Kevin Hill, Executive Director, summarized the Construction/Architect
interviews that took place last week. He talked about the process of interviews and the
selection to narrow down to 3 teams. The District will meet this week to review the RFP and
make a selection of team to work with through the construction of the new schools. He listed
the names and location of the companies/team in the final round.
He reported the permits from the Yakama Nation have been approved to move forward with
bid proposal to find a drilling company to drill a new well for Wapato High School. The District
is planning to apply for grants to help offset the cost of the project.
Summer School Update: Ken Olden, Director and Teacher Leaders: Bethany Hunter, Michelle
Howell, Casey Huibregtse presented a recap of 2023-24 Summer School. The presented data
on enrollment of PK-12, secondary credits earned, enrichment highlights, challenges
attendance data.
