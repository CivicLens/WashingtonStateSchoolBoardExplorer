Dayton School District No. 2

Regular Board Meeting
January 17, 2024

1. MEETING OPENING
A. Call to Order
Board chair McCowen called the meeting to order at 6:35 p.m. The meeting was held via Zoom due to inclement weather. Board
members present included Jeff McCowen, Zac Fabian, Ryan Paulson and Aneesha Dieu. Board member Griffen was absent (excused).
Also present was business manager Jana Eaton. Superintendent Stewart was unable to attend due to a previous commitment (levy
presentation).
B. Flag Salute
Board chair McCowen led the flag salute.
C. Recognition of Representatives
None
D. Additions or Changes to the Agenda
The FFA Overnight Stay Should read Wenatchee. Board member McCowen moved the FFA request to action items for further
discussion.
2. CONSENT AGENDA
A. Recommendation to approve School Board Minutes from December 20, 2023 and January 3, 2024 meetings;
B. Recommendation to approve January Bills and Payroll - General Fund warrants 121707-121747 totaling $94,285.85; Capital Projects
Fund warrants 121748-121749 totaling $10,836.58; Private Purpose Trust Fund warrant 121750 totaling 4,000; ASB Fund warrants
121751-121759 totaling $4,505.35; General Fund warrants 121760-121762 totaling $164,117.31; Capital Projects warrant 121763
totaling $562,364.79; Payroll warrants 121764-121783 totaling $479,540.66;
C. Recommendation to Approve - Donation of $2,000.00 for Yamate Student Exchange Program from Kim Carter
D. Recommendation to Approve - IRS Mileage Reimbursement for 2024 from $.655 to $.67
Board member Dieu moved, seconded by director Paulson to approve the consent agenda as presented. Motion carried 4-0.
E. Recommendation to Approve the Hire - Jeff McCowen HS Head Baseball Coach
Board member Fabian moved, seconded by director Paulson to approve Jeff McCowen as Head High School Baseball Coach.
Motion carried 3-0 (Board member McCowen recused himself).
3. PUBLIC COMMENT
None
4. REPORTS
Due to the meeting being moved to Zoom reports were submitted electronically.
A. Student Representative's Reports - Submitted electronically.
B. Athletic Director's Report - Submitted electronically.
C. Administrator Reports (Elementary and MS/HS Principal)
Elementary principal Amy Cox reported on the activities in December and January in the elementary. The Walk to Read program is
making great progress. This program is for district wide reading intervention program. There is additional information on this program on
our school website. For sixty minutes per day students receive targeted instruction on needed phonics skills. Elementary teachers have
contracted with Slingerland Literacy Institute to have trainers come on two Friday evenings and Saturday morning to provide focused
instruction on handwriting.
Secondary Principal Guin Joyce submitted her report electronically.

E. Facility Report - John Delp - Submitted electronically
F. Budget Status Report
Business manager Jana Eaton reviewed the December budget status and grant applications currently in progress.
5. POLICIES
Policies were tabled till next meeting.
6. ACTION ITEMS
A. Board member Dieu moved, seconded by director Paulson to table the first and second reading of policies to the next in person
meeting. Motion carried 4-0.
B. Discussion was held on the request from FFA advisor Melanie Morgan to attend the FFA State Trapshooting in Wenatchee on April 18
through 20th. Board members were concerned that the Air B&B in Leavenworth was too far away from the competition. Further
discussion was held on the safety and security of students staying at an Air B&B rather than a hotel/motel. The action item to approve
the overnight trip was tabled until further information could be provided on the trip.
7. EXECUTIVE SESSION
None
8. ADJOURNMENT
Board member McCowen adjourned the meeting at 7:37 p.m. The next meeting will be held on February 7, 2024 and will be a board
work session in the high school library/media center and via Zoom at 6:30 p.m.

Rich Stewart, Secretary to the Board of Directors
