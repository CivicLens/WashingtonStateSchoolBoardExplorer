Saturday, November 20, 2021
Special Meeting, 12:15 p.m.
The Board of Directors will attend the Washington State School Directors Association (WSSDA) Annual
Conference from Thursday, Nov. 18 to Saturday, Nov. 20 at the Hyatt Regency Bellevue in Bellevue,
Washington.
While in Bellevue, the board will hold a special meeting on Saturday, Nov. 20 at 12:15 p.m. in the
Executive Boardroom of the Hyatt Regency Bellevue, 900 Bellevue Way N.E., Bellevue, WA. The
purpose of the meeting is to discuss results from the board's recent WSSDA self-assessment survey.
This is not a regular school board meeting and will not include public comment time.
For more information, call 360-676-6501.
