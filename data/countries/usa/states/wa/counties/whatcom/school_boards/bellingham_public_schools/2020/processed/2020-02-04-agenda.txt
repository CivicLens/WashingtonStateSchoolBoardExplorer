School Board Visit - Shuksan Middle School

school year. The purpose of these visits is to learn how each school is delivering upon The Bellingham
Promise. The directors will visit Shuksan Middle School, 2717 Alderwood Ave., Bellingham, at 7:30 a.m.,
Tuesday, February 4, 2020.
