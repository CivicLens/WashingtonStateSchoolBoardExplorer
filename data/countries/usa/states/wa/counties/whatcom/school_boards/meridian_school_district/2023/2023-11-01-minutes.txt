MERIDIAN SCHOOL DISTRICT #505
REGULAR SCHOOL BOARD MEETING
MEETING HELD IN PERSON & VIA ZOOM
November 1, 2023

REGULAR MEETING
The regular meeting was called to order by Board Chair Sara Bernardy at 6:00 p.m.
The pledge of allegiance was recited.
Board Chair Sara Bernardy welcomed everyone that was in attendance.
Board members present at the meeting were Sara Bernardy, Craig Mitchell, Steve Corwin, Marty Gray and
Jarrod Karuza.
Student Board Representative Kennedy Brzozowski was present at the meeting, Morgan Adams was
excused from attending.
A motion to adopt the agenda was made by Craig Mitchell and seconded by Jarrod Karuza. The motion
passed 5-0.

A motion to adopt the consent agenda was made by Steve Corwin and seconded by Jarrod Karuza. The
motion passed 5-0.
REPORTS
Meridian Citizens for Education Committee
Bill Chambers from the Meridian Citizens for Education Committee presented an EP&O and Capital
Technology replacement levy recommendations to the Board.
Irene Reither Staff Recognition
Irene Reither Principal Tyler Dockins presented a staff recognition report celebrating new teaching staff
and highlighting the screening, selection and hiring process. Tyler also celebrated IRE’s writing rubric
alignment and the growth and spoke of the excitement they are seeing in students because of this school
wide aligned rubric. Additionally, Tyler celebrated the behavior growth they are seeing, increased family
engagement, and the amazing work the coaching team is doing throughout IRE.
PRESENTATIONS
PLC Presentations
MHS PE teachers Tylor Labelle and Shane Stacy presented health and fitness PLC information to the Board.
The presentation was focused on weight training and included what they want students to know, tools
used to measure student progress, and the steps that are taken to assist student progress.

MMS ELA teachers Bailey Drake, Merrit Michelbrink, and Courtney Price presented PLC information on
knowledge of language and its conventions when writing. The team shared the writing goals for each
grade level, the tools used to measure progress, and the reteaching strategies used to increase progress
and move on to the next steps.
IRE third-grade teachers Glenn DePeralta, Alyson Koerner, Katy Vance, Brynn Beatty, and Evelyn Aguilar
shared third-grade narrative writing PLC information. The team shared the pre-assessments rubric used,
the rubric used to measure progress, strategies the team uses to increase knowledge, and the next steps
taken once the skill is mastered.
PUBLIC COMMENT
There was no public comment.
OLD BUSINESS
Second Review and Approval: Policy and Procedure 3207 – Prohibition of Harassment,
Intimidation, and Bullying of Students

Assistant Superintendent Kurt Harvill presented Policy and Procedure 3207 – Prohibition of Harassment,
Intimidation, and Bullying of Students to the Board for a second review and approval. Student
Representative favors approval. A motion to adopt Policy and Procedure 3207 – Prohibition of
Harassment, Intimidation, and Bullying of Students as presented was made by Marty Gray and seconded
by Craig Mitchell. The motion passed 5-0.
Second Review and Approval: Policy 3210 - Nondiscrimination and Equity
Assistant Superintendent Kurt Harvill presented Policy 3210 - Nondiscrimination and Equity to the Board
for a second review and approval. Student Representative favors approval. After a lengthy discussion, a
motion to adopt Policy 3210 - Nondiscrimination and Equity as presented was made by Marty Gray and
seconded by Craig Mitchell. The motion passed 3-2.
Second Review and Approval: Policy and Procedure 6240 – Meals with Meetings
Assistant Superintendent Kurt Harvill presented Policy and Procedure 6240 – Meals with Meetings to the
Board for a second review and approval. Student Representative favors approval. A motion to adopt Policy
and Procedure 6240 – Meals with Meetings as presented was made by Steve Corwin and seconded by
Craig Mitchell. The motion passed 5-0.
Second Review and Approval: Procedure 3421F – Child Abuse, Neglect and Exploitation
Prevention

Assistant Superintendent Kurt Harvill presented Procedure 3421F – Child Abuse, Neglect and Exploitation
Prevention to the Board for a second review and approval. Student Representative favors approval. A
motion to adopt Procedure 3421F – Child Abuse, Neglect and Exploitation Prevention as presented was
made by Jarrod Karuza and seconded by Steve Corwin. The motion passed 5-0.
