BELLINGHAM PUBLIC SCHOOLS
Bellingham, Washington
MINUTES OF THE STUDY SESSION
OF THE
BELLINGHAM SCHOOL DISTRICT
BOARD OF DIRECTORS
December 6, 2011
The Bellingham School Board of Directors met in a study session December 6, 2011 in the
Board Room of the District Office, 1306 Dupont Street, Bellingham, Washington.
President Ann Whitmyer called the meeting to order at 6:05 p.m.

Roll Call
Present: Directors Kelly Bashaw, Steve Smith, Scott Stockburger, Ken Gass, President Ann
Whitmyer, incoming Director Camille Diaz Hackler, Superintendent Greg Baker and recorder
Nancy Merry.

Maintenance and Operations Levy and Technology Levy
The Board of Directors received information from Superintendent Greg Baker and Ron Cowan,
assistant superintendent of Business and Operations, about the expiring general operations
(M&O) levy, which supports nearly 25 percent of the district’s annual operating budget. The
levy helps Bellingham Public Schools’ maintain its current programs for students to the best of
its ability in this economy. The Board of Directors also received information about the district’s
expiring technology levy and future needs. Both levies will be on the ballot on Feb. 14, 2012.
Members of the Executive Team responded to questions from the Board.

Adjournment
The meeting adjourned at 7:30 p.m.
