Thursday, January 4, 2024
Special Board Meeting: Executive Session
Opening and adjournment of this meeting will take place in the districts boardroom at 770 Mitchell
Avenue in Blaine. The directors will meet in executive session for the purpose of reviewing the
performance of a public employee.

Public Content
Pursuant to Policy 1410, the board may choose to convene in executive session for specific purposes
allowed by state statute. President Leu will announce that the board will move into executive session for
the purpose of reviewing the performance of a public employee. The executive session is anticipated to
last until 7:00 p.m. Any extension of time will be announced verbally in the boardroom, and no final action
will be taken by the board at this meeting.
