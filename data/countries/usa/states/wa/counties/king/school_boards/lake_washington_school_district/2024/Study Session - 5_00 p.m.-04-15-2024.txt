Topic:
Highly Capable Program Update
Overview:
Associate Superintendent Dr. Rose and Director Myra Arnone will provide the Board with an
update on the Highly Capable Program Review and the recommendations being evaluated by
the committee. The Board will also receive information from consultant, Dr. Tamra Stambaugh,
on the needs and program benefits for gifted students. The Study Session will connect to the
various values that are being considered as the committee works to make a recommendation for
possible program shifts.
Board Policy Connection:
OE-9, OE-10, OE-11, and OE-14
Outcome:

The Board will have a depth of understanding of the recommendations and have the opportunity
to ask questions, connect to the Boards policies/values and understand next steps of the
decision-making process.
