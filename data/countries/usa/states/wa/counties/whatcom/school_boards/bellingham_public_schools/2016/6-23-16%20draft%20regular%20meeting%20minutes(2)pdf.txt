OPENING ITEMS
Meeting Date and Location
June 23, 2016 – 1306 Dupont St., Bellingham, Washington
Call to Order/Roll Call
Board President Camille Diaz Hackler called the meeting to order at 6:15 p.m. Present:
Directors Kelly Bashaw, Douglas Benjamin, Camille Diaz Hackler, Quenby Peterson,
Steven Smith, Superintendent Greg Baker, and recorder Ruth Rasmussen.
Audience: No audience presented
Public Hearing on adoption of 2016-17 Bellingham School District Budget: Ms. Diaz
Hackler opened the public hearing. She said this was an opportunity for members of the
public to comment either for or against the proposed budget, which the board will
consider as a consent agenda item later in the meeting. There was no public comment.
Superintendent’s Announcements and Report: Dr. Baker briefly discussed consent
agenda items 2.2 (2016-17 Budget) and 2.3 (School Bus Levy). He also updated the board
on the Options High School construction project.
CONSENT ITEMS: Dr. Baker, noting that Executive Limitations monitoring reports are
routinely part of the consent agenda, suggested that Item 3.1 (EL-6 – Academic Standards
and Practices) be moved to the consent agenda. The board members concurred.
Following discussion, Ms. Bashaw made a motion, seconded by Mr. Benjamin, to
approve the following consent agenda items. Unanimous affirmative vote.
