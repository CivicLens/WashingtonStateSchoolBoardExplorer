  6. APPOINTMENT OF DIRECTOR DISTRICT #3
6.01 Appointment of Director
  Dr. Erickson thanked all the candidates and indicated the decision was not easy.  The board agrees that the candidates were very well qualified and give their appreciation for their time. Ms. Uppiano remarked that sometimes districts have a difficult time finding applicants, and we were fortunately to have a group with the zeal and fortitude to step up and serve.
  Ms. Cool moves to appoint Toni Jefferson to the vacant District 3 position.  Dr. Erickson reminded the board that there must be 3 favorable votes and cannot have a 2-2 tie vote.  Roll call vote: Ms. Uppiano- yay, Ms. Cool - yay, Mr. Childs - yay, Dr. Erickson - yay.  Motion carries unanimously.
Ms. Jefferson will be sworn in at the next regular board meeting on Tuesday, February 28, 2023.
