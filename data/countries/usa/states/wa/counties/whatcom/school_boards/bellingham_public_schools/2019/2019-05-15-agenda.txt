School Board Special Meeting
The Board of Directors will meet in special session at 5:15 p.m. at Cafe Akroteri, 1219 Cornwall Avenue,
Bellingham, WA. The purpose of the meeting is to discuss school district initiatives, issues and business
in an unstructured manner. This is not a regular School Board meeting and will not include public
comment time.
For more information, call 360-676-6501.
