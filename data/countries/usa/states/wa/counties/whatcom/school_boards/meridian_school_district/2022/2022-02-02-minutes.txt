MERIDIAN SCHOOL DISTRICT #505
MINUTES OF EXECUTIVE SESSION AND REGULAR SCHOOL BOARD MEETING
MERIDIAN SCHOOL DISTRICT
MEETING HELD VIA ZOOM
February 2nd, 2022
Revised February 8, 2023
The regular meeting was called to order by Board Chair, Craig Mitchell, at 7:01 p.m. Chairman Mitchell welcomed
those in attendance.
Board members present at the meeting were Sara Bernardy; Marty Gray; Jarrod Karuza; Steve Corwin; Craig
Mitchell.
The Pledge of Allegiance was recited.
Jarrod Karuza made a motion to adopt the agenda. Sara Bernardy seconded the motion; the motion passed 5-0.
CONSENT AGENDA
A. Approval of Meeting Minutes
B. Accounts Payable
C. Payroll
D. Personnel Report
E. Student Travel
F. NWESD Technical Services Agreement
A motion to adopt the consent agenda was made by Steve Corwin and seconded by Marty Gray. The motion passed
5-0.
PUBLIC COMMENT
Michelle Simmons came to the Board to request that the School District would communicate as the process
to determine the district’s Sexual Health curriculum continues. She also requested that the Board would hold
small group sessions with the community as Board Director Sara Bernardy had suggested earlier in the year.
Michelle asked that the district would work to unite the community and set an example.
RECOGNITION
Honoring Jerry Sanderson
Superintendent, James Everett recognized Jerry Sanderson for his 40 years of service to the Meridian School
District.
PRESENTATIONS
MMS Staff Recognition
Meridian Middle School Principal, Todd Torgeson recognized Ron Zeagers and Doug Goodwin for their 30 years of
service to the Meridian School District and their upcoming retirement.

Revised and re-signed at Feb. 8, 2023 Board meeting to correct typographical errors that were
previously made in original minutes.

Curriculum Materials Update
Director of Teaching and Learning, Adrienne Somera gave an update on the district’s curriculum materials and
process for determining what curriculum to use.
Legislative Update
Superintendent, James Everett and Board Director, Sara Bernardy gave a legislative update to the Board.
OLD BUSINESS
Resolution 01-22
Superintendent, James Everett presented a first second reading of Resolution 01-22: Multi-Jurisdictional Natural
Hazards Mitigation Plan. A motion to adopt Resolution 01-22 was made by Marty Gray and seconded by Sara
Bernardy. The motion passed 5-0.
First Second Reading: Policy and Procedure 3115
Assistant Superintendent, Kurt Harvill presented a first second reading of Policy Policy and Procedure 3115:
Students Experiencing Homelessness to the Board. A motion to adopt Policy 3115 was made by Sara Bernardy and
seconded by Jarrod Karuza. The motion passed 5-0.
First Second Reading: Policy and Procedure 3116
Assistant Superintendent, Kurt Harvill presented a first second reading of Policy and Procedure 3116: Students in
Foster Care to the Board. A motion to adopt Policy 3116 was made by Sara Bernardy and seconded by Steve Corwin.
The motion passed 5-0.
NEW BUSINESS
First Reading: Policy and Procedure 5610
Assistant Superintendent, Kurt Harvill presented a first reading of Policy and Procedure 5610 – Substitute
Employment to the Board.
First Reading: Policy and Procedure 3207
Assistant Superintendent, Kurt Harvill presented a first reading of Policy and Procedure 3207 – Prohibition of
Harassment, Intimidation, or Bullying to the Board.
SUPERINTENDENT’S REPORT
Superintendent, Dr. James Everett presented about updated DOH Guidance, the district’s vaccination clinic and the
decline in student and staff absences.
INFORMATION FROM BOARD MEMBERS
The next Regular Board Meeting is scheduled for Wednesday, March 2nd at 7:00 p.m.
