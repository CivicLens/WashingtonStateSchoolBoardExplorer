Monday, January 29, 2024
Pack The Gym at Kentridge High School - 5:00 - 8:30 p.m. - See schedule below.
Several of the Kent School District Board of Directors may attend the 2024 Pack the Gym, which is being
held in the main gym of Kentridge High School on Monday, January 29, 2024.
With the Unifid movement growing throughout the Kent School District, the annual Kentridge "Pack the
Gym" event is also evolving. This year the event will be on Monday, January 29 at Kentridge High
School. It will feature a scrimmage format and include the Unified basketball teams from Kent-Meridian,
Kentridge and Kentwood high schools as part of the celebration of inclusion.
