BELLINGHAM PUBLIC SCHOOLS
Bellingham, Washington
MINUTES OF THE STUDY SESSION
OF THE
BELLINGHAM SCHOOL DISTRICT
BOARD OF DIRECTORS
February 16, 2012

The Bellingham School Board of Directors met in a study session February 16, 2012 at the
District Office, 1306 Dupont Street, Bellingham, Washington.
President Kelly Bashaw called the meeting to order at 6:00 p.m.
Roll Call
Present: Directors Camille Hackler Steve Smith, Ken Gass, President Kelly Bashaw, and
Superintendent Greg Baker.
The Board was joined by Executive Director of Human Resources Nora Klewiada, Deputy
Superintendent Tom Venable, Executive Director of Teaching and Learning Steve Clarke for a
portion of the meeting.
Agenda
• The Board heard an update on facilities and the budget process.
• The Board discussed possible topics for future linkages and study sessions.
• The Board discussed Governance Policies
• The Board discussed the Superintendent’s mid-year review
Adjournment
The meeting adjourned at 8:40 p.m.
