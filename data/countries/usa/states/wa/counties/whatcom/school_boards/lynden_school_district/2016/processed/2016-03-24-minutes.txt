March 24, 2016
School Board Meeting – Work Session
Minutes
Lynden Middle School Library
6:30 P.M.
1. Call to Order, Welcome, Pledge of Allegiance and Roll Call
Meeting called to order at 6:31P.M. Board members in attendance were Brian Johnson, Steve Jilk, Mike Haveman,
and Kevin Burke. Board member, Dominic Shiu arrived at 6:55 P.M. Student Directors, Tore Olson and Carson
Smith were absent and excused.
2. Approval of Meeting Minutes
 Meeting Minutes March 17, 2016
Steve Jilk motioned to approve the meeting minutes seconded by Mike Haveman. Motion carried by voice vote.
3. Consent Agenda
 2015-16 General Fund $171,990.93 Warrants 40454 through 40563 Dated March 15, 2016
 2015-16 Capital Projects $381,460.83Warrants 40564 through 40569 Dated March 15, 2016

 Personnel Action Items – March 24, 2016
 Overnight Trip Requests – March 24, 2016
Steve Jilk motioned to approve the consent agenda seconded by Mike Haveman. Motion carried by voice vote.
4. Information from the Audience
Music Booster Board of Director Representative, Carri Dodge, recognized the effort of the Wind Ensemble and their
achievement of the 25th “Superior” title at the San Juan Music Educators (SJMEA) competition at Western
Washington University.
5. Adjourn Meeting
Meeting adjourned at 6:48 P.M.
6. Board Work Session
 Professional Learning Community (PLC)
Jim Frey explained the reasons of a PLC and how we will define the work of improving teacher and
student engagement and how we plan to prepare teachers for a culture of collaboration.


Resource for Technology
Elizabeth Hamming provided an update on the work taking place by the Technology Committee and
explained the ideas the committee is looking into for technology instruction with the support of
technology devices. Elizabeth shared the article “Planning for 21st Century Technologies in Schools”.
Lynden Middle School Teacher, Terry Kaemingk, gave examples of his class use of technology devices
and access to the internet to provide an enhanced technology learning experience.



School Safety
The board discussed the All Hazard Mitigation Risk Assessment worksheet provided by the Washington
Schools Risk Management Pool. The board will be updated on changes that are made during the review
process.
