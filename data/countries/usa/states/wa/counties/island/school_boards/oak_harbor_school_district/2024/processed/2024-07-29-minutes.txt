Call to Order

A consent-agenda-only meeting of the Board of Directors was held on Monday, July 29,
2024, in the Administrative Service Center Board Room and was available for live
streaming. Vice President Jessica Aws called the meeting to order at 12:05 p.m. and led
the flag salute.

Roll Call

Board members Jessica Aws, Nicole Tesch, Sharon Jensen, and Josh MacLean were
present via Google Meet. President Lynn Goebel was absent and excused.

Adoption of Agenda

The agenda was adopted by unanimous consent.

Consent Agenda

Director Tesch moved to approve the consent agenda. Director Jensen seconded the
motion. The motion passed unanimously.
