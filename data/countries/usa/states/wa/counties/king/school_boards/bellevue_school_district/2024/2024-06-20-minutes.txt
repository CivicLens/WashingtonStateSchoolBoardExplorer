8.1 Approval of Superintendent Employment Contract
Recommended Action
That the Board of Directors approve the issuance of a Superintendent Employment Contract for Dr. Aramaki to
run from July 1, 2024, to June 30, 2027 that mirrors the existing Superintendent Employment Contract with Dr.
Aramaki with the below changes:
•

Heading and Paragraph 1 Term. Change to reflect a three-year employment agreement between the
Board and the Superintendent that begins on July 1, 2024 and ends of June 30, 2027;

•

Paragraph 2 Annual Salary. Apply the implicit price deflator (IDP) amount that is applied to the
salaries of educators pursuant to Washington law (RCW 28A.400.205) to the Superintendent’s
salary for the 2024-2025 school year and round up to the nearest hundred dollar value;

•

Paragraph 6 Stipends. Ensure that the Superintendent’s Technology Stipend is consistent with the
Technology Stipend amount approved for to all other certificated administrators in Item 5.1;

•

Paragraph 14 Covenants of the Superintendent. Remove the provision related Dr. Aramaki taking
the Oath of Office to the Superintendent; and

•

Signatories. Update the signature lines to reflect the current composition of the Board.
Motion 2024-0620-07: President Carolyn Watson moved to approve
The Motion was approved by unanimous voice at 6:50pm.
