BATTLE GROUND PUBLIC SCHOOLS
BOARD OF DIRECTORS
Special Meeting
May 28, 2024
4:00 P.M.
Lewisville Campus
406 NW Fifth Avenue
Battle Ground, WA

MINUTES

Members present:
Mary Snitily, Debbie Johnson, Ted Champine, Terry Tate, Jackie Maddux
Student Representatives present:
Sora Tolley and Julia Stiffler
Administrators present:
Superintendent Denny Waters, Deputy Superintendent Shelly Whitten
Recorder:
Administrative Assistant to the Deputy Superintendent Lisa Randle
Patron/Staff: 6
1. Call to Order
President Maddux called the meeting to order at 4:00 p.m.
2. Students on School Boards
A. Interviews for Students on Governing Boards
The board held interviews for the upcoming open student representative position. The selected student will start their
two-year term on July 1, 2024. The board interviewed the following 10th-grade students who were nominated to be
student representatives on the board. Interviews were conducted from 4:02 - 5:12 p.m.
1. Emma Metzger
2. Karoline Loose
3. Itzel Contreras Montiel
4. Aubrey Grier
3. Executive Session
A. Evaluate the Qualifications of Candidate RCW 42.30.110 (1)(h) - Action
Director Maddux announced the board would enter an executive session per RCW 42.30.110 (1)(h), with action for 30
minutes.
At 5:15 p.m., the Board entered the executive session. At 5:45 p.m., President Maddux extended the meeting for 5
minutes. The executive session ended with action at 5:50 p.m.
Motion by Ted Champine, The Board of Directors appoints Itzel Contreras Montiel as the student board
representative effective July 1, 2024, and selected Aubrey Grier as the alternate student
representative pending their acceptance.
Final Resolution: Motion Carries 5-0
Aye: Mary Snitily, Debbie Johnson, Ted Champine, Terry Tate, Jackie Maddux
4. Adjournment
The meeting adjourned at 5:51 p.m.

APPROVED: JUNE 10, 2024
