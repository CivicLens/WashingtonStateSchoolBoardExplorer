Call to Order

A regular meeting of the Board of Directors was held on Monday, August 12, 2024, in
the Administrative Service Center Board Room and was available for live streaming.
President Lynn Goebel called the meeting to order at 6:00 p.m. and led the flag salute.

Roll Call

Board members Lynn Goebel, Jessica Aws, Nicole Tesch, Sharon Jensen, and Josh
MacLean were present. Student representative Maggie Litvachuk was present.

Adoption of Agenda

The agenda was adopted by unanimous consent.

Consent Agenda

Director Aws moved to approve the consent agenda. Director Tesch seconded the
motion. The motion passed unanimously.

2024-2025 Board
Meeting Calendars
First Reading

Superintendent Michelle Kuss-Cybula reviewed the 2024-2025 board meeting calendars
for a first reading. The School Board Meeting Schedule lists all regular board meetings
for the year. The School Board Calendar displays the main topics and presentations for
each work study and business meeting and is subject to change.

Communications
Report | Work Study

Communications Officer Sarah Foy presented an update on the work of the
Communications Department with a focus on alignment with the Strategic Plan. The
new Work Study format allows for deeper discussion into the topic at hand.

Preliminary 20242025 Budget
Overview | Work
Study

Chief Financial Officer Amber Porter presented and discussed with the board an
overview of the 2024-2025 Budget including the required MSOC Disclosure Statement.

Board Comments

Director Tesch shared that her kids are excited for school to start up again. Vice
President Aws commented on the many variables involved with the budget and the
importance of the next levy to pass. President Goebel congratulated the
Communications team on their recent NSPRA award.

Superintendent
Comments

Dr. Kuss-Cybula thanked the presenters for their flexibility with the new meeting
format. She reinforced the critical importance of continuing programs that the levy pays
for, such as art and music.

Upcoming Meetings

Regular meetings are scheduled for Monday, August 26, and Monday, September 9,
2024, at 6:00 p.m. in the ASC Board Room.

Adjourn

The meeting adjourned at 7:27 p.m.

Minutes prepared by Katey Andrews.
