Recommended Action :

To adopt Resolution 1920-16 regarding the district's
revised reopening plan for the 2020-2021 school year

Public Content
At the Special Board Meeting on August 5, Superintendent Dr. Christopher Granger recommended
revising the district's 2020-2021 reopening plan, based upon Whatcom County Health Department
recommendations, to start the year with remote learning with the goal of bringing staff and students back
to the buildings incrementally in phases. The directors agreed to table consideration of this
recommendation in order to provide an opportunity for community input. Dr. Granger held a Community
Q&A session on August 6.
The purpose of this Special Board Meeting is for the board to review community input received, and to
consider adoption of updated Resolution 1920-16 regarding the district's revised reopening plan for the
2020-2021 school year. This plan must be provided to OSPI and SBE no later than two weeks before the
districts first day of school which is scheduled for August 26, 2020.
