Tuesday, March 26, 2024
Special Board Meeting - Executive Session starting at 5:15 p.m. and Regular Board Meeting - 6:00 p.m.
The regularly scheduled Board Meeting of Federal Way School Board will convene at 5:15 p.m. rather
than the regularly scheduled time of 6:00 p.m. The Board will immediately recess to a closed Executive
Session and reconvene to the Regular Board Meeting at 6:00 p.m. at Brigadoon Elementary, 3601 SW
336th Street, Federal Way, WA 98023.
Board of Directors: President Dr. Jennifer Jones, Vice President & Legislative Liaison Luckisha Phillips,
Trudy Davis, Quentin Morris, Joan Marie Murphy
Scholar Representatives: Joseph Coker, King Jackson, Alinea Kirshenbaum
Following current Washington State guidelines for board meetings, this meeting will be held in person
with an option to attend remotely.
ATTENDING THE BOARD MEETING IN PERSON:
The board meeting will be held at Brigadoon Elementary, 3601 SW 336th Street, Federal Way, WA
98023.

https://fwps.zoom.us/j/91575987832
Or Telephone: +1 253 205 0468 US, +1 253 215 8782 US (Tacoma)
