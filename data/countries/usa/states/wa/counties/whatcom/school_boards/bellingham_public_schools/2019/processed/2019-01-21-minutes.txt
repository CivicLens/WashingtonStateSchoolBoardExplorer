INFORMATIONAL ITEMS/DISCUSSION
The board members and Dr. Baker engaged in general discussion relating to school
district initiatives. Summary of discussion items:
• Communications
o Board reviewed recommendations from the board committee and discussed
potential next steps.
• Superintendent Mid-Year Report and Evaluation
o Superintendent gave an update on some major work priorities within the
district and received feedback from the Board.
• Board Assessments of Reports
o Board discussed how they evaluate superintendent through EL and Ends
reports and how to capture initial comments in the minutes, followed by the
board president refining into a final report.
• Policy Governance Discussion
o EL 1 – The board discussed whether to modify an EL-1 or not.
o Equity Policy – The Board discussed whether to engage in the development
of equity policy and potential next steps.
• Student Voice
o The Board discussed whether they might want to increase student voice in any
way.
• Tour of Sehome HS
o The board took a tour of the new Sehome High School.
No decisions were made.
