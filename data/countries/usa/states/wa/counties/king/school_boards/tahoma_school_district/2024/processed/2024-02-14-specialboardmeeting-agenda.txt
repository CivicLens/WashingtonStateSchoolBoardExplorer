Special Board Meeting
The Board will adjourn immediately into Executive Session at 7:00 a.m. after the Special Meeting is
called to order at 7:00 a.m. at the Central Services Center (25720 Maple Valley Black Diamond Rd. SE,
Maple Valley, WA.) The Board will adjourn into open session at 6:00 p.m. A virtual option will be available
for the open session via Zoom by clicking on the 'Video' link above.
.
A video/audio recording of the meeting will be available after the meeting by clicking on the 'Video' link
above.
.
Individuals with disabilities who may need a modification to participate in a meeting should contact the
Superintendent's Office no later than three days before a regular meeting and as soon as possible in
advance of a special meeting so that arrangements for the modifications can be made.

Public Content
The Board will adjourn into Executive Session (according to RCW 42.30.110) at 7:00 a.m. to discuss one or
more of the following:
To evaluate the qualifications of applicants for District employment or to review the performance of a
District employee.
To receive and/or evaluate a complaint against a District officer or employee.
To discuss plans, strategy or proposals made in collective bargaining, grievance or mediation
proceedings.
To consider the selection or acquisition of real estate.
To consider the price at which real estate will be offered for sale/lease.
To review negotiations on the performance of a District-bid contract.
To discuss litigation, potential litigation, or legal risks of a proposed action with District legal counsel.
To consider information related to data security breach disclosure.
To consider a quasi-judicial matter, such as appeals of student discipline.
To evaluate the qualifications of a candidate for appointment to the Board.
The Board will adjourn into open session at 6:00 p.m.
