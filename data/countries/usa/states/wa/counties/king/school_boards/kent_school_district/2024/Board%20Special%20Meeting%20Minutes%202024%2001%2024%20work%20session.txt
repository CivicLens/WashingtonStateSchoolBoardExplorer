SPECIAL MEETING
The Board of Directors of Kent School District No. 415 met in a special meeting on Wednesday,
January 24, 2024 at 4:00 p.m. for approximately two hours. In attendance: President Margel,
Awale Farah, Tim Clark, Donald Cook, and Andy Song. The purpose of the special meeting
was a work session to discuss a Financial Status Update with Mr. Ben Rarick, Associate
Superintendent of Finance and Board Training for Open Public Meetings Act (OPMA) with Mr.
Paul Brachvogel, General Counsel. Also in attendance: Superintendent Israel Vela. Click here
to view the meeting.

President Margel called the special meeting to order at 4:01 p.m.
A brief recess took place at 5:12 p.m.
The special meeting resumed at 5:15 p.m.

President Margel adjourned the meeting at 5:51 p.m.
