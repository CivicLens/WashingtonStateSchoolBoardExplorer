1. Call to Order, Welcome, Pledge of Allegiance and Roll Call
Meeting called to order at 7:01 P.M. Board Directors in attendance were CJ Costanti, David Vis, Tonya
Hickman, Cindy Petersen, Ken Owsley.
2. Lynden/Ferndale Masonic Lodge Educator of the Year Award – Tim Metz
Representatives of the Lynden/Ferndale Masonic Lodge, Dick Decima and Troy Luginbill, presented the
Educator of the Year Award to Tim Metz, Executive Director of Special Programs for the Lynden School
District. Superintendent, David VanderYacht, recognized Tim for his work in returning, and sustaining inperson learning for the Lynden School District students. Tim has also been instrumental in overseeing
the Social Emotional Learning for wellness and culturally responsive environment programs.
