1. Inclusionary Practices Program Update
Deputy Superintendents Matt Gillingham and Scott Beebe, and Associate Superintendents Dr. Jen Rose and Dale Cote, presented an update on inclusionary practices in Lake Washington School District. The Board has previously heard inclusionary practices updates in study sessions. The Board was given the opportunity to ask questions to the district presenters.
  G. Public and Community Affairs
1. Update on Public and Community Affairs
Associate Superintendent Barbara Posthumus provided a legislative update to the Board. We’re in the third week of the legislative session. January 30 is the cut-off for bills to make it out of each house for consideration. LWSD staff have testified on nine bills so far this session. They will testify on additional bills this week. Board members and staff will meet with
Congresswoman DelBene tomorrow. District staff will participate in WASA legislative meetings. The district was recently notified we will receive an EPA grant to purchase two electric school buses.
  H. Closing Items
1. School Visits

  2. Committee Updates
Director Guthrie shared an update on the Reading Advisory Committee.
Director Yocum shared updates from two strategic planning committees, including the group working on the student profile.

Vice President Laliberte also shared an update on the Lake Washington Schools Foundation Board.
President Choi gave an update on the K-8 Highly Capable Program Review. She also shared an update on the High School Day Evaluation Committee. Director Choi thanked all of our community members who volunteered to participate in these committees, and acknowledged the difficult tasks that each committee is undertaking.
