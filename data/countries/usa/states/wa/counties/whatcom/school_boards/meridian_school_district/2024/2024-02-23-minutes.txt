MERIDIAN SCHOOL DISTRICT #505
SPECIAL SESSION MEETING
MEETING HELD IN PERSON
February 23, 2024

SPECIAL MEETING
The special meeting was called to order by Board President Steve Cowin at 3:30 p.m.
Board members present at the meeting were Steve Corwin, Jarrod Karuza, Marty Gray, Jana Haugen, and
Michelle Simmons.
A motion to adopt the agenda was made by Jana Haugen and seconded by Jarrod Karuza. The motion
passed 5-0.
DISCUSSION AND POSSIBLE ACTION
Discussion and Possible Action Opposition to ESB 5462 and ESHB 2331
The Board engaged in a conversation regarding opposition to proposed ESB 5462 and ESHB 2331. Dr.
Everett shared information that Representative Timmons shared with him about the two bills. The Board
engaged in a discussion and decided to draft a letter of opposition to Senator Shewmake, Representative
Timmons, and Representative Rule instead of moving forward with a resolution. A special session meeting
was scheduled for 7:00 am on Monday, February 26, 2024, to continue the discussion and possibly move
forward with action. Director Marty Gray requested to abstain from attending the February 26, 2024
Special Session Meeting and requested that his name not be attached to a letter of opposition.
ADJOURNMENT
There being no further business, the Board meeting was adjourned at 4:12 pm. by Board President Steve
Corwin.
