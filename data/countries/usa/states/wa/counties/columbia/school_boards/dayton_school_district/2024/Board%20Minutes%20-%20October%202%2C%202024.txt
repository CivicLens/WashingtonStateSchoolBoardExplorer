School Board Work Session
October 2, 2024
1. MEETING OPENING
A. Call to Order
Board chair McCowen called the meeting to order at 6:30 p.m. Board members present were Grant Griffen, Jeff McCowen, Zac Fabian,
Ryan Paulson and Aneesha Dieu. Also present Superintendent Jeremy Wheatley and Business Manager Jana Eaton.
B. Flag Salute
Board chair McCowen led the flag salute.
D. Additions or Changes to the Agenda
Board chair McCowen discussed getting information to Director Dieu for the upcoming WSSDA meeting she will be attending regarding
setting legislative priorities. Ms. Dieu is asking for input from the other board members to bring forward to WSSDA.
3. WORK SESSION TOPICS
A. TOPIC 1 - Introduction to Policy Governance - Rick Maloney
Rick Maloney reviewed a series of handouts based on Policy Governance. Mr. Malony reviewed the history of policy governance and
some of the districts in the state who have adopted Policy Governance or its derivatives. Mr. Maloney covered management challenges,
the roles of the school board, the regulations placed on the school board by the State (RCW's and WAC's), the superintendent's role
and challenges to the board. Mr. Maloney state the role of the school board is as follows: The Board serves its community; The Board
governs via policy; The Board assigns responsibility; The Board delegates authority; The Board Assures accountability. More
information on the Policy Governance Model can be found at www.governforimpact.org or www.policy.goverance.com
B. Topic 2 - WSSDA - Board Self - Evaluation
Superintendent Wheatley will send the board a link to a board self -assessment they can complete over the weekend. WSSDA will
compile the information and send the report to Mr. Wheatley.
6. ADJOURNMENT
Board member McCowen adjourned the meeting at 8:12 p.m. The next meeting will be a regular board meeting on October 16, 2024 at
6:30 p.m. in the high school media center and via Zoom.
Respectfully Submitted,
Jeremy Wheatley, Secretary to the Board of Directors
