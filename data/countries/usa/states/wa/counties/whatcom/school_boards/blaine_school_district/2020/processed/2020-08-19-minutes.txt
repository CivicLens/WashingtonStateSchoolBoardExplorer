  Blaine School District No. 503
  Special Board Meeting: Executive Session
Wednesday, August 19, 2020
  Minutes
  1. Special Board Meeting
A. Call to Order
Due to restrictions resulting from the COVID-19 pandemic emergency, this meeting was conducted with remote director participation.
President Gibson called the meeting to order at 6:00 p.m.
Director Nunamaker was unable to attend and was excused.
All other directors were in attendance.
     Dougal Thomas, Joan Lotze, Charles E Gibson, Laura McKinney
  2. Executive Session
A. Executive Session
At 6:00 p.m., it was announced that the board will move into executive session for the purpose of considering the position to be taken by the district in collective bargaining. The executive session was anticipated to last for up to ninety (90) minutes. No action was anticipated following the executive session.

At 7:29 p.m., it was announced that the board's executive session would be extended for an additional fifteen (15) minutes.
  3. Reconvene and Adjourn
A. Reconvene and Adjourn
At 7:40 p.m. the executive session ended, and the meeting reconvened into regular session. There being no further business, the meeting was adjourned by a unanimous vote.
Motion by Laura McKinney, second by Joan Lotze.
Final Resolution: Motion Carries 5-0
Yea: Dougal Thomas, Joan Lotze, Charles E Gibson, Laura McKinney
