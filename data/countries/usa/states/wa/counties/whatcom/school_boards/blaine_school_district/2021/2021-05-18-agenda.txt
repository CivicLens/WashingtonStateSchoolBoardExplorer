Tuesday, May 18, 2021
Special Board Meeting
The purpose of this special meeting will be to appoint a President Pro Tem, who will assume the role of
meeting facilitator in the event that both the President and Vice President are absent from the Regular
Board Meeting on Monday, May 24, 2021. Pursuant to extended proclamations from the Washington
State Governor related to the COVID-19 pandemic emergency, participation in this meeting will take
place remotely. Live public observation of this meeting will be available online. Click on the "Video" link
above to access this meeting as an observer. (Meeting Passcode: 002003)
