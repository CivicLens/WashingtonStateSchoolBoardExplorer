The open Director Position was announced

Screening 
Policy states that if there are more than five applicants, the Board will select for interviews the five whose prior experience and expressed interest suggest that they will be most able to contribute to the Board’s deliberations as well as effectively represent a large segment of the community 
Interviews will take place in a meeting open to the public 
The board will appoint the nominee who in the judgement of at least three members of the Board is most likely to contribute to the growth and development of the district’s educational programs and operations
