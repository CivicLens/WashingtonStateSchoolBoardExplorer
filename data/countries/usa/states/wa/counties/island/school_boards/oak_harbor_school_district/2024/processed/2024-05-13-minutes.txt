Call to Order

A regular meeting of the Board of Directors was held on Monday, May 13, 2024, in the
Administrative Service Center Board Room and was available for live streaming.
President Lynn Goebel called the meeting to order at 6:02 p.m. and led the flag salute.

Roll Call

Board members Lynn Goebel, Nicole Tesch, Sharon Jensen, and Josh MacLean were
present. Jessica Aws was present virtually. Student board representatives Josiah Villano
and Maggie Litvachuk were present.

Adoption of Agenda

The agenda was adopted by unanimous consent.

Student Board
Representative
Interviews

Six Oak Harbor High School sophomores were interviewed by the board for the position
of Student Board Representative for the 2024-2025 and 2025-2026 school years.

Executive Session

At 6:50 p.m., the board entered Executive Session to consider the candidates for
Student Board Representative. President Goebel announced that the board would
return to Regular Session at 7:10 p.m.
The board returned to Regular Session at 7:10 p.m.

Selection of
Student Board
Representative

Director MacLean nominated Allena Locklear to be selected for Student Board
Representative for the 2024-2025 and 2025-2026 school years. Director Tesch seconded
the motion. The motion passed unanimously.

Consent Agenda

Director Tesch moved to approve the consent agenda. Director MacLean seconded the
motion. The motion passed unanimously.

Student
Representative
Report

Student Representative Josiah Villano spoke about spring athletics, highlighting the girls
tennis team accomplishments. He shared about upcoming music events at Oak Harbor
Intermediate School and Oak Harbor High School. Student Representative Maggie
Litvachuk commented on the high school wrestlers who placed at State and the senior
track athlete who broke a 30-year school record for the 200-yard dash. She mentioned
music events happening this month throughout the district.

Superintendent
Report

Superintendent Michelle Kuss-Cybula congratulated Principal Nate Salisbury and Oak
Harbor High School for achieving accreditation. Dr. Kuss-Cybula recently participated in
the Soroptimist scholarship process and awards event, and she attended an award
ceremony for Navy Chief William Thiel. She spoke about the strategic planning tagline
and board mission statement. She provided updates on testing data, fiscal
management, budget reductions, construction, and the work of the Capital Facilities
Advisory Committee. She pointed to the recent release of the 2024-2025 community
mailer that was sent to the greater Oak Harbor community.

Board Comments

Director Tesch mentioned a fundraiser for the Oak Harbor Intermediate School Honors
Society, testing season for students, and a 3-by-3 basketball tournament. Director
Jensen reported that the WIAA did not end up sanctioning badminton and girls flag
football as new WIAA sports. One of the amendments that passed reduced the number
of practices for most sports. President Goebel spoke about district sports and music
events she attended and highlighted the upcoming Empty Bowls event to support the

Upcoming Meetings

Regular meetings are scheduled for Tuesday, May 28, and Monday, June 10, 2024, at
6:00 p.m. in the Administrative Service Center Board Room. Instructional Reviews are
scheduled for Thursday, June 6, 2024, at 8:00 a.m. in the ASC Board Room. The annual
Retirement Social is scheduled for Thursday, June 6, 2024, at 4:30 p.m. in the ASC Board
Room. Graduation will take place on Saturday, June 15, 2024, at 1:00 p.m. at Oak
Harbor High School.

Adjourn

The meeting adjourned at 7:46 p.m.
