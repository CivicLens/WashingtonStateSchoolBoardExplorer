Finance and Operations
Budget Update - April
The Board reviewed information on the budget for April containing a summary of financial status,
enrollment, general fund comparative, and revenues and expenditures.
Budget Update – 2020-2021 School Year
Patti Fairbanks provided information on the current outlook of the 2020-2021 school year budget. Patti
informed the Board of enrollment projections, revenue, levy information, state funded programs, and
grant allocation funding. Patti spoke about the Coronavirus Aid, Relief, and Economic Security (CARES)
Act and how we will continue to analyze for budgeting.

7. Board Workshop
 Focus on Equality
Jim Frey addressed the focus on equality and the School District and Board of Directors commitment to
support, teach, and reinforce the value of diversity as a community and ensure every student feels safe
and is valued as a member of our community. Principals and Administration spoke about how to engage
students in the schools.


Re-Opening School in 2020-2021
The Board discussed the re-engagement of students and staff in an in-person school setting and the
continued advisement from our local health department that will provide guidelines. In the coming
months, based on guidelines, the Lynden School District will adjust planning as needed and communicate
with the community.

8. Action Items
 Resolution No. 07-20 – WIAA Membership
The Board reviewed this resolution approving membership with the WIAA for the 2020-2021 school year.
Brian Johnson motioned to approve Resolution No. 07-20 seconded by David Vis. Motion carried by voice vote.
