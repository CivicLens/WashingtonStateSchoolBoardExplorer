Resolution

Meeting :

Dec 8, 2021 - Regular Board Meeting via

Type :

Action

Public Content
Staff Contact: Simone Sangster, Assistant Superintendent of Finance
Explanation of Issue:

Following a thorough and detailed review of current facilities and our greatest facility needs, the Facilities Planning Task Force made a
recommendation to the superintendent to request bond funds for buildings and improvements. The Facilities Planning Task Force was
comprised of 33 members representing staff, students, community members, parents, alumni and representatives from the City of
Bellingham, and it met throughout the fall of 2021. The task force recommendation is reflected in the attached Resolution 25-21. This
resolution requests that the Whatcom County Auditor place a ballot proposition for the issuance of general obligation bonds as part of
the February 8, 2022 special election.
