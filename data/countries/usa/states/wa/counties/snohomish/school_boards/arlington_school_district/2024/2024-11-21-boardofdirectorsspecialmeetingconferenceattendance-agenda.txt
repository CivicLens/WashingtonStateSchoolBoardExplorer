ARLINGTON SCHOOL DISTRICT NO. 16
SNOHOMISH COUNTY, WASHINGTON
NOTICE OF SPECIAL MEETING
CONFERENCE ATTENDANCE
NOTICE is hereby given that the Board of Directors of Arlington School District No. 16, Snohomish County, Washington (the
District) will hold a Special Board Meeting on Thursday through Saturday, November 21 through 23, 2024, starting at 9:00 am
and at times designated by the agenda thereafter, for the purpose of attending the 2024 Washington School Directors
Association Annual Conference at the Spokane Convention Center, 334 W Spokane Falls Blvd, Spokane, Washington.
This special meeting is for the purpose of attending the 2024 Washington School Directors Association Annual Conference at the
Spokane Convention Center, 334 W Spokane Falls Blvd, Spokane, Washington. Public comment will not be taken during this
meeting, and no action will be taken.

General comments to the Board can always be submitted in writing:
through the Contact the Board link on the District website, under Administration/School Board; or
