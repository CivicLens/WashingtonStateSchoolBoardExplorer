Blaine School District No. 503
  Special Board Meeting
Tuesday, May 18, 2021
  Minutes
  1. Special Board Meeting
A. Call to Order
Due to restrictions resulting from the COVID-19 pandemic emergency, this meeting was conducted with remote director participation. There was no in-person participation.
President Gibson called the meeting to order at 7:37 a.m.
All Directors were in attendance.
     Todd Nunamaker, Dougal Thomas, Joan Lotze, Charles E Gibson, Laura McKinney
  2. Action Items
A. President Pro Tem (May 24, 2021 Meeting)
Director McKinney nominated Dougal Thomas to facilitate the Regular Board Meeting on May 24 as President Pro Tem in the event that both the President and Vice President are absent.
The nomination was seconded by Director Lotze.
There were no further nominations.
Roll call vote: Directors Lotze, McKinney, Nunamaker, Gibson, and Thomas voted in favor of election.
  3. Adjourn
A. Adjourn
There being no further business, the meeting was adjourned at 7:42am by a unanimous vote.
Motion by Laura McKinney, second by Todd Nunamaker.
Final Resolution: Motion Carries 5-0
Yea: Todd Nunamaker, Dougal Thomas, Joan Lotze, Charles E Gibson, Laura McKinney
