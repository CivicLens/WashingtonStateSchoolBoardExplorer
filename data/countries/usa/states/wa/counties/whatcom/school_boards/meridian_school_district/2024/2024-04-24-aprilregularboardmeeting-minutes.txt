MERIDIAN SCHOOL DISTRICT #505
REGULAR SCHOOL BOARD MEETING
MEETING HELD IN PERSON & VIA ZOOM
April 24, 2024

REGULAR MEETING
The regular meeting was called to order by Board President Steve Cowin at 6:00 p.m.
Board directors present at the meeting were Steve Corwin, Jarrod Karuza, Marty Gray, Jana Haugen, and
Michelle Simmons.
Student Board Representatives Kennedy Brzozowski and Morgan Adams were absent from the meeting.
Assistant Superintendent Kurt Harvill was present at the meeting in the absence of Superintendent Dr.
James Everett.
The pledge of allegiance was recited.
A motion to adopt the agenda was made by Jana Haugen and seconded by Michelle Simmons. The
motion passed 5-0.
