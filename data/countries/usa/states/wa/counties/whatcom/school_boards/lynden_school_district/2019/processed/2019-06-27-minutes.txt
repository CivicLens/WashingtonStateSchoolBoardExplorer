4. Information from the Audience
None at this meeting.
5. Finance and Operations
 Budget Update – May
The Board reviewed information from the budget for May containing a summary of financial status,
enrollment, general fund comparative, and revenues and expenditures. Patti will provide an update on ASB
revenues and expenditures. Patti informed the Board of transportation expenses of purchasing 3 busses.
6. Adjourn Meeting to go into Board Work Session
Meeting adjourned at 6:38 P.M.

7. Board Work Session
 Secondary School Improvement Plan (SIP)
Lynden Academy Principal, Ellie Meenk, Lynden High School Principal, Ian Freeman and Assistant Principal,
Michelle Nielson, Lynden Middle School Principal, Molly Mitchell-Mumma and Assistant Principal, Cyndi
Selcho, Rex Frasier, Dean of Students, informed the Board about the progress of expected learning targets
in their school, how they respond and provide intervention when students are not making progress, and
how their staff is working to meet the social emotional needs of students as part of a Multi-tiered System
of Support (MTSS). The Board thanked the Principals for the hard work they do on behalf of their students
and families.


Alternative Learning Education (ALE) Report
Jim Frey shared the 4 elements of an ALE program that students are enrolled. The largest program is
Lynden Academy, followed by BTC Impact Reengagement, and Graduation Alliance. Jim explained how
important these programs are, as they provide an alternative route to school success.



Budget Update 2019-2020
Patti Fairbanks provided an update on the budget development for 2019-2020. Patti explained the work
being done to increase revenues and reduce expenditures. Jim Frey spoke about the options of increasing
the levy collection amount that was approved in 2016.

8. Executive Session (30 minutes)
Executive Session began at 8:45 P.M.

10. Open Meeting
Meeting Opened at 8:50 P.M.
 Superintendent Contract
David Vis motioned to approve the Contract seconded by CJ Costanti. Motion carried by voice vote.

Meeting adjourned at 8:52 P.M.
