School Board Visit - Wade King Elementary

school year. The purpose of these visits is to learn how each school is delivering upon The Bellingham
Promise. The directors will visit Wade King Elementary, 2155 Yew Street, Bellingham, at 7:30 a.m.,
Tuesday, June 4, 2019.
