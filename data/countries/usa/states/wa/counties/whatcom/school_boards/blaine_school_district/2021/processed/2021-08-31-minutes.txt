Blaine School District No. 503
  Special Board Meeting:Executive Session
Tuesday, August 31, 2021
  Minutes
  1. Special Board Meeting
A. Call to Order
In accordance with guidelines related to the COVID-19 pandemic emergency, this meeting was conducted with both virtual and in-person components. President Gibson called the meeting to order at 6:00 p.m.
  All Directors were in attendance. Director Lotze was out of the area and attended remotely.
     Todd Nunamaker, Dougal Thomas, Joan Lotze, Charles E Gibson, Laura McKinney
  2. Executive Session
A. Executive Session
President Gibson announced that the board will move into executive session for the purpose of reviewing the performance of a public employee. The executive session was anticipated to last for up to two (2) hours, and no action would be taken by the board after the executive session.

There was an announcement at 7:53 p.m. that the executive session would be extended for one (1) hour.

There was an announcement at 8:58 p.m. that the executive session would be extended for one (1) hour.

Director Lotze left the meeting at 9:43 p.m.
  3. Reconvene and Adjourn
A. Reconvene and Adjourn
At 9:45 p.m., the executive session ended and the board reconvened in regular session. There being no further business, the directors still present voted to adjourn the meeting.
Motion by Laura McKinney, second by Dougal Thomas
Final Resolution: Motion Carries 4-0, 1 not present at vote
Yea: Todd Nunamaker, Dougal Thomas, Charles E Gibson, Laura McKinney
Not Present at Vote: Joan Lotze
