November 20, 2019
School Board Meeting
Minutes
516 Main Street
6:30 P.M.
1. Call to Order, Welcome, Pledge of Allegiance and Roll Call
Meeting called to order at 6:30 P.M. Board members in attendance were Steve Jilk, Brian Johnson, Kelli Kettels, CJ
Costanti, and David Vis. Student Directors Ruby VanderHaak and Riley Anderson were absent and excused.
2. Main Street Campus Bidding
The School Board discussed the bid proposals for the Main Street Campus renovation. It was decided that
further discussion will take place at a December 5th Special Meeting for a possible determination of
construction contract award.

Meeting adjourned at 7:30 P.M.
