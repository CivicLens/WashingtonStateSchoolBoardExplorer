8. Work Session
Work session was called to order at 8:02pm. Board of Directors in attendance were David Vis,
Cindy Petersen, Tonya Hickman, Ken Owsley and CJ Costanti. Also in attendance were
Superintendent David VanderYacht and Student Director Cooper Graddon. Student Director
Aislinn Nilsen was absent and excused. Members of the Facilities Planning Committee were also
in attendance.

The work session began with an introduction of the Facilities Planning Committee members and
an explanation of the purpose of the work session, which was to provide an opportunity for the
board to engage with members of the Capital Facilities Planning Committee.
Superintendent VanderYacht distributed a summary report to the group and made a short
presentation regarding the findings, recommendations, and next steps. Discussion was held on
topics including current capacity to support enrollment, building conditions, and the ability of
current facilities to support the education program and strategic priorities of the district. It
concluded with a Q & A session and discussion among directors and committee members.
