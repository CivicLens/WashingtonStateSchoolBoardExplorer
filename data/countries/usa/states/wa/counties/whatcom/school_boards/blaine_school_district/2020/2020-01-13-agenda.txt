A. Policy 3211 Gender-Inclusive Schools: First
Reading

Meeting :

Jan 13, 2020 - Special Board Meeting

Category :

2. Action Items

Type :

Action

Recommended Action :

To approve "First Reading" of Policy 3211 GenderInclusive Schools

Public Content
The board is asked to approve "First Reading" of Policy 3211 Gender-Inclusive Schools. This new policy
is being adopted in accordance with WSSDA recommendations, as required by Senate Bill (SB) 5689.
The procedure does not require formal approval but is provided for additional review and information.
Language in this procedure will replace the "Transgender Students" section currently located in the
Nondiscrimination procedure (3210P). A separate markup of updates to this language is also attached for
reference.
The board will be asked to approve "Second Reading" and adoption of this policy at the January 27, 2020
meeting.

3. Executive Session
Subject :

A. Executive Session

Meeting :

Jan 13, 2020 - Special Board Meeting

Category :

3. Executive Session

Type :

Discussion

Public Content
As per legal guidelines, the board may choose to recess into executive session to consider employment
or dismissal of personnel, to review the performance of a public employee, to consult with legal counsel,
to consider the position to be taken in collective bargaining, or to consider acquisition or sale of real
estate.
Prior to the start of executive session, the President will announce the purpose and length of the
session.
At the close of the executive session, the Directors will reconvene in regular session at which point
they may take formal action or the meeting will be adjourned.
President Gibson will announce that the board will move into executive session for the purpose of
reviewing the performance of a public employee. The executive session is anticipated to last for sixty (60)
minutes, and no action is scheduled to be taken after the executive session. Dr. Larry Francois,
Superintendent of NWESD 189, will serve as facilitator of the executive session.
