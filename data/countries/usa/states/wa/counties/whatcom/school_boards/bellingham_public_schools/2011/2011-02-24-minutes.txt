BELLINGHAM PUBLIC SCHOOLS
Bellingham, Washington
MINUTES OF THE REGULAR MEETING
OF THE
BELLINGHAM SCHOOL DISTRICT
BOARD OF DIRECTORS
February 24, 2011
The Bellingham School Board of Directors met in regular session February 24, 2011 in the
Board Room of the District Office, 1306 Dupont Street, Bellingham, Washington.
OPENING ITEMS
At 6:30 p.m. President Ann Whitmyer called the regular meeting to order.
Roll Call
Present: Directors Kelly Bashaw, Steve Smith, Scott Stockburger, Ken Gass, President Ann
Whitmyer, Superintendent Greg Baker and recorder Nancy Merry.
CONSENT AGENDA ITEMS
Ken Gass moved and Scott Stockburger seconded approval of the consent agenda. The motion
carried unanimously.
Approval of Minutes
Action on the minutes of February 10, 2011 School Board Meeting.
Approval of Invoice
Action on invoice submitted by Dr. Ken Gass for purchase of books.
Out-of-State Travel
a) Two members of the Categorical Programs and Support Services Department to attend
the Scholastic Math Leadership Summit in Austin, Texas on March 2–4, 2011.
b) Bellingham High School science teacher to attend the National Science Teachers
Association meeting in San Francisco, California, March 10–13, 2011.
c) Three members of the Network Services Department to attend the Zangle National Users
Group annual conference in Palm Springs, California, May 2–6, 2011.
d) One member of the Network Services Department to attend the Northwest Council for
Computer Education (NCCE) conference in Portland, Oregon, March 1–5, 2011.
e) Shuksan Middle School assistant principal to attend the Standards and Assessment annual
conference in Scottsdale, Arizona, April 27–29, 2011.
f) Three science teachers to attend the National Science Teachers Association annual
meeting in San Francisco, California, March 10–13, 2011.
