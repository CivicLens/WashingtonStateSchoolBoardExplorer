Friday, November 17, 2023
Special Meeting, 4:40 p.m.
The Board of Directors will attend the Washington State School Directors Association (WSSDA) Annual
Conference from Thursday, Nov. 16 to Saturday, Nov. 18, 2023 at the Hyatt Regency in Bellevue,
Washington.
While in Bellevue, the board will hold a special meeting on Friday, Nov. 17 at 4:40 p.m. in the Executive
Boardroom of the Hyatt Regency, 900 Bellevue Way NW, Bellevue, WA. The purpose of the meeting is to
discuss effective board governance.
This is not a regular school board meeting and will not include public comment time.
For more information, call 360-676-6501.
