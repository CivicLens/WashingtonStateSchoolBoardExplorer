Blaine School District No. 503
  Special Board Meeting:Executive Session
Monday, May 23, 2022
  Minutes
  1. Special Board Meeting
A. Call to Order
In accordance with guidelines related to the COVID-19 pandemic emergency, this meeting was conducted with both virtual and in-person components. President Thomas called the meeting to order, in the district boardroom, at 6:00 p.m. All Directors were in attendance.
     Todd Nunamaker, Dougal Thomas, Erika Creydt, Charles E Gibson, Don Leu
  2. Executive Session
A. Executive Session
President Thomas announced that the board would move immediately into executive session for the purpose of reviewing the performance of a public employee. The executive session was anticipated to last for no more than forty-five (45) minutes, and no action would be taken by the board during this special meeting. The directors moved to the Executive Session meeting room, and the executive session started at 6:05 p.m.
  At 6:45 p.m., it was announced that the executive session would be extended for an additional 10 minutes.
  3. Reconvene and Adjourn
A. Reconvene and Adjourn
At 6:55 p.m., the directors returned to the boardroom and there was a short break before meeting reconvened in regular session. There being no further business, the meeting was adjourned by consensus of the directors at 7:00 p.m.
