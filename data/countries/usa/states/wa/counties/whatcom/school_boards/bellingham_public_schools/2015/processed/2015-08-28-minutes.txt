DRAFT MINUTES OF THE SPECIAL SESSION OF THE
BELLINGHAM SCHOOL DISTRICT BOARD OF DIRECTORS
August 28, 2015
OPENING ITEMS
Meeting Date and Location
August 28, 2015 – Chuckanut Health Foundation – 1500 Cornwell Ave., Ste. 201, Bellingham,
Washington
Call to Order/Roll Call
Ms. Bashaw called the meeting to order at 2 p.m. Present: Directors Kelly Bashaw (President),
Douglas Benjamin, Kenneth Gass, Camille Hackler, Steven Smith, and Superintendent Greg
Baker. Members of the Executive Team joined the directors for a portion of the meeting.
INFORMATIONAL ITEMS/DISCUSSION
Policy Governance Review/Discussion
 B/SR 3.1.1 – Board members reviewed the current Monitoring Response
Document and considered revisions. This will be an agenda item at the Sept. 17
regular meeting.
GP-7.1 - 2015-16 Annual Calendar – General review. The following topics were selected for
linkages and study sessions:
 Linkages:
 Connecting with the community’s pediatrics and family practice
health professionals
 Connecting with district partners in higher education
 Connecting with the community’s behavioral health practitioners
 Study Sessions:
 Educational technology/1:1 initiatives/levy planning
 A review of topics related to college-bound students and college
preparedness
 Balanced calendar
2015-16 Adoptions of Schools by Board Members and 2015-16 School Board Visits
 The directors reviewed the proposed schedules, which will be included on the annual
calendar to be adopted at the Sept. 17 regular meeting.
Other Discussion Items
 The directors briefly discussed a variety of other topics, including professional
development, new board member mentoring, and the Boards of Distinction process.

CLOSING ITEMS
3.1
Adjournment
 The meeting adjourned at 4:55 p.m.
