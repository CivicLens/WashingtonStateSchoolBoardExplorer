OPENING ITEMS
Meeting Date and Location
February 19, 2016 – WECU Education Center, 511 East Holly St., Bellingham, WA
Call to Order/Roll Call
Board President Camille Diaz Hackler called the meeting to order at 2:30 p.m. Present: Directors
Kelly Bashaw, Douglas Benjamin, Camille Diaz Hackler, Quenby Peterson, Steven Smith, and
Superintendent Greg Baker.
INFORMATIONAL ITEMS/DISCUSSION
Policy Governance Review/Discussion
 Ends 3 – Outcomes Focused on Character and Action: General discussion on how the
board might measure these outcomes.
Other Discussion Items
 School Board Handbook: The board reviewed the draft handbook and gave feedback on
improvements/refinements. It was agreed to review the handbook each year during the winter
retreat. Mr. Benjamin will take the lead for the time being.
 Community Communications:
o Discussed how the board responds if a concern is shared with the board; i.e. through
Listening Post.
o Discussed how the board might communicate with the public about their work,
specifically in terms of long-term forward thinking. Next steps: review with
Executive Director Tanya Rowe options/story lines for use of the column in the
newsletter that is distributed quarterly to the community.
o Discussed how the board might react in a community forum (i.e. linkage) if an
inappropriate comment is made by an attendee.
 Superintendent Informal Mid-Year Update and Evaluation: General discussion.
 Board Budget Report: Discussed the report from staff and how frequently it should be
reviewed.
