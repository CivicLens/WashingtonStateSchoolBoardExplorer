or to review the performance of a public employee. However, subject to RCW
42.30.140(4), discussion by a governing body of salaries, wages, and other conditions

Report
VISD 2024-25 Budget Planning
Kim Mayer, Amy Sassara, Dr. Slade McSheehy presented VISD 2024-25 Budget
Planning documents.
Dr. Slade McSheehy presented documents and highlighted some information on the
reports that included VISD student calendar, Board Budget Assumption Priorities,
Organization Chart, Enrollment Projections, VISD Budget Shortfall FAQ. Final budget
documents will be presented in May 2024. Reviewed some of the reduction challenges
and goals and future discussion opportunities with the board and community.
Amy Sassara commented on staffing budget process. Staff adjustments will be ongoing
in the coming months and board will be presented with updated information at the April
18th Special Board Meeting.

Kim Mayer commented on the process of reviewing district MSOCS and program with
building leaders and staff heads. Revenue Budget Tool should be provided by WA State
by mid-April and that will give actual state funding numbers.
Dr. Slade McSheehy projected a current 1.3 million shortfall. A reduction plan will be
provided at the April 18th Special Board Meeting. Programs and FTE will be identified
and RIF notices for staff will be presented. Invited community to reach out to Board
members for ideas, comments, questions.
