Tuesday, October 24, 2017
School Board Special Meeting
The Board of Directors will meet in special session at 6:30 p.m. at Brandywine Kitchen, 1317 Commercial
St., Bellingham, WA. The purpose of the meeting is to discuss school district initiatives, issues and
business in an unstructured manner. This is not a regular School Board meeting and will not include
public comment time.
For more information, call 360-676-6501.
