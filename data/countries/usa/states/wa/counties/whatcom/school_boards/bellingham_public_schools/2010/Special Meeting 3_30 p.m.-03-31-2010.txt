The Board of Directors of Bellingham Public Schools will meet in Special Session at 3:30 p.m.,
Wednesday, March 31, 2010 in the Board Room at the Administration Building, 1306 Dupont Street,
Bellingham, WA 98225. The meeting will begin with roll call and the Board will immediately adjourn to
Executive Session to review community, staff and committee input regarding the qualifications of the
finalists. The Board will also meet with Search Consultants Janet Barry and Brian Benzel, Hazard,
Young, Attea & Associates, Ltd. to review the qualifications of the finalists.
