Tuesday, October 3, 2017
School Board Visit - Birchwood Elementary School
The Board of Directors of Bellingham Public Schools regularly visits a different school throughout the
school year. The purpose of these visits is to learn how each school is delivering upon The Bellingham
Promise. The directors will visit Birchwood Elementary school at 7:30 a.m., Oct. 3, 2017. Birchwood
Elementary School is located at 3200 Pinewood Avenue.
