Tuesday, October 22, 2024
Special Board Meeting - Executive Session starting at 5:25 p.m. and Regular Board Meeting - 6:00 p.m.
The regularly scheduled Board Meeting of Federal Way School Board will convene at 5:25 p.m. rather
than the regularly scheduled time of 6:00 p.m. The Board will immediately recess to a closed Executive
Session and reconvene to the Regular Board Meeting at 6:00 p.m. at Sacajawea Middle School, 1101 S.
Dash Point Rd., Federal Way, WA 98003.
Board of Directors: President Dr. Jennifer Jones, Vice President & Legislative Liaison Luckisha Phillips,
Trudy Davis, Quentin Morris, Joan Marie Murphy
Following current Washington State guidelines for board meetings, this meeting will be held in-person
with an option to attend remotely.
ATTENDING THE BOARD MEETING IN-PERSON:
The board meeting will be held at Sacajawea Middle School, 1101 S. Dash Point Rd., Federal Way, WA
98003.
