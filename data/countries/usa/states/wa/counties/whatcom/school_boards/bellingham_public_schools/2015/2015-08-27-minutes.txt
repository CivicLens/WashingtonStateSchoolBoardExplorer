Superintendent's Announcements and Report:
o Dr. Baker visited each of our 22 schools on the first two days of school. He
summarized his observations, noting that the positive impact of increased
staffing can be felt district-wide.
o On the first day of school, Alderwood Elementary School introduced the
universal breakfast and lunch program, which means all students at the school
are eligible for free breakfast and lunch, if they choose to participate,
regardless of income status.
o Silver Beach Elementary School has joined Alderwood and Birchwood in
having students take recess before lunch. As a result, students are exhibiting
calmer behavior and bigger appetites at lunchtime.
o Students and staff at Lowell Elementary School are excited about the
renovations to their building. A community celebration is planned for Oct. 8 at
3 p.m.
o Executive Team members are meeting daily to review enrollment numbers
and working with principals to determine where additional support is needed.
o Jerry Jenkins is retiring as superintendent of Northwest Educational Service
District (ESD) 189 at the end of this year. He has been a great support to our
region. Additionally, Leann Swanson is retiring as an ESD board member.
She represents Bellingham and Meridian School Districts.
