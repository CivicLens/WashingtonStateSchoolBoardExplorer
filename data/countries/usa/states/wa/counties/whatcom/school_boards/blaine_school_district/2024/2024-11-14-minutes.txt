Blaine School District No. 503
  Special Board Meeting: Executive Session
Thursday, November 14, 2024
  Minutes
  1. Special Board Meeting
A. Call to Order
President Freeman called the meeting to order, in the district's boardroom, at 6:00 p.m.
All directors were in attendance. Director Swinburnson was delayed and joined the meeting telephonically in the executive session until his arrival in person.
     Erika Creydt, Ben Lazarus, Cliff Freeman, Ryan Swinburnson, Steve Galbraith
  2. Executive Session
A. Executive Session
President Freeman announced that the board would move into executive session for the purpose of reviewing the performance of a public employee. The executive session was anticipated to last forty-five (45) minutes, or until 6:45 p.m. Any extension of time would be announced verbally in the boardroom, and no final action will be taken by the board following the executive session. Directors Creydt, Lazarus, Freeman, and Galbraith moved to a private room for the closed session. Superintendent Dr. Christopher Granger remained in the boardroom. Director Swinburnson arrived in person at 6:17 p.m. and joined the closed session.

At 6:47 p.m., President Freeman returned to the boardroom and announced that the executive session would be extended for 30 minutes until 7:15 p.m.

At 6:19 p.m., President Freeman returned to the boardroom and announced that the executive session would be extended for 30 minutes until 7:45 p.m.

At 7:40 p.m., President Freeman returned to the boardroom and announced that the executive session would be extended for 15 minutes until 8:00 p.m. He also asked Dr. Granger for five copies of a complaint, and those copies were provided.
  3. Reconvene and Adjourn
A. Reconvene and Adjourn
At 8:00 p.m., the directors returned to the boardroom and the meeting reconvened in regular session.
There being no further business, the meeting was adjourned by a unanimous vote.
Motion by Ryan Swinburnson, second by Steve Galbraith
Final Resolution: Motion Carries 5-0
Yea: Erika Creydt, Ben Lazarus, Cliff Freeman, Ryan Swinburnson, Steve Galbraith
  DATE MINUTES APPROVED: November 25, 2024
