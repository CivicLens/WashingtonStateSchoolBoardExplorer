October 12, 2017
School Board Meeting
Minutes
Lynden High School Library
6:30 P.M.
1. Call to Order, Welcome, Pledge of Allegiance and Roll Call
Meeting called to order at 6:31 P.M. Board members in attendance were Kevin Burke, and Kelli Kettels. Board
members Brian Johnson and Steve Jilk were absent and excused. Student Directors Leslie Strandgard and Rubia
Orellana were absent and excused.
2. Open Public Hearing
Public Meeting Opened at 6:32 P.M.
Surplus of Lynden School District Property
• Sean-Jean Withrow, a Lynden community member spoke of his confidence in the School Board
Members. He requested the considerations of the Glenning Street property be held as civic property
and provided recommendations for use of the Glenning Street property.
•

Paul Harris, a Lynden community member appreciated how the School Board meeting is conducted.
Paul provided information about his concerns and expressed his ideas on how to utilize the Glenning
Street property for recreational use. Paul asked the Board to consider the neighborhood & community
needs.

•

Michael Baglioni, a Lynden Community member expressed his interest in seeing the Glenning Street
remain an open space for the community as part of the City Parks and Recreation Department.

3. Close Public Hearing
Public Hearing closed at 7:05 P.M.
4. Adjourn Meeting
Meeting adjourned at 7:45 P.M.
