Blaine School District No. 503
  Special Board Meeting: Executive Session
Thursday, December 5, 2024
  Minutes
  1. Special Board Meeting
A. Call to Order
President Freeman called the meeting to order, in the district's boardroom, at 6:00 p.m.
All directors were in attendance.
     Erika Creydt, Ben Lazarus, Cliff Freeman, Ryan Swinburnson, Steve Galbraith
  2. Executive Session
A. Executive Session
President Freeman announced that the board will move into executive session for the purpose of reviewing the performance of a public employee. The executive session is anticipated to last two (2) hours and any extension of time will be announced verbally in the boardroom. The directors moved to the superintendent's office for the closed session. Superintendent Dr. Christopher Granger participated in the executive session until he was excused at 8:47 p.m.

At 7:59 p.m., President Freeman returned to the boardroom and announced that the executive session would be extended for sixty (60) minutes until 9:00 p.m.

At 8:59 p.m., President Freeman returned to the boardroom and announced that the executive session would be extended for thirty (30) minutes until 9:30 p.m.
  3. Reconvene and Action
The directors returned to the boardroom at 9:28 p.m. and the meeting reconvened in regular session.
President Freeman called for a motion to adjourn.
Motion by Ryan Swinburnson, second by Steve Galbraith
Motion Carries 5-0
Yea: Erika Creydt, Ben Lazarus, Cliff Freeman, Ryan Swinburnson, Steve Galbraith
  Dr. Granger asked about the agenda's action item regarding his contract. President Freeman suggested it be tabled until the December 10 meeting. This meeting was reconvened to take formal action.
  A. Action on Superintendent Evaluation
The board was asked to consider action (motion) specific to acceptance of the annual performance evaluation of the superintendent as satisfactory, with concurrent intent to move forward with extension of the employment contract of the superintendent through June 30, 2028. The board was further asked to consider, under separate motion, formally designating the board president and vice-president to enter into discussion with the superintendent on employment contract parameters, with direction to the board president to bring a final contract recommendation to the full board for review and consideration of approval no later than the May 27, 2025 regular meeting.

President Freeman called for a motion to table this action item. Director Lazarus expressed concern about not being in attendance on December 10, but stated that he may be able to participate in that meeting by phone.
  The board voted to table this action item until the regular meeting on Tuesday, December 10, 2024.
Motion by Steve Galbraith, second by Ryan Swinburnson
Final Resolution: Motion Carries 5-0
Yea: Erika Creydt, Ben Lazarus, Cliff Freeman, Ryan Swinburnson, Steve Galbraith
  4. Adjourn
A. Adjourn
There being no further business, the meeting was adjourned by a unanimous vote at 9:28 p.m.
Motion by Steve Galbraith, second by Ryan Swinburnson
Final Resolution: Motion Carries 5-0
Yea: Erika Creydt, Ben Lazarus, Cliff Freeman, Ryan Swinburnson, Steve Galbraith
  DATE MINUTES APPROVED: December 10, 2024
