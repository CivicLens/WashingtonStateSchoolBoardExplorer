Washtucna School District #109
Special Board of Directors Meeting
Washtucna, WA
May 24, 2022

The special meeting of the Washtucna School Board was called to order at 4:00 p.m. by board
chair Steve Crouse. Attending in-person were board members Steve Crouse, Jeremy Jessop, Jake
Sitton and Ginger McKenzie, Superintendent Dr. Rob Clark, Staci Gloyn, Debbie Sitton, Lacii
Rundquist, and Kara Pavone.
AGENDA
Jake sitton motioned to approve the agenda. Passed 4-0.
EXECUTIVE SESSION
Jake Sitton motioned for the board to go into executive session with no action to follow pursuant
to RCW 42.30.110(1)(g) to interview candidates for the principal position, for 3 hours. Passed
4-0. Executive session began at 4:00 p.m. Executive session ended at 7:00 p.m.
ADJOURNMENT
Jeremy Jessop moved to adjourn the meeting at 7:00 p.m. Passed 4-0.
