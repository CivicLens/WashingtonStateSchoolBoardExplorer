CALL TO ORDER
The special session of the Nooksack Valley School Board of Directors was called to order by
Chairman, Steve Jones, at 7:00 p.m. on Wednesday, May 10, 2023.

DIRECTOR CANDIDATE INTERVIEWS
Steve Jones welcomed the group, gave a brief explanation of the order of events, stated that each board
candidate would be interviewed separately and that guests could stay in the room if they chose to, and
thanked each candidate for their interest and application for the open position.

EXECUTIVE SESSION
Chairman Jones announced at 7:45 p.m. that the board would move into executive session for the
purpose of discussing the appointment of a candidate. The executive session was expected to last
approximately 30 minutes, and if there is consensus, the board may take action to appoint a new
director after returning to open public meeting.
RECONVENE AND ACTION
At 8:10 the executive session ended, and the regular session resumed. Mark Olson made a motion to
appoint Ty Elmendorf to the At Large Position #1 and Halli Kimball seconded.
M.C. 4-0
ADJOURNMENT
Meeting adjourned at 8:16.
