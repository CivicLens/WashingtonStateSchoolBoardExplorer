School Board Meeting
Minutes
Lynden High School Library
6:30 P.M.
1. Call to Order, Welcome, Pledge of Allegiance and Roll Call
Meeting called to order at 6:32 P.M. Board members in attendance were Kevin Burke, Steve Jilk, Brian Johnson,
Kelli Kettels and CJ Costanti. Student Directors Ruby VanderHaak and Cynthia Torres were absent and excused.
2. Good News
•
•
•
•

The Maintenance, Grounds, Custodians and Summer Help crews did an outstanding job moving items
from LMS Main St. to LMS Line Rd. schools!
The Transportation Department has again received a superior inspection from the Washington State Patrol,
they had exceptional results.
An animal shelter was built by high school students Kevin Castaneda, Oscar de la Rosa Lopez and
Francisco Lopez with the assistance of Mr. & Mrs. Toften, ensuring a nice area for animals during
inclement weather.
City of Lynden has started working on the Jim Kaemingk Sr. Trail near Fisher Elementary for access for the
community and students, families and community.

Finance and Operations
• Budget Update 2018-2019
Patti Fairbanks informed the Board on the preliminary version of the 2018-2019 budget. Patti explained
the next steps that will be taken to provide a final version to the Board for Budget approval at a future
meeting. A decision was made to present the budget for approval at the August 16th meeting rather than
the August 9th meeting.
9. Board Workshop
None at this time.
10. Action Items
• Resolution No. 09-18 – Bus Surplus
Patti Fairbanks provided information for the Board to surplus a bus.
Steve Jilk motioned to approve Resolution No. 09-18 seconded by Brian Johnson. Motion carried by voice vote.
•

NWESD HiCap Agreement
The Board reviewed the Highly Capable (HiCap) Cooperative Program with the ESD.
Steve Jilk motioned to approve the Hi-Cap Agreement seconded by Brian Johnson. Motion carried by voice vote.
•

Instructional Materials List
The Board reviewed the list of instructional materials approved by the Instructional Materials
Committee. The Board gave consideration and expressed concern about the use of the “This Is America”
video. The Board required this item to be further reviewed by the Instructional Material Committee and
provide additional information.
Brian Johnson motioned to approve the Instructional Materials List with the exception of “This Is America” video
seconded by Kellie Kettels. Motion carried by a 3 to 2 voice vote.
•

Sale of Surplus Property Process – LMS Playfield
Jim Frey explained the process to surplus the Lynden Middle School Playfield area. The Board needs to
approve a motion to notify the public of the proposed sale for public comments at a future meeting.
Steve Jilk motioned to approve a Notification to the Public seconded by Brian Johnson. Motion carried by voice
vote
•

Fisher Waterline Agreement
The Board reviewed the Bill of Sale and Easement Agreement with the City for the Fisher waterline.
Steve Jilk motioned to approve the Bill of Sale and Easement Agreement seconded by Brian Johnson. Motion
carried by voice vote.
