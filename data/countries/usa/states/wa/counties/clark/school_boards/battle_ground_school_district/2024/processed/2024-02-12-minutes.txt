D. First Reading of Policy 6815 Advertising on District Property 
Director of Student Services Tom Adams presented the first reading of Policy 6815 Advertising on District Property to the board. He explained that this new policy formalizes practices the district has been doing for years. The policy takes into account prohibiting discrimination, freedom of speech and allows us to approve advertising consistent with the mission and policies of the district. It also recognizes the need for additional revenue sources to support our school programs.

The board members asked clarifying questions.
  The Board of Directors accept the first reading of Policy 6815 Advertising on District Property.
Student Advisory Vote: Pro: Sora Tolley, Julia Stiffler
Motion by Debbie Johnson, second by Mary Snitily.
Final Resolution: Motion Carries, 4-0.
Aye: Mary Snitily, Debbie Johnson, Ted Champine, Terry Tate
