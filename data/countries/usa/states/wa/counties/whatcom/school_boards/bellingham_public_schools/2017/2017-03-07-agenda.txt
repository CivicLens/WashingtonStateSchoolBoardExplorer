School Board Visit - Silver Beach Elementary School

school year. The purpose of the visits is to learn how each school is delivering upon The Bellingham
Promise. The directors will visit Silver Beach Elementary School at 7:30 a.m. Mar. 7, 2017. Silver Beach
Elementary School is located at 4101 Academy St.
