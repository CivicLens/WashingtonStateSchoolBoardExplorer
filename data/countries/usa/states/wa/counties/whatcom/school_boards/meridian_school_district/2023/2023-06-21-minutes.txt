MERIDIAN SCHOOL DISTRICT #505
REGULAR SCHOOL BOARD MEETING AND BUDGET HEARING
MEETING HELD IN PERSON & VIA ZOOM
June 21, 2023
REGULAR MEETING
The regular meeting was called to order by Board Chair Sara Bernardy at 7:00 p.m.
Board Chair Sara Bernardy welcomed everyone that was in attendance.
Board members present at the meeting were Sara Bernardy, Marty Gray, Craig Mitchell, and Steve Corwin.
Board member Jarrod Karuza was excused from attending.
The pledge of allegiance was recited.
A motion to adopt the agenda was made by Craig Mitchell and seconded by Steve Corwin. The motion
passed 4-0.
CONSENT AGENDA
A. Approval of Meeting Minutes
B. Accounts Payable
C. Personnel Report
D. PdEnroller Agreement
E. Voluntary Student Accident and Sickness Insurance Program
F. Student Travel
A motion to adopt the consent agenda was made by Marty Gray and seconded by Craig Mitchell. The
motion passed 4-0.
PUBLIC COMMENT
There was no public comment.
A motion to amend the agenda to move the budget hearing to after old business was made by Craig
Mitchell and seconded by Steve Corwin. The motion passed 4-0.
OLD BUSINESS
Curriculum Materials Proposal
Dr. James Everett presented Discovery Education Techbook, World History Project 1200-Present, and
Bridge to College Math curriculum materials to the Board for a second review and approval. A motion to
adopt the curriculum materials as presented was made by Craig Mitchell and seconded by Marty Gray.
The motion passed 4-0
BUDGET HEARING
A. Form F-195 (2023-2024 District Budget)
B. Public Comment on 2023-2024 Budget
 There was no public comment regarding the budget.
C. Budget Adoption Resolution 06-23
A motion to adopt the 2023-24 Budget, Resolution 06-23, was made by Steve Corwin and seconded by
Marty Gray. The motion passed 4-0.

NEW BUSINESS
First Review: Agreement for Legal Services
Dr. Everett presented an Agreement for Legal Services to the Board for a first review. This agreement
will be brought back to the Board for a second review and approval at the July 26, 2023, Board meeting.
First Review: NWESD 2023-24 MHP Contract
Dr. Everett presented the NWESD 2023-24 MHP contract to the Board for a first review. This agreement
will be brought back to the Board for a second review and approval at the July 26, 2023, Board meeting.
First Reading: Procedure 3205P – Sexual Harassment of Students Prohibited
Assistant Superintendent, Kurt Harvill presented a first reading of Procedure 3205P: Sexual Harassment
of Students Prohibited. This procedure will be brought back to the Board for a second review and
approval at the July 26, 2023, Board meeting.
First Review: 2023-24 MSD Board Calendar
Dr. Everett presented the 2023-24 MSD Board calendar to the Board for a first review. The 2023-24 MSD
Board calendar will be brought back to the Board for a second review and approval at the July 26, 2023,
Board meeting.
FINANCIAL REPORT
Financial Report
Director of Finance, Daniel Yorton gave a financial update of our audit to the Board.
SUPERINTENDENT’S REPORT
Dr. Everett presented to the Board updates on the close of the school year activities, the formation of a
facilities task force, and updates on NCTA Whatcom. Dr. Everett shared the names of the school Board
candidates, spoke about HB 1238 that will allow us to serve free breakfast and lunch to approximately
four hundred students at IRE. Dr. Everett spoke about his WASA leadership involvement, our newly
selected student representatives, as well as upcoming training workshops and opportunities.
INFORMATION FROM BOARD MEMBERS
The first football game is this Friday. There was a lot of volunteers and donors that worked on the
football/soccer fields and with the drainage last weekend.
BOARD SIGNATURE OF OFFICIAL DOCUMENTS
ADJOURNMENT
There being no further business, the Board meeting was adjourned at 8:35 p.m. by Board Chair Sara
Bernardy.
