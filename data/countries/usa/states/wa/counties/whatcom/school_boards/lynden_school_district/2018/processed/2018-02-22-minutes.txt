February 22, 2018
School Board Meeting
Minutes
Lynden High School Library
6:30 P.M.
1. Call to Order, Welcome, Pledge of Allegiance and Roll Call
Meeting called to order at 6:32P.M. Board members in attendance were Kevin Burke, Brian Steve Jilk, Kelli Kettels,
and CJ Costanti. Board member Brian Johnson was absent and excused. Student Directors Rubia Orellana and
Leslie Strandgard were present.
2. Approval of Meeting Minutes
• January 11, 2018
Steve Jilk motioned to approve the meeting minutes seconded by CJ Costanti. Motion carried by voice vote.
3. Consent Agenda
• 2017-18 General Fund $288,059.26 Warrants 47172 – 47296 dated February 15, 2018
• 2017-18 Capital Projects $2,441,899.80 Warrants 47297 – 47303 dated February 15, 2018
• 2017-18 ASB Fund $1,487.19 Warrants 47304 – 47312 dated February 15, 2018
• 2017-18 ACH General Fund $1,721.37 Warrants 171800224 – 171800241 dated February 15, 2018
• 2017-18 ACH ASB Fund $448.39 Warrants 171800202 – 171800207 dated January 16, 2018
Voided ck #46871 – Health Ed $917.51
• Personnel Action Items 2.22.18
CJ Costanti motioned to approve the consent agenda seconded by Steve Jilk. Motion carried by voice vote.
4. Information from the Audience
None at this meeting.
5. Finance and Operations
• Budget Update - January
The Board reviewed information on the budget for January containing a summary of financial status,
enrollment, general fund comparative, and revenues and expenditures.
6. Action Items
• School Year Calendar
The Board reviewed the 2018-2019 School Year Calendar after a revision was suggested to LEA and the
LEA made adjustments to the calendar for a second review by the Board.
CJ Costanti motioned to approve the 2018-2019 School Year Calendar seconded by Steve Jilk. Motion carried by
voice vote.
• Fisher Trail Easement
The Board reviewed the easement with the City of Lynden to give access at Fisher Elementary for the City
to construct and maintain the on-going addition of the Jim Kaemingk Sr. Trail.
Steve Jilk motioned to approve the Fisher Trail Easement seconded by CJ Costanti. Motion carried 3 to1.
7. Adjourn Meeting to go into Board Work Session
Meeting adjourned at 6:40 P.M.

8. Board Work Session
• School Board Linkage – LHS Students
Lynden High School Student Board Directors, Leslie Strandgard and Rubia Orellano directed a linkage activity
between board members and Lynden High School students about the following questions as a whole School
District; 1) Where does bullying happen & how can it be prevented?; 2) Do students feel prepared to go to
college and/or begin a career. Why or why not?; 3) Why do you think young people are experiencing an
increase in anxiety and what can be done to help? LHS students and the Board gathered in groups to express
their thoughts on these topics.
