Special Board Meeting
The purpose of the meeting will be for the board to consider adoption of Resolution 2122-06 Certification
of 2022 Excess Property Taxes. Pursuant to extended proclamations from the Washington State
Governor related to the COVID-19 pandemic emergency, participation in this meeting will take place
remotely. Live public observation of this meeting will be available online. Click on the "Video" link above
to access this meeting as an observer.

Recommended Action :

To adopt Resolution 2122-06 Certification of 2022
Excess Property Taxes

Public Content
At this time, the board is asked to adopt Resolution 2122-06 to certify the Replacement Maintenance &
Operations levy collection for calendar year 2022 equal to the District's maximum legal levy authority,
$6,082,990. This revises and replaces the amount of$5,909,503originallyprojected in the 2021-22
Budget. The amount authorized by voters for collection in 2022 is $6,400,000.The deadline to certify this
adjustment is November 30, 2021. At this time, the board is asked to adopt Resolution 2122-06 which
certifies this increase in levy collection for 2022.
