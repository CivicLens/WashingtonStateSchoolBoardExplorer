Call to Order

A regular meeting of the Board of Directors was held on Monday, September 9, 2024, in
the Administrative Service Center Board Room and was available for live streaming.
President Lynn Goebel called the meeting to order at 6:00 p.m. and led the flag salute.

Roll Call

Board members Lynn Goebel, Nicole Tesch, Sharon Jensen, and Josh MacLean were
present. Board member Jessica Aws was absent and excused. Student board
representatives Maggie Litvachuk and Allena Locklear were present.

Adoption of Agenda

The agenda was adopted by unanimous consent.

Student Board
Representative
Oath of Office

Newly appointed Student Board Representative Allena Locklear took the Oath of Office
led by President Lynn Goebel.

Consent Agenda

Director Jensen moved to approve the consent agenda. Director MacLean seconded the
motion. The motion passed unanimously.
