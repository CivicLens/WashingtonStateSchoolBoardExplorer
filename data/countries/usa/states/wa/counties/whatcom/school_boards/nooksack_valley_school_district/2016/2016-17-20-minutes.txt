REGULAR MEETING

COMMUNICATIONS

Jerry Marschke, Executive Director of Cardiovascular Services,
Peace Health Medical Group and Lynette Ondeck, District RN,
were present to provide an overview of the Teen Heart Screening
that took place out our High School. The board thanked them for
providing this amazing service to our students and community.

TEEN HEALTHY HEART
SCREENING
PRESENTATION UPDATE

Ken Quehrn, High School Music Teacher, was present to seek
approval for a combined choir trip to southern California. The
Board enthusiastically approved this request. Cheryl Thornton
recommended that he contact several Nooksack Valley graduates
who are engaged in the music industry.
