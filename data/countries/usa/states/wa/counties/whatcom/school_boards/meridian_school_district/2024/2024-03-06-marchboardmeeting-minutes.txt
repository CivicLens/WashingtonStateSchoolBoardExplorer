WORK SESSION & REGULAR SCHOOL BOARD MEETING

BOARD WORK SESSION & REGULAR MEETING
The meeting was called to order by Board President Steve Cowin at 5:00 p.m.
Board Directors present at the Special Session meeting were Jana Haugen and Michelle Simmons. Steve
Corwin was in attendance via zoom.
BOARD WORK SESSION
The board moved immediately into a WSSDA Board Bootcamp work session. No action was taken during
the work session.
RECONVENE REGULAR BOARD MEETING
Board Vice-President Jarrod Karuza welcomed everyone to the regular board meeting at 6:00 pm.
Board Directors Marty Gray and Jarrod Karuza joined the regular meeting at 6:00 pm. Steve Corwin joined
the regular meeting via zoom.
Student Board Representatives Kennedy Brzozowski and Morgan Adams were present at the regular
meeting.
The pledge of allegiance was recited.
A motion to adopt the agenda was made by Jana Haugen and seconded by Michelle Simmons. The
motion passed 5-0.
CONSENT AGENDA
A. Approval of Meeting Minutes
B. Accounts Payable
C. Personnel Report
D. Payroll
E. District Credit Card: Authorized Users
A motion to adopt the consent agenda was made by Michelle Simmons and seconded by Marty Gray.
The motion passed 5-0.
RECOGNITION
MHS Staff Recognition
MHS Principal, Derek Forbes recognized the MHS Food Service Staff for the positive supportive student
and staff interactions, flexibility, problem solving and contribution to making MHS a great place for
students and staff.
REPORTS
PLC Presentations
The Art teachers from IRE, MMS and MHS presented a combined PLC report to the Board. The report
focused on creating a sense of space in artwork and included data, knowledge measurement strategies,

and techniques used to increase skills. The art teacher at IRE, MMS and MHS each shared how this looks
in each of their building.
IRE First Grade team presented a PLC report to the Board. The report was focused on a first-grade
informational explanatory writing. The team shared initial data, the tools the team is using to measure
progress, and the resources the team is using to assist with improving student progress.
MMS Science team presented a PLC report to the Board. The presentation introduced the science
practices with an emphasis on developing and using models. The presentation included the common
rubric used to measure progress, and techniques used to improve skills.
MHS School Report
MHS Principal Derek Forbes and Assistant Principal Shaun Doffing presented a MHS school report to the
Board. Updates were shared about the new accreditation process, on time graduation rate, ninth grade
on track rate, ninth grade success program, and the 2023-24 Goals.
CTE Program Report
Derek Forbes presented an update on the CTE program at MHS. Information was shared about the class
offerings, student industries, and community partnerships.
PUBLIC COMMENT
There was no public comment.
OLD BUSINESS
Confirmation of MHS graduation
Confirmation of the MHS graduation date was presented for second review and approval. Students favor
approval. A motioned to confirm graduation as presented was made by Jana Haugen and seconded by
Michelle Simmons. The motion passed 5-0.
Second Review and Approval: Policy and Procedure 5271 – Reporting Improper Governmental
Action

Policy and Procedure 5271 – Reporting Improper Governmental Action was presented to the Board for a
second review and approval. Student Representatives favor approval. A motion to adopt Policy and
Procedure 5271 – Reporting Improper Governmental Action as presented was made by Steve Corwin and
seconded by Jana Haugen. The motion passed 5-0.
Second Review and Approval: Policy and Procedure 5282 - Civility

Policy and Procedure 5282 - Civility was presented to the Board for a second review and approval.
Students favor approval. A motion to adopt Policy and Procedure 5282 - Civility as presented was made
by Michelle Simmons and seconded by Jana Haugen. The motion passed 5-0.
Second Review and Approval: Policy 6111 - Tuition

Policy 6111 - Tuition was presented to the Board for a second review and approval. Students favor
approval. A motion to adopt Policy 6111 - Tuition as presented was made by Marty Gray and seconded by
Steve Corwin. The motion passed 5-0.
Second Review and Approval: 2024-2027 School Year Calendars
The 2024-2027 School Year Calendars were presented to the Board for a second review and approval.
Students favor approval. A motion to adopt the 2024-2027 School Year Calendars as presented was made

by Jana Haugen and seconded by Marty Gray. The motion passed 5-0.
