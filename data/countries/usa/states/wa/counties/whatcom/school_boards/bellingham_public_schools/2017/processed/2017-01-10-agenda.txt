Tuesday, January 10, 2017
School Board Visit - Shuksan Elementary School
The Board of Directors of Bellingham Public Schools regularly visits a different school throughout the
school year. The purpose of the visits is to learn how each school is delivering upon The Bellingham
Promise. The directors will visit Shuksan Middle School at 7:30 a.m. on Jan. 10, 2017. Shuksan Middle
School is located at 2717 Alderwood Avenue.
