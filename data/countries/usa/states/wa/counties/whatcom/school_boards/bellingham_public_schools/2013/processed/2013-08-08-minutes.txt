BELLINGHAM PUBLIC SCHOOLS
Bellingham, Washington
MINUTES OF THE SPECIAL SESSION
OF THE
BELLINGHAM SCHOOL DISTRICT
BOARD OF DIRECTORS
August 8, 2013
The Bellingham School Board of Directors met in special session August 8, 2013, in Conference
Room 205 at the District Office, 1306 Dupont Street, Bellingham, Washington.
President Ken Gass called the meeting to order at 6:10 p.m.
Roll Call
Present: Directors Kelly Bashaw, Camille Hackler, Steve Smith, President Ken Gass, and
Superintendent Greg Baker.
For a portion of the meeting, the Board was joined by Deputy Superintendent Mike Copland,
Assistant Superintendent Steve Clarke, Assistant Superintendent Ron Cowan, Executive Director
of Human Resources Nora Klewiada, Executive Director of Communications and Community
Relations Tanya Rowe, Executive Administrator Rob McElroy, and Doctoral Resident Justin
May.
Following introductions, the Board discussed the following agenda items:
Annual Board Agenda 2013-14
Governance Process/Board-Superintendent Relationship/Executive Limitations/Ends
(GP/BSR/EL/ENDS
o Per Governance Process 7 (GP-7), the Board discussed Agenda Planning.
o The Board discussed Governance Process GP-7.1, Annual Board Agenda
2013-2014.
o The Board discussed Executive Limitation monitoring reports:
 These will be consent agenda items, unless the Superintendent or the
Board determines an exception is warranted.
 Board Response to Monitoring Reports will occur as an action agenda
item on the same date the report is presented.
 The Board will respond electronically to Ends and Means reports, with
support by the superintendent’s secretary. Board members will
provide feedback to the superintendent’s office by 4 p.m. the day
before the Board meeting.
 During the subsequent Board meeting, the policies will be reviewed in
more detail to determine whether changes are needed.
