Tuesday, May 7, 2024
School Board Visit - Alderwood Elementary School
The Board of Directors of Bellingham Public Schools regularly visits schools throughout the school year.
The purpose of these visits is to learn how each school is delivering upon The Bellingham Promise. The
directors will visit Alderwood Elementary School, 3400 Hollywood Ave., at 7:30 a.m., on Tuesday, May 7,
2024. This is not a regular school board meeting and will not include public comment time.
For more information, call 360-676-6501.
