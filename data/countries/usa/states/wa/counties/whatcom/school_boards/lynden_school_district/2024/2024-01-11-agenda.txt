2. Good News
January is School Board Appreciation Month! Thank you to our five board directors for their commitment to the education and
well-being of our students. Your efforts in ensuring a positive educational experience for our students is appreciated.
Students in Mrs. Munkres' class at Fisher Elementary spent time learning about radio from KAFE 104.1 radio. Broadcasters
Dave and Alan recorded a spot for the morning KAFE Kids show about Christmas, which ran on December 15th.
This year, our Holiday Assistance Program coordinated by Family Community Services was able to provide 465 children with
gifts for the holiday. A huge thank you to our community sponsors and individual staff and community members who donated
and helped. We are so thankful for the generosity of this community.
Lynden Middle School ended the year with a spirit week! Students and staff led a fun-filled door decorating contest, parent
volunteers facilitated their PBiS store, and the week ended with an outstanding assembly where they celebrated their Food
Drive contributions and played community-building games.
Cascadia Daily News ran an article featuring LHS athlete Jesse Stewart and his journey as a deaf football player. A separate
article featured LHS student Brant Heppner and told the story of his journey to becoming a dual sport athlete in basketball and
football.
