The Lynden High School wind ensemble brought home their 28th consecutive Superior rating at the San

None at this meeting.
Finance and Operations
Patti Fairbanks updated the Board on the timeline of the budget process for 2019-2020 school year, and the
next steps to make adjustments to a proposed budget. Patti informed the Board that although the first
projection for the 2019-2020 expenditures are ready, we are awaiting what the state will provide in revenues
for the next year. Patti will provide another update at a May meeting.
9. Board Workshop
• School Board Year End Calendar and Meeting Topics
Jim provided an update on topics of discussion for the end of the year board meetings. The Board will
review a full calendar and agenda in June or July for the 2019-2020 school year.
10. Action Items
• Resolution No. 05-19 – Asset Preservation Program (APP)
Jim explained that the APP is a statement that the Lynden School District will employ a system to preserve
district facilities that includes a yearly evaluation and a six-year evaluation through a study and survey
process that includes a more rigorous evaluation intended to ensure facilities sustain their expected life
cycle. This will be active for all school facilities and will be reported annually to the state (OSPI).
Kelli Kettels motioned to approve Resolution No. 05-19 seconded by David Vis. Motion carried by voice vote.
