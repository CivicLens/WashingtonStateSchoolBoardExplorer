Wednesday, February 7, 2024
Board Executive Session: Application Review
The ESD Board of Directors will meet in Executive Session at the Ellensburg School District, located at
1300 E. 3rd Ave, on Wednesday, Feb. 7, 2024 from 2-4 PM to review applications for the permanent
superintendent position and choose the final candidates. In the interest of public health, the meeting will
also be available via Zoom. Comments can be submitted to the Board at board@esd401.org.
