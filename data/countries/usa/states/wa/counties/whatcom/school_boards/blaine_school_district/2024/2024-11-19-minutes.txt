Blaine School District No. 503
  Special Board Meeting: Executive Session
Tuesday, November 19, 2024
  Minutes
  1. Special Board Meeting
A. Call to Order
President Freeman called the meeting to order, in the district's boardroom, at 6:00 p.m.
Directors Creydt, Lazarus, Freeman, and Galbraith were in attendance in person. Director Swinburnson attended the meeting telephonically, only during the executive session.
     Erika Creydt, Ben Lazarus, Cliff Freeman, Ryan Swinburnson, Steve Galbraith
  2. Executive Session
A. Executive Session
President Freeman announced that the board would move into executive session for the purpose of reviewing the performance of a public employee. The executive session was anticipated to last forty-five (45) minutes, or until approximately 6:45 p.m. Any extension of time would be announced verbally in the boardroom, and no final action would be taken by the board following the executive session. Directors Creydt, Lazarus, Freeman, and Galbraith moved to a private room for the closed session. Superintendent Dr. Christopher Granger remained in the boardroom.
  3. Reconvene and Adjourn
A. Reconvene and Adjourn
At 6:45 p.m., the directors returned to the boardroom and the meeting reconvened in regular session.
There being no further business, the meeting was adjourned by a unanimous vote.
Motion by Ben Lazarus, second by Steve Galbraith
Final Resolution: Motion Carries 4-0, 1 not present
Yea: Erika Creydt, Ben Lazarus, Cliff Freeman, Steve Galbraith
Not Present at Vote: Ryan Swinburnson
  DATE MINUTES APPROVED: November 25, 2024
