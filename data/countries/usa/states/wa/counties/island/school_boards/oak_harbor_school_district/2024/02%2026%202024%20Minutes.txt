Call to Order

A regular meeting of the Board of Directors was held on Monday, February 26, 2024, in
the Administrative Service Center Board Room and was available for live streaming.
President Lynn Goebel called the meeting to order at 6:00 p.m. and led the flag salute.

Roll Call

Board members Lynn Goebel, Jessica Aws, Nicole Tesch, Sharon Jensen, and Josh
MacLean were present. Student board representative Josiah Villano was present.
Student representative Maggie Litvachuk was absent and excused.

Adoption of Agenda

The agenda was adopted by unanimous consent.

Consent Agenda

Vice President Aws moved to approve the consent agenda. Director Tesch seconded the
motion. The motion passed unanimously.

Student
Representative
Report

Student Representative Josiah Villano congratulated the NJROTC students for placing in
every event at the divisional competition, earning their way to Regionals, and he
reported that the Macho Volleyball event raised over $600. He announced several
upcoming high school music and art events as well as family events at other schools in
the district.

Visitors

Student Nolan Saltonstall spoke about a community recreational center.

Superintendent
Report

Superintendent Kuss-Cybula shared about the Study Session following the meeting to
discuss the budget process.

6210P Purchasing
Authorization and
Control Policy
Updates First
Reading

Dr. Kuss-Cybula spoke about updates needed to Board Policy 6210P Purchasing
Authorization and Control in order to ensure the construction projects move forward
and stay on track.

Legislative Update

Director Tesch reported that the Legislative Session is on Day 50, and the 2-year budget
was unveiled. There is legislation in process to increase the amount of funding for
Materials, Supplies, and Operating Costs (MSOCs).

Board Comments

Vice President Aws shared about the trip she recently took with Oak Harbor High School
students to Olympia advocating for bills about substance use prevention and education.
She praised the students for their participation. Director Jensen spoke about her visits
to school events. Director Tesch also attended several school events and mentioned
local community group fundraisers. President Goebel commented on her school visits
and complimented Rotary on their work to feed children in the community.

Superintendent
Comments

Dr. Kuss-Cybula thanked new Chief Financial Officer Amber Porter for her report,
announced the start of spring sports, and thanked the 80+ staff members for their work
on the strategic plan. There will be opportunities this spring to gather another round of
student feedback regarding the strategic plan.

Upcoming Meetings

Regular Board Meetings are scheduled for Monday, March 11, and Monday, March 25,
2024, at 6:00 p.m. in the Administrative Service Center Board Room.

Adjourn

The meeting adjourned at 6:41 p.m.
