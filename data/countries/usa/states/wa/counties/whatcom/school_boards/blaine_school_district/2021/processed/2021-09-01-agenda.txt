Wednesday, September 1, 2021
Special Board Meeting
The purpose of the meeting will be for the board to consider ratification of the 2021-2023 collective
bargaining agreement with SEIU Classified Staff, as well as the associated SEIU wage schedule.
Pursuant to extended proclamations from the Washington State Governor related to the COVID-19
pandemic emergency, participation in this meeting will take place remotely. Live public observation of this
meeting will be available online. Click on the "Video" link above to access this meeting as an observer.
