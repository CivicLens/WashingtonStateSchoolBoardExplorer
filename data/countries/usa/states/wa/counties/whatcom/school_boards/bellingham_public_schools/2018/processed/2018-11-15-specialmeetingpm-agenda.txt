Thursday, November 15, 2018
Special Meeting, 6:30 p.m.
The Board of Directors will attend the Washington State School Directors Association (WSSDA) Annual
Conference from Wednesday, Nov. 14 to Saturday, Nov. 17 at the Spokane Convention Center in
Spokane, Washington.
While in Spokane, the board will hold a special meeting on Thursday, Nov. 15 at 6:30 p.m. at Spencer's
for Steak and Chops, 322 N. Spokane Falls Court, Spokane, WA. The purpose of the meeting is to
discuss school district initiatives, issues and business in an unstructured manner. This is not a regular
School Board meeting and will not include public comment time.
For more information, call 360-676-6501.
