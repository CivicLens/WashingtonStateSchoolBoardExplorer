Administrators fromPrairie High School and River HomeLink will be presenting their School Improvement Plans.

Each year schools engage in school improvement planning as a continuous process to ensure that all students are achieving at
high levels. PerWAC 180-16-220, the Board shall annually approve each schools School Improvement Plan.The development of
the plan shall include active participation and meaningful input by building staff, students, parents, and community members. The
plan shall be data driven, promote a positive impact on student learning, and include a continuous improvement process to
increase student achievement.
