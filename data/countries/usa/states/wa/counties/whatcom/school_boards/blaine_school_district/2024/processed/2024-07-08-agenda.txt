Monday, July 8, 2024
Special Board Meeting: Audit Exit Conference
The board will meet virtually with the State Auditor's Office, with public observation available in the
districts boardroom at 770 Mitchell Avenue in Blaine. Anyone desiring to observe virtually may contact
the Superintendent's Office by email (cgranger@blainesd.org) before 1:00 p.m. on July 8 to request a
meeting link.
