Tuesday, August 18, 2020
Special Meeting-Public Hearing 6:45 p.m.
Ferndale School District No. 502 School Board Special Meeting - Public Hearing
The Board of Directors will hold a public hearing for the purpose of receiving comments from the public
on the 2020-2021 budget. Any person may appear at the virtual hearing and be heard for or against any
part of such budget, the four-year budget plan, or any proposed changes to uses of enrichment funding
under RCW 28A.505.240.
Please register in advance to speak at the virtual hearing at the following link: https://zoom.us/webinar
/register/WN_cRgBxj5DT_uc-5yYpNk4cA
After registering, you will receive a confirmation email containing information about joining the webinar.
Attendees wishing to make a comment should use the chat or raise your hand feature in Zoom to make
the comment.
Written comment may also be submitted prior to the public hearing to the following email address:
fsdinfo@ferndalesd.org
The completed budget is on file at the District Office located at 6041 Vista Drive and on our website:
https://www.ferndalesd.org/business-support-services/budget.
Governor Inslees Amendment to Proclamation 20-05 waives and suspends the portions of RCW 42.30
and RCW 42.56 that require in-person meetings or contact.
Also, at any time that you would like to contact your board members, you may do so by using their
contact information that is displayed on this webpage at this link https://www.ferndalesd.org/school-board
/school-board-contacts
