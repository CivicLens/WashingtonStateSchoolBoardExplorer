School Board Work Session
November 6, 2024

1. Opening Items
A. Call to Order
Board chair McCowen called the meeting to order at 6:30 p.m. Board members present were Grant Griffen, Jeff McCowen, Zac Fabian,
Ryan Paulson and Aneesha Dieu. Also present Superintendent Jeremy Wheatley and Business Manager Jana Eaton.
B. Flag Salute
Board Chair McCowen led the flag salute
C. Additions or Changes to the Agenda
None
2. Consent Agenda
None
3. Discussion Items
A. Review of WSSDA Board Self Assessment Survey with WSSDA Facilitator Mary Fertakis
Superintendent Wheatley introduced Mary Fertakis and Heather Curl from WSSDA to go over the school board self-assessment survey
results. Board members completed a self assessment survey last month. Previous board members have taken this same survey which
gives Dayton some baseline data. Board members have copies of the survey and the scoring tool. Ms. Fertakis opened with an icebreaker exercise asking board members for one word or phrase to describe their motivation for being here and serving students and
what they would like to accomplish this year for students. Over the two hour session the board worked on different goals and focus
areas by breaking into small groups and working on specific topics. Ms. Fertakis will send the document with the completed comments
for the board review in a future workshop. In closing, the board summed up their feelings on the workshop with very positive and
inspirational comments.
4. Adjournment
Board member McCowen adjourned the meeting at 8:34 p.m. The next meeting will be a regular board meeting on November 20, 2024
at 6:30 p.m. in the high school media center and via Zoom.
Respectfully Submitted,
Jeremy Wheatley, Secretary to the Board of Directors
