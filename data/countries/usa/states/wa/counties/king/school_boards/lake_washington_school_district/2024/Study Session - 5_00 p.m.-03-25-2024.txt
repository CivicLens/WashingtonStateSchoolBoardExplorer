Topic: Performance Based Graduation Pathways
Overview:
Associate Superintendent Dr. Jen Rose will provide an overview of the change in graduation requirements
from the State Board of Education in adding graduation pathways, the options students have had for meeting
pathways, the recent addition of the option of performance based pathways, and our structures and supports
for students in meeting the graduation pathways. We will also review waivers that have been available for this
requirement for the graduating classes for 2020 - 2023, current conditions and data for the class of 2024, and
work-in-progress by the SBE and by LWSD to support our students. Christine Bell, principal at Lake
Washington High School, and Kate Atvars, Teaching and Learning Specialist will provide additional information
in the presentation.
Board Policy Connection:
R-1: Each student will graduate prepared to lead a rewarding, responsible life as a contributing member of our
community and greater society.
OE-11.8 Encourage new and innovative programs, approaches, and learning opportunities to increase student
engagement and achievement in learning.
OE 11.9 Regularly evaluate and modify all instructional programs and practices as necessary to assure their
continuing effectiveness.
Outcome:

The Board will understand the history and impact of graduation pathways, the current situation for our
students, and work the District is undertaking to support students.
