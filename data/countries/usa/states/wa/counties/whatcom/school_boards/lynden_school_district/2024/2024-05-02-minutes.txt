6. Reports
a. Superintendent
b. Teaching & Learning
Tim Doering, Principal of Isom Elementary School, presented the board with a progress
update related to Isom’s School Improvement Plan. Their first goal for this year is for
75% of Isom students to be proficient on the STAR reading fluency assessment. At the
beginning of the year, 47% of Isom students were proficient. Winter scores showed
growth toward goal at 60%. Isom’s second goal is that 85% of students will attend
regularly by the end of 23/24. At the beginning of year, 75% of students were attending
regularly. As of the end of April just over 90% attending regularly.
Ian Freeman, Principal of Lynden High School, presented the board with a progress
update related to LHS’s School Improvement Plan. Their first goal for this year is that all
teachers at LHS will increase understanding and use of UDL as measured by selfassessment surveys in spring 2024. Principal Freeman explained the steps that have
taken place so far to reach this goal. Their second goal is to create and implement Tier 1,
2, and 3 systems of academic support to positively impact student success in classes as
measured by semester grades with a goal of <15% of D and F grades. Semester 1 had a
13.9% D/F rate.
