6. Reports
• Superintendent
WIAA Guidelines for Athletics
Jim Frey informed the Board of the recent updates from WIAA regarding sport specific training and
practices. Based on varying guidelines, the WIAA document would allow for larger pods and more
activities such as limited inter-squad scrimmaging. We are not considering league games at this time.
Reopening Update
Jim Frey updated the Board on the first days of in-person learning for grades k-2 students grades 3-5.
Secondary level students to begin in-person learning on November 16th.

inform, apply and implement best practices to support and instruct students with dyslexic tendencies

Budget Update - September
