March 27, 2024
The Board of Directors of Kent School District No. 415 met in meetings on Wednesday, March 27, 2024, beginning

Agenda Review
A motion was made to add to the agenda, ‘Recission of Resolution No. 1669’.
Due to Resolution No. 1669 having previously been moved, seconded, discussed, and voted upon at a previous
meeting, President Margel’s stated that the current motion should be voted down, and a new motion should be
made to re-open Resolution No. 1669. Curtis Leonard, acting legal counsel, confirmed President Margel’s proposal
in keeping with Robert’s Rules.
The motion failed.
A motion was made to add to the agenda, ‘Reopening of the Vote of Resolution No. 1669 - A Resolution of Kent
School District No. 415 relating to school board proceedings; creating a Labor Policy Committee of the Board;
appointing initial members and delegating authority; and providing for related matters.’
Director Cook requested a roll call vote.
