Dayton School District No. 2
609 S 2nd St. Dayton WA 99328
Special Board Meeting
March 29, 2024
1. MEETING OPENING
A. Call to Order
Board chair McCowen called the special meeting to order at 3:32 p.m. via Zoom. Board members
present were Jeff McCowen, Zac Fabian and Ryan Paulson in the Dayton School District conference
room. Participating via Zoom were Grant Griffen, Aneesha Dieu, Superintendent Rich Stewart and
business manager Jana Eaton. Administrative assistant Suzi Tasker was also in attendance.
B. Flag Salute
Board chair McCowen led the flag salute.
C. Recognition of Representatives
None
2. ACTION ITEMS
Board member Fabian moved, seconded by director Paulson, to approve the three year
superintendent contract for Jeremy Wheatley. Motion carried 5-0.
3. ADJOURNMENT
The meeting was adjourned at 3:40 p.m. The next meeting will be a regular meeting on April 17,
2024 at 6:30 p.m. in the high school media center/library.
Respectfully submitted,

Rich Stewart, Secretary to the Board of Directors
