Tuesday, October 5, 2021
School Board Visit - Sehome High School
The Board of Directors of Bellingham Public Schools regularly visits schools throughout the school year.
The purpose of these visits is to learn how each school is delivering upon The Bellingham Promise. The
directors will visit Sehome High School, 2700 Bill McDonald Parkway, Bellingham, at 7:30 a.m., Tuesday,
October 5, 2021. This is not a regular school board meeting and will not include public comment time.
For more information, call 360-676-6501.
