SPECIAL MEETING
The Board of Directors of Kent School District No. 415 met in a special meeting on Wednesday,
January 31, 2024 at 4:00 p.m. for approximately two hours. In attendance: President Meghin
Margel, Awale Farah, Tim Clark, Donald Cook, and Andy Song. The purpose of the special
meeting was a work session to discuss the Winter Quarterly Progress Review with Dr. Larry
Nyland, and the Capital Projects and Technology Replacement Levy discussion with Dr. Wade
Barringer. Also in attendance: Superintendent Israel Vela. Click here to view the meeting.
Call to Order
President Margel called the special meeting to order at 4:00 p.m.
A brief recess took place at 5:07 p.m.
The special meeting resumed at 5:11 p.m.
Adjournment
President Margel adjourned the meeting at 6:02 p.m.
