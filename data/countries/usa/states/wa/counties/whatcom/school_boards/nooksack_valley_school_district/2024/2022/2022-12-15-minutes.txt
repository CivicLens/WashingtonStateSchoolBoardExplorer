Jason Heutink inquired about elementary schools no longer providing holiday concerts. Many
factors led to the decision, all shared in elementary notes home. The district will seek feedback
before making long term future plans.
Halli Kimball indicated that vaping is still a rampant issue. Supt. Galley committed to
discussing with the HS admin team and redoubling efforts to monitor the student restrooms.
Mark Olson asked about district policy regarding Narcan use in the event of a fentanyl
overdose. Policy parameters were discussed, and Supt. Galley will direct staff to move towards
policy adoption.
Hannah Ordos shared her concerns about sexual harassment and inquired about our policies
regarding mandatory reporting, staff education, and safety. Supt. Galley explained the
procedures NV district admin follow, including seeking legal counsel in all incidents.
