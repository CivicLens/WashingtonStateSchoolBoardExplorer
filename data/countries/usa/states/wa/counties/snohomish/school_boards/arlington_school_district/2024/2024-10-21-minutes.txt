          Board of Directors Special Joint Meeting
Lakewood and Arlington School Districts
Date: Monday, October 21, 2024
In-Person: LHS Library, 17023 11th Ave NE, Arlington, WA 98223 
    1. OPENING OF MEETING 
A. Meeting Called to Order
Vice President Tocco called the special joint meeting to order at 5:02 p.m. 
  B. Pledge of Allegiance
Vice President Tocco conducted the Pledge of Allegiance. 
  C. Roll Call Attendance
Vice President Tocco called roll for Lakewood. Directors Leah Tocco, Dana Krueger, Steve Larson, Dawn Taylor and Superintendent Dr.Murphy were in attendance. Director Gotts was excused. President Levesque called roll for Arlington. Directors Mary Levesque, EricaKnapp, Sheri Kelly, Nicole Watts, Matt Dimond, Student Advisor Nathan Nichols, Student Advisor Deja Reed and Superintendent Dr.Sweeting were in attendance. 
  D. Approval of the Agenda
Vice President Tocco asked if there were any changes to the special board meeting agenda. There were none. 
  Motion: Approval of the special board meeting agenda as presented. 
Motion by Vice President Tocco, no second was made.
Final Resolution: Motion Carries
Yea: Leah Tocco, Dana Krueger, Steve Larson, Dawn Taylor, Mary Levesque, Erica Knapp, Sheri Kelly, Nicole Watts, Matt Dimond 
  2. MEETING PURPOSE & LEARNING OUTCOMES 
A. Purpose of the Joint Meeting - Dr. Erin Murphy, Superintendent, Lakewood School District
Dr. Murphy, Lakewood's Superintendent, provided an overview of the special joint meeting, which aimed to facilitate collaborationbetween Lakewood and Arlington Boards of Directors on legislative priorities. 
  3. LEGISLATIVE PRIORITIES 
A. 2024-25 Legislative Priorities Discussion
Vice President Tocco opened the floor for discussion of legislative priorities. Each board presented its document of legislative prioritiesfor review. Both boards stressed the importance of advocating for their priorities as well as building strong partnerships with each otherand legislators. 
  4. JOINT MEETING PLANNING 
A. Discuss Topics and Dates for Future Joint Meeting(s)
Vice President Tocco asked directors for topics of focus for a future meeting. Several topics were discussed, and Arlington SchoolDistrict agreed to host the next joint meeting on January 22, 2025. 
  5. INFORMATIONAL ITEMS 
A. Next Meetings
Lakewood School District - Study Session is October 21, 2024 at 6:30 p.m. and next Regular Meeting is November 6, 2024.
Arlington School District - Study Session and Regular Board Meeting, October 28, 2024 
  6. ADJOURNMENT 
A. Adjourn the Meeting 
Vice President Tocco adjourned the meeting at 6:24 p.m.
