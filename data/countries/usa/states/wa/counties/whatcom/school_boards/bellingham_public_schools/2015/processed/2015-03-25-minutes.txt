DRAFT MINUTES OF THE SPECIAL MEETING AND EXECUTIVE SESSION OF
THE BELLINGHAM PUBLIC SCHOOLS BOARD OF DIRECTORS
March 25, 2015
The Bellingham School Board of Directors held a special meeting March 25, 2015 at the District
Office, 1306 Dupont Street, Bellingham, Washington.
President Kelly Bashaw called the meeting to order at 6 p.m.
Roll Call
Present: Directors Kelly Bashaw, Douglas Benjamin, Kenneth Gass, Camille Hackler, Steven
Smith, and Superintendent Greg Baker.
Agenda
The Board reviewed and discussed recent revisions to the School Board Handbook.
Executive Session
At 7 p.m., the board entered Executive Session for the purpose of reviewing the performance of a
public employee, per RCW 42.30.110(1)(g).
Reconvene to open session
At 7:40 p.m., the board reconvened to open session.
Adjournment
The special meeting adjourned at 7:45 p.m.
