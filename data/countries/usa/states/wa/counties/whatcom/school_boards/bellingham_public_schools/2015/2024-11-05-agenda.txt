Wednesday, March 25, 2015
Special Meeting and Executive Session, 6 p.m.
The Board of Directors will hold a special meeting at 6 p.m. in Room 205 of the District Office, 1306
Dupont St., Bellingham. The purpose of the meeting is to review and discuss the School Board
Handbook. This is not a regular School Board meeting and will not include public comment time.
Following the special meeting, the Board will meet in Executive Session to review the performance of a
public employee, per RCW 42.30.110(1)(g).
