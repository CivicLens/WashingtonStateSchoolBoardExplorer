OLD BUSINESS
WIAA, Board Resolution 04-21
The board adopted Board Resolution 04-21: WIAA Membership. The School Board is asked to adopt a resolution
annually, indicating that they have approved the District's membership with the Washington Interscholastic
Activities Association (WIAA) and as members, the district will follow the WIAA Rules and Regulations. Sara
Bernardy made a motion to adopt WIAA, Board Resolution 04-21, Craig Mitchell seconded the motion. The
motion passed 4-0.
Recovery Plan, Board Resolution 05-21
The Board approved the Academic and Student Well-Being Plan Resolution. Emily Norton made a motion to
adopt Recovery Plan, Board Resolution 05-21. Sara Bernardy seconded the motion. The motion passed 4-0.
