L. Election of Legislative
Representative

Meeting :

Jun 22, 2020 - Regular Board Meeting

Category :

6. Action Items

Type :

Action

Public Content
A Legislative Representative serves as the board's liaison with the Washington State School Directors' Association Legislative
Assembly. The Legislative Representative assumes office July 1st in an even year for a two-year period. The Legislative
Representative attends Washington State School Directors' Association Assemblies, conveying local views and concerns to that
body and participating in the formulation of state legislative programs. The Legislative Representative monitors proposed school
legislation and informs the board of the issues.
President Gibson will call for nominations for Legislative Representative to serve a 2-year term beginning on July 1.
The nominations will close and the Legislative Representative will be elected by a roll call vote.

8. Executive Session
Subject :

A. Executive Session

Meeting :

Jun 22, 2020 - Regular Board Meeting

Category :

8. Executive Session

Type :

Discussion

Public Content
As per legal guidelines, the board may choose to recess into executive session to consider employment
or dismissal of personnel, to review the performance of a public employee, to consult with legal counsel,
to consider the position to be taken in collective bargaining, or to consider acquisition or sale of real
estate.
Prior to the start of executive session, the president will announce the purpose and length of the
session.
At the close of the executive session, the directors will reconvene in regular session at which point
they may take formal action or the meeting will be adjourned.
The Board will move into executive session for the purpose of reviewing the performance of a public employee. The executive session is
anticipated to last for up to thirty (30) minutes. No action is anticipated following the executive session.
