MINUTES APPROVED
REGULAR MEETING

Matt Galley and Joel VanderYacht shared plans for the expansion of
in-person learning. Mark Johnson indicated that we would be able to
move forward with these plans, pending health department approval.
He also indicated that these plans are not yet approved. Question and
comments occurred throughout, with the encouragement to keep
focused on increasing student in-person learning. A few of the guests
communicated the struggles that their students have with remote
learning and advocated for us to continue increasing in-person learning.
Steve Jones reiterated the board’s stance to get kids back in school as
soon as possible and with health department approval.
