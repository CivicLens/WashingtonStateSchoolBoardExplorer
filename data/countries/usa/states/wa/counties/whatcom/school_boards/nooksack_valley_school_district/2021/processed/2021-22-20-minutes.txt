MINUTES APPROVED
REGULAR MEETING

The president of each youth sports board gave presentations on the
state of each sport. Sharing celebrations and expressing supports.
Six community members entered public comment into the record.
Topics included questions about the efficacy of masks, district use
of ESSER funding and continuing to advocate for parental choice.
