Thursday, June 20, 2024
Special Board Meeting: Hearing on Resolution 2024-07
Date: Thursday, June 20th, 2024
Time: 3:45pm
Location: Wilburton Instructional Service Center, Rainier Room and Virtual Teams Meeting
PURPOSE:
The Board of Directors of the Bellevue School District will hold a public hearing at 3:45 pm on Thursday,
June, 20, 2024 to take testimony regarding the proposed of the sale of district property. Testimony will be
taken both in-person at the Wilburton Instructional Services Center and remotely in the same manner that
the Board takes remote public comment at its Regular Business Meetings. No action will be taken by the
Board at the public hearing.
The Board will also accept remote public testimony specific to this recommendation at the same meeting
in the same manner that it accepts remote public comment at its regular business meetings. No action
will be taken at this meeting.
This meeting will be streamed live and can be joined online by clicking the 'Video' button that will appear
above on the day of the meeting.
In-person attendance is also available:
