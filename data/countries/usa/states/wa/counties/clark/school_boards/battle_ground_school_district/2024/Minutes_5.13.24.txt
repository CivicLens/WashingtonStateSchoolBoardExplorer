2. Work Session
A. Wasingington Clean Building Act and Energy Service Company Discussion
Executive Director of Facilities and Operations, Kevin Jolma and Ameresco representative, Brian Jewett
explained the Clean Building Standards (CBS) that are required by law for every building in Washington State.
During the next six months, Ameresco will identify the path toward compliance per building and create an
Energy Management Plan for our buildings that will guide the implementation of energy-saving measures over
the next serval years. There are three phases of compliance dates of the CBS based on building size. Our only building
included in Phase 1 is BGHS.
