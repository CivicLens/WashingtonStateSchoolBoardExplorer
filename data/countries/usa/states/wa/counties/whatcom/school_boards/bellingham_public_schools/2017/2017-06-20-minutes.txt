APPOINT WSSDA LEGISLATIVE REPRESENTATIVE
Appoint 2017-18 School Board Legislative Representative
At the Dec. 15, 2016 regular meeting, the board agreed to the following:
 Camille Diaz Hackler would continue to serve as WSSDA Legislative
Representative through June 2017.
 In June 2017, the agenda would include the appointment of a legislative
representative for the ensuing year (July 1, 2017 - June 30, 2018)
 In June 2018, in accordance with Policy 1225, the board will appoint a legislative
representative for a two-year term (July 1, 2018 - June 30, 2020).
Following a brief discussion, Mr. Benjamin opened nominations for the position. Ms.
Peterson made a motion, seconded by Dr. Smith, to nominate Ms. Diaz Hackler for the
position of WSSDA Legislative Representative for a one-year term (July 1, 2017 through
June 30, 2018) and Ms. Diaz Hackler agreed to accept the position. Unanimous
affirmative vote.
