  B. SCHOOL SITE VISIT REPORT
1. Yakima Online - Principal Lois Menard
Principal Menard reported on Yakima Online (YOL) and Open Doors, highlighting significant growth from 153 to 687 students in five years. YOL, a diploma-only school, and Open Doors, offering GED and diploma options, aim to provide alternative educational pathways. She emphasized their missions focused on creating supportive, non-traditional learning environments for students. Key achievements include increased enrollment, the introduction of new courses, and a 10% increase in graduation rates for both programs. A survey revealed that students value the flexibility and the ability to work at their own pace. Director Rice and VP Villanueva raised questions about the programs' details and challenges. Community support, including resources from the faith community and local services, was acknowledged. The board discussed potential improvements and the importance of these alternative education options before visiting the program facilities.
The board left the portable to take a quick field trip to the other portables at 4:44pm. They returned at 5:00pm.
