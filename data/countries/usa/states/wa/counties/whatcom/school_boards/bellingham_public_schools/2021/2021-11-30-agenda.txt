Tuesday, November 30, 2021
School Board Visit - Parkview Elementary School
The Board of Directors of Bellingham Public Schools regularly visits schools throughout the school year.
The purpose of these visits is to learn how each school is delivering upon The Bellingham Promise. The
directors will visit Parkview Elementary School, 3030 Cornwall Avenue, Bellingham, at 7:30 a.m.,
Tuesday, November 30, 2021. This is not a regular school board meeting and will not include public
comment time.
For more information, call 360-676-6501.
