Tuesday, February 7, 2023
School Board Visit - Bellingham Family Partnership Program
The Board of Directors of Bellingham Public Schools regularly visits schools throughout the school year.
The purpose of these visits is to learn how each school is delivering upon The Bellingham Promise. The
directors will visit the Bellingham Family Partnership Program, 1409 18th Street, Bellingham, at 7:30 a.
m., Tuesday, Feb. 7, 2023. This is not a regular school board meeting and will not include public
comment time.
For more information, call 360-676-6501.
