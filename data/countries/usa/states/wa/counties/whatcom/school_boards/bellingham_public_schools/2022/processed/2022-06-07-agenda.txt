Tuesday, June 7, 2022
School Board Visit - Silver Beach Elementary School
The Board of Directors of Bellingham Public Schools regularly visits schools throughout the school year.
The purpose of these visits is to learn how each school is delivering upon The Bellingham Promise. The
directors will visit Silver Beach Elementary School, 4101 Academy Street, Bellingham, at 7:30 a.m.,
Tuesday, June 7, 2022. This is not a regular school board meeting and will not include public comment
time.
For more information, call 360-676-6501.
