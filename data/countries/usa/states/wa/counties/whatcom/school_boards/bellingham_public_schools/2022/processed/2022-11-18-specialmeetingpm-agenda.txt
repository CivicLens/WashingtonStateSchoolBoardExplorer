Friday, November 18, 2022
Special Meeting, 3:30 p.m.
The Board of Directors will attend the Washington State School Directors Association (WSSDA) Annual
Conference from Thursday, Nov. 17 to Saturday, Nov. 19, 2022 at the Spokane Convention Center in
Spokane, Washington.
While in Spokane, the board will hold a special meeting on Friday, Nov. 18 at 3:30 p.m. in the Skybridge
Boardroom of the Davenport Grand Hotel, 333 W Spokane Falls Boulevard, Spokane, WA. The purpose
of the meeting is to discuss policy governance items and for the superintendent to update the board on
items of interest.
This is not a regular school board meeting and will not include public comment time.
For more information, call 360-676-6501.
