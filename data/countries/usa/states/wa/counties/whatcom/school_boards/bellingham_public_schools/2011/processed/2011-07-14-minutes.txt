BELLINGHAM PUBLIC SCHOOLS
Bellingham, Washington
MINUTES OF THE SPECIAL MEETING
OF THE
BELLINGHAM SCHOOL DISTRICT
BOARD OF DIRECTORS
July 14, 2011
The Bellingham School Board of Directors met in special session July 14, 2011.
President Ann Whitmyer called the meeting to order.
At 5:15 p.m. the Board of Directors met at Whatcom Middle School for a tour of the school,
which is scheduled to open for students for the 2011–12 school year. Following the tour, the
Board reconvened at the District Office, 1306 Dupont Street. The Special Session was
reconvened at 6:40 p.m.

Roll Call
Present: Directors Kelly Bashaw; Ken Gass, Steve Smith; Scott Stockburger, President Ann
Whitmyer, Superintendent Greg Baker and recorder Nancy Merry.
During the public portion of the meeting, the Board discussed the following:
• Possible Community Linkages
• Work Priorities for the 2011–12 School Year
• Policy Governance Professional Development
• November 2011 Election

Adjournment
The meeting was adjourned at 9:15 p.m.
