Thursday, November 21, 2024
Special Board Meeting:Executive Session
While in attendance at the annual WSSDA Conference in Spokane, Washington, the directors will hold a
special meeting, moving into executive session, for the purpose of considering the selection of a site or
the acquisition of real estate by purchase. Telephonic public observation of this meeting's opening and
adjournment will be available locally in the districts boardroom at 770 Mitchell Avenue in Blaine.

President Freeman will call the meeting to order at 5:30 p.m.

Public Content
Pursuant to Policy 1410, the board may choose to convene in executive session for specific purposes
allowed by state statute. President Freeman will announce that the board will move into executive
session for the purpose of considering the selection of a site or the acquisition of real estate by purchase.
The executive session is anticipated to last approximately thirty (30) minutes, or until 6:00 p.m. Any
extension of time will be announced, and no final action will be taken by the board at this meeting.
