Thursday, November 16, 2023
Special Board Meeting:Executive Session
While in attendance at the annual WSSDA Conference in Bellevue, Washington, the directors will hold a
special meeting, moving into executive session, for the purpose of reviewing the performance of a public
employee. Telephonic public observation of this meeting's opening and adjournment will be available
locally in the districts boardroom at 770 Mitchell Avenue in Blaine.

President Thomas will call the meeting to order at 5:30 p.m. All directors are expected to be in
attendance while at the WSSDA Conference in Bellevue, Washington. Telephonic observation of the
meeting's opening and adjournment will be provided in the districts boardroom at 770 Mitchell Avenue in
Blaine.

Public Content
Pursuant to Policy 1410, the board may choose to convene in executive session for specific purposes
allowed by state statute. President Thomas will announce that the board will move into executive session
for the purpose of reviewing the performance of a public employee. The executive session is anticipated
to last until 7:30 p.m. Any extension of time will be announced, and no final action will be taken by the
board at this meeting.
