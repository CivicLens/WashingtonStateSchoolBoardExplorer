## 2021-07-26 - BLAINE_SCHOOL_DISTRICT (Minutes)

The Olympia School Board held a meeting where they approved the first reading of two new policies. Policy 5410 Holidays was updated and carried with a 4-0 vote.  Policy 5515 Workforce Secondary Traumatic Stress, focused on addressing staff emotional well-being, was also approved in its first reading. Dr. Granger explained that committee discussions related to this policy would focus on general practices rather than individual cases to protect privacy. The board discussed scheduling a special meeting for September 1st to address the SEIU agreement and considered holding executive sessions at 6:00 p.m. prior to regular meetings. Future agenda items include a public hearing on the 2021-2022 budget, a superintendent's report, consideration of the 2021-2022 budget adoption, approval of the 2021-2022 Highly Capable Program Plan, and policy updates. The meeting adjourned at 7:50 p.m.
