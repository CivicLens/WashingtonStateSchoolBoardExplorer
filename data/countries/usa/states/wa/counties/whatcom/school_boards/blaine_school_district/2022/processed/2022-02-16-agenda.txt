Public Content

As per legal guidelines, the board may choose to recess into executive session to consider employment or dismissal of personnel, to review
the performance of a public employee, to consult with legal counsel, to consider the position to be taken in collective bargaining, or to
consider acquisition or sale of real estate.

Prior to the start of executive session, the president will announce the purpose and length of the
session.
At the close of the executive session, the directors will reconvene in regular session at which point
they may take formal action or the meeting will be adjourned.
The board will hold an executive session for the purpose of discussing the appointment of a candidate to
the vacant board position representing Director District 5. The executive session will be no longer than
thirty (30) minutes. If there is consensus, the board may take action to appoint a new director after
returning to the regular open public meeting.
