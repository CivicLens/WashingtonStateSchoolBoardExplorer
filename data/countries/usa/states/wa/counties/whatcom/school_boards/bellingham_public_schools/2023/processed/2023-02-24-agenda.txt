2.01 Resolution 08-23, Declaration of an Emergency and
Waiver of Bidding Requirements Relating to Water
Damage Repairs of Whatcom Middle School

Meeting :

Feb 24, 2023 - Special Meeting

Type :

Action (Consent)

Recommended Action :

The attached resolution waives the competitive bidding
process for emergency repairs at Whatcom Middle School.
As superintendent I support the adoption of this resolution.

Public Content
Staff Contact: Jessica Sankey, Chief Operations Officer
Explanation of Issue:
Whatcom Middle School experienced extensive water damage from a major roof leak during a recent
winter storm which threatens the performance of the essential functions of the school unless immediate
repairs are made. It is requested that competetive bidding and other requirements of RCW 28A.335.190
and Chapter 39.04 RCW be waived in order to expedite the repairs.
