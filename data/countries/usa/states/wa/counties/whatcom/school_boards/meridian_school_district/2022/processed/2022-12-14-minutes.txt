MERIDIAN SCHOOL DISTRICT #505
MINUTES OF EXECUTIVE SESSION AND REGULAR SCHOOL BOARD MEETING
MERIDIAN SCHOOL DISTRICT
MEETING HELD VIA ZOOM
December 14, 2022
The regular meeting was called to order by Board Chair, Craig Mitchell at 7:00 p.m. Board Chair Mitchell welcomed
those in attendance.
Board members present at the meeting were Craig Mitchell, Steve Corwin, Marty Gray, and Sara Bernardy. Also in
attendance was student Board member Kurtis Kettman.
Board member Jarrod Karuza and student Board member Mirage DeNeal were excused from the meeting.
The Pledge of Allegiance was recited.
Sara Bernardy made a motion to adopt the agenda. Steve Corwin seconded the motion; the motion passed 4-0.
CONSENT AGENDA
A. Approval of Meeting Minutes
B. Accounts Payable
C. Personnel Report
D. Payroll
E. Student Travel
A motion to adopt the consent agenda was made by Sara Bernardy and seconded by Steve Corwin. The motion
passed 4-0.
ANNUAL REORGANIZATION OF SCHOOL BOARD
Marty Gray nominated Sara Bernardy to serve as Chair, Sara Bernardy accepted. Steve Corwin seconded the
motion. The motion passed 4-0
Sara Bernardy nominated Craig Mitchell to serve as Vice Chair, Marty Gray Seconded. Craig Mitchell accepted. The
motion passed 4-0.
PRESENTATIONS
MHS Staff Recognition
MHS Principal, Derek Forbes recognized the Sunshine Team and Coalition Team. He wanted to recognize this group
for their commitment to the school and culture and their leadership amongst colleagues.

SUPERINTENDENT’S REPORT
Superintendent, Dr. James Everett presented to the Board on the recent district events including Multilingual
Family Night, Desserts & Drinks with the Doc, and the Superintendent’s Advisory Council, Student Representative
Engagement, and the MMS and MHS Concerts. He then presented updates on the “Whatcom Career and Technical
Academy”. Following this, he presented the idea of updating the 2023-24 calendar and would bring it to the Board
at the next meeting. Then he provided an update that the maintenance team finished the handrails in the gym.
