HOOD CANAL SCHOOL DISTRICT
REGULAR BOARD OF EDUCATION MEETING
Thursday, July 25, 2024
  Meeting Minutes
    1. Opening Items
Members Present: Edie Reclusado, Annie Robbins, Jimmy Harris, Kimberly Miller and Paul Thompson.
  Staff Present: Lance Gibbon, Superintendent; Jeanie Beebe, Director of Finance and Operations, Susan Vining, Recording Secretary.
  Call to Order: Ms. Reclusado, Board Chair, called the meeting to order at 3:17pm in the Hood Canal School Library.
  2. Revision and Approval of Agenda
Ms. Robbins made a motion to approve the agenda as presented. Ms. Miller seconded. All were in favor, motion passed unanimously.
  3. Consent Agenda
Vouchers audited and certified by the auditing officer as required by RCW 42.24.080, and those expense reimbursement claims certified as required by RCW 42.24.090, have been recorded on a listing, which have been made available to the Board.
  Ms. Robbins made a motion to approve the consent agenda. Mr. Harris seconded. All were in favor, motion passed.
  4. Audience Comments
There were no audience comments.
  5. Personnel Addendum
Mr. Thompson made a motion to approve the personnel addendum as presented. Ms. Robbins seconded. All were in favor, motion passed.
  6. Board of Education Report
Ms. Reclusado expressed gratitude to the Board Directors and staff for their flexibility in adjusting their schedules for the meeting.
  7. Finance and Operations Report
The Board was provided with a detailed report of the district financials for all funds through the month of June. The funds were in balance and the district continues to be in a financially stable condition.
  Public Hearing was opened at 3:30pm for:
General Fund Budget Extension for SY 2023-2024
Budget Adoption for SY 2024-2025
There were no questions or comments. The Public Hearing was closed at 3:31pm.
  8. Superintendent Report
Dr. Gibbon provided an update on the following:
Summer school
New school district website
Refinishing the gym floor and timetable for installation of motorized bleachers
Installation of interactive projectors
  9. Action Item
Mr. Harris made a motion to approve the following action items:
A. 2024-2025 School Board Meeting Schedule
B. South Sound Parent to Parent Interagency Agreement for SY 2024-2025
C. Resolution 06-2324 Budget Adoption for FY 2024-2025
D. Resolution 07-2324 GF Budget Extension for FY 2023-2024
E. Surplus
  Ms. Robbins seconded. Motion passed.
  10. Future Dates
The future meeting dates were reviewed as stated on the agenda.
  11. Board Comments
Ms. Robbins praised art teacher Max Cowdery, along with the summer school students, for their efforts in updating the look of the pumphouse.
  Mr. Harris thanked the staff for another successful summer school.
  12. Adjournment
Mr. Thompson made a motion to adjourn the meeting. Mr. Harris seconded. All were in favor, motion passed unanimously. Meeting was adjourned at 4:05pm.
