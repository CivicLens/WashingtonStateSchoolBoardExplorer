COMMUNICATIONS
a. Lane Stremler started a conversation about gender inclusion policies in public schools and ensuring
safety for all students. A number of community members raised questions about sex segregated
facilities and activities. Supt. Galley explained the policies and supports that have been put into
place, and explained where students can go if they request greater privacy. Parents and community
members were directed to reach out to school administration or counselors if they have further
concerns.

b. Steve VanMiddendorp commented on ensuring continued transparency and communication
between schools parents to ensure trust is maintained.
c. Hannah Ordos commented on the common desire to be preventative, and how important it is that
the district continues to encourage parents in our schools, and how eye opening it can be to be in
schools.
d. Katie DeVries raised awareness around potential challenges of communication during natural
disasters. She questioned how the district would ensure communication between schools and
parents. The district will research this question.
