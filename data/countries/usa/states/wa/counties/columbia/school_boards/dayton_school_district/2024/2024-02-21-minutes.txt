  4. REPORTS
The reports section of tonight's meeting was postponed so board members could discuss the qualifications of the superintendent candidates in executive session. All reports were submitted electronically for board review however due to the time constraints, the FFA trip request was presented in person. Board reports can be viewed on Board Docs for tonight's meeting.
  A. FFA Trip Presentation - Melanie Morgan
FFA Advisor Melanie Morgan presented information on the request for the FFA trapshooting team to attend the FFA State Trapshooting Conference in Wenatchee. Also present were chaperone Lucas Collier and students Lyam Donohue, Luke Collier and Andrew Wertz. In addition to the contest students would also tour the Aplets & Cotlets facility and the Rocky Reach Discovery Center. Students would leave Dayton on April 18th and would return the evening of the 20th.

  7. EXECUTIVE SESSION
  The board went into executive session at 6:50 p.m. to discuss the qualifications of a candidate for public employment per RCW 42.30.110. The session is expected to last for 2 hours with possible action to follow. At 8:50 p.m. the session was extended for another 15 minutes. The board returned to regular session at 9:05 p.m. after reviewing nine candidates for the position of superintendent.
  Board member Dieu moved seconded by director Paulson to interview the following superintendent candidates on March 6, 2024.
Dr. Jodi Thew
Guin Joyce
Jeremy Wheatley
Motion carried 5-0
