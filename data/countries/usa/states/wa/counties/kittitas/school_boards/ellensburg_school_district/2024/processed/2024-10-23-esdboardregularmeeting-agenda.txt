Wednesday, October 23, 2024
ESD Board Regular Meeting
The Ellensburg School District Board of Directors will hold a School Board Regular Meeting on
Wednesday, October 23, 2024 at 5:30 PM at Ida Nason Aronica Elementary School. In the interest of
public health, the meeting will also be available via Zoom. Comments can be submitted to the Board at
board@esd401.org.
