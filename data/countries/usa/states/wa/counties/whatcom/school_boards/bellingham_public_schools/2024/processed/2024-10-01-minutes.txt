School Visit
The board heard from the school principal about the work of school staff around our
social emotional learning curriculum and its connections to our IB (International
Baccalaureate) work, TPEP (Teacher Principal Evaluation Program) and UDL (Universal
Design for Learning). A rubric was shared that the board then used as they visited
classrooms throughout the building. The visit ended with a debrief of take-aways from
the visit.
