TOUTLE LAKE SCHOOL DISTRICT #130
Board of Director’s Meeting
Thursday, December 21, 2023 @ 6:00 pm in the High School Library
MINUTES
Members Present: Board Chairman Sherry Schwinn, Board of Directors Susan Dorcheus; Heather Schoonover (via Zoom)
and Janell Wheatley. Superintendent Scott Fenter
1. Flag Salute – Flag salute was led by Sherry Schwinn
2. Approval of Minutes of the Regular Board Meeting on November 28, 2023
Motion: Director Janell Wheatley made a motion to approve minutes of the regular board meeting on November
28, 2023 as presented. The motion unanimously carried by a vote of 4-0.
3. Adoption of Agenda
Motion: Director Janell Wheatley made a motion to amend the agenda to add Executive session. The motion
unanimously carried by a vote of 4-0.
4. Consent Agenda
A. Payroll and Vouchers
B. Personnel
Motion: Director Janell Wheatley made a motion to approve the consent agenda as presented. The motion
unanimously carried by a vote of 4-0.
5. Reports
A. Business Manager
See handouts
B. Elementary
In Len Hiatt’s absence, Scott Fenter recognized the teachers for working hard on the 4th grade shift.
C. Secondary
Scott Fenter in Henry Karnofski’s absence informed the board how the HS boys basketball team played in
Ellensburg at their game today, but lost in OT. He also shared that the HS will be going back to a 7-period
schedule due to the influx of new students.
D. ASB
Mason Grose shared with the board they will be holding a MLK Assembly on January 5th and a Winter
Social on January 27th at Dave Schaffran’s Barn. The theme for the dance will be “Winter Wonderland”.
E. PTO
No Report
F. Superintendent
Scott Fenter updated the board about the emergency grant for the MPR roof repair. Dillon and Kirk
Reinbold are getting bids for the shop wiring using the John Moffitt Grant, and that he has a meeting with
Nathan McCann tomorrow to go over the 5-6 applicants for the superintendent position.

8. Old Business
A. Approval of Revised Policies and Procedures
2162P – Education of Students with Disabilities Procedure
2230 – Transition to Kindergarten Program
3122 – Excused and Unexcused Absences
3207/P – Prohibition of Harassment, Intimidation, and Bullying of Students & Procedure
3225 – School-Based Threat Assessment
Motion: Director Susan Dorcheus made a motion to approve revised policies and procedures 2162P –
Education of Students with Disabilities Procedure; 2230 – Transition to Kindergarten Program; 3207/P –
Prohibition of Harassment, Intimidation, and Bullying of Students & Procedure & 3225 – School-Based Threat
Assessment as presented; and table policy 3122 – Excused and Unexcused Absences until the January meeting.
The motion unanimously carried by a vote of 4-0.
9. New Business
A. Jennifer Halleck – Presentation of Modernization Grant Process
Motion: Director Janell Wheatley made a motion to approve pursuing the modernization grant process with ESD
112 as presented. The motion unanimously carried by a vote of 4-0.

Motion: Director Janell Wheatley made a motion to approve the amendment to the ASB Constitution “Article
VIII Section 3” as presented. The motion unanimously carried by a vote of 4-0.
10. Executive Session – Personnel
The Board adjourned to executive session at 6:50 pm for 15 minutes. Sherry Schwinn came out at 7:05 pm stating
it would be another 12 to 15 minutes. At 7:17 pm the meeting was called back to regular session. No action
taken.
