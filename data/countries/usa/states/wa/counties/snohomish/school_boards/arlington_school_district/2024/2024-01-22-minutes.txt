Informational Items
A. Superintendent's Report
Superintendent Sweeting shared that this week is the end of first semester. High school students will be taking finals, and all schools will be closed on Monday, January 29th, for grading. 
Thursday, January 25th, Eagle Creek and Kent Prairie Elementary Schools will be hosting Winter Olympic Games at Post Middle School. Families are invited to attend and participate in the fun. There will be games and activities, as well as a tour of Post Middle School. The event starts at 6:00 pm. Dr. Sweeting commented that last week was a crazy week with the snow and ice conditions throughout the district. She thanked Brian Lewis, Charity Prueher and Julie Davidson for driving the roads in the district during the early hours of the morning to assess road conditions each day. She informed the Board that in addition to road conditions, Stillaguamish Valley Learning Center's water pipes were frozen which resulted in their campus being closed for several days, and Eagle Creek Elementary lost electricity due to a transformer problem. Snohomish County PUD responded quickly to replace the transformer, and students were able to return to school today.
  B. Legislative Update and Discussion
Director Kelly shared that Wednesday, January 29th, is the bill submission cutoff date for this legislative session. After the 29th, she will be able to see what education bills will be moved forward. This is the third week of the legislative session, and there are seven more weeks to go. She will be attending the Legislative Conference this Sunday. She mentioned that House Bill HB1368 is being proposed, and it would require and fund the purchase of zero emission school buses. She will watch the progress of this bill, as it would have a significant impact on our district in the future.
