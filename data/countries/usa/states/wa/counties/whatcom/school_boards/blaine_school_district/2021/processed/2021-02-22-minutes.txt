Blaine School District No. 503
  Special Board Meeting: Executive Session
Monday, February 22, 2021
  Minutes
  1. Special Board Meeting
A. Call to Order
Due to restrictions resulting from the COVID-19 pandemic emergency, this meeting was conducted with remote director participation. President Gibson called the meeting to order at 6:01 p.m.
All Directors were in attendance.
     Todd Nunamaker, Dougal Thomas, Joan Lotze, Charles E Gibson, Laura McKinney
  2. Executive Session
A. Executive Session
President Gibson announced that the board would move immediately into executive session for the purpose of reviewing the performance of a public employee. The executive session was anticipated to last for no more than one (1) hour. No action would be taken by the board after the executive session.

The directors left the open meeting and transitioned to the closed session. The executive session started at 6:04 p.m.
  3. Reconvene and Adjourn
A. Reconvene and Adjourn
After the executive session, the board transitioned back to the open meeting and reconvened in regular session at 6:52 p.m. Due to technical difficulties, President Gibson was not able to participate in the open meeting. There being no further business, Vice-President McKinney called for a motion and the meeting was adjourned. 
Motion by Joan Lotze, second by Todd Nunamaker
Final Resolution: Motion Carries 4-0, 1 "not present" at vote
Yea: Todd Nunamaker, Dougal Thomas, Joan Lotze, Laura McKinney
Not Present at Vote: Charles E Gibson
