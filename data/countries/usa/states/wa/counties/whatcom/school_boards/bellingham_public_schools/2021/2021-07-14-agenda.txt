Staff Contact: Curtis Lawyer, Director Capital Projects

Explanation of Issue:
Because the properties at Sunnyland Elementary School are divided by the public right of way (ROW), E.
Maryland Street and the alley between James and King Streets, any utilities that pass between the
properties encroach in the public ROW. This is true of the stormwater and electrical piping to be put in
place as part of the construction for the new Sunnyland Elementary School. The legal document to allow
for construction of our infrastructure in the public ROW is an Encroachment License between the City of
Bellingham and Bellingham Public Schools.
