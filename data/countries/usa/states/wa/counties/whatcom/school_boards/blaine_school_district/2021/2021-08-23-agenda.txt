Monday, August 23, 2021
Board Work Session: Strategic & Campus Plans
The purpose of this work session is to review the District's 3-year Strategic Plan as well as annual School
Improvement Plans. Pursuant to the Governor's Proclamation 20-28.14 related to the COVID-19
pandemic emergency, this work session will include both virtual and in-person components for
participation. Live public observation of this work session will be available online. Click on the "Video" link
above to access this work session as an observer. Anyone who desires to attend in person must
preregister by calling the District Office at 360-332-5881 no later than 4pm on the day of the work
session. Symptom screening and facial coverings are required for all in-person attendees.
