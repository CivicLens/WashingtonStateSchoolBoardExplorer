5. Reports
Superintendent
• Reopening Updates
Jim Frey provided an update on the recent information from Governor Inslee’s Proclamation of
reopening plans for K-12 students. These plans included graduation, social gatherings, and
social distancing recommendations. Jim shared that Lynden School District employee’s have
taken part in vaccination clinics and have a result of 60% of staff being vaccinated, with more
opportunities available. Jim spoke about the recommendation from Governor Inslee to
transition from a 6ft. to 3ft. social distancing in a school environment and the challenges and

impacts of deciding what is best for students and staff. Jim explained the case rate within a
community could have an impact on the decision of a 6ft to 3ft consideration based on county
wide transmission.
