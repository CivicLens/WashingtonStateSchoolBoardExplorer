CALL TO ORDER

Convene 3:02 pm
1.01 Establishment of Quorum - The special school board meeting was called to order
at 3:00 p.m. at the school district office. A quorum was established with Directors Barbara Bevens, T.J. Heller, John Kurtz, and Brian Moore.
1.02 Pledge of Allegiance
1.03 Approval of Agenda – There were no changes to the agenda. Director Bevens
considered the agenda approved.
1.04 Public Comment – There were no public comments.

SPECIAL TOPIC

2.01 Interviews to Fill the Board Vacancy for School Board Director Position #1 (Interviews) – The board interviewed candidates for position #1, vacated on July 31, 2024,
by Sarah Werling-Sandwith. This was a public meeting for a formal interview process.
The successful candidate was to fill the board vacancy until the term ending in November, 2025. The interviews for a replacement board member took place at the
following times:
• Beth Spaulding 3:05 pm - 3:18 pm
• Melanie Hess 3:21 pm - 3:41 pm
Each board member asked pre-determined questions, in turn. A short break was
taken between 3:18 pm and 3:21 pm.
2.02 Executive Session as per RCW 42.30 110(h) 3:41 pm – 3:58 pm (Action) – After
the interviews, at 3:41 pm, the board recessed into executive session for 15 minutes
(until 3:56 pm) in accordance with RCW 42.30.110(h), to evaluate the qualifications
of candidate for appointment to elective office. At 3:56 pm Board Chair Bevens
requested an additional 2 minutes. At 3:58 pm the board re-convened into public
session. No action had been taken during the executive session.
2.03 Selection of New Board Member (Action) – The board reconvened after the
executive session. Director Kurtz moved to nominate Melanie Hess to fill the position
#1 vacancy on the board. Director Bevans seconded the motion. The board roll call
vote was as follows:
