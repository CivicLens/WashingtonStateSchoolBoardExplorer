School Board Special Meeting Minutes
August 7, 2024, 3:00 p.m.
Oak Harbor Public Schools
Board Study Session

350 S. Oak Harbor Street, Oak Harbor, WA 98277

360.279.5000

A special meeting of the Board of Directors was held on Wednesday, August 7, 2024,
from 3:00-7:00 p.m. in the Administrative Service Center Board Room.
Board members Lynn Goebel, Jessica Aws, Nicole Tesch, Sharon Jensen, Josh MacLean,
and student board representatives Maggie Litvachuk and Allena Locklear were present.
Superintendent Michelle Kuss-Cybula, CFO Amber Porter, and Communications Officer
Sarah Foy were also present. The board discussed budget, calendars, and goal-setting.
No final board action was taken.

Minutes prepared by Katey Andrews.

Michelle Kuss-Cybula Ed.D., Board Secretary

Lynn Goebel, Board President
