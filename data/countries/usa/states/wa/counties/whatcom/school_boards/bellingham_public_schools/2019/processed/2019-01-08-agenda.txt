Tuesday, January 8, 2019
School Board Visit - Roosevelt Elementary School
The Board of Directors of Bellingham Public Schools regularly visits a different school throughout the
school year. The purpose of these visits is to learn how each school is delivering upon The Bellingham
Promise. The directors will visit Roosevelt Elementary School, 2900 Yew Street, at 7:30 a.m., Tuesday,
January 8, 2019.
