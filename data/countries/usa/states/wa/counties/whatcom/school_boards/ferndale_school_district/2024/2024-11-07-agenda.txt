Governor Inslees Amendment to Proclamation 20-05 waives and suspends the portions of RCW 42.30
and RCW 42.56 that require in-person meetings or contact.
Also, in order to conduct essential business of the district, the school board will not receive oral
comments during its meeting. We ask the public, upon entering the online meeting, to please disable their
audio. This will enable all persons to readily hear deliberations by board members.
Persons wishing to make public comment to the school board may do so through the board's online
public comment portal. To access the portal, please visit this link https://www.ferndalesd.org/school-board
/school-board-public-comment-form.
Comments received will be read during the meeting by Mr. McLaurin.
Also, at any time that you would like to contact your board members, you may do so by using their
contact information that is displayed on this webpage at this link https://www.ferndalesd.org/school-board
