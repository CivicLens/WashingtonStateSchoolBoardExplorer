POLICY GOVERNANCE MONITORING REPORT
Monitoring Report EL-4, Staff Treatment, Compensation and Evaluation
Dr. Gass said all board members have reviewed Monitoring Report EL-4. He commended the
Human Resources Department for a thorough report and for its work in all aspects of staff
treatment, compensation, and evaluation, particularly in view of the work required with the
implementation of the Washington State Teacher and Principal Evaluation Project (TPEP).
BOARD RESPONSE TO MONITORING
Monitoring Report EL-4, Staff Treatment, Compensation and Evaluation
The Board confirmed the Superintendent is in compliance with Monitoring Report EL-4,
Compensation and Evaluation.
Camille Hackler said she would like to ensure the district is consistent in meeting HCL needs at
the middle school level, including staffing.
Dr. Stockburger asked when the Affirmative Action plan would be updated. In response, Dr.
Gass said this is a multi-year plan, and the Human Resources Department updates the Board
annually on its status.
POLICY GOVERNANCE REVIEW
EL-6, Academic Standards and Practices
At the June 27, 2013 meeting, the Board heard the Monitoring Report for EL-6 and confirmed
the Superintendent is in compliance. It was the consensus of the Board to accept the report with
no changes.
INFORMATION ITEMS
School Board Directors’ Reports
Dr. Stockburger will have a luncheon meeting with Dr. Baker on July 17.
Ms. Hackler volunteered at Alderwood Elementary School and helped the PTA upgrade the
school garden with use of bricks salvaged from the demolition of the old Birchwood Elementary
School.
Dr. Gass participated today in a video conference sponsored by the Washington State School
Directors’ Association (WSSDA) as part of the year-long Outcomes-Based Superintendent
Evaluations project.
Dr. Gass also referenced a link to the State Board of Education website, which includes minutes
of their meetings. He noted that at the meeting held earlier this month, topics included a
discussion of school districts that had applied to be charter school authorizers; also, a summary
was presented of the new regulations being written for those districts authorizing charter schools.
