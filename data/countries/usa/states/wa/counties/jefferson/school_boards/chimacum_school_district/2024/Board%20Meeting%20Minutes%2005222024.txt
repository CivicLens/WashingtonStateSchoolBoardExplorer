Superintendent/Management Reports
Special Education Program Update
Inclusionary practices director Sarah Walker presented an update on the Special education program in
the school district. Highlighting what important practices they focus on and what important practices
they are working on to bring to the program in the future. She stressed that the program’s success
cannot be achieved over night and thanked her staff for all their hard work.
Budget Report
Assistant Superintendent Art Clarke presented a brief presentation on what the budget for the school
year 2024-25 looked like. He shared the fund balance and explained how much of a deficit we will be
facing. He explained how we have secured positions in the school district by strategically hiring staff to
meet the district’s needs.
Superintendent Report
Dr. Mauk updated the board on the CES principal hiring process. He shared the dates and times the
interviews will take place and the process that is set in place for site visits and public forums.
Student Director Report
Student directors Reid Martin and Isaac Johnson presented their report on what is happening with the
band and the parades they are getting ready to participate in, ASB activities and class updates.
Public Comments (General Comments)
Devon Buckham made a public comment about the AAUW thanking them for all the work they are doing
to help our students succeed.
Brian MacKenzie made a public comment on behalf of the CEA.
Chloe Mouser made a public comment highlighting her concerns about using the toilets in the schools
and requested toilet seat covers to be available.
Executive Session RCW 42.30.110 (E)
The board proceeded to a short recess at 7:26pm and reconvened at 7:30pm for an estimated 60
minutes for an Executive Session. No action to follow.
RCW 42.30.110 Executive Sessions
E. To evaluate the qualifications of an applicant for public employment or to review the performance of
a staff member; however, discussion of salaries, wages, and other conditions of employment to be
generally applied within the district will occur in a meeting open to the public, and when the board
elects to take the final action of hiring, setting the salary of an individual staff member or class of staff
members, or discharging or disciplining an employee, that action will be taken in a meeting open to the
public;
