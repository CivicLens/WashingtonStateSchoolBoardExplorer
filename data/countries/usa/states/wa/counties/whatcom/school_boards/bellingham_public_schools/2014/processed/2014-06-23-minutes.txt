DRAFT MINUTES OF THE SPECIAL MEETING OF THE
BELLINGHAM SCHOOL DISTRICT
BOARD OF DIRECTORS
June 23, 2014
The Bellingham School Board of Directors held a special meeting June 23, 2014 at the District
Office, 1306 Dupont Street, Bellingham, Washington.
Vice President Kelly Bashaw called the meeting to order at 6 p.m.
Roll Call
Present: Directors Kelly Bashaw, Ken Gass, Camille Hackler, Superintendent Greg Baker, and
Recorder Ruth Rasmussen. Board President Steve Smith, who had an out-of-town commitment,
listened to the session via a conference call.

Agenda
Dr. Gass facilitated an orientation session for individuals who may be interested in serving on the
school board. Discussion focused on handouts which included Orientation for School Board
Candidates (attached) and Board Participation/Time Expectations (attached).
Adjournment
The meeting adjourned at 7:10 p.m.
