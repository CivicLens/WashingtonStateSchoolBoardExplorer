4. Reports
Superintendent's Report:  Kelly Garza, Superintendent, reported:
He attended the 2024 NAFIS Fall Conference. The purpose of the conference is to advocate for legislative action to continue and increase funding of Impact Aid.  Mr. Garza talked about conference events and had the opportunity to meet with Senators Cantwell and Murray.  He informed impact aid has not been fully funded since 1936 and currently Wapato SD is funded at 40%.
Tomorrow is the 1st PACE Design Team meeting. The purpose of the committee is to develop and design educational specifications for the new PACE High School construction project. Staff, Students, and patrons have committed to participate in this process.
Kevin Hill, Executive Director reported representatives from ALSC Architects visited PACE HS last week to meet with staff to gather feedback and input of classroom needs and wants for the new PACE HS. This information will be used to draft tentative square footage based on those needs. Mr. Garza invited the Board to participate in this process. He commented the District will embark in the same process when its ready to begin the design for the new elementary school.
Safety and Security in Wapato Public Schools: Tavis Peterson, Assistant Superintendent
Presented the District’s procedures, practices, implementation and training in Physical, Emotional and Behavioral Safety.  The Board engaged in discussion regarding Safety and Security.
WSD Student Attendance Report: Maria Batarao, Executive Director of Instructional Technology and Assessment, presented information about the District’s current attendance policy and process, explained the difference between chronic absenteeism vs truancy, the role of Community Engagement Board, and awareness information. The Board engaged in discussion.
Yakama Tribal Council Update: Elese Washines, Tribal Council Liaison was not present and did not report.
Financial Report: Sylvia Bazan, Director of Fiscal Services, presented the August 2024 Cash Flow Analysis report. She explained in detail the expenditures and revenues for each fund category. She noted the District is expecting to receive 5 new buses previously ordered that will reduce the presented amount in the Transportation Vehicle Fund.  She explained and discussed the Vehicle Surplus recommendation that is proposed for action. Mrs. Bazan explained the fund balance designations as presented on the report. The Board engaged in discussion regarding Compensated Absences and Unemployment Co-op.
