EXECUTIVE SESSION & REGULAR SCHOOL BOARD MEETING

EXECUTIVE SESSION & REGULAR MEETING
The meeting was called to order by Board President Steve Cowin at 5:07 p.m.
Board Directors Steve Corwin, Jarrod Karuza, Marty Gray, and Michelle Simmons were in attendance.
Jana Haugen was absent from the meeting.
EXECUTIVE SESSION
The board moved immediately into executive session for the purpose of reviewing the performance of a
public employee. No action was taken during the executive session.
ADJOURN EXECUTIVE SESSION
The executive Session was adjourned at 5:51 pm
RECONVENE REGULAR BOARD MEETING
Board President Steve Corwin welcomed everyone to the regular board meeting at 6:00 pm.
Student Board Representatives Kennedy Brzozowski and Morgan Adams were present at the regular
meeting.
The pledge of allegiance was recited.
A motion to adopt the agenda was made by Marty Gray and seconded by Jarrod Karuza. The motion
passed 4-0.
CONSENT AGENDA
A. Approval of Meeting Minutes
B. Personnel Report
C. Accounts Payable
D. Payroll
E. Student Travel
F. Annual Title l Contract with Bellingham School District
A motion to adopt the consent agenda was made by Jarrod Karuza and seconded by Michelle Simmons.
The motion passed 4-0.
RECOGNITION
MMS Student Recognition
MMS Principal, Todd Torgeson recognized MMS students for their work on National History Day
projects. Several MMS students gave an overview report of their National History Day projects.
REPORTS
PLC Presentations
The first through fourth grade Literacy team at MP3 presented a PLC report to the Board. The report
focused on second grade reading and writing fluency. The report included the rubrics used, progress
monitoring data, and techniques used to grow skills.

The MMS and MHS Spanish team presented a combined Spanish PLC report to the Board. The report
focused on the skills the team wants students to know, how they will know if students know the
content, and the tools used to increase student performance. The team shared data from three data
points.
IRE second grade team presented a narrative writing PLC report to the Board. The presentation included
the skills the team wants students to know, how they will know if students know the content, and the
tools used to increase student performance.
MMS School Report
MMS Principal Todd Torgeson and Assistant Principal Robert Kratzig presented a MMS school report to
the Board. Information was shared about the MMS students and staff, clubs and activities, the focus on
two-way communication with families, providing targeted support to students and physical & social
emotional well-being. Mr. Torgeson and Mr. Kratzig highlighted assessments, and celebrated the
learning and behavior growth that is happening at MMS.
PUBLIC COMMENT
There was no public comment.
