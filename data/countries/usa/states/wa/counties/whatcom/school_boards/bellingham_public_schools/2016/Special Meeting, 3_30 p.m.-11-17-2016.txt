Thursday, November 17, 2016
Special Meeting, 3:30 p.m.
The Board of Directors will attend the Washington State School Directors Association (WSSDA) Annual
Conference from Wednesday, Nov. 16 to Saturday, Nov. 19 at the Spokane Convention Center in
Spokane, Washington.
While in Spokane, the board will hold a special meeting on Thursday, Nov. 17 at 3:30 p.m. in Room 6 of
The Davenport Grand, 333 W. Spokane Falls Blvd, Spokane, WA. The purpose of the meeting is to
discuss topics relating to policy governance, including board advocacy and superintendent-board
communication.
