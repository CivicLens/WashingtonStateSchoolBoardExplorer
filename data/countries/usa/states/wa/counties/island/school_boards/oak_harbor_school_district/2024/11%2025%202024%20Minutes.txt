Call to Order

A regular meeting of the Board of Directors was held on Monday, November 25, 2024,
in the Administrative Service Center Board Room and was available for live streaming.
President Lynn Goebel called the meeting to order at 6:00 p.m. and led the flag salute.

Roll Call

Board members Lynn Goebel, Sharon Jensen, Nicole Tesch, and Josh MacLean were
present. Board member Jessica Aws was absent and excused. Student board
representatives Maggie Litvachuk and Allena Locklear were present.

Adoption of Agenda

The agenda was adopted by unanimous consent.

Consent Agenda

Director MacLean moved to approve the consent agenda. Director Tesch seconded the
motion. The motion passed unanimously.

Superintendent
Report

Superintendent Michelle Kuss-Cybula presented information on instructional reviews,
attendance, data, standards, and the ELA curriculum pilot and how they align to the
district strategic plan, including the ways the district is engaging with the community.
Dr. Kuss-Cybula provided a school construction update. She shared about the recent
day of walking in students’ shoes to monitor the student vision experience. Students
shared their experience of having Superintendent Kuss-Cybula and Assistant
Superintendent Dwight Lundstrom attend school with them for the day.

Student
Representative
Report

Student Representatives Maggie Litvachuk and Allena Locklear spoke about their goals,
the new student-superintendent group, area student representative retreat, and the
land acknowledgement. Allena Locklear highlighted the student vision experience.

Levy Presentation

Dr. Kuss-Cybula presented information about the upcoming renewal levy which
included an announcement about appointing for and against committees for the Voter
Pamphlet.

Recognitions

The Trunk or Treat community organizers, North Whidbey Middle School staff, and
Administrative Service Center Staff were recognized.

Public Comment

Casara Elliff, parent, spoke about parent rights. Corey Elliff, parent, spoke about parent
rights. Allie Hanford, parent, spoke about special education. Students from the Robotics
Club spoke about installing electric vehicle chargers at district sites.

October 2024
Financial Report

Chief Financial Officer Amber Porter presented the October 2024 financial report.

Legislative Update

Director Tesch spoke about the upcoming Legislative Session and the new legislative
website.

Board Comments

Director Tesch shared about the Mr. Oak Harbor event and parent-teacher conferences.
She expressed her thankfulness for the community and district. President Goebel
mentioned the North Whidbey Middle School instructional review, the board meeting
recognitions, and the levy presentation.
