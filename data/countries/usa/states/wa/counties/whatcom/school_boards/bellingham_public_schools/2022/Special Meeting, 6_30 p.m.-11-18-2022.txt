Friday, November 18, 2022
Special Meeting, 6:30 p.m.
The Board of Directors will attend the Washington State School Directors Association (WSSDA) Annual
Conference from Thursday, Nov. 17 to Saturday, Nov. 19, 2022 at the Spokane Convention Center in
Spokane, Washington.
While in Spokane, the board will hold a special meeting on Friday, Nov. 18 at 6:30 p.m. at Spencer's for
Steak and Chops, 322 N. Spokane Falls Court, Spokane, WA. The purpose of the meeting is to discuss
school district initiatives, issues and business in an unstructured manner. This is not a regular School
Board meeting and will not include public comment time.
For more information, call 360-676-6501.
