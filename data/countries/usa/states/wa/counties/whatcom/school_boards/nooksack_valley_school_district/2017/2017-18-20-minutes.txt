MINUTES APPROVED
REGULAR MEETING

There were no formal communications.

COMMUNICATIONS

The Nooksack Valley Counseling Team was present to discuss
With The Board the work that they have been doing in our schools.
They shared about their roles, how they work as a team, and provided
specific information a) how they have aligned our mission b) bullying,
and c) suicide/self-harm. They also talked about how they are tied into
the attendance, parent engagement, and resilience work through each
schools’ improvement plan. Dialogue occurred throughout and the
School Board was very appreciative of their work.
