Call to Order

A regular meeting of the Board of Directors was held on Monday, September 30, 2024,
in the Administrative Service Center Board Room and was available for live streaming.
President Lynn Goebel called the meeting to order at 6:00 p.m. and led the flag salute.

Roll Call

Board members Lynn Goebel, Jessica Aws, Nicole Tesch, Sharon Jensen, and Josh
MacLean were present. Student board representatives Maggie Litvachuk and Allena
Locklear were present.

Adoption of Agenda

The agenda was adopted by unanimous consent.

Consent Agenda

Director MacLean moved to approve the consent agenda. Director Jensen seconded the
motion. The motion passed unanimously.

Superintendent
Report

Superintendent Dr. Michelle Kuss-Cybula presented the goal areas of the district
Strategic Plan and demonstrated progress by discussing a forthcoming community
mailer, Impact Aid, a construction parent night at Hand-in-Hand/HomeConnection,
groundbreaking ceremonies for the school construction projects, a ribbon cutting
ceremony for playground communication boards, early learning work, and instructional
reviews.

Superintendent Goal
Setting

Dr. Kuss-Cybula presented her draft goals to the board for consideration.

Student
Representative
Report

Student Board Representatives Maggie Litvachuk and Allena Locklear spoke about the
Strategic Plan Student Vision Statement, the start of the school year, and upcoming
school activities and events.

recognized the work of Vic Rikard, Student Union Building Coordinator.

staff who prepared and served meals to students over the summer.

Board Comments

Director Tesch spoke about making a public comment at a recent City Council meeting
regarding a derelict vehicle near North Whidbey Middle School. She highlighted the
upcoming Mr. Oak Harbor event. Director MacLean commented on the importance of
the levy and thanked the community members who made public comment during the
meeting. Director Jensen also emphasized the importance of the levy and voter turnout.
President Goebel agreed and shared her excitement about the recognitions that were
celebrated during the meeting.

Superintendent
Comments

Dr. Kuss Cybula shared about a recent meeting with the police chief about new
municipal code language and thanked Director Tesch for speaking at a City Council
meeting. She highlighted the approaching Homecoming events and thanked the board
and presenters for the discussions.

Upcoming Meetings

Regular meetings are scheduled for Monday, October 14, and Monday, October 28,
2024, at 6:00 p.m. in the Administrative Service Center Board Room.
