OPENING ITEMS
Meeting Date and Location
12:35 p.m., February 24, 2023 – Hotel Bellwether Board Room, 1 Bellwether Way,
Bellingham, Washington.
Roll Call:
Ms. Diaz Hackler called the meeting to order at 12:45 p.m. Directors Kelly Bashaw,
Douglas Benjamin, Camille Diaz Hackler (President), Jenn Mason, Katie Rose and
Superintendent Greg Baker were present.
INFORMATIONAL ITEMS/DISCUSSION
Board Improvement Plan
• The board reviewed the history of the board’s use of the WSSDA board selfassessment and reviewed the latest research/adjustments coming to the tool.
• The board discussed possible items for consideration for a future conversation on
board improvement.
Student Voice
The board discussed the idea of student involvement on the board, particularly in the
context of policy governance. The board validated the importance of student voice and
appreciates the student roundtables. One idea for future consideration is the board having
a student roundtable with the Student Advisory Committee to the Superintendent, which
is one way the superintendent gathers student input.
Regional Skills Center
The board was informed of a request by another district for a resolution for a potential
future Whatcom County skills center. The board reviewed the history of skills centers in
our region.
Superintendent’s Mid-Year Report/Evaluation
The board discussed accomplishments and bodies of work this year from the
superintendent. The board acknowledged the hard work from the superintendent and staff
and looks forward to continued partnership.

Adjournment – 5:30 p.m.
