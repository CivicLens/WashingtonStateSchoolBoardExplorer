OPENING ITEMS
Meeting Date and Location
1 p.m., February 25, 2022 – via Zoom.
Roll Call:
Ms. Bashaw called the meeting to order at 1 p.m. Directors Kelly Bashaw (President),
Douglas Benjamin, Camille Diaz Hackler, Jenn Mason, Katie Rose and Superintendent
Greg Baker were present.
INFORMATIONAL ITEMS/DISCUSSION
Review of Policy EL-9, Asset Maintenance and Protection: The board discussed
possible changes to EL-9 (Asset Maintenance and Protection) and discussed thoughts
about future information to be considered in the policy monitoring reports.
Review of Policy EL-10, Race and Equity: The board discussed whether possible
changes should be made to EL-10 (Race and Equity) and discussed thoughts about future
information to be considered in the policy monitoring reports.
Linkages/Study Sessions/Roundtable Review: The board discussed thoughts on
whether to change/modify any upcoming study sessions, linkages or roundtables.
Board Improvement Plan Review: The board reviewed progress on their own
improvement plan.
Discussion of Board Routines: The board discussed the structure for board meetings,
board dinner meetings and the National School Board Association (NSBA).
Superintendent’s Mid-Year Report/Evaluation: The board heard highlights from the
school year and provided feedback to the superintendent on his performance.
