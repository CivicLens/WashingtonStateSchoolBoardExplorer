DRAFT MINUTES OF THE SPECIAL MEETING OF
THE BELLINGHAM PUBLIC SCHOOLS BOARD OF DIRECTORS
May 19, 2015

The Bellingham School Board of Directors held a special meeting May 19, 2015 at the District
Office, 1306 Dupont Street, Bellingham, Washington.
President Kelly Bashaw called the meeting to order at 12:00 p.m.
Roll Call
Present: Directors Kelly Bashaw, Douglas Benjamin, Camille Hackler, Steven Smith, Ken Gass
and Superintendent Greg Baker.
District Administrators/Staff: Assistant Superintendent, Human Resources, Bob Kuehl;
Executive Director, Human Resources Nora Klewiada and recorder Nancy Merry.
Audience: Jono Manion, candidate for School Board Position No. 2.
Consent Agenda
Bob Kuehl, Assistant Superintendent of Human Resources and Nora Klewiada, Executive
Director of Human Resources presented an election list for administrators and certificated
employees for the 2015-2016 school year. Camille Diaz Hackler moved and Doug Benjamin
seconded the motion to approve the list as submitted. The motion carried unanimously.
Adjournment
The special meeting adjourned at 12:01 p.m.
