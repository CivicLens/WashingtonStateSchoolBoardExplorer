5. Reports
• Budget Presentation and Report 2020-2021
Jim Frey and interim Finance Director, Jim Baker presented the final budget for the 2020-2021 school
year. The budget reflects expenditures and revenues based on Enrollment, General Funds, ASB, Debt
Services, Capital Projects, and Transportation. Jim Baker also spoke about the effects that COVID-19 may
have and how to maximize the use of resources to provide the best possible education for students in the
Lynden School District. The Board thanked Patti Fairbanks, Jim Baker and Mandi Lenaburg for their efforts
in finalizing the 2020-2021 budget.
