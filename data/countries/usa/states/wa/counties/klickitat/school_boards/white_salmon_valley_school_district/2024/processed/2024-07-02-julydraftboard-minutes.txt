The regular meeting of the Board of Education, White Salmon Valley School District, Klickitat and Skamania Counties, White
Salmon, WA was called to order at 7:01 p.m. Thursday, July 18, 2024, at the WSVSD Board Room, Park Center Building, located
at 170 NW Washington St., White Salmon, WA 98672, and via a ZOOM public meeting. Present: Board Chair Laurie Stanton,
Vice-chair Peter Harkema, Kris Ostness (online), Matthew Byrne, Pat Dempsey, Superintendent Rich Polkinghorn, and guests.
Board Chair Laurie Stanton led the Board in the flag salute.
Additions/Deletions to Agenda:
• Addition of: Reports: Superintendent Report
• Deletion of: Consent Agenda Item B: Affidavit of Payroll and Expenditures for the Month of July 2024
Matthew Byrne moved to adopt the agenda as amended. Seconded by Peter Harkema. Carried.
Public Hearing 2024-2025 Final Budget:
a) Open Public Hearing: At 7:03 p.m. Laurie Stanton opened the public hearing.
b) Board Member Discussion: Superintendent Rich Polkinghorn shared a brief update on the four-year projections for the
budget.
c) Public Comment on the 2024-2025 Budget: At 7:05 p.m. Laurie Stanton opened the hearing for public comment on the
2024-2025 Budget. No comment was made.
d) Close Public Hearing: At 7:06 p.m. the public hearing was closed.
Reports:
a) Superintendent Update: Superintendent Polkinghorn provided a staffing update. The district recently completed the
hiring of several vacancies and is working to fill the remaining open positions. Superintendent Polkinghorn shared about
the CLC A-List summer school program. Superintendent Polkinghorn updated the Board regarding his recent work with
the ESD 112 Constructions Service Group on the Small School Modernization Grant and the future work to be
completed.

Public Comments: No public comment was received.
Consent Agenda: Peter Harkema moved to accept the Consent Agenda; June 27, 2024, Regular Board Meeting Minutes and
personnel actions. Seconded by Matthew Byrne. Carried.
Action Items:
a) Second Reading of Board Policies: Pat Dempsey motioned to approve the policies. Seconded by Peter Harkema.
Carried.

The next regular board meeting will be at 7:00 p.m., Thursday, August 22, 2024, held at the WSVSD Board Room, Park Center
Building, located at 170 NW Washington St., White Salmon, WA 98672. The meeting will also be hosted remotely via ZOOM.
