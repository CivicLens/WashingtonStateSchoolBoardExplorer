2. Advisory Voting Protocol
Superintendent VanderYacht explained that student advisory votes will now be taken for each
agenda item that is voted on by the board. This is a result of updated policy 1250- Students on
Governing Boards that was approved at the February 9, 2023 meeting. Advisory votes and
director votes will now be tallied and noted in the meeting minutes.

9. Board Workshop
The board reviewed a draft the Capital Facilities Planning Framework which included a proposed
timeline, as well as proposed roles and responsibilities for a Capital Facilities Long-Term Planning
Committee and a Capital Bond Planning Task Force.
Discussion was held on strategies to obtain data from the community to inform decision-making
on the bond.
Next steps will be to create and finalize members of Capital Bond Planning Committee and Long
Range Facilities Committee. The Board would also like to move forward with beginning the
surveying process to obtain necessary data points.
10. Information from Board Members
Tonya Hickman spoke about sharing our story to legislators regarding Special Education.
CJ Costanti, Tonya Hickman, Cooper Graddon and David VanderYacht will be attending the
WASA legislative conference this weekend and will report on their experience at the next board
meeting.
