2.5 Request to Join Vaping Lawsuit

Meeting :

Mar 16, 2022 - Regular Board Meeting via
Zoom

Type :

Action (Consent)

Recommended Action :

As superintendent, I request the board authorize the
district to join the lawsuit against Juul and Altria.

Public Content
Staff Contact: Greg Baker, Superintendent
Explanation of Issue:
According to the Washington Healthy Youth Survey, approximately 30% of 12th graders in Washington
self-reported vaping in the last 30 days of 2018, which was a 50% increase from a 20% vaping rate in
2016 (the Health Youth Survey was not administered in 2020 due to the COVID pandemic).The district is
requesting board approval to work with the Stevens Clay law firm to join the pending vaping lawsuit
against Juul and Altria, the successor to Philip Morris. School districts have joined nationwide, seeking
damages for illegally targeting underage children. There is no upfront cost for joining the lawsuit, which is
scheduled to go to trial in approximately November 2022, if not settled prior to trial.
The attached memo provides more information about the lawsuit.
