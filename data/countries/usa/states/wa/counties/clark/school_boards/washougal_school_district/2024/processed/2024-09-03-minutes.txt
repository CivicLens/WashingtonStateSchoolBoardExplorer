1. Special Meeting, 4:00 p.m.
Jim Cooper called the meeting to order at 6:30 p.m.
A. Board Director District #2 Interviews
Interim Superintendent Aaron Hansen said there is one candidate here tonight to interview, and introduced Jane Long.
Aaron said he appreciated Jane being here. The board interviewed the candidate, and then recessed to executive
session at 6:54 p.m. for approximately 30 minutes.
2. Executive Session
A. RCW 42.30.10(b) - Real Estate
B. RCW 42.30.110(1)(b) - Evaluate the Qualifications of a Candidate for Appointment to Elected
Office
The board returned from executive session at 7:25 p.m.
3. Board Organization
A. Discussion and Appointment of Board Director District #2 Position
Jim Cooper thanked all the candidates for their interest in the district. Chuck Carpenter moved to appoint Jane Long
to the Board Director District #2 position, and Sadie McKenzie seconded. The board voted by roll-call vote as follows:
McKenzie, aye; Carpenter, aye; Royer, aye; Cooper, aye. The motion carried unanimously. Jim again thanked everyone
for their interest in the position. Jane will take the oath of office and join the board at our next meeting on September
10th.
4. Adjourn
Chuck Carpenter moved to adjourn at 7:45 p.m., and Ida Royer seconded. The motion carried unanimously.
