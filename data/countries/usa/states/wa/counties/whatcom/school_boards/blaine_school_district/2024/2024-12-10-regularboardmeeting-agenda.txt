2. Public Comment
Subject :

A. Public Comment

Meeting :

Dec 10, 2024 - Regular Board
Meeting

Category :

2. Public Comment

Type :

Procedural

Public Content
A dedicated email has been established for the purpose of accepting written public comments prior to
each regular meeting. Patrons may send their written comments tocomments@blainesd.org. All
comments received at least 3 hours prior to the meeting will be provided to each director for review
individually prior to the meeting. Written comments received less than three (3) hours prior to the start of
the meeting may not be reviewed by the directors until after the meeting.
Pursuant toPolicy 1400, the board will set aside up to thirty (30) minutes at the beginning of the regular
monthly meeting to allow an opportunity for the public to address the board on any topic within the scope
of the board's responsibility. These public comments will be included in the official audio recording of the
meeting. By sharing comments during the meeting, an individual agrees to having their remarks recorded.
During the Public Comment section of the agenda, the board president will recognize those who wish to
provide oral comments to the directors.Patrons who wish to address the board during the Public
Comment sectionmust sign in between 11:45am-11:55amand acknowledge their topic of interest. The
following information should be included when signing up to address the board with oral comments:
1. Name
2. Contact Information (Address/Phone/Email)
3. Topic of Interest

Participants are asked to keep their comments to three (3) minutes. At any given board meeting,
comments on a common topic may be limited to three (3) individuals. Other individuals interested in
making comments specific to that common topic will be asked to put their comments into writing, sent to
the email address shown above.
Parents and patrons are asked to follow Policy 4220and its related procedure regarding complaints about
staff, students, or programs. Those presenting concerns in an open meeting venue, are asked to refrain
from reference (by name) to a specific staff member or a specific student. Any complaints specific to an
individual staff member or student should be submitted in writing to the email address shown above.
Anyone with questions prior to the meeting may contact the administrative assistant at 360-332-0711.

I. Reorganization of the Board: Election of
Officers

Meeting :

Dec 10, 2024 - Regular Board Meeting

Category :

5. Action Items

Type :

Action

Public Content
Pursuant to Policy 1210, the board will elect a president and a vice president to serve one-year terms in
2025, as well as a legislative representative for a two-year term from 2025-2027.
President Freeman will call for nominations for president.
The nominations will close and the president will be elected by a roll call vote.
The elected board president will take office and call for nominations for vice president.
The nominations will close and the vice president will be elected by roll call vote.
The elected board president will call for nominations for legislative representative.
The nominations will close and the legislative representative will be elected by a roll call vote.
The meeting will continue following the published agenda.
