  6. New Business
Information: A. Budget Update--See Link
Carol Lewis shared a budget update that is found at the following link. https://docs.google.com/presentation/d/1GsuFQbGkbXr1_ZMiKFdgo9RPtIvBTNqkMx1aJMwYFf8/edit?usp=sharing
Ms. Lewis shared that 83% of the budget is staffing. She also mentioned staffing reductions have been made and the cash balance has decreased over the past few years.
Ms. Lewis noted she would like to do a little education concerning school budgets at Board meetings from this point forward to help all better understand.
She explained enrollment projection and the importance of projecting a bit lower than the number who actually show up for school.
Ms. Lewis explained that federal program grants are very specific in how the money needs to be spent.
The budget needs to be built with capacity.
