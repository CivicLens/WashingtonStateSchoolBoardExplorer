Tuesday, February 14, 2017
School Board Visit - Bellingham High School
The Board of Directors of Bellingham Public Schools regularly visits a different school throughout the
school year. The purpose of the visits is to learn how each school is delivering upon The Bellingham
Promise. The directors will visit Bellingham High School at 7:30 a.m. on Feb. 14, 2017. Bellingham High
School is located at 2020 Cornwall Avenue.
