ARLINGTON SCHOOL DISTRICT NO. 16

NOTICE OF SPECIAL MEETING
EVENT ATTENDANCE
NOTICE is hereby given that the Board of Directors of Arlington School District No. 16, Snohomish County, Washington (the
District) will hold a Special Board Meeting on Wednesday, November 6, 2024, at 6:00 pm, for the purpose of attending the
Student Advisory Council Youth Mental Health Forum at Post Middle School, 1220 E. 5th Street, Arlington, Washington.
This special meeting is for the purpose of attending the Student Advisory Council Youth Mental Health Forum at Post Middle
School, 1220 E. 5th Street, Arlington, Washington. Public comment will not be taken during this meeting, and no action will be
taken.
