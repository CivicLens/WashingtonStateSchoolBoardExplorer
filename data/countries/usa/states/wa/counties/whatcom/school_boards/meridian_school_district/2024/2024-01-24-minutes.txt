MERIDIAN SCHOOL DISTRICT #505
WORK SESSION & REGULAR SCHOOL BOARD MEETING
MEETING HELD IN PERSON & VIA ZOOM
January 24, 2024

REGULAR MEETING
The regular meeting was called to order by Board President Steve Cowin at 5:00 p.m.
Board members present at the meeting were Steve Corwin, Jana Haugen, and Michelle Simmons.
BOARD WORK SESSION
The board moved immediately into a WSSDA Board Bootcamp work session. No action was taken during
the work session.
Board Director’s Marty Gray and Jarrod Karuza joined the regular meeting at 6:00 pm.
RECONVENE REGULAR BOARD MEETING
Board President Steve Corwin welcomed everyone to the regular board meeting at 6:00 pm.
The pledge of allegiance was recited.
Student Board Representative Kennedy Brzozowski was present at the meeting. Morgan Adams was
absent from the meeting.
Dr. Everett asked the Board to make a motion to add a late student travel request approval into the

consent agenda. Jarrod Karuza made a motion to modify the consent agenda to include student travel
as presented. Jana Haugen Seconded the motion. The motion passed 5-0.

A motion to adopt the agenda was made by Michelle Simmons and seconded by Jana Haugen. The
motion passed 5-0.
CONSENT AGENDA
A. Approval of Meeting Minutes
B. Personnel Report
C. Accounts Payable
D. Payroll
E. Approval of WGU Affiliation Agreement (AAMC Model)
F. Addition of Instructional Materials Committee Members
G. Student Travel
A motion to adopt the consent agenda was made by Jana Haugen and seconded by Michelle Simmons.
The motion passed 5-0.
PRESENTATIONS
PLC Presentations
MHS CTE team gave a PLC presentation to the Board. The presentation highlighted the standard area of
focus. The team shared initial data, the resources the team is using to assist with improving student
progress, the rubric the team uses to measure progress.

REPORTS
Special Services Intervention Program Report
Director of Special Services, Aaron Jacoby presented a Special Services Intervention Programs Report to
the Board. Aaron spoke about health services, family services, and special education. Aaron’s report
included celebrations and goals for the future.
PUBLIC COMMENT
There was no public comment.
FINANCE REPORT
Finance Report
Director of Finance, Daniel Yorton reported to the Board on our enrollment projections, each budget
status report, and budget projections.
OLD BUSINESS

Second Review and Approval: MSD Board Operating Protocols

Dr. Everett presented the MSD Board Operating Protocols for second review and approval. Student
Representative favor approval. A motion to adopt MSD Board Operating Protocols as presented was made
by Michelle Simmons and seconded by Jana Haugen. The motion passed 5-0

Second Review and Approval: Revised 2023-24 Plan of Work
Dr. Everett presented the Revised 2023-24 Plan of Work for second review and approval. Student
Representative favors approval. A motion to adopt the Revised 2023-24 Plan of Work as presented was
made by Jarrod Karuza and seconded by Jana Haugen. The motion passed 5-0

NEW BUSINESS

First Reading: Policy and Procedure 4260F - Use of School Facilities

Assistant Superintendent Kurt Harvill gave an overview of the policy and procedure WSSDA review and
classification process. Kurt presented Policy and Procedure 4260 Form - Use of School Facilities to the
Board for a first review. This will be brought back before the Board for second review and approval at the
February 7, 2024, Regular Board meeting.
First Reading: Policy 5005 - Employment and Volunteers: Disclosures, Certification
Requirements, Assurances and Approval
Assistant Superintendent Kurt Harvill presented Policy 5005 - Employment and Volunteers: Disclosures,
Certification Requirements, Assurances and Approval to the Board for a first review. This will be brought

back before the Board for second review and approval at the February 7, 2024, Regular Board meeting.

First Reading: Policy 5280 - Separation from Employment
Assistant Superintendent Kurt Harvill presented Policy 5280 - Separation from Employment to the Board

for a first review. This will be brought back before the Board for second review and approval at the
February 7, 2024, Regular Board meeting.
Appoint Board Director Legislative and WIAA Representatives

Steve Corwin announced that Michelle Simmons has volunteered to serve as the Board Director
Legislative Representative and Jana Haugen volunteered to serve as the WIAA Board Representative.
SUPERINTENDENT’S REPORT
Dr. Everett began his report by recognizing the maintenance and operations team for the work they have
recently done with the challenging weather we recently experienced. Dr. Everett shared information
about a partnership opportunity with UW AmplifyLearn.AI, the upcoming replacement levy election, and
the newsletter that will be arriving in mailboxes soon. Dr. Everett also spoke about legislative and
advocacy work being done as well as upcoming training opportunities.

INFORMATION FROM STUDENT BOARD REPRESENTATIVES

Everett. The groups focus is on wellness this month, discussions also included the upcoming principal
interviews.

Several Board Directors attended the Share Fair at MP3 and described the event as magical.
