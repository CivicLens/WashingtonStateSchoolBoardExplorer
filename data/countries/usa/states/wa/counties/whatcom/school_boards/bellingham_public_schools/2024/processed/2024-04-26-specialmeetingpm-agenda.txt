Friday, April 26, 2024
Special Meeting, 6:30 p.m.
The Board of Directors will attend the WSSDA DA-1 Regional Meeting on Saturday, April 27, 2024 at
Friday Harbor High School, 45 Blair Avenue, Friday Harbor, Washington.
While in Friday Harbor, the board will hold a special meeting on Friday, April 26 at 6:30 p.m. at Duck
Soup, 50 Duck Soup Lane, Friday Harbor, WA. The purpose of the meeting is to discuss school district
initiatives, issues and business in an unstructured manner. This is not a regular School Board meeting
and will not include public comment time.
For more information, call 360-676-6501.
