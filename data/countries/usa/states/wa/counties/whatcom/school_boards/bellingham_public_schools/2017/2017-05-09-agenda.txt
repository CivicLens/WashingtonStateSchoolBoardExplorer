Tuesday, May 9, 2017
School Board Visit - Sunnyland Elementary School
The Board of Directors of Bellingham Public Schools regularly visits a different school throughout the
school year. The purpose of the visits is to learn how each school is delivering upon The Bellingham
Promise. The directors will visit Sunnyland Elementary School at 7:30 a.m. May 9, 2017. Sunnyland
Elementary School is located at 2800 James St.
