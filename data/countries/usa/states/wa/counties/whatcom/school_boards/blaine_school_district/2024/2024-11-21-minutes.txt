  A. Executive Session
President Freeman announced that the board will move into executive session for the purpose of considering the selection of a site or the acquisition of real estate by purchase and to review the performance of a public employee. The executive session was anticipated to last approximately thirty (30) minutes, or until 6:00 p.m. Any extension of time will be announced.
