3. Workshop Activities
Subject :

K. Board Self-Assessment Results/Review, Adjusting
Board/Superintendent Goals & Professional Development
Plan to Support Goals

Meeting :

Aug 19, 2024 - Board All-Day Workshop

Type :

Discussion, Information

Public Content
Submitted by:
Dr. Chrys Sweeting, Superintendent
Background Information:
Board Policy Policy 1820&Procedure 1820Prequire the Board to assess its own performance in terms of generally accepted principles of
success at the conclusion of each academic year. Annually, usually in June, Board members complete the Washington State School
Director's association (WSSDA) self-assessment individually. In the fall, the collective results are analyzed to identify areas of strength and
areas for growth in order to develop goals and a professional development plan.
Administrative Considerations:
During this agenda item, the combined results of the Board self-assessment completed last July will be analyzed. Strengths and areas for
growth will be identified and Board and Superintendent Goals and the Professional Development Plan will be adjusted for 2024-25.
