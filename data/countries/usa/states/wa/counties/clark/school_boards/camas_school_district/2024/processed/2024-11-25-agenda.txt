B. Apptegy Contract
Meeting :

Nov 25, 2024 - School Board Meeting

Category :

7. SYSTEMS/PATHWAYS: INFORMED AND
RESPONSIBLE USE OF RESOURCES

Type :

Action

Public Content
Under this contract, Camas School District will receive comprehensive website and mobile app services,
primarily focusing on website functionality. The agreement includes website redesigns, static content
migration, hosting, ongoing support, service, and training, ensuring seamless functionality and
accessibility across the district. Additionally, the contract provides for the development of a mobile app for
iOS and Android to serve the district and its 14 schools. The Thrillshare Publishing Platform, accessible
on desktop and mobile devices, enables efficient content management and communication, supporting
approximately 7,300 students. These integrated services enhance communication, accessibility, and user
engagement across the district. The cost of this contract will be offset by not renewing a separate
communication tool called ThoughtExchange.
