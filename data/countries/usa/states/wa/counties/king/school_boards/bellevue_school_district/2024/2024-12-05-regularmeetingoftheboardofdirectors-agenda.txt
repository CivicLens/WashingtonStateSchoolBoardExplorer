Procedure 3510P - Associated Student Body, Part C.2states:
2. At the beginning of each school year, the primary advisor at each school will review the schools existing constitution and bylaws
with the student council. Any proposed changes to the constitution or bylaws must be voted on by the student body, and then
submitted to the Board for final approval on the Consent Agenda. In the absence of such approved changes, the constitution and
bylaws will remain in effect from year to year.
