Washtucna School District #109
Regular Board of Directors Meeting
Washtucna, WA
March 26, 2024

The meeting was called to order at 7:00 p.m. by board chair Jake Sitton. Other board members
present were Steve Crouse, Alta Hunt and Derrick Kehn. Bridget Coon arrived at 7:02 p.m. Also
attending in person were Temporary Superintendent Staci Gloyn, Business Manager Katie Lasen,
and Carol Blauert. Attending online via Zoom was Rob Clark, Debbie Sitton and La Rena Draper.
ADOPTION OF THE AGENDA
Steve Crouse moved to approve the agenda. Passed 4-0.
COMMENTS FROM GUESTS
La Rena Draper updated the board on FFA state contests and asked for approval of State FFA
overnight May 9 and 10 for 15-17 students. Steve Crouse moved to approve the overnight with
the District paying for qualifying students. Passed 5-0.
The board took a field trip with Transportation Director Carol Blauert to check out the newest
school bus and tour the bus barn.

Steve Crouse motioned to approve the consent agenda. Passed 5-0.
BUSINESS MANAGER’S REPORT
Budget Status Reports for February were included in board packets and discussed at the meeting.
PRINCIPAL’S REPORT
Staci Gloyn updated the board on March events including Speech, Spelling, Math Competition,
Band and FFA trips, Missoula Children’s Theater, Reading Month, Sports, Screenagers, and
Ballet. She also updated the board on Graduation Pathway options.
ADMINISTRATIVE REPORT
Staci Gloyn reminded the board of WSSDA Trainings. Steve Crouse moved to approve the
graduation date as set (June 1st at 11 a.m.). Passed 5-0. Math adoption samples were available for
board preview.
Steve Crouse motioned to approve the second reading of the 2024-25 School Calendar. Passed

5-0. Steve Crouse motioned to approve the 2023-24 Highly Capable Program Plan. Passed 5-0.
EXECUTIVE/CLOSED SESSION
Steve Crouse motioned for the board to go into executive session for personnel pursuant to
RCW 42.30.110(1)(g), with possible action to follow, for 10 minutes beginning at 7:41 p.m.
Passed 5-0. Executive session ended at 7:52 p.m. The board set a special meeting for April 8,
2024 at 6 p.m. for hiring purposes for the Superintendent position.
ADJOURNMENT
Steve Crouse motioned to adjourn the meeting at 7:53 p.m. Passed 5-0.
