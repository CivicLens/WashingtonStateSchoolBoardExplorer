City of Pullman
Council Minutes
Special Meeting
City Council Chambers
190 SE Crestview Street
Pullman, WA 99163
6:00 p.m.
Monday, November 4, 2024
Members present
Mayor Francis Benjamin, Ann Parks, Nathan Weller, Pat Wright, Megan Guido, Eric
Fejeran, Carla De Lira, Trymaine Gaither
The meeting was called to order at 6:00 p.m.
A. Roll Call
This meeting was the second budget workshop in the 2025-2026 Budget process.
Finance Director Jeff Elbracht presented the budgets for the Finance and City Clerks
Departments.
Human Resources Manager Deb Peschel presented the budget for the Human
Resources Department.
IT Manager Morgan Sherwood presented the budget for the IT Department.
City Administrator Mike Urban presented the budgets for the Legislative, Executive, and
City Attorney Departments.
Communications Coordinator Meghan Ferrin presented the budget for the
Communications Department.
Community Development Director RJ Lott presented budgets for the Planning,
Economic Development, Graphic Information Services, and Protective Inspections
Departments.
Public Works Director Sean Wells presented budgets for Public Works, Transit, Arterial
Streets, Utilities, Utility Capital Projects, Stormwater, and the Equipment Rental Divison.
Finance Director Jeff Elbracht presented budgets for the Firefighters Relief and Pension
Fund, the Civic Improvement Fund, the Bond Fund, the Transportation Benefit District,
the Metropolitan Parks District, and Pullman-Moscow Regional Airport.
R. Adjournment
Motion by Pat Wright, second by Ann Parks
Final Resolution: Motion Carries
Yea: Ann Parks, Nathan Weller, Pat Wright, Megan Guido, Eric Fejeran, Carla De Lira,
Trymaine Gaither
The meeting adjourned at 8:47 p.m.
