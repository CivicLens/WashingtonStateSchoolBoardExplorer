Tuesday, May 1, 2018
School Board Visit - Happy Valley Elementary School
The Board of Directors of Bellingham Public Schools regularly visits a different school throughout the
school year. The purpose of these visits is to learn how each school is delivering upon The Bellingham
Promise. The directors will visit Happy Valley Elementary School at 7:30 a.m., Tuesday, May 1. Happy
Valley Elementary School is located at 1041 24th St.
