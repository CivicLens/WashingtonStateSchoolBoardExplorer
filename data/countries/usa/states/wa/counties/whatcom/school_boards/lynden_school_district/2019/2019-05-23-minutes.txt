May 23, 2019
School Board Meeting
Minutes
Lynden High School Library
6:30 P.M.
1. Call to Order, Welcome, Pledge of Allegiance and Roll Call
Meeting called to order at 6:32 P.M. Board members in attendance were Steve Jilk, Brian Johnson, CJ Costanti and
David Vis. Board member Kelli Kettels was absent and excused. Student Director Ruby VanderHaak was present
and Cynthia Torres was absent and excused.
2. Approval of Meeting Minutes
• May 9, 2019
• CJ Costanti motioned to approve the meeting minutes seconded by David Vis. Motion carried by voice
vote.

• 2018-19 Capital Projects $72.65 Warrants 51764 – 51764 dated May 15, 2019

• 2018-19 ACH General Fund $3,171.06 Warrants 181900343 – 181900360 dated May 15, 2019
• 2018-19 ACH ASB Fund $347.71 Warrants 181900361 – 181900363 dated May 15, 2019
• 2018-19 ACH General Fund $102.66 Warrants 181900364 – 181900364 dated May 15, 2019
• Personnel Action Items 5.23.19
• Donations 5.23.19
Brian Johnson motioned to approve the Consent Agenda seconded by CJ Costanti. Motion carried by voice vote.
4. Information from the Audience
None at this meeting.
5. Open Public Hearing
• Surplus of Property – Main St. Campus (old LMS) Ball Fields
No public comment pertaining to the surplus of this property
6. Adjourn Public Hearing
Public Hearing adjourned at 6:40 P.M.
7. Finance and Operations
Budget Update - April
The Board reviewed information on the budget for April containing a summary of financial status, enrollment,
general fund comparative, and revenues and expenditures.

The Board moved the approval of the School Improvement Plans until after the SIP presentations by elementary
principals.
8A. Action Items
• Access Agreement
The Board reviewed the Access Agreement with the City of Lynden for the Glenning St. property.
Brian Johnson motioned to approve the Access Agreement seconded by CJ Costanti. Motion carried by voice vote.
•

Purchase and Sale Agreement – Glenning St.
The Board reviewed the Purchase and Sale Agreement for surplus of the Glenning St. property.
CJ Costanti motioned to approve the Access Agreement seconded by CJ Costanti. Motion carried by voice vote.
9A. Board Workshop
• School Improvement Plans (SIP’s)
Elementary Principals, Courtney Ross, Ellie Meenk, Wendy Bartlett and Patrick McClure shared the work
that is taking place in their schools. The principals addressed the questions of: How does your school know
students are making progress towards expected learning targets?; How is your school responding when
students are not making progress?; How is your school working to meet the social emotional needs of
students as part of a Multi-tiered System of Support?
Reconvene Meeting
Meeting reconvened at 8:20 P.M. to take action on School Improvement Plans.
8B. Action Items
• School Improvement Plans (SIP)
Brian Johnson motioned to approve the 4 elementary SIP’s seconded by David Vis. Motion carried by voice vote.
9B. Board Workshop
• District Planning
The Board discussed the levy collection options that are currently approved by voters and if there should be
an adjustment after the levy lift from the Legislature. Conversation about the timeline for maintenance &
operations levy, and technology & facilities levy, and high school and elementary school capital bond will
continue.
10. Student Director
After interviews with some fine candidates, it was decided that Riley Anderson will join Ruby VanderHaak as
the Student Directors for the 2019-2020 school year.
Brian Johnson motioned to approve the selection of Student Director seconded by CJ Costanti. Motion carried by
voice vote.
Meeting Adjourned at 8:41 PM
