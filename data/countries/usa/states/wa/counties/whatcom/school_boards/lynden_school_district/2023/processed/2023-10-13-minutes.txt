Board Meeting

10/13/2023
Lynden School District
Board Room

1. Call to Order, Welcome & Pledge of Allegiance, Roll Call
Meeting called to order by CJ Costanti at 8:00am. Board of Directors in attendance were CJ
Costanti, David Vis, Ken Owsley, Cindy Petersen, and Tonya Hickman. Superintendent David
VanderYacht was also in attendance.
2. Board Handbook
Board Directors came prepared with suggested revisions to the board handbook. Discussion was
held regarding those revisions. Director Costanti offered to draft an updated version of the
handbook with the revisions agreed upon.
3. WSSDA Positions
In preparation for this discussion, board directors previously submitted unranked lists pertaining
to WSSDA legislative positions which they believe to be top priority. Those lists were combined to
form a master list for review and ranking during today’s board meeting. The top 10 highest
ranked priorities will be submitted to WSSDA as Lynden’s input for priority positions for 2024.
4. District Performance Measures
Superintendent VanderYacht presented the board with his recommendation of district
performance indicators with the goal of finalizing a set of measures to be used for tracking
student learning goals.
5. Adjourn Meeting
CJ Costanti motioned to adjourn the meeting at 12:14pm
