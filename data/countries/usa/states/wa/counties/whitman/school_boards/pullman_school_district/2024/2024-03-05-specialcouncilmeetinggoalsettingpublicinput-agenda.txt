Tuesday, March 5, 2024
Special Council Meeting - Goal Setting Public Input
The City of Pullman will host a drop-in open house on Tuesday, March 5, 2024. Residents are
encouraged to attend anytime between 6:00 and 8:00 p.m. to share ideas and suggestions with the
Council on its upcoming Goals & Priorities setting session.
*
NOTICE OF SPECIAL MEETING OF PULLMAN CITY COUNCIL
YOU AND EACH OF YOU ARE HEREBY NOTIFIED that I have called
a special meeting of the Pullman City Council to be held:
DATE: Tuesday, March 5, 2024
TIME: 6:00 p.m. to 8:00 p.m.
LOCATION: Pullman Senior Center; 190 SE Crestview; Building B
THE PURPOSE OF THE SPECIAL MEETING IS:
To provide an opportunity for the public to give input and suggestions on Council Goals.
DATED this 29th day of February 2024.
Francis Benjamin, Mayor
