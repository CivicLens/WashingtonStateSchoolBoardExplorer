3. Reports
Superintendent's Report: Kelly Garza, Superintendent, reported:
Today was the 1st Day of School. Mr. Garza spent the day visiting and welcoming students and families
throughout the District. He observed a successful school day. Schools will monitor daily attendance and
contact those families that did not make it to school today. He reported meeting with Admim staff to
recap the day and problem solve any issues that may have occurred during the day. He reported today’s
enrollment at 2814 which is below projected. Mr. Garza explained the 10-day count and its significance
to the District’s budget and enrollment projection for the school year. He commented kindergarten
enrollment is at projected and slightly increasing.
An update of the District’s accountability audit. He met with State Auditors to review the final findings.
The audit did not report any findings and an outstanding outcome.
The all staff Welcome Back event was held last week and Mr. Garza reported staff recived a goodie bag.
He showed the board his presentation and talked about the events of the day.
Shannon Torres reported GEARUP will be giving graduation packets to RISEUP 2023 & 2024 students.
The packets include school supplies, backpack, blanket and other items.
Construction Update: Kevin Hill, Executive Director, presented and explained the recommendation to
hire a construction/architect team. He explained the process and next steps. The Board engaged in
discussion and reviewed the recommendation.
Action: 4.11 Approval of the recommendation to hire Construction/Architect Design-Builder Team:
Absher Construction/ALSC Architects as presented.
Motion by Lorenzo Alvarado, second by Javier Vela.
Final Resolution: Motion Carries
Yea: John Francisco, Sarah Divina, Lorenzo Alvarado, Lori Garcia, Javier Vela

General, Capital Projects, Debt Service, ASB, Transportation Vehicle, and Private Purpose.
