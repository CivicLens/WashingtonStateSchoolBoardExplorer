Special Meeting
Spokane Public Schools
September 25, 2024
4:30 p.m.
    OPENING OF MEETING
The Spokane Public Schools Board of Directors convened in a Special Meeting on September 25, 2024, at 4:31 p.m. in-person and via Zoom Meetings.
  EXECUTIVE SESSION
The Board went into Executive Session for the purpose of considering the acquisition of a site of real estate for lease or purchase and to evaluate the qualifications of a candidate for appointment to elected office.  President Lockwood announced that they would be in Executive Session until 4:52 p.m. Executive Session was extended until 5:00 p.m.  The Board went into Open Session at 5:00 p.m.
  OPEN SESSION
A. Legislative Priorities - LATE HANDOUT
The intent of this agenda item is to continue discussion on legislative priorities for the upcoming legislative session. No discussion.
  B. WSSDA General Assembly and Legislative Priorities Discussion
The intent of this agenda item is to review the topics, positions, and priorities under review during the 2024 General Assembly.  Priorities are due October 8, 2024.
  C. District Book Study Discussion
The intent of this agenda item is to discuss the book, The Anxious Generation.  They discussed their main take-aways from the book and strategies to continue community learning opportunities.
  ADJOURNMENT
President Lockwood adjourned the Special Meeting at 5:50 p.m.
