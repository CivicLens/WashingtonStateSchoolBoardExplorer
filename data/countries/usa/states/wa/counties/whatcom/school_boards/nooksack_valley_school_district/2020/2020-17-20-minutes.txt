M.C. 5-0

MINUTES APPROVED
REGULAR MEETING

A few community members shared their desire to get our children
back to in person learning. Mark Johnson shared some of the
background that goes into these types of decisions. He also shared
the tentative plan to move back to grade 6-12 hybrid model after the
first of the year. The tentative date was January 12th, but further
discussions were needed. Discussion occurred throughout.
