Wednesday, November 6, 2024
ESD Board Study Session
The Ellensburg School District Board of Directors will hold a School Board Study Session on Wednesday,
Nov. 6, 2024 at 5:30 PM at Ida Nason Aronica Elementary School. In the interest of public health, the
meeting will also be available via Zoom. Comments can be submitted to the Board at board@esd401.org.
The Districts internet provider will be performing emergency maintenance on our internet circuit this
evening from 5:30-8 PM. Due to this required maintenance, the District will not be able to provide Zoom
coverage of tonights Board Study Session. You are welcome to attend in person at Ida Nason Aronica
Elementary, located at 2100 N. Cora St.
