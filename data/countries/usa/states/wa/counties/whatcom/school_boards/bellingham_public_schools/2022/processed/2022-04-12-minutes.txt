School Visit
Principal Nick Hayes and staff shared some of the work being done at Happy Valley and
the connections to The Bellingham Promise. This included the school’s mission and
vision statements and work associated to key strategies such as Great Teaching with
Strong Support and Equity, Diversity and Inclusion. The Board and Executive Team
visited classrooms to see the work in action.
