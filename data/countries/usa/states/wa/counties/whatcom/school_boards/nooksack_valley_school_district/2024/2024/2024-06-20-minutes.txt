SUPERINTENDENTS REPORT
Megan Vigre gave a description of the grant opportunity the district would like approval to move
forward in applying. This grant would give the district an opportunity to partially fund a Turf field at
the high school.
• Ty Elmendorf shared his participation in a recent family/community meeting endorsing the need
for a turf field. He also shared that it would be the priority of the district to not use district funding
for the turf field that would otherwise go to support instruction.
• Megan Vigre shared about the local Nooksack Indian Tribe’s participation in feedback around a
turf field and shared that the Tribe had many ideas for how this could benefit our tribal community
and families.
