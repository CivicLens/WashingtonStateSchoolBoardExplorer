The board, comprised of members Mike Bly, Justin Voelker, Theresa Noack, and Jonathan Horsley, unanimously approved the consent agenda, which included financial vouchers for December bills totaling $273,267.27, as well as payroll payments. A significant donation of $1,899.58 from the East Valley Booster Club was approved for volleyball uniforms. The meeting also featured a strategic planning presentation led by Dr. Tammy Campbell, and Mr. Voelker reported on a recent wrestling match, while Ms. Noack reminded attendees about the upcoming Golden Throne game. The board convened an executive session to review the performance of a public employee, after which the personnel report was approved. The meeting concluded at 7:50 PM.
