Equity Policy 3212 Report & Review
Dr. Slade McSheehy and Dr. Stephanie Spencer presented
Dr. Slade McSheehy and Dr. Stephanie Spenser presented the board policy and a 2024
bi-annual report that included rating policy objectives, implementation actions & reports,
areas for growth and improvement planning, and program priorities relating to racial
equity.

when reporting to the school board.

Spencer and Dr. Slade McSheehy throughout the report.

report the budget status information. The Board will decide on a point person to work
with Kim Mayer.
