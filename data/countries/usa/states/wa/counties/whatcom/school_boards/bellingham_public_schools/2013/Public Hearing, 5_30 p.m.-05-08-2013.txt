Wednesday, May 8, 2013

The Board of Directors will hold a public hearing at 5:30 p.m. Wednesday, May 8, 2013 in Larrabee
Elementary School's gym, 1409 18th Street, Bellingham, WA 98225. The purpose of the public hearing is
to receive verbal or written public comment on the possible retirement/closure of Larrabee Elementary
School for use as a K-5 elementary school and on the school district's analysis of the effects of the
proposed closure.

Public Content
The purpose of the public hearing is to receive verbal or written public comment on the possible retirement/closure of Larrabee
Elementary School for use as a K-5 elementary school and on the school district's analysis of the effects of the proposed closure.
Those who wish to speak to the Board may sign up on the sheet provided from 5:15 to 5:35 p.m. May 8 in the Larrabee
Elementary School gym.
Written public comment forms will also be available for those who prefer not to speak, want to submit both verbal and written
comments, or arrive after the sign-up process is complete. Each person may speak for 3 minutes per the Board's standard public
comment procedure. The public may also email the School Board through their Listening Post.
