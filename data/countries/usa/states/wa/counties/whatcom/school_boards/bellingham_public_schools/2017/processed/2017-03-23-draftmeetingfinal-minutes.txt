Meeting Date and Location

Call to Order/Roll Call
Board President Douglas Benjamin called the meeting to order at 6:15 p.m. Present:
Directors Kelly Bashaw, Douglas Benjamin, Quenby Peterson, Steven Smith,
Superintendent Greg Baker, and recorder Ruth Rasmussen. Mr. Benjamin noted that Ms.
Diaz Hackler is traveling to Denver, CO, to attend the National School Boards
Association (NSBA) Annual Conference.
Audience: no audience presented.
Superintendent’s Announcements and Report: Summary of Dr. Baker’s comments:
 Update on Snow Days: The board reviewed a revised draft of a letter to OSPI outlining
an application for waiver of three school days under WAC 392-129-140. Ms. Bashaw
then made a motion, seconded by Dr. Smith, confirming board approval of the proposed
plan as outlined in the letter to OSPI requesting a three-day waiver from the 180-school
day requirement without the loss of state funding. Unanimous affirmative vote.



Update on 2017-18 Budget: The Executive Team recently met off-site for a work
session on the budget.

Prior to approval of the consent agenda, the board agreed to reorder the agenda so that
Informational Item 5.1 would be considered next.

{Previously 5.0} INFORMATIONAL ITEMS
{Previously 5.1} Annual Report on Asset Preservation Program
Ron Cowan, Executive Director, Capital Projects and School Facilities, said formal board
approval of the annual report is not required. To comply with the provisions of the Asset
Preservation Program, the board must receive an annual report pertaining to the condition
of the district’s facilities that were built after Dec. 31, 1993, and for which the district
received state construction funding assistance. He added that going forward, a draft of the
annual report would be included in the EL-9 monitoring report.
