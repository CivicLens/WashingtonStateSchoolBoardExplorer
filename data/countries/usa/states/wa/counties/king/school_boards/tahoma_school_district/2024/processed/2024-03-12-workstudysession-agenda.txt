The Board will adjourn into Executive Session (according to RCW 42.30.110) at 4:00 p.m. to
discuss one or more of the following:
To evaluate the qualifications of applicants for District employment or to review the
performance of a District employee.
To receive and/or evaluate a complaint against a District officer or employee.
To discuss plans, strategy or proposals made in collective bargaining, grievance or
mediation proceedings.
To consider the selection or acquisition of real estate.
To consider the price at which real estate will be offered for sale/lease.
To review negotiations on the performance of a District-bid contract.
To discuss litigation, potential litigation, or legal risks of a proposed action with District
legal counsel.
To consider information related to data security breach disclosure.
