Tuesday, July 25, 2023
Special Meeting - Public Hearing 5:45 p.m.
Ferndale School District No. 502 School Board Special Meeting - Public Hearing, 5780 Hendrickson Ave,
Ferndale, WA
The Board of Directors will hold a public hearing for the purpose of receiving comments from the public
on the 2023-2024 budget. Any person may appear at the hearing and be heard for or against any part of
such budget, the four-year budget plan, or any proposed changes to uses of enrichment funding under
RCW 28A.505.240.

Written comment may be submitted prior to the public hearing to the following email address:
fsdinfo@ferndalesd.org
The completed budget is on file at the District Office located at 6041 Vista Drive and on our website:
https://www.ferndalesd.org/deptprograms/business-support-services/budget.
Also, at any time that you would like to contact your board members, you may do so by using their
contact information that is displayed on this webpage at this link https://www.ferndalesd.org/school-board
/school-board-contacts.
The video link above will provide access, via Zoom, to the board meeting. The live recording will begin
when the board meeting starts.
