Washtucna School District #109
Special Board of Directors Meeting
Washtucna, WA
March 19, 2024

The special meeting of the Washtucna School Board was called to order at 7:00 p.m. by board
chair Jake Sitton. Attending in-person were board members Jake Sitton, Bridget Coon, Steve
Crouse, Alta Hunt, and Derrick Kehn, and Principal/Temporary Superintendent Staci Gloyn,
Attending online via Zoom were Business Manager Katie Lasen, Rob Clark, and Bryan Visscher.
AGENDA
Steve Crouse motioned to approve the agenda. Passed 5-0.
CONSTRUCTION PROJECT CONTRACTS
Steve Crouse moved to approve the Agreement with Asbestos Central for no more than the
maximum amount listed. Passed 5-0. Steve Crouse moved to approve the Superintendent sign
the Public Works Construction Agreement at no more than the agreed amount. Passed 5-0.
ADJOURNMENT
Bridget Coon moved to adjourn the meeting at 7:13 p.m. Passed 5-0.
