Monday, February 12, 2024
ESD Board Special Meeting: Superintendent Candidate Interview #1
The Ellensburg School District Board of Directors will hold a School Board Regular Meeting on Monday,
Feb. 12, 2024 at 5 PM at Ida Nason Aronica Elementary School. Stakeholders are invited to meet the
candidate from 5-5:45 PM, after which the Board will go into Executive Session to conduct their interview
of the candidate.
In the interest of public health, the meeting will also be available via Zoom. Comments can be submitted
to the Board at board@esd401.org.
