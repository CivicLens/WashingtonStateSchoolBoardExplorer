Thursday, April 2, 2015
Special School Board Meeting Scheduled
The Board of Directors will hold a special meeting at 6:30 p.m., April 2, 2015 in Room 205 of the District
Office, 1306 Dupont St., Bellingham, Wash. The purpose of the meeting is to present an orientation for
individuals interested in serving on the board. Those considering running for school board will receive
important candidate information at this meeting. The filing opportunity for the fall election with the
Whatcom County Auditor's Office is May 11-15.
