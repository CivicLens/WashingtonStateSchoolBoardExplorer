Recommended Action :

To adopt Resolution 2324-08 Adoption of 2024-2025
Reduced Educational Program

Public Content
At the board's regular meeting on April 22, 2024, the board voted to table this action item.
Resolution 2324-08 adopts a Reduced Educational Program Plan for the 2024-2025 school year. This plan for reduction has
been developed by the district's leadership team, incorporating recommendations made by campus and department
administrators. There have also been multiple opportunities for staff and community feedback, including staff presentations on
each campus and department, a staff survey, Supper with the Superintendent presentations, and a special meeting with the
board on February 20, 2024.
In addition to the resolution is the proposal submitted to the board by SEIU Local 925 Blaine School District Chapter Leadership.
