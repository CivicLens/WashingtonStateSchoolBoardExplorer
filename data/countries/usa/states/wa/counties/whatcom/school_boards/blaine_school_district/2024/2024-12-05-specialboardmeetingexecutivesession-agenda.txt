Thursday, December 5, 2024
Special Board Meeting: Executive Session
Opening and adjournment of this meeting will take place in the districts boardroom at 770 Mitchell
Avenue in Blaine. The directors will meet in executive session for the purpose of reviewing the
performance of a public employee. Audio recording of this meeting, not including the executive session,
can now be accessed by clicking on the button marked "Video".

Category :

Public Content
Pursuant to Policy 1410, the board may choose to convene in executive session for specific purposes
allowed by state statute. President Freeman will announce that the board will move into executive
session for the purpose of reviewing the performance of a public employee. The executive session is
anticipated to last two (2) hours, or until approximately 8:00 p.m. Any extension of time will be announced
verbally in the boardroom, and the board may take formal action following the executive session.

Recommended Action :

The board will consider action (motion) specific to acceptance
of the annual performance evaluation of the superintendent as
satisfactory, with concurrent intent to move forward with

extension of the employment contract of the superintendent
through June 30, 2028. Under separate motion, the board will
also consider formally designating the board president and
vice-president to enter into discussion with the superintendent
on employment contract parameters, with direction to the
board president to bring a final contract recommendation to
the full board for review and consideration of approval no later
than the May 27, 2025 regular meeting.

Public Content
After the executive session, the board will reconvene in regular session.
The board will consider action (motion) specific to acceptance of the annual performance evaluation of
the superintendent as satisfactory, with concurrent intent to move forward with extension of the
employment contract of the superintendent through June 30, 2028.
Under separate motion, the board will also consider formally designating the board president and vicepresident to enter into discussion with the superintendent on employment contract parameters, with
direction to the board president to bring a final contract recommendation to the full board for review and
consideration of approval no later than the May 27, 2025 regular meeting.

Motion & Voting
The board voted to table this action item until the regular meeting on Tuesday, December 10, 2024.
