11. Board Work Session
 Esports Club Presentation
Kent Victor, CTE Coordinator, Jody Bogues & Hans Henderson, LHS teachers and club advisors, along with
students Michael Patterson, Matthew Landis, Cole Fierst, and Austin Greenwood presented how the
Esports Club began, what types of games and activities are performed and how to utilize their experiences
in this club for multiple career opportunities. The students shared that the skills teach them about
communication, collaboration, teamwork and planning, and how it builds student relationships and
confidence. The students also shared about opportunities for scholarships at colleges in the Esports.


Middle School Counseling
Lynden Middle School counselors, Coral Bartlett and Laura Lupo provided an overview of the counseling
program at Lynden Middle School. Coral and Laura shared about student and family events and
opportunities to engage with school staff throughout the year and the services and supports that are
provided for each grade level. A strong focus this year is on respect, using PRIDE (Positivity, Respect,
Involvement, Dependability, Effort) characteristics as the unifying theme.



High School Diploma
Ian Freeman, Lynden High School Principal, shared the recent changes to the high school diploma
requirements that will impact Lynden High School and Lynden Academy. Ian provided documents
explaining the different pathways to meet graduation requirements and a 4-year planning guide for
Lynden High School to ensure students complete their high school career and guide them to college and
career readiness.



School Safety
David VanderYacht spoke about the ongoing efforts to improve safety in schools and what steps have
already been taken to provide for a safe environment. David spoke of organizational preparedness and
best practices for incidents and the regular drills and safety meetings taking place throughout the school
year. David gave examples of how employee safety is being addressed for accident prevention.
