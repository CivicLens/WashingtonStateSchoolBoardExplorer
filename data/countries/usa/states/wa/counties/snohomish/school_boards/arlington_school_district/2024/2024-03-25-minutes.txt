  Informational Items
A. Superintendent's Report
Superintendent Sweeting shared that she is trying a new meeting format for Staff Voices. She will be modeling it after the Student Voices events. Beginning in April, there will be four, one-hour meeting opportunities held in different locations and times across the district. The meetings are open to all staff, and will be a conversation around what is working well, what needs improvement and what they would like to see at their schools or in the district in the future. She mentioned that there might be an opportunity for the Board to attend a regional meeting on April 27. She will share more information via email. She reminded the Board that elementary conferences are being held this week, and next week is Spring Break. She also shared that there was a bus accident today, but there were no injuries.

B. Legislative Update and Discussion
Director Kelly reported that she will be attending WSSDA leadership training this week in Olympia. She has a meeting with Melissa Rathbone tomorrow, and will report any pertinent information to the Board at the next regular meeting.
She also shared about the YMCA Mock Trial Program, and encouraged the high school to check into this opportunity.
