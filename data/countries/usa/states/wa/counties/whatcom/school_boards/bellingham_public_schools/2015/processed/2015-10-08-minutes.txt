OPENING ITEMS
Meeting Date and Location:
October 8, 2015 - 1306 Dupont St., Bellingham, Washington
Call to Order/Roll Call: Kelly Bashaw called the meeting to order at 6:15 p.m. Present:
Directors Kelly Bashaw (President), Douglas Benjamin, Kenneth Gass, Camille Hackler,
Steven Smith, Superintendent Greg Baker, and recorder Ruth Rasmussen
Audience:
No Audience Presented
Superintendent's Announcements and Report:
 Lowell Celebration: Dr. Baker thanked those board members who
attended today’s community gathering to celebrate the completion of
renovations to the building, and he expressed appreciation to Ron Cowan,
Executive Director, Capital Projects and School Facilities, and Curtis
Lawyer, Capital Projects Manager, for their roles in leading this project.
 Educational Technology Levy Update: In preparation for the board’s
study session on Oct. 22, Dr. Baker provided a technology levy update,
including an overview of the current technology levy and the projects it
has funded. He summarized the work of the Student Technology Think
Tank, which has been meeting since early September to study the concept
of 1:1 computer technology for students. The group is now in the process
of writing its recommendations, which Dr. Baker will share with the board
during the upcoming study session.
