The Board reviewed information on the budget for May containing a summary of financial

6. Board Workshop
• School Improvement Plan (SIP) Reports - Secondary
Secondary principals, Ellie Meenk, Ian Freeman, Rex Fraser, David Kish, Molly Mitchell-Mumma,
Cyndi Selcho provided their plans to provide support to students by reducing the achievement
gap by using data; assessing health and safety standards, and what their needs are from the
district to help accomplish their goals. They spoke of the transition from elementary school to
middle school and middle school to high school and meeting with teachers to discuss placement
in classrooms and advisory in the next school year and beyond the high school level, and the
evaluation of student needs in academics and social emotion health. for each student by using
the Character Strong and the Home Room programs for social emotional learning for all
secondary students.
The principals thanked the hard work of their staff and the cooperation and patience of
families.
The Board thanked and appreciated the secondary principals on the execution of how they
managed the 2020-2021 school year with caring for both the children and staff.
Brian Johnson motioned to approve the SIP Report seconded by CJ Costanti. Motion carried by voice vote.
