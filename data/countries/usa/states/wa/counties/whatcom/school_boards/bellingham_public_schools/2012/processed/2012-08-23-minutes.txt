BELLINGHAM PUBLIC SCHOOLS
Bellingham, Washington
MINUTES OF THE STUDY SESSION
OF THE
BELLINGHAM SCHOOL DISTRICT
BOARD OF DIRECTORS
August 23, 2012

The Bellingham School Board of Directors met in a study session August 23, 2012 at the District
Office, 1306 Dupont Street, Bellingham, Washington.
President Kelly Bashaw called the meeting to order at 6:00 p.m.

Roll Call
Present: Directors Camille Hackler Steve Smith, Ken Gass, Scott Stockburger, President Kelly
Bashaw, and Superintendent Greg Baker.
The Board was joined by Deputy Superintendent Tom Venable, Assistant Superintendent Ron
Cowan, Executive Director of Human Resources Nora Klewiada, Executive Director of Human
Resources Hank Harris, Executive Director of Teaching and Learning Steve Clarke, Executive
Director of Communications and Community Relations Tanya Rowe, and Executive
Administrator on Special Assignment Rob McElroy for a portion of the meeting.

Agenda
• The Board discussed possible topics for future linkages and study sessions.
• The Board discussed Governance Policies for possible revisions
• The Board heard an update on the facility study and achievement scores
• The Board discussed upcoming professional development opportunities
• The Board discussed Board governance

Adjournment
The meeting adjourned at 9:25 p.m.
