The board identified Tiger Construction as providing the lowest bid and identified alternate bids 1-6, 8 & 9 as

considered after more information is gathered.
Steve Jilk motioned to approve Resolution No. 13-16 seconded by Mike Haveman. Motion carried by voice vote.
