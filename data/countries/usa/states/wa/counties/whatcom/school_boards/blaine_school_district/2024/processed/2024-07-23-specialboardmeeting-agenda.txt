Tuesday, July 23, 2024
Special Board Meeting
The purpose of this meeting is to review and discuss the district's proposed budget for the 2024-2025
fiscal year. Members of the community and staff are invited to attend.

Public Content
Vice-President Lazarus will call the meeting to order, in the district's boardroom, at 6:00 p.m. All directors are expected to be in
attendance, with President Freeman attending telephonically.

2. 2024-2025 Draft Budget
Subject :

A. Review of 2024-2025 Draft Budget

Meeting :

Jul 23, 2024 - Special Board Meeting

Category :

2. 2024-2025 Draft Budget

Type :

Discussion

Public Content
Blaine School District's Financial Consultant, Amber Porter, will provide an overview of the proposed draft budget for the 20242025 fiscal year, including preliminary revenue, expenditure, and enrollment projections. The expectation for maintaining a 6%
ending fund balance remains intact. This presentation will also address directors' specific questions and topics of concern that
were provided in advance of this meeting.
Vice-President Lazarus will open the floor to receive public comment.
The board will take action on approval of the budget at the regular meeting on August 26, 2024.
