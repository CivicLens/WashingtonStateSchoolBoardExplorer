MINUTES OF REGULAR SCHOOL BOARD MEETING
MERIDIAN SCHOOL DISTRICT
MEETING HELD VIA ZOOM
February 24, 2021
Revised February 8, 2023

The regular meeting was called to order by Board Chair, Marty Gray, at 7:00 p.m. Chairman Gray welcomed those
in attendance on the remote Zoom meeting.
Board members present on the Zoom meeting were Sara Bernardy; Marty Gray; Emily Norton; Renier Elenbaas,
Craig Mitchell.
The Pledge of Allegiance was recited.
Emily Norton made a motion to adopt the agenda. Sara Bernardy seconded the motion; the motion passed 5-0.
CONSENT AGENDA
A. Approval of Meeting Minutes
B. Accounts Payable
C. Personnel Report
A motion to adopt the consent agenda was made by Renier Elenbaas and seconded by Craig Mitchell. The motion
passed 5-0.
PUBLIC COMMENT
There was no Public Comment
PRESENTATIONS
MP3 School Presentation
Meridian Parent Partnership Principal, Michelle Siadal, recognized Ethan Oscarson, a MP3 student who started a
school newspaper. Michelle also updated about the changes, triumphs, and challenges of school during the COVID19 period.
OLD BUSINESS
Meridian High School Fee Schedule
Superintendent, James Everett presented a Second Reading the Meridian School District Secondary fee
schedule. Sara Bernardy made a motion to adopt the fee schedule, Craig Mitchell seconded the motion, the
motion passed 4-1.
Second Reading: Policy 2421
Assistant Superintendent, Kurt Harvill presented a Second reading of Policy 2421: Promotion/Retention to the
Board. Renier Elenbaas made a motion to adopt the policy, Craig Mitchell seconded the motion, the motion
passed 5-0.

Revised and re-signed at Feb. 8, 2023 Board meeting to correct typographical errors that were
previously made in original minutes.

Graduation Date
Superintendent, James Everett asked the school board to finalize the 2021 graduation date. Sara Berndardy
made a motion to adopt the graduation date, Craig Mitchell seconded the motion, the motion passed 5-0.
NEW BUSINESS

2021-22 NWESD Whatcom Detention Center Interlocal
Assistant Superintendent, Kurt Harvill presented the 2020-21 NWESD Whatcom Detention Center Interlocal
Agreement to the Board. This is a yearly Interlocal Agreement.
Second First Reading: Policy 2107
Assistant Superintendent, Kurt Harvill presented a first reading of Policy 2107: Comprehensive Early Literacy
Plan.
Second First Reading: Policy and Procedure 3115
Assistant Superintendent, Kurt Harvill presented a first reading of Policy 3115: Homeless Students
Experiencing Homelessness – Enrollment Rights and Services.
FINANCIAL UPDATE
Director of Business and Finance, Daniel Yorton, presented a financial update to the Board.
SUPERINTENDENT’S REPORT
Superintendent, Dr. James Everett presented about the creation of committees and the upcoming vaccination clinic
and MSD Athletics.
INFORMATION FROM STUDENT BOARD MEMBERS
Student Board Members, Karissa Benson and Jenna McCauley shared about the sports teams performances thus
far and leadership class events.
